'''Marianne Lake''' is the former chief financial officer of the bank [[JPMorgan Chase]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/SB10001424127887324307204578129181205549670|title=J.P. Morgan Names Lake as CFO|last=Fitzpatrick|first=Daniel|date=19 November 2012|publisher=The Wall Street Journal|accessdate=5 June 2016}}</ref> She was appointed on 20 November 2012, replacing Douglas Braunstein. [[Blythe Masters]] and Lou Rauchenberger were also considered for the role.
She is a Chartered Accountant from [[Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://find.icaew.com/members/new-york/marianne-lake/Zq5mvi|title=ICAEW Members list'|last=UK|first=ICAEW|date=6 October 2018|publisher=ICAEW website|accessdate=6 October 2018}}</ref> and did her bachelor's degree in physics from the [[University of Reading]] in United Kingdom<ref> She previously trained and worked for [[PwC]]

{{cite web |url=https://www.jpmorganchase.com/corporate/investor-relations/document/2016_senior_management_biographies.pdf |title=2016 Senior Management Biographies |last=JPMorgan Chase & Co |first= |publisher=JPMorgan Chase & Co |date=December 31, 2016 |website=JPMorganChase.com |access-date=October 6, 2018}}</ref>.

Lake has been the chief executive officer of consumer lending at JPMorgan Chase since May 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.jpmorganchase.com/corporate/investor-relations/press-releases.htm |title=JPMorgan Chase Appoints Jennifer Piepszak Chief Financial Officer; Marianne Lake Named Chief Executive Officer of Consumer Lending â€“ Encompassing Card Services, Home Lending and Auto Finance |publisher=[[JPMorgan Chase]] |accessdate=April 17, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2019/04/17/jp-morgan-names-new-cfo-and-marianne-lake-moves-to-top-consumer-lending-role.html |last1=Wang |first1=Christine |last2=Frost |first2=Wilfred |date=April 17, 2019 |title=JP Morgan names new CFO, and Marianne Lake moves to top consumer lending role |publisher=[[CNBC]] |accessdate=April 17, 2019}}</ref>

Lake has emphasized the company's role as a technological innovator.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.businessinsider.com/marianne-lake-says-jpmorgan-is-a-tech-company-2016-2|title=JPMORGAN: 'We are a technology company'|last=Crowe|first=Portia|date=23 February 2016|publisher=Business Insider|accessdate=5 June 2016}}</ref>
