'''Jessica Uhl''' (born 1968) is the [[chief financial officer]] of [[Royal Dutch Shell]] from March 2017 to replace [[Simon Henry]]. In October 2019 she was ranked 24th on Fortune list of Most Powerful International Business Women. <ref>{{Cite web|title=Most Powerful Women International|url=https://fortune.com/most-powerful-women-international/2019/|access-date=2020-12-02|website=Fortune|language=en}} </ref> She is the second woman to have been appointed as Shell's CFO, after [[Judy Boynton]] (2001–2004).<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/business/2016/12/15/names-jessica-uhl-replace-simon-henry-finance-chief/|title=Shell names Jessica Uhl to replace Simon Henry as finance chief|publisher=|accessdate=19 December 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.shell.com/media/news-and-media-releases/2016/jessica-uhl-to-succeed-simon-henry-as-chief-financial-officer.html|title=Jessica Uhl to succeed Simon Henry as Chief Financial Officer|publisher=|accessdate=19 December 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2016-12-15/shell-says-jessica-uhl-to-succeed-simon-henry-as-finance-chief|title=Shell Replaces Finance Chief Simon Henry With Jessica Uhl|date=15 December 2016|publisher=|accessdate=19 December 2016|via=www.bloomberg.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.energyvoice.com/oilandgas/126863/shell-names-jessica-uhl-cfo/|title=Shell names Jessica Uhl as CFO – News for the Oil and Gas Sector|date=15 December 2016|publisher=|accessdate=19 December 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.financialdirector.co.uk/2016/12/15/shell-appoints-jessica-uhl-as-cfo-simon-henrys-successor/|title=Shell appoints Jessica Uhl as CFO Simon Henry's successor – Financial Director|publisher=|accessdate=19 December 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.upi.com/Jessica-Uhl-to-replace-Simon-Henry-as-Shell-CFO/2191481797365/|title=Jessica Uhl to replace Simon Henry as Shell CFO|publisher=|accessdate=19 December 2016}}</ref>

Uhl was raised in California and has degrees from the [[University of California, Berkeley]], and [[INSEAD]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=Jessica Uhl, Royal Dutch Shell PLC: Profile and Biography - Bloomberg Markets|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/profile/person/20017404|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-12-17|website=Bloomberg.com}}</ref> She formerly worked for [[Enron]] and [[Citibank]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://business.rice.edu/person/jessica-uhl|title=Jessica Uhl|date=2016-03-16|website=Jones Graduate School of Business at Rice University|language=en|access-date=2019-12-17}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.shell.com/about-us/leadership/executive-committee/jessica-uhl.html|title=Jessica Uhl|website=www.shell.com|language=en|access-date=2019-12-17}}</ref> She is married to Michael Payne with three children.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.whidbeynewstimes.com/news/a-dutch-treat-at-whidbey-general/|title=A Dutch treat at Whidbey General|date=2007-08-15|website=Whidbey News-Times|language=en-US|access-date=2019-12-17}}</ref>
