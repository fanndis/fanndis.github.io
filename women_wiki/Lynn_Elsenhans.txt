'''Lynn Laverty Elsenhans''' is the former Chairperson, Chief Executive Officer and President of [[Sunoco]].<ref name=bloomberg>{{cite web | url = https://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/people/person.asp?personId=13755250&privcapId=19007| title = Executive Profile Lynn Laverty Elsenhans | publisher = [[Bloomberg L.P.]]| accessdate = March 17, 2017}}</ref>

==Career==
Lynn Laverty Elsenhans served as the company's Chairperson and as the company's Chief Executive Officer and President until 2012. She is also the Chairwoman of Sunoco Partners LLC since October 2008.<ref name=bloomberg/> Prior to joining Sunoco, Elsenhans served as the Executive Vice President of Global Manufacturing for Shell Downstream Inc., a subsidiary of [[Royal Dutch Shell]] Group for more than 28 years.

She also served on Baker Hughes's board of directors from 2012 to July 2017 and sits on the board of GlaxoSmithKline.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://gulfbusiness.com/saudi-aramco-appoints-new-board-members-including-woman/|title=saudi-aramco-appoints-new-board-members-including-woman|date=2018-04-29|work=The Economic Times|access-date=2018-04-29}}</ref>

In April 2018, she was the first woman to be appointed as board member in the state run [[Saudi Aramco]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/international/business/saudi-aramco-appoints-first-woman-to-the-board/articleshow/63959270.cms|title=Saudi Aramco appoints first woman to the board|date=2018-04-29|work=The Economic Times|access-date=2018-04-29}}</ref>

Elsenhans was voted number 10 on Forbes'2009 The 100 Most Powerful Women list.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.forbes.com/lists/2009/11/power-women-09_The-100-Most-Powerful-Women_Rank.html | title = The 100 Most Powerful Women | work = Forbes.com}}</ref>

==Compensation==
While CEO of Sunoco in 2008, Elsenhans earned a total compensation of $12,062,024, which included a base salary of $515,077, a cash bonus of $794,007, stock granted of $8,313,716, and options granted of $2,265,934.<ref>[http://www.equilar.com/CEO_Compensation/Sunoco_Lynn_Laverty_Elsenhans.php 2008 CEO Compensation for Lynn Laverty Elsenhans] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091101110644/http://www.equilar.com/CEO_Compensation/Sunoco_Lynn_Laverty_Elsenhans.php |date=2009-11-01 }}, Equilar.com</ref>

==Education==
Lynn Laverty Elsenhans received a B.A degree in Applied Mathematics from [[Rice University]] and a M.B.A degree from [[Harvard Business School]].<ref name=bloomberg/>
