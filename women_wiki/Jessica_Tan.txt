'''Jessica Tan Soon Neo''' ({{zh|s=陈舜娘|zh|t=陳舜娘|p=Chén Shùnniáng}}; born 28 May 1966) is a [[Singapore]]an. She is currently the [[Speaker of the Parliament of Singapore|Deputy Speaker of the Parliament of Singapore]] with [[Christopher de Souza]] and the chairman of the East Coast Town Council.

She is also the MP of [[List of Singapore MPs|Member of Parliament]] for [[East Coast GRC]]. She is a [[Roman Catholic]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.sg/mp/jessica-tan-soon-neo?viewcv=Jessica%20Tan%20Soon%20Neo |title=Archived copy |access-date=2012-02-06 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160213141102/http://www.parliament.gov.sg/mp/jessica-tan-soon-neo?viewcv=Jessica%20Tan%20Soon%20Neo |archive-date=2016-02-13 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

== Politics ==
Tan became an MP for the first time after the [[2006 Singapore general election|2006 general election]], when Tan's five man team, representing the [[People's Action Party]], defeated the [[Workers' Party of Singapore]] with 63.86% of the votes for East Coast GRC. The votes in the 2011 general election narrowed to 54.8% for the PAP.

In the [[2015 Singaporean general election]], she returned to Parliament after her team defeated the Worker's Party with 60.7% of the popular vote, an increase of 5.9%.

She was part of the PAP team defending [[East Coast GRC]] in the [[2020 Singaporean general election|2020 general election]],<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=GE2020: From East Coast to Bukit Panjang, 7 key battlegrounds to watch|url=https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/singapore/ge2020-7-key-battlegrounds-nomination-day-12886644|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200702115938/https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/singapore/ge2020-7-key-battlegrounds-nomination-day-12886644|archive-date=2 July 2020|access-date=1 July 2020|website=CNA|language=en}}</ref> which eventually won with a 53.41% vote share against WP.

On 31 August 2020, Tan was elected as deputy speaker of the parliament, alongside Christopher de Souza.<ref>{{cite news | url=https://www.straitstimes.com/politics/parliament-christopher-de-souza-and-jessica-tan-elected-as-deputy-speakers-of-parliament | title=Parliament: Christopher De Souza and Jessica Tan elected as Deputy Speakers of Parliament | accessdate=31 August 2020 | archive-date=31 August 2020 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200831092026/https://www.straitstimes.com/politics/parliament-christopher-de-souza-and-jessica-tan-elected-as-deputy-speakers-of-parliament | url-status=live }}</ref>
