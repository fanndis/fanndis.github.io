'''Marquess of Borghetto''' ({{lang-es|Marqués de Borghetto}}) is a [[noble title]] in the [[Spanish nobility|peerage of Spain]], granted originally in the [[Duchy of Parma|peerage of Parma]] to Catalina de Bassecourt, by [[Philip, Duke of Parma|Philip I of Parma]], member of the [[Spanish royal family]] and younger brother of [[Charles III of Spain]], on July 1765.<ref>[https://www.edicioneshidalguia.es/?product=elenco-de-grandezas-y-titulos-nobiliarios-espanoles-2018 Real Asociación de Hidalgos de España, ''Elenco de Grandezas y Títulos Nobiliarios Españoles'', Ediciones Hidalguía, Vol. 50 (Madrid, 2018), p. 167]</ref><ref>[http://www.diputaciondelagrandeza.es/guiadetitulo/ Search of title "BORGHETTO, marqués de"]</ref>

Catalina de Bassecourt was the honorary [[Lady-in-waiting|lady-in-waiting]] of [[Elisabeth Farnese]], Queen of Spain as wife of [[Philip V of Spain|Philip V]] and mother of the Duke of Parma, and later of [[Maria Luisa of Parma|María Luisa of Parma]].<ref>[https://books.google.es/books?id=VR2Y7Dnnjo8C&pg=PA36&lpg=PA36&dq=Catalina+de+Bassecourt+thieulaine&source=bl&ots=5V0I7W8kjf&sig=ACfU3U3ZGDdLaVReI-eZ4uttY8FMxy2qxw&hl=es&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwik39n91_TpAhXr1uAKHQBgBX0Q6AEwBHoECAoQAQ#v=snippet&q=catalina&f=false Revista Hidalguía número 1. (Madrid, 1953), p. 41]</ref><ref>[https://books.google.es/books?id=D2n00QMqA4YC&pg=PA504&lpg=PA504&dq=Catalina+de+Bassecourt+thieulaine&source=bl&ots=Com8g5oJnz&sig=ACfU3U1ztqQ2M3Gft5kUbSbF-iu_M1A6Hg&hl=es&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwik39n91_TpAhXr1uAKHQBgBX0Q6AEwBnoECAYQAQ#v=onepage&q=Catalina%20de%20Bassecourt%20thieulaine&f=false Pedralbes: Revista Histórica Moderna, University of Barcelona (1998), p. 504]</ref>

[[File:La famiglia di Don Filippo di Borbone by Giuseppe Baldrighi (C. 1757).jpg|thumb|200px|''The family of [[Philip, Duke of Parma|Philip I of Parma]]'' by [[Giuseppe Baldrighi]]. To the right, the 1st Marchioness of Borghetto, 1757]]

In 1903, [[Alfonso XIII of Spain|Alfonso XIII]] recognised it as a title of the Kingdom and peerage of Spain, issuing a [[Royal Decree-Law (Spain)|Royal Decree]] in favour of Felipe Morenés y García-Alessón, in memory of his ancestors' parmesan title.<ref>[https://books.google.es/books?id=uvs-346euMgC&pg=PA721&lpg=PA721&dq=alfonso+xiii+1903+marques+borghetto&source=bl&ots=ooKnp0tIku&sig=ACfU3U3W_GyVRkjndKIa1krsNjgssBtayA&hl=es&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwilmsig2fTpAhWLA2MBHYeaB3QQ6AEwBHoECAoQAQ#v=onepage&q=alfonso%20xiii%201903%20marques%20borghetto&f=false Revista Hidalguía número 264. (Madrid, 1997), p. 721]</ref>
