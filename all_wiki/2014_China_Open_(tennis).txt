The '''2014 China Open''' was a [[tennis]] tournament played on [[Tennis court#Hard courts|outdoor hard courts]]. It was the 16th edition of the [[China Open (tennis)|China Open]] for the men (18th for the women). It was part of [[ATP World Tour 500 series]] on the [[2014 ATP World Tour]], and the last [[WTA Premier tournaments|WTA Premier Mandatory tournament]] of the [[2014 WTA Tour]]. Both the men's and the women's events were held at the [[Olympic Green Tennis Center, Beijing|National Tennis Center]] in [[Beijing]], [[People's Republic of China|China]], from September 27 to October 5, 2014.
