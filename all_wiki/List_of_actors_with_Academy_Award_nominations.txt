This '''list of actors with Academy Award nominations''' includes all male and female actors with [[Academy Awards|Academy Award]] nominations for lead and supporting roles in [[Film|motion pictures]], and the total nominations and wins for each actor. Nominations in non-acting categories, such as for producing, directing or writing, are not included.

The winners for films released in 2019 are [[Joaquin Phoenix]], best actor for ''[[Joker (2019 film)|Joker]]''; [[Renée Zellweger]], best actress for ''[[Judy (film)|Judy]]''; [[Brad Pitt]], best supporting actor for ''[[Once Upon a Time in Hollywood]]''; and [[Laura Dern]], best supporting actress for ''[[Marriage Story]]''.

The award information is available on the Academy Awards website via dynamically-generated lists for specific actors,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://awardsdatabase.oscars.org/ |title=The Official Academy Awards® Database |publisher=[[Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences]] (AMPAS) |accessdate=April 13, 2017}}{{cbignore}}</ref> and for each year's nominees and winners via a scrolling timeline of all ceremonies.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/oscars/ceremonies |title=Experience over eight decades of the Oscars from 1927.... |publisher=AMPAS |accessdate=January 7, 2018}}</ref>

==Statistics==
<!-- {{Table row counter|tableno=2}} - a template that can be used to check the total count; remove leading "<" & click "Show preview", then cancel or back out -->
A total of 933 actors appear in the list—469 males and 464 females. Non-winning nominees include 317 males and 312 females—a total of 629. One time winners include 130 males and 132 females—a total of 262. Only 43 actors—22 males and 21 females—are [https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Actors_with_multiple_Academy_Award_wins multiple Academy Award winners].

[[Katharine Hepburn]] won four times from twelve nominations—all for lead roles—making her the actor with the most wins in Academy Awards history. [[Daniel Day-Lewis]] has won three times from six lead actor nominations—the most wins for any male in the lead actor category.

[[Meryl Streep]] is the most-nominated actor of all with twenty one nominations. [[Jack Nicholson]] has received the most Academy Award nominations for any male actor with twelve nominations. Both actors have had three wins which included two for lead roles and one for a supporting role.

==List of actors==
The list is designed to be sortable by clicking on any of the column headings; however, sorting is only possible if [[JavaScript]] is enabled in your web browser. If viewing on a mobile device, switch to the ''desktop view'' to enable sorting by clicking on the word ''Desktop'' at the [[List of actors with Academy Award nominations#External links|bottom of the page]].

The initial sort order is by actor surnames. Clicking the same column heading a second time sorts in the reverse order. For example:
* to bring the latest nominees to the top of the list, click twice on the '''Last year''' (nomination year) column heading (first click for ascending order; second click for descending order)
* to see the actors with the most nominations or wins at the top, click twice on the '''Nominations''' or '''Wins''' column heading
* to group the actors by gender, click once on the second column heading for females first; click again for males first
* to sort from youngest to oldest, click the '''Age''' column heading once; click again to reverse the order.
