'''Sir Ralph James Norris'''  {{post-nominals|country=NZL|KNZM}} (born 1949) is a New Zealand banker and businessman. He is chair of [[Contact Energy]],<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.nzherald.co.nz/business/news/article.cfm?c_id=3&objectid=11558438| title=Highly charged AGM for Contact Energy| work=[[The New Zealand Herald]]| date=9 December 2015}}</ref> [[Fletcher Building]] and RANQX Holdings.

He was the chief executive officer (CEO) of the [[Commonwealth Bank]] of Australia (CBA) until 30 November 2011. He was previously CEO of the [[ASB Bank]], the Commonwealth Bank of Australia's New Zealand subsidiary.  He left this role for several years to be the CEO of [[Air New Zealand]] before leaving to head the CBA.

Prior to being CEO at the ASB Bank, Norris was that company's [[chief information officer]].

Norris is an Honorary Fellow of the [[Institute of IT Professionals]], and an ambassador of the [[Australian Indigenous Education Foundation]].

==Education==
Norris was educated at [[Lynfield College]]. He has no formal tertiary qualifications.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.stuff.co.nz/business/better-business/10068628/Sir-Ralph-Norris-shares-his-road-to-success|title=Sir Ralph Norris shares his road to success|last=Rawson|first=Emma|date=21 May 2014|work=Stuff.co.nz|access-date=24 August 2016}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://minterellison.co.nz/our-view/the-great-achiever-an-interview-with-sir-ralph-norris|title=The great achiever.|last=Quinn|first=Cathy|last2=Kavanagh|first2=Lloyd|date=12 May 2017|website=minterellison.co.nz|access-date=23 August 2018}}</ref>

== Honours==
In the [[2006 New Year Honours (New Zealand)|2006 New Year Honours]], Norris was appointed a [[New Zealand Order of Merit|Distinguished Companion of the New Zealand Order of Merit]], for services to business.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.dpmc.govt.nz/publications/new-year-honours-list-2006 | title=New Year honours list 2006 |date=31 December 2005 | publisher=Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet | accessdate=9 June 2019}}</ref> In the [[2009 Special Honours (New Zealand)|2009 Special Honours]] Norris accepted redesignation as a [[New Zealand Order of Merit|Knight Companion of the New Zealand Order of Merit]] following the restoration of titular honours by the New Zealand government.<ref>[http://www.dia.govt.nz/MSOS118/On-Line/NZGazette.nsf/6cee7698a9bbc7cfcc256d510059ed0b/d7761a3b0258fb29cc257612004d01c2!OpenDocument Special Honours List] (12 August 2009) 118 ''[[New Zealand Gazette]]'' 2691</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Gibson |first=Nevil |title=Arise Sir... or Dame... for 70 on honours list |work=[[National Business Review]] |url=http://www.nbr.co.nz/article/arise-sir-or-dame-70-honours-list-106848 |date=1 August 2009 |accessdate=6 April 2011}}</ref>
