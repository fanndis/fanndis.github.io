'''Zhu Jianghong''' ({{zh|c=朱江洪|p=}}; born November 1945) is a [[Chinese people|Chinese]] businessman who served as chairman of the board of [[Gree Electric]] between April 2001 and May 2012.<ref>{{cite news |author1=Ken Kuwahara |title=Looming retirements of big-name CEOs pose another risk for China |url=https://asia.nikkei.com/Business/Business-trends/Looming-retirements-of-big-name-CEOs-pose-another-risk-for-China |accessdate=1 November 2019 |work=nikkei |date=29 May 2014}}</ref> He is a visiting professor at [[South China University of Technology]] and [[Northwest University (China)|Northwest University]]. Zhu has been hailed as "Father of Gree".<ref>{{cite news |title=Zhu Jianghong: my struggle and regret for 24 years at Gree |url=http://www.bestchinanews.com/Science-Technology/12156.html |accessdate=1 November 2019 |work=bestchinanews |date=2 August 2017}}</ref>

==Early life and education==
Zhu was born in [[Zhuhai|Zhongshan County]], [[Guangdong]], in November 1945, during the [[Chinese Civil War]], while his [[Ancestral home (Chinese)|ancestral home]] in [[Xinhui District]] of [[Jiangmen]], Guangdong.{{sfnb|Han Xiao|2017|p=47}} In 1970 he graduated from [[South China University of Technology]], where he majored in machinery.{{sfnb|Han Xiao|2017|p=47&ndash;48}}

==Career==
After university, he was assigned to Baise Mining Machinery Factory ({{zh|s=百色矿山机械厂|labels=no}}) in [[Guangxi]].{{sfnb|Han Xiao|2017|p=48}} A few years later, he was promoted to director of the factory.{{sfnb|Han Xiao|2017|p=48}} In 1988, he returned to his homeland, Zhuhai, becoming the general manager of the Guanxiong Rubber Company ({{zh|s=冠雄塑胶公司|labels=no}}) and the director of Zhuhai Air-Conditional Factory successively.<ref name="cat"/> When the Gree Electrical Equipment Company (formerly Zhuhai City Haili Cooling Engineering Company Limited) was created in 1992, Zhu took over the position of the company's general manager.<ref name="cat"/> He transformed the small factory with an annual production of only 20 thousand are-conditioned appliances into a sector giant with the annual production of 2.5 million units.<ref name="cat">{{cite news |title=hu Jianghong, presidente da Gree |url=http://portuguese.cri.cn/541/2008/09/22/1s96134.htm |accessdate=1 November 2019 |work=CRI.cn |date=22 September 2008 |language=pt}}</ref> In April 2001 he became chairman of the board of [[Gree Electric]], and held that office until May 2012. In 1999 he was proposed as vice president of the China Household Electrical Appliances Association (CHEAA).
