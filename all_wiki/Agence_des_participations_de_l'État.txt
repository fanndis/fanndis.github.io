'''''Agence des participations de l'État''''' ('''APE''')("State participation agency") is a special agency of the [[French Republic]] managing the state's holdings in about 70 firms, including [[Orange S.A.|Orange]], [[Renault]] and [[Air France]].<ref name=lasecon>{{cite web|title=Business in France: The long arm of the state|url=http://www.economist.com/node/9725749|publisher=The Economist|accessdate=19 January 2014}}</ref> It was established in 2004.<ref>{{cite web|title=Agence des Participations de l'État|url=https://www.tresor.economie.gouv.fr/ape|publisher=Site Internet de la direction générale du Trésor|accessdate=19 January 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140117130134/http://www.tresor.economie.gouv.fr/ape|archive-date=2014-01-17|url-status=dead}}</ref>  These firms can all be considered [[Government-owned corporation|state-owned enterprise]]s.

==List of firms==
The agency divides its holdings into enterprises that are [[Public company|freely traded]] on a stock exchange, and those that are not. Major enterprises in 2015, excluding minor entities, were:<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.economie.gouv.fr/agence-participations-etat/Les-participations-publiques|title=Les participations publiques |date=30 April 2015|access-date=21 February 2016|publisher=Agence des participations de l'État|language=French}}</ref>
