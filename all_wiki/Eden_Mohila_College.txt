'''Eden Mohila College''' (known as '''Eden College'''), is a women's college in [[Azimpur]], [[Dhaka]], [[Bangladesh]]. It was established in 1873 in the Farashganj area of Dhaka. In 1878 the school was named after [[Ashley Eden]], Lieutenant Governor of Bengal.<ref name=Banglapedia/> The college moved to its present premises in 1963.<ref name=Banglapedia>{{cite book
