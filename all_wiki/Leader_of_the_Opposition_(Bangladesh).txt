{{Politics of Bangladesh}}

The '''Leader of the Opposition''' ({{lang-bn|বিরোধীদলীয় নেতা  }}) leads the Official Opposition in the [[Jatiya Sangsad]], the national parliament of [[Bangladesh]]. The Leader of the Opposition is normally the leader of the largest party not within the government which is usually the second largest political party in the [[Jatiya Sangsad]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/key_person.html |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2013-06-08 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130602064818/http://www.parliament.gov.bd/key_person.html |archivedate=2013-06-02 }}</ref>

The post also carries weight-age of cabinet minister and is seen comparable to [[Prime Minister of Bangladesh|prime minister]] who is leader of house and also ruling party.
