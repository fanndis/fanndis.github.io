The '''Russian Union of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs''' (RSPP), a lobby group based in [[Moscow]], promotes the interests of [[business sector|business]] in [[Russia]]. It has over 1,000 members, including both private and state-owned companies, factories, and foreign and Russian plants. The RSPP represents the successor in the [[the Russian Federation|Russian Federation]] of the previous USSR scientific and industrial union<ref name=icc2003>{{cite web|title= The Russian Union of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs  (Employers) (RUIE)|url= http://www.hik-russland.de/dateien-de/business-unions-and-associations-in-the-russian-federation.pdf|publisher= International Chamber of Commerce (ICC) and Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Russian Federation|accessdate= 12 April 2013|date= November 2003|url-status= dead|archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20141223002741/http://www.hik-russland.de/dateien-de/business-unions-and-associations-in-the-russian-federation.pdf|archivedate= 23 December 2014}}</ref> founded in the summer of 1990.

[[Arkady Volsky]] founded the Union in 1991<ref name=aslund2007>{{cite book|author= Anders Åslund|title= Russia's Capitalist Revolution: Why Market Reform Succeeded and Democracy Failed?|url= https://books.google.com/books?id=rAYEwq45fioC&pg=PR19|accessdate= 30 March 2013|year= 2007|publisher= Peterson Institute|isbn= 978-0-88132-537-9|pages= 19}}</ref> and headed it until 2005.<ref name=rfe11sep>{{cite news|title=Prominent Russian businessman dies|url=http://www.rferl.org/content/article/1143713.html|accessdate=12 April 2013|newspaper=Radio Free Europa|date=11 September 2006}}</ref> He was succeeded by, {{Interlanguage link multi|Aleksandr Nikolayevich Shokhin|ru|3=Шохин, Александр Николаевич}}, [[Vice Premier of Russia|vice-premier of Russia]] from 1991 to 1994, and subsequently a [[Duma]] deputy for eight years.<ref name=pravda99>{{cite news|title=Prominent Russian business leader dies, colleague says|url= http://english.pravda.ru/news/russia/09-09-2006/84363-volsky-0/|accessdate= 12 April 2013|newspaper=Pravda|date=9 September 2006}}</ref>

The current president is [[Alexander Shokhin]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://eng.rspp.ru/about/president|title=rspp.ru About President page}}</ref> The current vice-president, Igor Yurgens, a graduate of [[Moscow State University]], has served as an adviser to [[UNESCO]] and to the Trade Unions Council, and chaired the [[General Confederation of Trade Unions]] in 1996. Yurgens is sometimes interviewed by Western media on issues concerning Russian industry.

At a meeting of the lobby in July 2009, [[telecommunication]]s executives portrayed the most popular [[VoIP]] programs like [[Skype]] and [[ICQ]] as encroaching foreign entities that the government must control.<ref>
