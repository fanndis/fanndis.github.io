'''The Dorothy and Lillian Gish Prize''' or '''Gish Prize''' is given annually to "a man or woman who has made an outstanding contribution to the beauty of the world and to mankind's enjoyment and understanding of life."<ref name=about>{{Cite web|url=http://gishprize.org |title=The Prize |publisher=The Dorothy and Lillian Gish Prize |accessdate=October 18, 2018 }}</ref> It is among the most prestigious and one of the [[List of the world's richest literary prizes|richest prizes in the American arts]]. The 2017 winner [[Meredith Monk]] received $300,000<ref name=value>{{Cite web|url=http://990s.foundationcenter.org/990pf_pdf_archive/133/133751413/133751413_201706_990PF.pdf |title=Dorothy and Lillian Gish Prize Trust 2017 Form 990-PF |publisher=Internal Revenue Service |accessdate=October 18, 2018}}</ref>. The founders [[Dorothy Gish]] (1898–1968) and [[Lillian Gish]] (1893–1993) were sisters, famous as actresses from the silent era of film and mid-century theatre. About the prize, established in Lillian Gish's will, she said: "It is my desire, by establishing this prize, to give recipients of the prize the recognition they deserve, to bring attention to their contributions to society and encourage others to follow in their path."<ref name=about/> It was established in 1994 by the Dorothy and Lillian Gish Prize Trust and is administered by [[JPMorgan Chase Bank]].<ref name=about/>
