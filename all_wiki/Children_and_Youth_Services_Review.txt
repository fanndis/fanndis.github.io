'''''Children and Youth Services Review''''' is a monthly multidisciplinary journal covering the study of [[social service]] programs pertaining to children and youth. It was established in 1979 and is published by [[Elsevier]]. The [[editor-in-chief]] is Duncan Lindsey ([[University of California, Los Angeles]]). According to the ''[[Journal Citation Reports]]'', the journal has a 2016 [[impact factor]] of 1.226.<ref name=WoS>{{cite book |year=2017 |chapter=Children and Youth Services Review |title=2016 Journal Citation Reports |publisher=[[Clarivate Analytics]] |edition=Science |series=[[Web of Science]]|title-link=Journal Citation Reports }}</ref>
