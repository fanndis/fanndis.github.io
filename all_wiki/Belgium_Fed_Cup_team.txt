The '''Belgian Fed Cup team''', managed by the [[Royal Belgian Tennis Federation|Belgian Tennis Federation]], has been competing in the [[International Tennis Federation]] sanctioned [[Fed Cup]] since the very beginning: 1963. It has won the competition once, in 2001. Both [[Kim Clijsters]] and [[Justine Henin]] won their singles matches to seal the first victory for Belgium against Russia in Madrid.
