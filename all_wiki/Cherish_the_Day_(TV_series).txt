'''''Cherish the Day''''' is an American romantic drama anthology television series created and produced by [[Ava DuVernay]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Petski|first=Denise|title=Ava DuVernay Anthology Series 'Cherish The Day' Ordered By OWN|url=https://deadline.com/2019/03/ava-duvernay-anthology-series-cherish-the-day-ordered-own-oprah-winfrey-1202579200/|work=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|date=March 20, 2019}}</ref> The series chronicles the relationship of one couple, with each episode spanning a single day marking a significant moment. The full season of 8 episodes crossed 5 years. The series stars [[Xosha Roquemore]], [[Alano Miller]] and [[Cicely Tyson]], and the title references the [[Sade (band)|Sade]] song "[[Cherish the Day]]". 

The series premiered on February 11, 2020 on [[Oprah Winfrey Network]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Obenson|first=Tambay|title='Cherish the Day' First Look: OWN Teases Ava DuVernay's 'When They See Us' Follow-Up|url=https://www.indiewire.com/2019/12/cherish-the-day-ava-duvernay-when-they-see-us-own-1202198380/|work=[[IndieWire]]|date=December 19, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Video: OWN Sets Premiere Date and Unveils Official Trailer for New Anthology Drama "Cherish the Day" from Award-Winning Creator Ava DuVernay|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/video/2020/01/16/video-own-sets-premiere-date-and-unveils-official-trailer-for-new-anthology-drama-cherish-the-day-from-award-winning-creator-ava-duvernay-893404/20200116own01/|work=[[The Futon Critic]]|date=January 16, 2020}}</ref> On October 26, 2020, the series was renewed for a second season.<ref>{{cite web|last=Petski|first=Denise|title=Ava DuVernay's 'Cherish The Day' Renewed For Season 2 By OWN|url=https://deadline.com/2020/10/cherish-the-day-renewed-season-2-own-ava-duvernay-1234603355/|work=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|date=October 26, 2020|access-date=October 26, 2020}}</ref>
