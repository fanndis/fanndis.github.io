'''Yahoo! Movies''' (formerly '''Upcoming Movies'''), provided by the [[Yahoo!]] network, is home to a large collection of information on movies, past and new releases, [[Trailer (film)|trailers]] and clips, [[box office]] information, and showtimes and [[movie theater]] information. Yahoo! Movies also includes [[red carpet]] photos, actor galleries, and production stills. Users can read [[film critic|critic's reviews]], write and read other user reviews, get personalized movie recommendations, purchase movie tickets online, and create and view other user's lists of their favorite movies.

==Special coverage==

Yahoo! Movies devotes special coverage to the [[Academy Awards]] with a special Oscars site. The Oscars site includes articles, show coverage, a list of the night's big winners, photos, videos, and polls.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.yahoo.com/movies/tagged/oscars/|title=Oscars|publisher=Yahoo.com|accessdate=January 6, 2016}}</ref>

From 2002 to 2007, Yahoo! Movies was the home of Greg's Previews of Upcoming Movies, an enhanced version of Upcomingmovies.com, written by its creator, [[Greg Dean Schmitz]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2003/digital/columns/upcoming-movies-covers-first-hype-1117885021/|title=Upcoming Movies covers first hype|publisher=Variety.com|first=Travis F|last=Smith|date=April 23, 2003|accessdate=January 6, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://editorial.rottentomatoes.com/author/greg-dean-schmitz/|title=Greg Dean Schmitz|publisher=[[RottenTomatoes.com]]|accessdate=January 6, 2016}}</ref>

Yahoo! Movies also releases special guides, such as the Summer Movie Guide, which contains information on the major releases of the summer with exclusive trailers and clips, photos, box office information, polls, and unique editorial content.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://movies.yahoo.com/summer-movies/|title=Summer Movie Guide 2013|publisher=Yahoo.com|accessdate=January 6, 2016}}</ref>

Additionally, Yahoo! Movies teamed up with [[MTV]] to host a special site for the [[MTV Movie Awards]], which will feature show information and a section where users can submit original movie shorts parodying last year's movies for the chance to win the new award, ''Best Movie Spoof''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.comingsoon.net/movies/news/517338-2007_mtv_movie_awards_spoofs_nominees|title=2007 MTV Movie Awards Spoofs Nominees|publisher=Comingsoon.net|first=Brad|last=Brevet|date=May 29, 2007|accessdate=January 6, 2016}}</ref>
