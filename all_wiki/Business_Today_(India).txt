'''''Business Today''''' is an Indian fortnightly business magazine published by [[Living Media|Living Media India Limited]],<ref>{{cite book|title=Advertising N Promotion|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=QQEiBAAAQBAJ&pg=PA713|year=2009|publisher=Tata McGraw-Hill Education|isbn=978-0-07-008031-7|page=713}}</ref> in publication since 1992. In August 2008 Rohit Saran became the editor of the magazine, replacing [[Sanjoy Narayan]] in the post.<ref>{{cite news|title=Business Today appoints Rohit Saran as editor|url=http://www.campaignindia.in/Article/225012,business-today-appoints-rohit-saran-as-editor.aspx|accessdate=5 May 2016|work=Campaign India|date=25 July 2008}}</ref> It had a second quarter 2010 readership of 338,000 per issue, ninth among English-language magazines surveyed in India.<ref name=H01/>
