The '''Royal Norwegian Ministry of Agriculture and Food''' ({{lang-no|Landbruks- og matdepartementet}}) is a [[Norway|Norwegian]] [[Ministry (government department)|ministry]] established on 17 February 1900, and is responsible for [[agriculture]], [[forestry]] and [[food]] in Norway. It is since January 2019 led by [[Minister of Agriculture and Food (Norway)|Minister of Agriculture and Food]] [[Olaug Bollestad]] ([[Christian Democratic Party (Norway)|Christian Democratic]]). The department reports to the parliament ([[Stortinget]]).

==Organisation==
The ministry is divided into the following sections:<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.regjeringen.no/en/dep/lmd/The-Ministry-of-Agriculture-and-Food/The-Ministrys-departments.html?id=651 |title=The Ministry’s departments |accessdate=2007-11-25 |author=Norwegian Ministry of Agriculture and Food}}</ref>
* Political staff
* Communication unit
* Department of Administrative and Economic Affairs
* Department of Forest- and Natural Resource Policy
* Department of Food Policy
* Department of Agricultural Policy
* Department of Research, Innovation and Regional Policy

===Political staff===
* Minister [[Sylvi Listhaug]] ([[Progress Party (Norway)|Progress Party]])
* State Secretary [[Hanne Blåfjelldal]] (Progress Party)

===Subsidiaries===
Under the ministry there are four administrative agencies and two state-owned companies:
* [[County governor (Norway)|County Governor]], or ''Fylkesmannen'' ([http://www.fylkesmannen.no official site]) Regional authority of the Government, with a Governor in each of 18 counties.
* [[Norwegian Agriculture Authority]], or ''Statens landbruksforvaltning'' ([http://www.slf.dep.no official site]) Authority for the agriculture industry.
* [[Norwegian Food Safety Authority]], or ''Mattilsynet'' ([http://www.mattilsynet.no official site]) Controls all aspects of food safety, including agriculture, import and trade.
* [[Reindeer Husbandry Administration]], or ''Reindriftforvaltningen'' ([http://www.reindrift.no official site]) Authority for the reindeer husbandry industry.

[[Statsforetak|State enterprises]]:
* [[Statskog]] ([http://www.statskog.no official site]) Manages the state owned forests and natural property.

Limited companies:
* [[Staur Farm]], or ''Staur Gård'' ([https://web.archive.org/web/20070929100853/http://www.staurgard.no/ official site]) A farm.
