'''''BW Businessworld''''' is an Indian business magazine. The magazine was published by [[Ananda Publishers|ABP Group]], whose most prominent publications are ''[[The Telegraph (Kolkata)|The Telegraph]]'', Anandabazar Patrika, Sananda, Anandamela and others.  On 19 September 2013 ABP Group owners [[Ananda Publishers]] sold ''Businessworld'' to Anurag Batra, owner of media group [http://www.exchange4media.com/ exchange4media],<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://etailingindiaexpo.com/speakers/anurag-batra/|title=Anurag Batra Â« India's Largest eCommerce Platform|website=etailingindiaexpo.com|language=en|access-date=5 February 2017}}</ref> and Vikram Jhunjhunwala, who runs investment banking and asset management firm Shrine Capital for an undisclosed amount.<ref name="dealc">{{cite news|author = Pranali Shah|title = ABP Group to Sell Its Business Magazine - BusinessWorld|url = http://www.dealcurry.com/2013095-ABP-Group-To-Sell-Its-Business-Magazine-BusinessWorld.htm|accessdate = 2 August 2015|work = DealCurry|date = 5 September 2013|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20150923213203/http://www.dealcurry.com/2013095-ABP-Group-To-Sell-Its-Business-Magazine-BusinessWorld.htm|archive-date = 23 September 2015|url-status = dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thehindubusinessline.com/companies/abp-group-sells-businessworld-magazine/article5146667.ece|title=ABP Group sells Businessworld magazine|work=The Hindu Business Line}}</ref>

''BW Businessworld'' offices are located in [[New Delhi]], [[Mumbai]], [[Bangalore]] and [[Chennai]].
