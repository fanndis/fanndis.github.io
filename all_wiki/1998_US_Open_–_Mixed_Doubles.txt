{{main article|1998 US Open (tennis)}}

[[Manon Bollegraf]] and [[Rick Leach]] were the defending champions but lost in the first round to [[Lindsay Davenport]] and [[Jan-Michael Gambill]].

[[Serena Williams]] and [[Max Mirnyi]] won in the final 6&ndash;2, 6&ndash;2 against [[Lisa Raymond]] and [[Patrick Galbraith]].
