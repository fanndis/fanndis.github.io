'''Dion Joseph Weisler''' (born 20 August 1967<ref name="Dion Weisler Contact Info">{{Cite web|url=https://www.linkedin.com/in/dion-weisler-4355183/detail/contact-info/|title=Dion Weisler Contact Info|date=2 May 2019|website=LinkedIn}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://beta.companieshouse.gov.uk/officers/quE7kF1fhu4kR2k1lpV_F0P66do/appointments|title=Dion WEISLER - Personal Appointments (free information from Companies House)|author=|date=|website=beta.companieshouse.gov.uk|accessdate=24 August 2017}}</ref><ref name="bloomberg1">{{cite web|author=|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/people/person.asp?personId=241848331&privcapId=108856 |title=Executive Profile: Dion J. Weisler |publisher=Bloomberg |date= |accessdate=2016-08-27}}</ref>) is an Australian-born businessman, and was the CEO of [[HP Inc.]] from November 2015, when HP Inc. and [[Hewlett Packard Enterprise]] split into separate companies,<ref name="bloomberg1"/><ref>{{cite web|author=Madhumita Murgia |url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/technology/2016/04/09/hp-boss-dion-weisler-apples-strategy-is-apples-strategy-but-we/ |title=HP boss Dion Weisler: Apple's strategy is Apple's strategy but we want to create categories |publisher=Telegraph.co.uk |date=2016-04-09 |accessdate=2016-08-27}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;United Kingdom&nbsp; |url=http://www8.hp.com/uk/en/hp-information/executive-team/team.html#dion-weisler |title=Leadership team &#124; HP United Kingdom |publisher=.hp.com |date= |accessdate=2016-08-27}}</ref><ref name="fortune1">{{cite web|url=http://fortune.com/2014/10/21/hp-dion-weisler/ |title=Who is Dion Weisler? |publisher=Fortune.com |date=2014-10-21 |accessdate=2016-08-27}}</ref> to November 2019.

Weisler has a bachelor's degree in computing from [[Monash University]].<ref name="fortune1"/> He is married with two children, and lives in [[Palo Alto]], California.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.boardroominsiders.com/executive-profiles/1004523/HP-Inc./Dion-Weisler |title=Executive Profile &#124; HP Inc. &#124; Dion Weisler &#124; Customer Intelligence |publisher=Boardroominsiders.com |date= |accessdate=2016-08-27}}</ref> Weisler is a pilot who owns and flies a [[Pilatus PC-12]].<ref name="Pilatus">{{cite web | url=https://www.flightglobal.com/news/articles/pilatus-names-three-customers-for-new-pc-12-ngx-461704/ | title=Pilatus names three customers for new PC-12 NGX | publisher=FlghtGlobal | accessdate=24 October 2019}}</ref>

On November 1, 2019, Weisler stepped down as CEO of HP Inc due to "a family health matter".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2019/08/22/hp-ceo-dion-weisler-to-step-down.html|title=HP CEO Dion Weisler will step down 'due to a family health matter'|accessdate=2019-11-01}}</ref>
