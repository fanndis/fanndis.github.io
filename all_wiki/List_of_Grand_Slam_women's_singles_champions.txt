List of Women's singles [[Grand Slam (tennis)|Grand Slam]] tournaments [[tennis]] champions. Note that some major changes have taken place over the years that have affected how many titles have been won by various players. Most notable included the elimination of the challenge round in 1922, the opening of the French national championships to international competition in 1925, and the admission of professional players in 1968 (the start of the "Open" era). Since then, 55 women have won at least one grand slam.<ref>ESPN Sports Almanac 2006, page 814</ref><ref>World Book Encyclopedia 2008 T p166a</ref><ref>Compton Encyclopedia 1975 ST p301</ref><ref>Concord Encyclopedia</ref><ref>CBS Sports "[http://www.cbssports.com/tennis/history/slams/women] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090204095407/http://www.cbssports.com/tennis/history/slams/women# |date=2009-02-04 }}"</ref><ref>Top End Sports "[http://www.topendsports.com/events/tennis-grand-slam/] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090710195114/http://www.topendsports.com/events/tennis-grand-slam/# |date=2009-07-10 }}"</ref><ref>[https://www.usatoday.com/sports/tennis/00wim/sampras2.htm] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110122235012/http://www.usatoday.com/sports/tennis/00wim/sampras2.htm# |date=2011-01-22 }}"</ref><ref>Tennis 28 Slams "[http://www.tennis28.com/slams/wins_alltime.html] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090722022238/http://www.tennis28.com/slams/wins_alltime.html# |date=2009-07-22 }}"</ref>
