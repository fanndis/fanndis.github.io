'''''The Alcalde''''' ({{IPAc-en|æ|l|ˈ|k|æ|l|d|i}}; {{IPA-es|alˈkalde|lang}}) has been the alumni magazine of [[University of Texas at Austin|The University of Texas at Austin]] since 1913, and is published by the university's [[alumni association]], the [[Texas Exes]].<ref name="Handbook">[https://tshaonline.org/handbook/online/articles/eetcw "Texas Alcalde"], ''[[Handbook of Texas|Handbook of Texas Online]]'' (Retrieved 19 March 2012).</ref>

The magazine was named for [[Oran M. Roberts]], the governor who signed the university into existence and whose nickname was "Old Alcalde."<ref name="Handbook"/><ref>[http://www.chron.com/opinion/editorials/article/Break-with-the-past-Changing-a-dorm-name-marks-a-1613469.php "Break with the past: Changing a dorm name marks a good step toward healing UT’s troubled racial history"], ''[[Houston Chronicle]]'', 14 July 2010.</ref>

It is published six times a year and mailed to the 96,000 members of the Texas Exes.
