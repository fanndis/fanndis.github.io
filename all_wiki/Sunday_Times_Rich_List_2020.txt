The '''''Sunday Times Rich List 2020''''' is the 32nd annual survey of the wealthiest people resident in the United Kingdom, published by ''[[The Sunday Times (UK)|The Sunday Times]]'' on 17 May 2020.<ref name="auto">{{cite news|title=The Sunday Times Rich List |url=https://www.thetimes.co.uk/sunday-times-rich-list |date=17 May 2020|work=The Sunday Times|editor1=Watts, Robert|accessdate=17 May 2020}}</ref>

The List was edited by Robert Watts who succeeded long-term compiler [[Philip Beresford]] in 2017.

The List was previewed in the previous week's ''Sunday Times'' and widely reported by other media.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/business/2020/may/16/rich-list-women-uk-25-female-billionaires|title=Women of means: UK now home to 25 female billionaires. Kirsten Rausing of Tetra Pak family tops 2020 Sunday Times Rich List with £12.1bn fortune|work=The Guardian}}</ref>
