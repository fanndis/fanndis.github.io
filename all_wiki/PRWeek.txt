'''''PRWeek''''' is a [[trade magazine]] for the [[public relations]] industry. The original UK edition, edited by Danny Rogers, has been in print for 24 years. There is also a US edition, which launched in 1998,<ref name=mas>{{cite news |author=Mark Sweney |title=Haymarket title says sorry for data leak |url=https://www.theguardian.com/media/2008/jan/31/digitalmedia.pressandpublishing |accessdate=31 July 2016 |work=[[The Guardian]] |date=31 January 2008}}</ref> as well as a German edition. The magazine is published by the UK's largest independent publishing group, [[Haymarket Media Group]].<ref>{{cite news |title=From PR Week to PR Month - trade magazine scraps weekly edition |url=http://www.thedrum.com/news/2013/09/03/pr-week-pr-month-trade-magazine-scraps-weekly-edition |accessdate=31 July 2016 |work=The Drum |date=3 September 2013}}</ref> The US edition, which is independent from the UK edition, is published by Haymarket Media.<ref name=mas/> The company has representation in Asia, via ''Campaign Asia-Pacific'' magazine.

''PRWeek'' also produces research about the UK public relations industry including the annual ranking of Top 150 PR consultancies and Salary Survey as well as surveys into industry-relevant issues such as [[Employee retention|staff retention]].

In October 2013, the UK print edition of ''PRWeek'' changed from a weekly to a monthly edition;<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.prweek.com/article/1215718/pr-industry-welcomes-new-monthly-edition-prweek |title=PR industry welcomes new monthly edition of PRWeek |work=PRWeek |accessdate=19 April 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Last weekly edition of PR Week as Haymarket takes print edition monthly |url=http://www.pressgazette.co.uk/last-weekly-edition-pr-week-haymarket-takes-print-edition-monthly/ |accessdate=10 January 2017 |work=[[Press Gazette]] |date=4 September 2013}}</ref> in September 2016 it switched from monthly to being bi-monthly.
