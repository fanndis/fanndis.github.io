'''Jessica Elizabeth Sanchez'''<ref name=ftl06142012>{{cite web|url=http://www.familytreelegends.com/records/calbirths?c=search&first=jessica+elizabeth&last=sanchez&spelling=Exact&4_year=1995&4_month=8&4_day=4&5=female&7=san+diego&SubmitSearch.x=30&SubmitSearch.y=5 |title=California Births, 2005 – 1995 |publisher=Family Tree Legends |accessdate=June 14, 2012}}</ref> (born August 4, 1995) is an American singer-songwriter. She rose to prominence following her finish as the runner-up on the [[American Idol (season 11)|eleventh season]] of ''[[American Idol]]''.<ref name="billboard.biz">{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.biz/bbbiz/industry/record-labels/american-idol-s-jessica-sanchez-signs-with-1007467552.story|title=American Idol's Jessica Sanchez Signs With Interscope, Kellie Pickler Released By Sony Nashville|date=June 29, 2012|work=Billboard|accessdate=November 1, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.beautymania.biz/2012/05/entertainment-paradise-american-idol_23.html|title='American Idol'Season 11 Winner is Phillip Phillips!|author=BeautyMania.Biz editors|date=May 23, 2012|publisher=BeautyMania.Biz|accessdate=May 23, 2012}}</ref><ref name="no-regrets">{{cite news|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/american-idol-runner-up-jessica-sanchez-i-have-no-real-regrets-20120524|title='American Idol' Runner-up Jessica Sanchez: 'I Have No Real Regrets'|last=Locker|first=Melissa|date=May 24, 2012|newspaper=[[Rolling Stone]]|accessdate=May 25, 2012}}</ref><ref name="runner-up">{{cite news|url=http://news.manilastandardtoday.com/2012/05/25/heartbreak-loss-for-jessica-as-phillip-takes-idol-title/|title=Heartbreak loss for Jessica as Phillip takes ‘Idol’ title|last1=Gunio|first1=Butch|date=May 25, 2012|newspaper=[[Manila Standard Today]]|accessdate=May 25, 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120526011332/http://news.manilastandardtoday.com/2012/05/25/heartbreak-loss-for-jessica-as-phillip-takes-idol-title/|archive-date=May 26, 2012|url-status=dead|last2=?|first2=Maricel}}</ref>

==Early life==
Jessica Elizabeth Sanchez was born in [[Chula Vista, California]] on August 4, 1995 to Edita Sanchez (née Bugay), whose family is from [[Samal, Bataan]] in the [[Philippines]],<ref name="inquirer20120428">{{cite web|url=http://newsinfo.inquirer.net/183767/jessica-sanchez-makes-it-to-idols-top-5-rocker-elise-testone-out|title=Jessica Sanchez makes it to Idol's Top 5; rocker Elise Testone out|agency=Associated Press|date=April 28, 2012|work=Newsinfo|accessdate=April 28, 2012}}</ref> and Gilbert Sanchez, a [[Mexican Americans|Mexican American]] originally from [[Texas]] and is an [[aviation ordnanceman]] First Class Petty Officer in the United States Navy Reserves.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/U.S.NavyReserve/posts/366983916683620|title=Navy Reserve – American Idol finalist Jessica Sanchez is... – Facebook|accessdate=April 21, 2015}}{{Primary source inline|date=March 2020}}</ref> She is the eldest of three children, having two younger brothers.<ref name="eastlake20120414">{{cite web|url=http://www.eastlaketimes.com/jessican-sanchez-gets-save-from-idol-judges/|title=Jessican Sanchez-Gets "Save" From Idol Judges|work=Eastlake Times|date=April 14, 2012|accessdate=April 14, 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150224013400/http://www.eastlaketimes.com/jessican-sanchez-gets-save-from-idol-judges/|archive-date=February 24, 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref> Sanchez was raised in [[Eastlake, San Diego County, California|Eastlake]], [[Chula Vista, California|Chula Vista]].

==Career==

===2006–2011: Career beginnings===
In 2005, Sanchez performed "[[Respect (song)|Respect]]" by [[Aretha Franklin]] on the music television show ''[[Showtime at the Apollo]]''.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://lifestyle.abs-cbn.com/articles/390/jessica-sanchez-the-girl-with-fulfilled-dreams|title=Jessica Sanchez: The Girl with Fulfilled Dreams|website=lifestyle.abs-cbn.com|access-date=July 31, 2019}}</ref> In 2006, she competed on the [[America's Got Talent (season 1)|first season]] of [[NBC]]'s talent show competition ''[[America's Got Talent]]'',<ref name=":0" /> in which she became a wildcard act but ultimately did not qualify for the grand finals. During the [[2008 NFL season]], Sanchez performed "[[The Star-Spangled Banner]]" prior to the [[Los Angeles Chargers|San Diego Chargers]]' match against the [[New York Jets]]. In 2009, she recorded a cover "[[Don't Stop the Music (Rihanna song)|Don't Stop the Music]]" by [[Rihanna]] for [[iTunes]] with independent record label [[Spin Move Records]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://a-tunes.net/2009/11/24/jessica-sanchez-covers-rihannas-dont-stop-the-music/|title=Jessica Sanchez Covers Rihanna's "Don't Stop The Music"|author=a-Tunes.net|date=November 24, 2009|publisher=a-Tunes.net|accessdate=March 25, 2012}}</ref> Sanchez collaborated with the label once again in 2010 for a cover of "[[I'd Rather Go Blind]]" by [[Etta James]] for YouTube and was released as an exclusive download on the label's website.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nme.com/nme-video/youtube/id/WfkLO-Zk0tQ/search/jessica-sanchez|title=Etta James – I'd Rather Go Blind (Jessica Sanchez cover) video|work=NME|accessdate=March 25, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=SpinMoveRecords.com|date=January 20, 2012|url=http://www.spinmoverecords.com/2012/01/jessica-sanchez-covers-id-rather-go-blind-by-etta-james/|title=Jessica Sanchez Covers "I'd Rather Go Blind" by Etta James|publisher=SpinMoveRecords.com|accessdate=March 25, 2012}}</ref> Earlier same year, she collaborated with Brandon Kane for the single "Just You and Me".<ref>{{Citation|title=Just You And Me (feat. Jessica Sanchez) – Single by Brandon Kane|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/just-you-and-me-feat-jessica-sanchez-single/408331846|access-date=July 31, 2019}}</ref> Additionally, she was among the fifty-seven singers who contributed in a YouTube edition of the charity single "[[We Are the World 25 for Haiti]]", which was produced by [[Lisa Lavie]].<ref name="WATWYE">{{cite web |author=Lisa Lavie |date=February 20, 2010 |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9hhX0KkQBW4 |title=We Are The World 25 For Haiti (YouTube Edition) |work=Music |via=YouTube |accessdate=March 6, 2012 |url-status=bot: unknown |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5we6XK7Jl?url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9hhX0KkQBW4 |archivedate=February 20, 2011 }}()(click video description to expand and see participant listing)</ref>

=== 2013: ''Me, You & the Music'' ===
{{Main|Me, You & the Music}}

Easter Egg Roll.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.huffpost.com/entry/jessica-sanchez-performs-at-white-house-easter-egg-roll_n_2994769|title=LISTEN: Jessica Sanchez Wows White House Easter
