The formal '''jubilees of British monarchs''' started with [[George III of the United Kingdom|George III]].  At the start of the 50th year of his reign, his [[Golden jubilee|jubilee]] was celebrated throughout the islands of Britain and Ireland and his colonial possessions.  Later monarchs added other jubilee years.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.royal.uk/history-jubilees |title=A history of Jubilees &#124; The Royal Family |publisher=Royal.uk |date= |accessdate=2017-02-22}}</ref>
