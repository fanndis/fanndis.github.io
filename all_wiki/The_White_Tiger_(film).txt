'''''The White Tiger''''' is an upcoming film directed by [[Ramin Bahrani]]. The film is [[film adaptation|adapted]] from [[Aravind Adiga]]'s 2008 novel, ''[[The White Tiger (Adiga novel)|The White Tiger]]''. It stars [[Priyanka Chopra]], [[Rajkummar Rao]], and Adarsh Gourav.
