{{main article|2018 Western & Southern Open}}

[[Garbiñe Muguruza]] was the defending champion, but lost in the second round to [[Lesia Tsurenko]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.wtatennis.com/tournament/wta-cincinnati-open#results|title=Western & Southern Open Results Archive|publisher=WTA Tour |accessdate=9 August 2018}}</ref>

[[Kiki Bertens]], en route to her first hard court & Premier 5 level title, defeated the world no. 2 & no. 1 players, in the second round and final respectively. She became the first Dutch woman to win a title in Cincinnati by defeating [[Simona Halep]] in the final, 2–6, 7–6<sup>(8–6)</sup>, 6–2.
