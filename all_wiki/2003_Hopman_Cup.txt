The '''2003 Hopman Cup''' (also known as the '''Hyundai Hopman Cup''' for sponsorship reasons) was the fifteenth edition of the [[Hopman Cup]] tennis sporting event, held on 28 December 2002 through 4 January 2003 at the [[Burswood Entertainment Complex]] in [[Perth]], Western Australia. The champions are [[Serena Williams]] and [[James Blake (tennis)|James Blake]] of the United States, who defeated [[Alicia Molik]] and [[Lleyton Hewitt]] of Australia in the final.
