'''''The Masked Dancer''''' is an upcoming American [[reality competition]] television series set to premiere on [[Fox Broadcasting Company|Fox]] on December 27, 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thewrap.com/fox-premiere-dates-911-lone-star-masked-dancer-last-man-standing/|title=Fox Sets Premiere Dates for ''9-1-1'', ''Lone Star'', ''Masked Dancer'' and ''Last Man Standing'' Final Season|work=[[TheWrap]]|author=Jennifer Maas|date=November 10, 2020|access-date=November 10, 2020|archive-date=November 10, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201110193029/https://www.thewrap.com/fox-premiere-dates-911-lone-star-masked-dancer-last-man-standing/|url-status=live}}</ref> [[Craig Robinson (actor)|Craig Robinson]] will host the show, and [[Ken Jeong]], [[Paula Abdul]], [[Brian Austin Green]], and [[Ashley Tisdale]] will act as panelists. Like ''[[The Masked Singer (American TV series)|The Masked Singer]]''{{'s}} format, celebrity contestants will wear head-to-toe costumes and face masks that conceal their identities, but will perform alongside a "masked partner" in different dance styles.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://variety.com/2020/tv/news/the-masked-singer-spinoff-the-masked-dancer-host-craig-robinson-1234817918/ |title=''The Masked Singer'' Spinoff ''The Masked Dancer'' Taps Craig Robinson as Host |last=Thorne |first=Will |work=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]] |date=October 28, 2020 |access-date=October 28, 2020 |archive-date=November 1, 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201101073203/https://variety.com/2020/tv/news/the-masked-singer-spinoff-the-masked-dancer-host-craig-robinson-1234817918/ |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://deadline.com/2020/10/the-masked-dancer-craig-robinson-host-masked-singer-spin-off-fox-1234604904/ |title=''The Masked Dancer'': Craig Robinson Set To Host ''The Masked Singer'' Spinoff |last=Del Rosario |first=Alexandra |work=[[Deadline Hollywood]] |date=October 28, 2020 |access-date=October 28, 2020 |archive-date=November 2, 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201102051013/https://deadline.com/2020/10/the-masked-dancer-craig-robinson-host-masked-singer-spin-off-fox-1234604904/ |url-status=live }}</ref> The show began as a recurring segment on [[Ellen DeGeneres]]' [[The Ellen DeGeneres Show|daytime talk show]] which spoofed ''The Masked Singer''. In pre-production since January 2020, filming was delayed until October due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic in the United States|COVID-19 pandemic in the United States]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tvfanatic.com/2020/04/tv-ratings-modern-family-series-finale-hits-3-year-high/|title=TV Ratings: Modern Family Series Finale Hits 3-Year High|last=Dailly|first=Paul|date=2020-04-09|website=TV Fanatic|language=en|access-date=2020-04-10|archive-date=April 10, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200410234838/https://www.tvfanatic.com/2020/04/tv-ratings-modern-family-series-finale-hits-3-year-high/|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Production==
===Background and development===
''The Masked Dancer'' is a spin-off of ''[[The Masked Singer (American TV series)|The Masked Singer]]'' which debuted in January 2019 and became a surprise hit for [[Fox Broadcasting Company|Fox]],<ref name="THR May 9">{{Cite web |url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/fox-launching-unscripted-studio-masked-singer-1209087 |title=Fox Launching Unscripted Studio With ''The Masked Singer'' (Exclusive) |last=Goldberg |first=Lesley |work=[[The Hollywood Reporter]] |date=May 9, 2019 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190817034711/https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/fox-launching-unscripted-studio-masked-singer-1209087 |archive-date=August 17, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/tv/ny-ent-why-hard-look-away-from-the-masked-singer-20190115-story.html |title=Why it's hard to look away from Fox's surprise hit ''The Masked Singer'' |last=Desantis |first=Rachel |work=[[New York Daily News]] |date=January 15, 2019 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190205194720/https://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/tv/ny-ent-why-hard-look-away-from-the-masked-singer-20190115-story.html |archive-date=February 5, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.tvguide.com/news/features/the-masked-singer-undestimated-tv-yearbook/ |title=''The Masked Singer'' Was the Most Underestimated Show of the TV Season |last=Venable |first=Malcolm |work=[[TV Guide]] |date=June 7, 2019 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200701180905/https://www.tvguide.com/news/features/the-masked-singer-undestimated-tv-yearbook/ |archive-date=July 1, 2020}}</ref> averaging about 11.5 million viewers per episode in its [[The Masked Singer (American season 1)|first season]]. The show continued as the highest-[[Nielsen ratings|rated]] non-sports program in the United States during its next two seasons.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/live-7-ratings-broadcast-tv-series-2018-19-1217293 |title=2018–19 TV Season: Live-Plus-7 Ratings for Every Broadcast Series |last=Porter |first=Rick |website=[[The Hollywood Reporter]] |date=June 10, 2019 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200527085042/https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/live-7-ratings-broadcast-tv-series-2018-19-1217293 |archive-date=May 27, 2020}}</ref><ref name="S2/3AvgRatings">{{cite web |url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/tv-ratings-7-day-season-averages-2019-20-broadcast-series-1297228 |title=TV Ratings: 7-Day Season Averages for Every 2019–20 Broadcast Series |last=Porter |first=Rick |website=[[The Hollywood Reporter]] |date=June 4, 2020 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200606003358/https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/tv-ratings-7-day-season-averages-2019-20-broadcast-series-1297228 |archive-date=June 6, 2020}}</ref> The series involves celebrities who are disguised in full costumes and face masks which conceal their identities. After performing a song, a panel attempts to guess their identities using clues given. After all have performed in an episode, the panelists and an audience vote for the favorite, and the least popular must take off their mask to reveal their identity. It is a derivative of the ''[[Masked Singer]]'' format which originated in South Korea.

Within a week of ''The Masked Singer''{{'}}s premiere, [[Ellen DeGeneres]] introduced a spoof segment, "The Masked Dancer", on her [[The Ellen DeGeneres Show|eponymous talk show]]. Segments featured disguised celebrities [[Sean Hayes]], [[Howie Mandel]], [[Colton Underwood]], and [[Derek Hough]]. ''Masked Singer'' panelists [[Ken Jeong]] and [[Nicole Scherzinger]] participated in later segments, as did the show's host, [[Nick Cannon]]. ===Filming===	
Production films in Los Angeles at [[Red Studios Hollywood|Red Studios]] where ''The Masked Singer'' has also filmed.<ref name=":336" /><ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=White|first=Peter|last2=|first2=|date=2020-09-21|title=''The Masked Singer'': Fox’s Rob Wade On How The Mystery Singing Competition Got Back On Stage|url=https://deadline.com/2020/09/the-masked-singer-rob-wade-return-to-production-1234581115/|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201004034427/https://deadline.com/2020/09/the-masked-singer-rob-wade-return-to-production-1234581115/|archive-date=October 4, 2020|access-date=2020-11-05|website=Deadline|language=en-US}}</ref> As of January 2020, they had started casting the show.<ref name=":336" /> Some of the show challenges for the production is how the dancing is to be presented, as a solo, like ''The Masked Singer'', in duets, which is more traditional for dancing competitions, and in group numbers, or a combination.<ref name=":337" /> Additionally they accepted that the contestants need to speak, but like ''The Masked Singer'', they would need to in some way disguise the voice. Like ''The Masked Singer'', the contestants will utilize elaborate costumes, made easier to dance in, but with a "similar eccentricity."<ref name=":337" /> The show also features celebrities in elaborate costumes including headpieces and they compete by performing dances that "could be a group dance or dances with another person". Like ''The Masked Singer'' their voices will be altered and disguised. Unlike that show ''The Masked Dancer'' will require more rehearsals as the celebrity contestants have to learn the choreography.<ref name=":0" />

Originally expected to debut in the middle of 2020, production was delayed until October due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=White|first=Peter|date=October 13, 2020|title=Fox's ''The Masked Dancer'' To Shoot This Weekend With Health & Safety Protocols & Virtual Audience|url=https://deadline.com/2020/10/fox-the-masked-dancer-shooting-health-safety-protocols-virtual-audience-1234596653/|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201014050723/https://deadline.com/2020/10/fox-the-masked-dancer-shooting-health-safety-protocols-virtual-audience-1234596653/|archive-date=October 14, 2020|work=[[Deadline Hollywood]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Goldberg|first=Lesley|date=April 7, 2020|title=A Puzzle Without All the Pieces: Broadcast Networks Scramble to Program an Uncertain Future|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/a-puzzle-all-pieces-broadcast-networks-scramble-program-an-uncertain-future-1288828|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200415043311/https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/a-puzzle-all-pieces-broadcast-networks-scramble-program-an-uncertain-future-1288828|archive-date=April 15, 2020|work=[[The Hollywood Reporter]]}}</ref>
