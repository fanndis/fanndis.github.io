'''Thomas Bernthal''' is an American marketing CEO and former [[NBC News]] producer.

== Early life ==
Bernthal was born in Washington, D.C., to Joan Lurie (née Marx) and Eric Lawrence "Rick" Bernthal, a former lawyer with [[Latham & Watkins LLP]] and currently chair of the board of directors for the [[The Humane Society of the United States|US Humane Society]].<ref name="frphgs1">{{cite web|url=http://freepages.genealogy.rootsweb.ancestry.com/~hoffmanfamily/nechah/d5.htm#i1414|title=Descendants of Nechah Oifman|publisher=[[Ancestry.com]]|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130820154051/http://freepages.genealogy.rootsweb.ancestry.com/~hoffmanfamily/nechah/d5.htm#i1414|archivedate=August 20, 2013|df=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.humanesociety.org/about/leadership/board/hsus_board_directors.html?credit=web_id93480558|title=Board of Directors and National Council : The Humane Society of the United States|website=humanesociety.org|access-date=November 30, 2017}}</ref> His paternal grandfather was musician and producer [[Murray Bernthal]].<ref name="broadwayworld">{{cite web|url=http://centralnewyork.broadwayworld.com/article/Murray_Bernthal_Dies_at_99_20101210|title=Murray Bernthal dies at 99|date=December 11, 2010|work=Broadway World}}</ref> He has two brothers, [[Jon Bernthal|Jon]], an actor, and Nicholas, an [[Orthopedic surgery|orthopedic surgeon]] and professor at [[University of California, Los Angeles|UCLA]].<ref name="poststand2010">{{cite news|url=http://obits.syracuse.com/obituaries/syracuse/obituary.aspx?n=murray-bernthal&pid=147101498|title=Obituary – Murray Bernthal|date=December 12, 2010|newspaper=[[The Post-Standard]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.uclahealth.org/nicholas-bernthal|title=Nicholas M. Bernthal, MD : Pediatric Orthopaedic Surgery {{!}} Surgery, Complex General Surgical Oncology – Santa Monica, California|website=www.uclahealth.org|access-date=December 5, 2017}}</ref> Bernthal and his family are [[Jewish]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.jweekly.com/2014/10/17/celebrity-jews-10-16-14/|title=Celebrity Jews|last=Bloom|first=Nate|date=October 17, 2014|website=[[J. The Jewish News of Northern California]]|accessdate=July 30, 2017}}</ref>

Bernthal grew up in [[Cabin John, Maryland]], a suburb of [[Washington D.C.]] He attended the [[Sidwell Friends School]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.esquire.com/entertainment/movies/a14443788/jon-bernthal-the-punisher-cover/|title=Jon Bernthal Is Learning to Keep His Demons at Bay|last=Grealy|first=Beau|date=2018-01-03|website=Esquire|language=en-US|access-date=2020-02-03}}</ref>

== Career ==
Bernthal began his career working at the White House's Communication Office during the [[Presidency of Bill Clinton|Bill Clinton administration]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www1.cfnc.org/Plan/For_A_Career/Career_Profile/Career_Profile.aspx?id=t9YSQBlUmh0GKrru7A13FQXAP3DPAXXAP3DPAX&screen=9|title=CFNC.org - Career Profile|website=www1.cfnc.org|access-date=2020-02-03}}</ref> Following this stint, he worked as a news producer and journalist for [[NBC News]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.keltonglobal.com/people/tom-bernthal/|title=Tom Bernthal {{!}} Co-President, LRW Group Action Division|website=Kelton Global|language=en-US|access-date=2020-02-03}}</ref> During his six years with NBC, Bernthal produced segments for the ''Today Show'', ''Nightly News'', ''Dateline'', MSNBC and CNBC and won three [[Emmy Award]]s for his work.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.insightsassociation.org/person/tom-bernthal|title=Tom Bernthal {{!}} Insights Association|website=www.insightsassociation.org|access-date=2020-02-03}}</ref>

In 2000, Bernthal moved to Los Angeles, where he continued to work for NBC before founding his own marketing and consulting agency in 2002, Kelton Global. He presently is the CEO and Co-President of the organization.<ref name=":0" />

== Personal life ==
Bernthal has three children.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://people.com/human-interest/sheryl-sandberg-engaged-tom-bernthal/|title=Sheryl Sandberg Is Engaged to Boyfriend Tom Bernthal|website=PEOPLE.com|language=en|access-date=2020-02-03}}</ref>

Bernthal began dating Facebook COO [[Sheryl Sandberg]] in Spring 2019. The two were engaged in February 2020.<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2020/02/03/technology/tom-bernthal-sheryl-sandberg.html|title=Sheryl Sandberg Is Engaged to Tom Bernthal|last=Padilla|first=Mariel|date=2020-02-03|work=The New York Times|access-date=2020-02-03|language=en-US|issn=0362-4331}}</ref>
