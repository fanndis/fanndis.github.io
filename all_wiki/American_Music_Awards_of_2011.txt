The '''39th Annual [[American Music Awards]]''' was held on November 20, 2011, at the [[Microsoft Theater|Nokia Theatre L.A. Live]] in Los Angeles.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nokiatheatrelalive.com/content.php?section=events&page=eventdetail&id=2607 |title=NOKIA Theatre L.A. this event is  LIVE: American Music Awards |accessdate=August 13, 2011 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110921154910/http://www.nokiatheatrelalive.com/content.php?section=events&page=eventdetail&id=2607 |archivedate=September 21, 2011 |df=mdy }}</ref> The awards recognized the most popular artists and albums from the year 2011. Nominees were announced on October 11, 2011.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/articles/1672323/american-music-awards-nominees-2011.jhtml|title=Lady Gaga, Adele Lead American Music Awards Noms|accessdate=October 11, 2011}}</ref> The awards ceremony was host-free and was broadcast on [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/articles/1673515/ama-performers.jhtml|title=Chris Brown, LMFAO Join American Music Awards Lineup|publisher=|accessdate=October 18, 2016}}</ref>
[[Taylor Swift]] and [[Adele]] were the big winners of the night picking up three awards in total.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/465045/amas-2011-taylor-swift-nicki-minaj-win-big-on-ladies-night|title=AMAs 2011: Taylor Swift, Nicki Minaj Win Big on Ladies' Night|date= November 20, 2011|work=Billboard.com|accessdate=November 23, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chron.com/entertainment/article/Taylor-Swift-wins-3-trophies-at-AMAs-2279762.php|title=Taylor Swift wins 3 trophies at AMAs|date= November 20, 2011|work=Houston Chronicle|accessdate=November 23, 2011}}</ref>
