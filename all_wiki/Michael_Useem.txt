'''Michael Useem''' is an American academic. He is the William and Jacalyn Egan Professor of Management at [[Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania]],<ref name="whartonbio">{{cite web|title=Michael Useem|url=https://mgmt.wharton.upenn.edu/profile/useem/#|website=Management Department|publisher=Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania|accessdate=March 4, 2018}}</ref> where he is also the director of the Center for Leadership and Change Management.<ref name="mckinseyinterview">{{cite web|last1=Javetski|first1=Bill|title=Leading in the 21st century: An interview with Michael Useem|url=https://www.mckinsey.com/global-themes/leadership/an-interview-with-michael-useem|website=McKinsey & Company|accessdate=March 4, 2018|date=September 2012}}</ref> He is the author of several books.
