'''''Manager Magazin''''' (stylized as ''manager magazin'') is a [[German language|German]] monthly business [[magazine]] focusing on business, finance and management based in [[Hamburg]], [[Germany]].

==History and profile==
''Manager Magazin'' was first published on 1 November 1971.<ref name=sgroup>{{cite web|title=First-hand business information|url=http://www.spiegelgruppe.de/spiegelgruppe/home.nsf/4E36CF1395E8C0AFC1257421002AD46A/$file/Engl_UnternBroschuere_2012.pdf|work=Spiegel Gruppe|accessdate=31 December 2014|page=29}}</ref> The magazine is part of the Spiegel Group which also owns ''[[Der Spiegel]]'' among the others.<ref>{{cite web|title=The SPIEGEL Group Relies on Quark Publishing System 8|url=http://www.quark.com/About_Quark/Press/PressDetail.aspx?ncid=1255|work=Quark Software Inc.|accessdate=31 December 2014|location=Hamburg|format=Press release|date=7 September 2010}}</ref>

The magazine is based in Hamburg<ref name=ecos/><ref>{{cite book|author1=Tristam Carrington-Windo|author2=Katrin Kohl|title=Dictionary of Contemporary Germany|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=_VypAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA281|date=11 April 2013|publisher=Routledge|isbn=978-1-136-59530-1|page=281}}</ref> and is published monthly<ref>{{cite web|title=German Magazines and Newspapers|url=http://www.vistawide.com/german/german_periodicals.htm|work=Vistawide|accessdate=1 March 2015}}</ref> by the Manager Magazin Verlagsgesellschaft.<ref>{{cite web|title=Manager Magazin|url=http://www.publicitas.com/switzerland/media-solutions/factsheet/mediadata/manager-magazin-germany/?PARAM1=MH2MH2#.VKOX8sAgEc|work=Publicitas|accessdate=31 December 2014}}</ref> Since 1986<ref name=eurot>{{cite web|title=manager magazin|url=http://www.eurotopics.net/en/home/medienindex/media_articles/?frommedia=21911|work=Euro Topics|accessdate=3 February 2015}}</ref> [[Gruner + Jahr]] has a 24.9 percent share in the publisher of the magazine.<ref name=fund>{{cite web|title=SPIEGEL-Verlag Rudolf Augstein GmbH & Co. KG History|url=http://www.fundinguniverse.com/company-histories/spiegel-verlag-rudolf-augstein-gmbh-co-kg-history/|work=Funding Universe|accessdate=31 December 2014}}</ref> The other owner of its publishing house is the Spiegel Group.<ref name=eurot/> [[Steffen Klusmann]] is the [[editor-in-chief]] of the monthly.<ref name=ecos>{{cite web|title=manager magazin Verlagsgesellschaft mbH, Germany|url=http://www.global-economic-symposium.org/review-2013/table-of-contents/partners-and-sponsors-2013/copy_of_manager-magazin-verlagsgesellschaft-mbh|work=Global Economic Symposium|accessdate=31 December 2014|date=2013}}</ref>

''Manager Magazin'' has a [[Liberalism in Germany|liberal]] stance.<ref name=eurot/> The magazine targets professional decision makers and [[managers]] in Germany.<ref>{{cite web|title=Manager Magazin|url=http://www.prismagroup.de/prisma_web.html|work=Prisma Group|accessdate=31 December 2014}}</ref> It covers business news, related data and background information concerning all economic areas.<ref name=ecos/> The online edition of the monthly was launched in 1998.<ref name=sgroup/> The magazine has a lifestyle supplement, ''Splendid'', which covers articles on fashion, beauty, and living sections.<ref>{{cite web|title=Splendid|url=http://www.publicitas.com/du/home/media-solutions/media-search/advanced-media-search/media-factsheet-detail/mediadata/splendid-supplement-to-manager-magazin/?PARAM1=MH2SH1#.VNBvC2SsXRA|work=Publicitas|accessdate=3 February 2015}}</ref> ''Splendid'', a 52-page lifestyle supplement, was started in 2014 and from 2015 its frequency became quarterly.<ref>{{cite news|title=Splendid to appear 4 times in 2015|url=http://www.publicitas.com/du/home/media-news-events/news-detail/?newsid=116627&title=germany-splendid-to-appear-4-times-in-2015#.VNBve2SsXRA|accessdate=3 February 2015|work=Publicitas|date=11 September 2014}}</ref>

The magazine publishes several ranking lists, including good companies<ref>{{cite book|editor1=Judith Hennigfeld|editor2=Manfred Pohl|editor3=Nick Tolhurst|title=The ICCA Handbook of Corporate Social Responsibility|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=5O8eOtSMhTcC&pg=PR14|accessdate=14 August 2015|date=2 October 2006|publisher=John Wiley & Sons|isbn=978-0-470-05787-2|page=14}}</ref> and the richest Germans.<ref>{{cite book|author=Erika Fischer-Lichte|title=Performance and the Politics of Space: Theatre and Topology|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=CTk0XSUJHOwC&pg=PA182|accessdate=14 August 2015|year=2013|publisher=Routledge|isbn=978-0-415-50968-8|page=182}}</ref>

From 2004 to 2008 the [[Polish language|Polish]] edition of the magazine, ''[[Manager Magazin (Poland)|Manager Magazin - Edycja Polska]]'', was published in [[Poland]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Manager Magazin to close|url=http://www.publicitas.com/du/home/media-news-events/news-detail/?newsid=25890&title=poland-manager-magazin-to-close#.VNBtomSsXRA|accessdate=3 February 2015|work=Publicitas|date=3 December 2008}}</ref>

==Circulation==
In 1999 the circulation of ''Manager Magazin'' was 125,200 copies.<ref>{{cite book|author1=Ingomar Kloss|author2=M. Abe|title=Advertising Worldwide: Advertising Conditions in Selected Countries|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=8-kg-A7_nyAC&pg=PA130|accessdate=29 March 2015|date=1 January 2001|publisher=Springer Science & Business Media|isbn=978-3-540-67713-0|page=130}}</ref> It rose to 129,888 copies in 2009.<ref name=ecos/> The monthly had a circulation of 113,774 copies in 2010.<ref name=fip>{{cite web|title=World Magazine Trends 2010/2011 |url=http://www.revistas-ari.com/attachments/209_WMT_2010_2011_Europe.pdf|work=FIPP|accessdate=31 March 2015|url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402165145/http://www.revistas-ari.com/attachments/209_WMT_2010_2011_Europe.pdf|archivedate=2 April 2015 }}</ref> Its total circulation was 109,222 copies in 2011.<ref name=sgroup/>
