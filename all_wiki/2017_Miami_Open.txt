The '''2017 Miami Open presented by Itaú''' (also known as '''2017 Miami Masters''') was a professional men and women's [[tennis]] tournament played on outdoor [[Tennis court#Hard courts|hard courts]].<ref>{{cite web|author1=Emon Reiser|title=Sony Open tennis tournament gets new sponsor, changes name|url=http://www.bizjournals.com/southflorida/news/2014/08/19/sony-open-tennis-tournament-gets-new-sponsor.html?page=all|publisher=South Florida Business Journal|date=August 19, 2014}}</ref> It was the 32nd edition of the [[Miami Open (tennis)|Miami Open]], and part of the [[ATP World Tour Masters 1000|Masters 1000]] category on the [[2017 ATP World Tour]], and of the [[WTA Premier tournaments|Premier Mandatory]] category on the [[2017 WTA Tour]]. All men and women's events took place at the [[Tennis Center at Crandon Park]] in [[Key Biscayne, Florida]], United States, from March 20 through April 2, 2017.
