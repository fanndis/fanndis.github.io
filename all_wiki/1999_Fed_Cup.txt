{{short description|1999 edition of the Fed Cup}}
The '''1999 Fed Cup''' was the 37th edition of the most important competition between national teams in women's [[tennis]]. In the final, the [[United States Fed Cup team|United States]] defeated [[Russia Fed Cup team|Russia]] at Taube Tennis Stadium in [[Stanford, California|Stanford, CA]], [[United States]], on 18–19 September, giving the Americans their 16th title.

==World Group II Play-offs==
The four losing teams from World Group II (Australia, Belarus, Japan and Netherlands) were divided into two pools of four with qualifiers from Zonal Group I. Two teams qualified from Europe/Africa Zone (Romania and Slovenia), one team from the Asia/Oceania Zone (Chinese Taipei), and one team from the Americas Zone (Argentina).

The two top teams from each pool played-off against each, with the winner promoted to [[2000 Fed Cup World Group|2000 World Group]]. All other teams were relegated to Zonal Competition in 2000.

Venue: [[University Sports Centre]], [[Amsterdam]], [[Netherlands]] (outdoor hard)

Dates: 21–24 July
