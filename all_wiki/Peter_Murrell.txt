'''Peter Tierney Murrell''' (born December 1964) is the Chief Executive Officer of the [[Scottish National Party]].<ref name="herald-whitaker">{{cite web|accessdate=2019-02-04|title=Just who is Petter Murrell?|url=https://www.heraldscotland.com/news/16175845.just-who-is-peter-murrell/|website=HeraldScotland}}</ref> He is married to [[Nicola Sturgeon]], the party's leader and [[First Minister of Scotland]].<ref name="sm" />

== Early life and education ==
Murrell was born in [[Edinburgh]] in December 1964.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Henden |first1=Amalie |title=Nicola Sturgeon husband: Who is Nicola Sturgeon married to? |url=https://www.express.co.uk/news/politics/1271904/Nicola-Sturgeon-husband-Who-Is-Nicola-Sturgeon-married-to-Peter-Murrell-SNP |accessdate=19 July 2020 |work=Express |publisher=Express Newspapers |date=21 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://companycheck.co.uk/company/SC203193/INDEPENDENCE-MERCHANDISING-LTD/companies-house-data|title=Company registration data for Independence Merchandising Ltd|last=|first=|date=|website=|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=}}</ref> He attended [[Craigmount High School]] and studied at the [[University of Glasgow]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/PeterMurrellSNP|title=Peter Murrell|last=|first=|date=2019-02-04|website=Facebook|archive-url=https://archive.today/20190204192626/https://www.facebook.com/PeterMurrellSNP|archive-date=2019-02-04|access-date=2019-02-04|url-status=live}}</ref>

== Career ==
Murrell has been Chief Executive Officer of the [[Scottish National Party]] (SNP) since 1999 when he took over from [[Michael Russell (politician)|Michael Russell]].<ref name="herald-whitaker"/> Previously he had worked in the Banff and Buchan constituency office of [[Alex Salmond]], who was former party leader.<ref name="scotsman-mccafferty">{{Cite web|url=https://www.scotsman.com/news/politics/who-is-peter-murrell-the-snp-chief-executive-and-why-is-he-facing-calls-to-quit-1-4861417|title=Who is Peter Murrell the SNP chief executive and why is he facing calls to quit?|website=www.scotsman.com|language=en|access-date=2019-02-03}}</ref> The party's success in the 2007 elections was credited to the organisation of Murrell.<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Camp|first=Edwige|date=2014-12-20|title=The Scottish National Party (SNP) : A Party of Government in the early 21st Century|url=http://journals.openedition.org/lisa/7008|journal=Revue LISA/LISA e-journal. XII–n°8|doi=10.4000/lisa.7008|issn=1762-6153|doi-access=free}}</ref>

Murrell was formerly the Company Secretary of Independence Merchandising Limited, a now-dissolved company that included as its directors SNP politicians [[Pete Wishart]] and [[Stewart Stevenson|Stuart Stevenson]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://companycheck.co.uk/company/SC203193/INDEPENDENCE-MERCHANDISING-LTD/companies-house-data|title=INDEPENDENCE MERCHANDISING LTD.. In January 2019 Murrell came under pressure to resign from [[Kenny MacAskill|Kenny Macaskill]], former SNP Justice Secretary.<ref name="scotsman-mccafferty"/>

== Personal life ==
Murrell married [[Nicola Sturgeon]] in July 2010 at Òran Mór Glasgow.<ref name="sm">{{Cite web |url=https://www.scotsman.com/news/nicola-sturgeon-ties-knot-she-wont-be-calling-herself-mrs-murrell-2442260 |title=Nicola Sturgeon ties the knot — but she won't be calling herself Mrs Murrell |website=www.scotsman.com |language=en |access-date=2019-02-03}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|accessdate=2019-02-04|title=Wedding for SNP's Nicola Sturgeon|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-glasgow-west-10652420|date=16 July 2010|via=www.bbc.co.uk|website=BBC News}}</ref> They first met in 1988 when Sturgeon, who volunteered at the Banff Buchan constituency office of Alex Salmond, attended an SNP youth camp organised by Murrell.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.express.co.uk/news/politics/570880/The-story-behind-the-rise-of-Nicola-Sturgeon-SNP-leader-opposes-right-to-buy|title=Nicola the Queen of Scots: The story behind the fiesty SNP leader|last=Midgley|first=Dominic|date=2015-04-16|website=Express.co.uk|language=en|access-date=2019-02-04}}</ref> Sturgeon and Murrell became a couple in 2003.<ref name="scotsman-mccafferty"/>
