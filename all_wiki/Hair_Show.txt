'''''Hair Show''''' is a 2004 comedy film directed by [[Leslie Small]] starring [[Mo'Nique]] and [[Kellita Smith]]. It is the theatrical directorial debut of [[Leslie Small]].

==Plot==
Peaches ([[Mo'Nique]]), a hair stylist from [[Baltimore, Maryland|Baltimore]], and her estranged sister, Angela ([[Kellita Smith]]), the owner of an upscale salon in [[Beverly Hills, California|Beverly Hills]], get reacquainted when Peaches decides to attend a celebration for Angela in [[Los Angeles, California|Los Angeles]]. The reunion is bittersweet and worsens when Angela finds out that Peaches is on the run from the [[Internal Revenue Service|IRS]] and only has 60 days to pay $50,000 in back taxes. After some hilarious moments and passionate exchanges, the two sisters join forces to fight off a pesky rival salon owner Marcella ([[Gina Torres]]) and save Peaches from her troubles by competing for a lucrative cash prize and bragging rights at the city's annual hair show.
