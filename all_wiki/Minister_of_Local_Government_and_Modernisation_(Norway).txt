The '''Minister of Local Government and Modernisation''' ({{lang-no|Kommunal- og moderniseringsministeren}}) is a [[Council of State (Norway)|Councillor of State]] and Chief of the [[Norway|Norwegian]] [[Ministry of Local Government and Regional Development (Norway)|Ministry of Local Government and Regional Development]]. Currently, [[Conservative Party (Norway)|Conservative]] [[Nikolai Astrup (politician)|Nikolai Astrup]] has held the post since 2020.<ref name=solberg /> The ministry is responsible for local administration, including [[municipalities of Norway|municipalities]] and [[county municipality (Norway)|county municipalities]], rural and regional policy, information technology, [[elections in Norway|elections]] and government administration, including management of state real estate and government employment. Major subordinate agencies include the [[Government Administration Services]], [[Statsbygg]], the [[Norwegian Competition Authority|Competition Authority]], the [[National Office of Building Technology and Administration]], the [[Norwegian State Housing Bank|State Housing Bank]] and the [[Norwegian Data Protection Authority|Data Protection Authority]].

The position was created in 1948 as a successor of the [[Minister of Labour (Norway)|Minister of Labour]], originally named the '''Minister of Local Government and Labour'''. The title changed to the '''Minister of Local Government'''. Labour issues were moved to the [[Minister of Government Administration (Norway)|Minister of Government Administration and Labour]] in 1997 and the title was changed to the '''Minister of Local Government and Regional Development'''. This lasted until 2013, when the position took over part of the portfolio of the Minister of Public Administration and took the current name.

==Minister of Digitalization==
The '''Minister of Digitalization''' is responsible for the government's digitalization policies, and to coordinate their [[United Nations|UN]] sustainability goals. The post is also responsible for the work and issues related to electronic communication.<ref name=government.no>{{cite web| url=https://www.regjeringen.no/no/dep/kmd/org/digitaliseringsminister-nikolai-astrup/id2626348/ |publisher=[[Government.no]] | title=Minister of Digitalization Nikolai Astrup|date=22 January 2019 |accessdate=23 January 2020}}</ref> In 2020, the title was changed to '''Minister of Districts and Digitalization''', and in addition to digitalization policies, the minister is now also responsible for overlooking the regional and district policies, privacy and policies related to the [[Sami people]] and other minorities in the country.<ref name=[[Government.no]]>{{cite web| url= https://www.regjeringen.no/no/dep/kmd/org/distrikts--og-digitaliseringsminister-linda-hofstad-helleland/id2687557/ | publisher= [[Government.no]] | title= Minister of Districts and Digitalization Linda Hofstad Helleland | date= 24 January 2020 | accessdate= 24 January 2020}}</ref>
