'''''TheWrap''''' is a left leaning, liberal news organization covering the business of entertainment and media via digital, print and live events. It was founded by journalist [[Sharon Waxman]] in 2009. 

==Background==
''TheWrap'' has won multiple awards<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://news.yahoo.com/thewrap-lands-14-socal-journalism-180621438.html|title=TheWrap Lands 14 SoCal Journalism Awards Nominations, Including Best Website|publisher=[[Yahoo! News]]}}</ref> for its journalism, including best website in 2018 for a news organization exclusive to the internet at the L.A. Press Club’s SoCal Journalism Awards and best entertainment website in 2018 at the National Arts and Entertainment Journalism (NAEJ) awards.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/2018-NAEJ-WINNERS-v2.pdf |title=Winners list |date=December 2018|publisher=[[Los Angeles Press Club]] |format=PDF|access-date=2020-03-25}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.yahoo.com/entertainment/thewrap-wins-best-entertainment-website-064307432.html|title=TheWrap Wins Best Entertainment Website and More First-Place Wins at National Entertainment Journalism Awards 2018|publisher=Yahoo! News}}</ref> In 2016, the L.A. Press Club’s NAEJ gave the site its top prizes for feature photography and Sharon Waxman’s WaxWord blog, as well as second place for Best Entertainment Website and Entertainment Publication. The site was named the best online news site in both 2012 and 2009 by the same group.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://lapressclub.org/ktlas-sam-rubin-roasted-and-honored-as-reporters-and-bloggers-recognized-3rd-annual-national-entertainment-journalism-awards/|title=KTLA's Sam Rubin Roasted and Honored as Reporters and Bloggers Recognized @ 3rd Annual National Entertainment Journalism Awards|date=2010-04-23|publisher=Los Angeles Press Club}}</ref>

In November 2019, ''TheWrap'' was nominated for the 12th annual Los Angeles Press Club’s National Arts & Entertainment Journalism Awards, including best website.<ref>{{cite web |url= http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/NAEJ-2019-Finalists-11062019-1149.pdf|title=Finalists list|date=November 2019 |publisher=Los Angeles Press Club |format=PDF|access-date=2020-03-25}}</ref>
