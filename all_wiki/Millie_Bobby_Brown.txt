'''Millie Bobby Brown''' (born 19 February 2004)<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.biography.com/people/millie-bobby-brown-9302016|title=Millie Bobby Brown (2004-)|publisher=[[Biography.com]]|access-date=10 August 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180811033220/https://www.biography.com/people/millie-bobby-brown-9302016|archive-date=11 August 2018|url-status=dead}}</ref> is an English actress and producer.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=PqGehRgKTLo|title=Millie Bobby Brown, Finn Wolfhard & Noah Schnapp Answer the Web's Most Searched Questions|last=|first=|date=|website=YouTube|access-date=21 April 2020}}</ref> At the age of twelve, she gained notability for her role as [[Eleven (Stranger Things)|Eleven]] in the first season of [[Netflix]] science fiction series ''[[Stranger Things]]'' (2016–present),<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.emmys.com/bios/millie-bobby-brown |title=Millie Bobby Brown |publisher=[[The Academy of Television Arts & Sciences]] (ATAS) |quote=[Brown] rose to prominence in her Primetime Emmy Award-nominated role (for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series) as Jane "Eleven" Ives in the Netflix science fiction horror series ''Stranger Things''. |access-date=12 September 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180912131246/https://www.emmys.com/bios/millie-bobby-brown |archive-date=12 September 2018 |url-status=live  }}<br/>{{cite web |url=https://ew.com/awards/2017/05/08/millie-bobby-brown-mtv-awards-instagram-video/ |title=Millie Bobby Brown mocks her MTV Movie & TV Awards breakdown in adorable video |website=[[Entertainment Weekly#Website|EW.com]] |last=Nolfi |first=Joey |date=8 May 2017 |access-date=12 September 2018 |quote=... [Brown] rose to prominence on the first season of the Netflix sci-fi thriller, [''Stranger Things''], as the character Eleven ... |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180912165638/https://ew.com/awards/2017/05/08/millie-bobby-brown-mtv-awards-instagram-video/ |archive-date=12 September 2018 |url-status=live  }}<br/>{{cite web |url=https://www.bustle.com/p/millie-bobby-brown-is-2018s-youngest-time-100-honoree-she-more-than-earned-her-spot-8839525 |title=Millie Bobby Brown Is 2018's Youngest 'TIME' 100 Honoree & She More Than Earned Her Spot |work=[[Bustle (magazine)|Bustle]] |last=Ziss |first=Sophy |date=19 April 2018 |access-date=12 September 2018 |quote=Brown rose to prominence in 2016 with her leading role in ''Stranger Things'', but she'd been working in the industry for a few years before that. |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180912131446/https://www.bustle.com/p/millie-bobby-brown-is-2018s-youngest-time-100-honoree-she-more-than-earned-her-spot-8839525 |archive-date=12 September 2018 |url-status=live  }}</ref> which was renewed for three seasons. She received [[Primetime Emmy Award]] nominations for [[Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series|Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series]] in 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.indiewire.com/2016/07/stranger-things-millie-bobby-brown-interview-eleven-11-netflix-season-2-1201707207/|title=Meet 'Stranger Things' Breakout Millie Bobby Brown, aka Lucky Number Eleven|last=Travers|first=Ben|date=19 July 2016|website=[[IndieWire]]|access-date=22 July 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160722135117/http://www.indiewire.com/2016/07/stranger-things-millie-bobby-brown-interview-eleven-11-netflix-season-2-1201707207/|archive-date=22 July 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> She starred in and produced the mystery film ''[[Enola Holmes (film)|Enola Holmes]]'' (2020), and starred in the monster film ''[[Godzilla: King of the Monsters (2019 film)|Godzilla: King of the Monsters]]'' (2019).

In 2018, Brown was featured in the [[Time 100|''Time'' 100]] list of the world's most influential people.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Millie Bobby Brown: The World’s 100 Most Influential People|url=https://time.com/collection/most-influential-people-2018/5238181/millie-bobby-brown/|website=Time|language=en-us|access-date=4 May 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200510221443/https://time.com/collection/most-influential-people-2018/5238181/millie-bobby-brown/|archive-date=10 May 2020|url-status=live}}</ref> That year she was appointed as a [[List of UNICEF Goodwill Ambassadors|UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador]], the youngest person selected for this position.

==Early life==
Brown was born in [[Marbella]], Málaga, [[Andalusia]], [[Spain]],<ref name="Diario Sur">{{cite news |url=https://www.diariosur.es/marbella-estepona/origenes-marbellies-millie-20171113202337-nt.html |title=Los orígenes marbellíes de Millie Bobby Brown, la joven estrella de 'Stranger Things' / The Marbella origins of Millie Bobby Brown, the young star of 'Stranger Things' |last=Sedano |first=Jon |last2=de los Ríos |first2=Ángel |date=15 November 2017 |access-date=31 July 2018 |newspaper=[[Diario Sur]] |language=es |publisher=[[Vocento]] |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180801004004/https://www.diariosur.es/marbella-estepona/origenes-marbellies-millie-20171113202337-nt.html |archive-date=1 August 2018 |url-status=dead |quote=...nació en Marbella en 2004, aunque tiene nacionalidad británica, ya que Robert y Kelly Brown, sus padres, eran unos ingleses asiduos veraneantes en la Costa del Sol. / ...was born in Marbella in 2004, although she has British nationality, since Robert and Kelly Brown, her parents, were regular English vacationers on the Costa del Sol. }}</ref> the third of four children of English parents, Kelly and Robert Brown. She has British citizenship through her parents.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/stranger-things-stars-father-demands-935013|title='Stranger Things' Star's Father Demands Cash From Agents to Represent Her (Exclusive)|work=[[The Hollywood Reporter]]|first1=Borys|last1=Kit|first2=Matthew|last2=Belloni|date=4 October 2016|access-date=4 December 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171205042024/https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/stranger-things-stars-father-demands-935013|archive-date=5 December 2017|url-status=live}}</ref> The family moved to [[Bournemouth]], Dorset, when she was around four years old. 

Four years later, her family moved to the United States, settling in [[Windermere, Florida]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://nypost.com/2017/09/15/at-13-stranger-things-star-millie-bobby-brown-is-an-icon-in-the-making/|title=At 13, 'Stranger Things' star Millie Bobby Brown is an icon in the making|website=[[New York Post]]|first=Gregory E.|last=Miller|date=15 September 2017|access-date=4 December 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171204222937/https://nypost.com/2017/09/15/at-13-stranger-things-star-millie-bobby-brown-is-an-icon-in-the-making/|archive-date=4 December 2017|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0icvJefZW8Q|title=Millie Bobby Brown Full Panel @Spooky Empire|last=|first=|website=YouTube|access-date=20 April 2020|date=4 December 2016}} She mentions that she resided in Windermere, Florida on the panel</ref> She was born with partial hearing in one ear, and gradually lost all hearing in that ear over several years.<ref name=variety/>

Brown now divides her time between [[London]] and [[Atlanta]].<ref name=variety>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2017/tv/features/millie-bobby-brown-stranger-things-season-2-eleven-1202602487/|title=How 'Stranger Things' Star Millie Bobby Brown Made Eleven 'Iconic' and Catapulted Into Pop Culture|website=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]]|first=Debra|last=Birnbaum|date=31 October 2017|access-date=4 December 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171222052825/https://variety.com/2017/tv/features/millie-bobby-brown-stranger-things-season-2-eleven-1202602487/|archive-date=22 December 2017|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Career==
In 2013, Brown made her acting debut as a guest star in the [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] fantasy drama series ''[[Once Upon a Time in Wonderland]]'', a spin-off of ''[[Once Upon a Time (TV series)|Once Upon a Time]]'', portraying the role of [[Alice (Alice's Adventures in Wonderland)|Young Alice]]. In 2014, she had a starring role in the [[BBC America]] paranormal drama-thriller series ''[[Intruders (TV series)|Intruders]]'' as Madison O'Donnell.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbcwpressroom.com/bbc-worldwide-productions/press/john-simm-mira-sorvino-start-filming-bbc-americas-intruders-james-frain-tory-kittles-millie-brown-join-cast/|title=John Simm and Mira Sorvino Start Filming BBC AMERICA's Intruders as James Frain, Tory Kittles, and Millie Brown Join Cast|publisher=[[BBC Worldwide]]|date=24 February 2014|access-date=23 November 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161123202606/http://www.bbcwpressroom.com/bbc-worldwide-productions/press/john-simm-mira-sorvino-start-filming-bbc-americas-intruders-james-frain-tory-kittles-millie-brown-join-cast/|archive-date=23 November 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref> She made guest appearances in the [[CBS]] police procedural drama ''[[NCIS (TV series)|NCIS]]'', the ABC sitcom ''[[Modern Family]]'', and the ABC medical drama series ''[[Grey's Anatomy]]''.

[[File:Millie Bobby Brown 2016.jpg|thumb|right|upright=1|Brown at the 2016 [[Phoenix Comic Fest]]]]

In 2016, Brown was cast to play [[Eleven (Stranger Things)|Eleven ('El')]] in the [[Netflix]] science fiction horror series ''[[Stranger Things]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2015/08/duffer-bros-netflix-series-stranger-things-young-cast-1201502552/|title=Duffer Bros. Netflix Supernatural Drama Series Sets Young Cast, Gets Title|work=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|first=Nellie|last=Andreeva|date=20 August 2016|access-date=16 September 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150822215815/http://deadline.com/2015/08/duffer-bros-netflix-series-stranger-things-young-cast-1201502552/|archive-date=22 August 2015|url-status=live}}</ref> She received critical praise and she was nominated for the [[Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Drama Series]] and the [[Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series]]. She won the [[Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Drama Series]] with her co-stars<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2016/11/first-cast-photo-for-stranger-things-season-2-released-online-1201849068/|title='Stranger Things' Season 2 Cast Photo Reveals Who Returns From The Upside Down|first=Ross A.|last=Lincoln|website=Deadline Hollywood|date=4 November 2016|access-date=2 January 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170103165543/http://deadline.com/2016/11/first-cast-photo-for-stranger-things-season-2-released-online-1201849068/|archive-date=3 January 2017|url-status=live}}</ref> and won the 43rd [[Saturn Award for Best Performance by a Younger Actor in a Television Series]].<ref name="Saturn Award 2017"/> For her role as Eleven in the second season of ''Stranger Things'', she received her second [[Emmy]] nomination in 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2018/tv/news/emmys-nominations-list-2018-1202871084/|date=12 July 2018|title=Emmy Nominations: The Complete List|website=Variety|access-date=12 July 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190331131215/https://variety.com/2018/tv/news/emmys-nominations-list-2018-1202871084/|archive-date=31 March 2019|url-status=live}}</ref>

In November 2016, Brown starred in the music video for [[Sigma (DJs)|Sigma]] and [[Birdy (singer)|Birdy]]'s single "[[Find Me (Sigma song)|Find Me]]". Since November 2016, she has appeared in commercial advertisements for [[Citigroup]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20161121005263/en/Citi-Launches-Digital-Campaign-Spark-Nationwide-Conversation|title=Citi Launches Digital Campaign To Spark a Nationwide Conversation About Money|work=[[Business Wire]]|date=21 November 2017|access-date=14 February 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170214175501/http://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20161121005263/en/Citi-Launches-Digital-Campaign-Spark-Nationwide-Conversation|archive-date=14 February 2017|url-status=live}}</ref>
