'''Taylor Jacks Kinney''' (born July 15, 1981<ref name="TVGuide-bio">{{cite web |url=http://www.tvguide.com/celebrities/taylor-kinney/282537/ |title=Taylor Kinney |work=[[TV Guide]] |accessdate=September 17, 2016}}</ref>) is an American actor and model. He is known for playing Mason Lockwood in ''[[The Vampire Diaries]]'', Jared in ''[[Zero Dark Thirty]]'', Phil in ''[[The Other Woman (2014 film)|The Other Woman]]'', and Chicago Fire Department [[Lieutenant]] [[Kelly Severide]] in ''[[Chicago Fire (TV series)|Chicago Fire]]'', ''[[Chicago P.D. (TV series)|Chicago P.D.]]'' and ''[[Chicago Med]]''.

== Early life ==
Taylor Jacks Kinney<ref name=FamilySearch-USPR-2009>{{cite web|title=Taylor Jacks Kinney - United States Public Records|url=https://familysearch.org/pal:/MM9.1.1/23W9-RZT|website=[[FamilySearch]]|accessdate=October 11, 2014}}</ref> was born in [[Lancaster, Pennsylvania]].<ref name="TVGuide-bio" />

In 2000, he graduated from [[Lancaster Mennonite School]].<ref name=Lancaster-GagaVisit-2013>{{cite web|url=http://lancasteronline.com/article/local/462488_Lady-Gaga-blends-in-during-visit-to-Lancaster-County.html|title=Lady Gaga blends in during visit to Lancaster County|work=[[Lancaster Online]]|date=March 28, 2013|accessdate=July 24, 2013|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130606070632/http://lancasteronline.com/article/local/462488_Lady-Gaga-blends-in-during-visit-to-Lancaster-County.html|archivedate=June 6, 2013|df=mdy-all}}</ref> Kinney studied Business Management at [[West Virginia University]] in [[Morgantown, West Virginia]].<ref name=USAToday-ChiFire-2012>{{cite web|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/life/2012/10/09/taylor-kinney-chicago-fire/1620559|title=Actor Taylor Kinney heats up 'Chicago Fire'|date=October 10, 2012|accessdate=March 29, 2014|work=[[USA Today]]|publisher=[[Gannett Company]]}}</ref>

== Career ==
Kinney played Luke Gianni on ''[[Fashion House]]'' (2006) on [[MyNetworkTV]], and played Glenn Morrison on ''[[Trauma (U.S. TV series)|Trauma]]''<ref name=NBC-Trauma-Bio>{{cite web|title=Taylor Kinney as Glenn Morris|url=http://www.nbc.com/trauma/bios/taylor_kinney.shtml|website=[[Trauma (U.S. TV series)|Trauma]] |publisher=[[NBC]] |accessdate=December 19, 2014 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100212072915/http://www.nbc.com/trauma/bios/taylor_kinney.shtml|archivedate=February 12, 2010}}</ref> on [[National Broadcasting Company|NBC]].<ref name=TVGuide-Vampire-2010>{{cite news|first=William|last=Keck|title=Keck's Exclusives! Vampire Diaries Casts Mason Lockwood|date=July 10, 2010|url=http://www.tvguide.com/News/Kecks-Exclusives-Vampire-1020342.aspx|work=[[TV Guide]]|accessdate=July 12, 2010}}</ref> Kinney had a recurring role on the supernatural drama ''[[The Vampire Diaries]]'', joining the cast during the second season as Mason Lockwood. In 2011, Kinney was featured in [[Lady Gaga]]'s music video for the song "[[You and I (Lady Gaga song)|You and I]]".<ref name=BelfastTelegraph-Gaga-Romance-2011>{{Cite news|url=http://www.belfasttelegraph.co.uk/entertainment/news/lady-gaga-goes-public-with-romance-16096646.html |date=December 29, 2011|title=Lady Gaga goes public with romance|work=[[The Belfast Telegraph]]|accessdate=January 6, 2012}}</ref>

In 2012, Kinney landed a starring role on ''[[Chicago Fire (TV series)|Chicago Fire]]'', a drama series that follows the lives of firefighters and paramedics. Kinney plays Lieutenant Kelly Severide on Rescue Squad 3 opposite former ''[[House (TV series)|House]]'' star [[Jesse Spencer]]. Also in 2012, Kinney appeared on the big screen in [[Kathryn Bigelow]]'s acclaimed war drama ''[[Zero Dark Thirty]]''. In 2012, he guest starred on ''[[Shameless (U.S. TV series)|Shameless]]'' as Craig, the former high school crush of the lead character Fiona (played by [[Emmy Rossum]]).<ref name=Zap2It-Shameless-2012>{{cite news|first=Jean|last=Bentley|title='Shameless': Emmy Rossum on romancing James Wolk, Taylor Kinney & buying a bra in Season 2|date=January 6, 2012|url=http://blog.zap2it.com/frominsidethebox/2012/01/emmy-rossum-talks-love-interests-nudity-in-shameless-season-2.html|work=[[Zap2it]]|accessdate=January 17, 2012|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120117152602/http://blog.zap2it.com/frominsidethebox/2012/01/emmy-rossum-talks-love-interests-nudity-in-shameless-season-2.html|archivedate=January 17, 2012|df=mdy-all}}</ref>

== Personal life ==

Kinney began dating singer-songwriter [[Lady Gaga]] in July 2011. They first met in [[Nebraska]] while working on the music video for her song "[[You and I (Lady Gaga song)|You and I]]", and started dating soon afterwards.<ref name=3News-YouandI-2013>{{cite web|title=Lady Gaga fell for Taylor Kinney on video shoot|url=http://www.3news.co.nz/Lady-Gaga-fell-for-Taylor-Kinney-on-video-shoot/tabid/418/articleID/322773/Default.aspx | work=[[3news.co.nz]]|date=November 25, 2013}}</ref> They became engaged in February 2015,<ref>{{cite news|last1=Lewis|first1=Barry|title=Our editor congratulated her: Lady Gaga is engaged|url=http://www.recordonline.com/article/20150216/ENTERTAINMENT/150219500|accessdate=February 16, 2015|work=[[Times-Herald Record]]|date=February 16, 2015}}</ref> but ended up calling off their engagement in July 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://people.com/celebrity/lady-gaga-and-taylor-kinney-split-couple-end-engagement/ |title=Lady Gaga and Taylor Kinney Split; Couple End Engagement |publisher=People.com |date=2016-07-19 |accessdate=July 19, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Mizoguchi|first1=Karen|title=Lady Gaga Breaks Her Silence Following Taylor Kinney Breakup: We 'Have Always Believed We Are Soulmates'|url=http://people.com/celebrity/lady-gaga-breaks-her-silence-following-taylor-kinney-breakup/|accessdate=November 30, 2016|work=[[People (magazine)|People]]|date=July 20, 2016}}</ref><!--Do NOT add any further detail unless one or both officially confirms a separation, marriage, or pregnancy per WP:BLPGOSSIP, WP:NOTGOSSIP, and WP:NOTNEWS-->
