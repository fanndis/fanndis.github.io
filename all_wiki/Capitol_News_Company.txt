'''Capitol News Company''', LLC is an American media company based in [[Arlington, Virginia]], United States. It is a private holding company owned by [[Robert Allbritton|Robert L. Allbritton]].<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20150402194412/http://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/private/person.asp?personId=617295&privcapId=775311&previousCapId=775311&previousTitle=Allbritton%20Communications%20Co Robert L. Allbritton], ''[[Bloomberg L.P.|Bloomberg]]'', April 21, 2015. Retrieved February 28, 2018.</ref> Its primary publication is ''[[Politico]]''.

Capitol News Company was created when Politico was spun out of [[Allbritton Communications]] Company in 2009. The company also publishes ''Politico New York'',<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/09/09/politico-capital-new-york-deal_n_3893579.html |title=Politico Expands In NY With Capital New York Purchase |website=[[HuffPost]]|date= 9 September 2013|accessdate=30 March 2015}}</ref> a web site and magazine focused on the politics of New York City and New York state, as well as the inner workings of the New York-based news media, and which later expanded to cover New Jersey and Florida before being rebranded as Politico websites.
