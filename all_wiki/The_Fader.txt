'''''The Fader''''' (stylized as '''''FADER''''') is a magazine based in [[New York City]] that was launched in 1999 by [[Rob Stone (entrepreneur)|Rob Stone]] and [[Jon Cohen (entrepreneur)|Jon Cohen]]. The magazine covers music, style and culture.<ref>{{cite web |url= http://advertising.thefader.com/about/ |title=Fader Media : About |work=thefader.com |year=2015 |accessdate=February 4, 2015}}</ref> It was the first print publication to be released on [[iTunes]].<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.mediapost.com/publications/article/44274/fader-mag-to-distribute-issues-on-itunes.html |title=Fader Mag To Distribute Issues on iTunes |first=Erik |last=Sass |publisher=Media Daily News |quote=This [the free Fader iTunes download] is the first exclusive sponsorship deal to distribute a magazine on iTunes |date=June 8, 2006 |accessdate=September 28, 2009}}</ref>
It is owned by The Fader Media group, which also includes its website, thefader.com, Fader films, [[Fader Label]] and Fader TV.

==The Fader Fort==
The Fader Fort is an annual invitation-only event at Austin Texas's [[South by Southwest]] (SXSW) founded in 2001. The four-day party features live performances. Fader Fort NYC is a party produced during the annual [[CMJ Music Marathon]].<ref>{{cite news | url=http://artsbeat.blogs.nytimes.com/2010/10/22/an-airy-respite-from-cmjs-subterranean-scene/ | title = An Airy Respite from CMJ's Subterranean Scene | newspaper=[[The New York Times]] | first=Ben |last=Sisario | date=October 22, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web| url=http://www.thefader.com/2010/10/19/presenting-the-fader-fort-nyc-2010-lineup-and-rsvp/ | publisher= Fader | title=Presenting the Fader Fort NYC 2010 Lineup | date= October 19, 2010}}</ref>
