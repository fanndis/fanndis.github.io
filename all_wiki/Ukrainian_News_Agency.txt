The '''Ukrainian News Agency''' ({{lang-uk|Інформаційне агентство "Українські Новини"}}; ''Informatsiyne ahentstvo "Ukrayins'ki Novyny"'') is a [[Kyiv]]-based<ref name=contact>[http://www.ukranews.com/eng/agency/contacts.html Contact section - official website]</ref> [[Ukraine|Ukrainian]] [[news agency]]. It produces and provides political, business and financial information, as well as a popular [[photo]] reporting service. Since February 2008<ref>[http://www.uaimg.com/en/press/ukrainski_novini_stali_chastju_u_a_inter_media_group_limited.html "Ukrainian News Agency Enters U.A. Inter Media Group"], U.A. Inter Media Group Ltd. (5 February 2008)</ref> the company is part of the Inter Media Group Ltd., formerly U.A. Inter Media Group, which itself is a subsidiary of the Russian-based company [[GDF Media Ltd.]]<ref>[https://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=37849678 "Company Overview of Inter Media Group Limited"] [[Bloomberg L.P.|Bloomberg]]</ref><ref>[https://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=228823703 "Company Overview of GDF Media Ltd."] [[Bloomberg L.P.|Bloomberg]]</ref><ref>[https://groupdf.com/en/press-center/news/gdf-media-limited-acquires-inter-media-group-limited/ "GDF Media Limited Acquires Inter Media Group Limited" (press release)] (7 February 2013) GDF</ref> Inter Media Group Limited also owns 61% of the Ukrainian TV channel [[Inter (TV channel)|Inter]].<ref>[http://www.uaimg.com/en/about.html U.A. Inter Media Group - Official website] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081121034304/http://www.uaimg.com/en/about.html |date=21 November 2008 }}</ref>

Founded in 1993,<ref name=history>[http://www.ukranews.com/eng/agency/about.html About the agency - Official website]</ref> by Mykhailo Kolomiets and Volodymyr Hranovskiy<ref name=killed>[http://www.freemedia.at/cms/ipi/statements_detail.html?ctxid=CH0055&docid=CMS1146579086027&year=2002 IPI concerned about death of independent news agency's director], [[International Press Institute]] (27 November 2002)</ref> the agency now used by about 10,000 clients daily. The agency offers 27 information and analytical products in [[Russian language|Russian]], [[Ukrainian language|Ukrainian]] and [[English language|English]]. Ukrainian News is the only agency in Ukraine that translates 100% of its products into English.<ref>[http://www.uaimg.com/en/structure/media/ukr_news.html The Ukrainian News agency], U.A. Inter Media Group Ltd. (5 February 2008)</ref>

On 18 November 2002 the body of founder Mykhailo Kolomiets was found dead hanging from a tree in [[Belarus]] after being missing for four weeks.<ref name=killed/>

In May 2006 the agency opened a modern [[press conference]] hall.<ref name=history/>

The agency has its own building. It is located just outside [[Palats Sportu (Kiev Metro)|Palats Sportu subway station]] in central [[Kyiv]].<ref name=contact/>
