'''Hotel Shilla''' ([[Hangul]]: 신라호텔) is a South Korean operator of luxury hotels and [[duty-free shop]]s. It is a member of [[The Leading Hotels of the World]]. [[Lee Boo-jin]] is the general manager and CEO of The Shilla. The company is an affiliate of [[Samsung]].

==History==
Hotel Shilla started operations in March 1979 at the direction of [[Lee Byung-chull]], founder of the [[Samsung|Samsung Group]]. Before 1979, it was the state guest house of [[Republic of Korea]] under the [[Park Chung-hee]] government. Now, it has been expanding into the commissioned management of fitness facilities as well as into the restaurant business. The Shilla focuses on "the harmony and beauty of modernism and tradition". They have hotels located in [[Seoul]], [[Jeju City|Jeju]], and [[Suzhou]], [[China]].

In January 2008, The Shilla was selected as one of the top 500 hotels in the world by ''[[Travel & Leisure]]''.<ref>[http://www.hotelshilla.net/en/company/history.jsp The Shilla] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100225062825/http://www.hotelshilla.net/en/company/history.jsp |date=2010-02-25 }}</ref>

The Seoul hotel reopened to the public on August 1, 2013 after seven months of renovations, featuring the first year-round outdoor pool in Seoul ("Urban Island") and the Shilla-[[John Sitaras|Sitaras]] Fitness Center.<ref>{{cite web | title = The Shilla reopens after 7 months | author= Kim Ji-soo | publisher = [[The Korea Times]] | date = August 1, 2013| url = https://www.koreatimes.co.kr/www/news/culture/2013/08/320_140348.html|accessdate = }}</ref><ref>{{cite web | title = After seven-month renovation, hotel unveils new plush, swanky look | author= Lee Woo-young| publisher = [[The Korea Herald]] | date =August 1, 2013| url = http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20130802000710&mod=skb|accessdate = }}</ref>

==Facilities==
There are two Presidential Suites at the Shilla Hotel, in [[Jangchung-dong]], [[Jung-gu, Seoul|Jung-gu]]: the {{convert|280.9|sqm|adj=on}} south wing with a blend of traditional Korean decor and the {{convert|390|sqm|adj=on}} north wing with a Parisian palace decor.<ref>{{cite news|title=Seoul's most expensive hotel suites|url=http://www.cnngo.com/seoul/visit/seouls-most-expensive-hotel-suites-005921|accessdate=3 June 2012|newspaper=CNN Go|date=3 January 2012|author=Tey-Marie Astudillo|author2=Frances Cha|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120724054230/http://www.cnngo.com/seoul/visit/seouls-most-expensive-hotel-suites-005921|archive-date=24 July 2012|url-status=dead}}</ref>

The fitness center, affiliated with [[John Sitaras#Sitaras Fitness|Sitaras Fitness]], is equipped with a digital measurement room, a first in Korea, and provides a differentiated fitness coaching service using a smart coaching system. A fitness program is customized for each client according to the results of a physical fitness assessment for systematic management.<ref>{{cite web | title = 새롭게 바뀐 서울신라호텔 피트니스 센터 | author= Park Han-Bitnoori | publisher = [[Men's Health (magazine)|Men's Health]] (Korean edition)| date = November 26, 2013 | url = http://e-menshealth.design.co.kr/in_magazine/sub.html?at=view&p_no=&info_id=64568&c_id=000000030002|accessdate = December 9, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | title = Shilla-Sitaras Fitness Center | author=  | publisher = | date =  | url = http://www.shilla.net/seoul/retreat/fnGym.do|accessdate = December 9, 2013}}</ref>
