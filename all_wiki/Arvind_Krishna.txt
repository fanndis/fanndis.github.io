'''Arvind Krishna''' (born 1962) is an [[Indian Americans|Indian-American]] business executive. He has been the CEO of  [[IBM]] since April 2020.<ref>{{cite news |title=IBM names Indian-origin Arvind Krishna as CEO |url=https://www.deccanchronicle.com/technology/in-other-news/310120/ibm-names-indian-origin-arvind-krishna-as-ceo.html |work=[[Deccan Chronicle]] |agency=[[Asian News International]] |date=January 31, 2020 |access-date=October 8, 2020 |archive-date=October 5, 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201005175348/https://www.deccanchronicle.com/technology/in-other-news/310120/ibm-names-indian-origin-arvind-krishna-as-ceo.html |url-status=live }}</ref> Krishna began his career at IBM in 1990, at IBM's [[Thomas J. Watson Research Center]],<ref name="IBM's 'surprise' CEO">{{cite news |url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-ibm-ceo-factbox/ibms-surprise-ceo-arvind-krishna-to-take-over-from-ginni-rometty-idUSKBN1ZU2LW |title=IBM's 'surprise' CEO: Arvind Krishna to take over from Ginni Rometty |agency=[[Reuters]] |date=January 31, 2020 |access-date=October 9, 2020 |archive-date=April 15, 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200415022512/https://www.reuters.com/article/us-ibm-ceo-factbox/ibms-surprise-ceo-arvind-krishna-to-take-over-from-ginni-rometty-idUSKBN1ZU2LW |url-status=live }}</ref> and was promoted to [[senior vice president]] in 2015.<ref name="LinkedIn">{{cite web |url=https://www.linkedin.com/in/arvindkrishna/ |title=Arvind Krishna |website=[[LinkedIn]] |access-date=October 8, 2020 |archive-date=September 14, 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150914013251/https://www.linkedin.com/in/arvindkrishna |url-status=live }}</ref><ref name="What you need to know">{{cite news|url=https://fortune.com/2020/02/03/ibm-ceo-arvind-krishna-what-you-need-to-know/|title=What you need to know about new IBM CEO Arvind Krishna|work=[[Fortune (magazine)|Fortune]]|first=David Z.|last=Morris|date=February 3, 2020|access-date=October 8, 2020|archive-date=October 5, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201005172758/https://fortune.com/2020/02/03/ibm-ceo-arvind-krishna-what-you-need-to-know/|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Career==
Krishna joined IBM in 1990, at IBM's [[Thomas J. Watson Research Center]],<ref name="IBM's 'surprise' CEO"/> and in 2015 was promoted to senior vice president of [[IBM Research]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.itjungle.com/2015/01/12/tfh011215-story02/ |title=IBM Reorganizes To Reflect Its New Business Machine |first=Timothy Prickett |last=Morgan |website=IT Jungle |date=January 12, 2015 later became senior vice president of IBM's cloud and cognitive software division.<ref name="Krishna ascends"/> He has co-authored more than a dozen patents, has been the editor of [[IEEE]] and [[Association for Computing Machinery|ACM]] journals, and has published extensively in technical journals.<ref name="Mint Meet"/> He also led the building and expansion of new markets for IBM in artificial intelligence, cloud, quantum computing, and blockchain.<ref>{{cite news
