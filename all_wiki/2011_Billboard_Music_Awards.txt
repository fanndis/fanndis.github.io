The '''2011 [[Billboard Music Award|''Billboard'' Music Awards]]''' were held May 22, 2011 at the [[MGM Grand Garden Arena]] in [[Las Vegas Valley|Las Vegas]], [[Nevada]] and hosted by [[Ken Jeong]]. The awards recognized the most popular artists and albums from 2010.

==Critical reception and controversy==
Overall, critics praised the performances in whole. Critics, however, noted Beyoncé as the standout performer of the night.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/1645345/first-mtv-tj-gabi-gregg-wants-to-give-followers-a-voice/|title=First MTV TJ Gabi Gregg Wants To Give Followers 'A Voice'|first=|last=archive-Fernanda-Diaz 08/09/2010|work=mtv.com|accessdate=9 March 2017}}</ref> However, some controversy was generated from the show. The Parents Television Council criticized Rihanna and Britney Spears's Performance of [[S&M (song)|S&M (Remix)]], calling it "explicit", with PTC president saying "I cannot imagine what would possibly lead the ABC television network to air a profanity-laced, S&M sex show on primetime broadcast television", while criticizing the choice to air the performance first, on what they claim is a normally family targeted time slot (the show aired at 8:00 p.m.).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://thehill.com/blogs/hillicon-valley/technology/162925-parents-group-targets-billboard-performance-by-rihanna-britney-spears|title=Parents group targets Billboard performance by Rihanna, Britney Spears|first=Gautham|last=Nagesh|date=24 May 2011|work=thehill.com|accessdate=9 March 2017}}</ref>
