{{main|2009 Wimbledon Championships}}
[[Serena Williams|Serena]] and [[Venus Williams]] successfully defended their title, defeating [[Samantha Stosur]] and [[Rennae Stubbs]] in the final, 7–6<sup>(7–4)</sup>, 6–4 to win the Ladies' Doubles tennis title at the 2009 Wimbledon Championships.<ref name="Barrett">{{cite book|last=Barrett|first=John|title=Wimbledon: The Official History|date=2014|publisher=Vision Sports Publishing|isbn=9-781909-534230|edition=4th}}</ref> As with the previous year, the [[Williams sisters]] won the title without dropping a set the entire tournament.
