'''Howard Stanley Baldwin''' (December 15, 1934 &ndash; July 22, 2008) was an American businessman and politician.

Born in [[New Haven, Connecticut]], Baldwin moved to [[Scottsdale, Arizona]] in 1960. Baldwin was in radio and television advertising business and later was also in the real estate and insurance business. Baldwin was involved with the [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican Party]]. Baldwin served in the [[Arizona State Senate]] from 1969 until 1975. He died suddenly in the [[Caribbean]] while on vacation with his family.<ref>[http://www.legacy.com/obituaries/azcentral/obituary.aspx?n=howard-s-baldwin&pid=114899345 Howard S.
