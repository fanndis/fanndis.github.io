'''A Very Good Production''' is an American film and television [[production company]] founded by comedian, television host, actress [[Ellen DeGeneres]] and [[Warner Bros. Television]]'s [[Telepictures]] in 2003. It is known for producing the long-running series ''[[The Ellen DeGeneres Show]]'' (2003–present).<ref name="adweek">{{cite web |last1=Granatstein |first1=Lisa |title=How Adweek Media Visionary Ellen DeGeneres Built a Media Empire |url=https://www.adweek.com/tv-video/how-adweek-media-visionary-ellen-degeneres-built-a-media-empire |website=[[Adweek]] |accessdate=December 4, 2018}}</ref><ref name="deadline-08-2012">{{cite web |last1=Andreeva |first1=Nellie |title=Jeff Kleeman To Run Ellen DeGeneres' Production Company |url=https://deadline.com/2012/08/jeff-kleeman-to-run-ellen-degeneres-production-company-320294 |website=[[Deadline Hollywood|Deadline]] |accessdate=December 4, 2018 |language=en |date=16 August 2012}}</ref>

The company co-founded the former record label, [[eleveneleven]] in 2010.<ref>{{cite web |title=Ellen Degeneres News - Yahoo! Music |url=http://music.yahoo.com/ellen-degeneres/news/ellen-degeneres-starts-own-record-label--62002061 |website=Yahoo! Music |accessdate=December 10, 2018 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120929075859/http://music.yahoo.com/ellen-degeneres/news/ellen-degeneres-starts-own-record-label--62002061 |archivedate=September 29, 2012 |date=May 26, 2010}}</ref>
