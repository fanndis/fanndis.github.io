{{Main article|2002 US Open (tennis)}}

World No. 1 [[Serena Williams]] defeated the two-time defending champion, [[Venus Williams]], 6-4, 6-3, to win the Women's Singles tennis title at the [[2002 US Open (tennis)|2002 US Open]]. This was rematch of the [[2001 US Open – Women's Singles|previous year's final]], was Serena Williams third consecutive Grand Slam title, and [[List_of_Grand_Slam_related_tennis_records#Won_a_title_without_losing_a_set|second consecutive without losing a set]].

Four-time Grand Slam champion and former World No. 1 [[Arantxa Sánchez Vicario]] made her final Grand Slam appearance, losing to French qualifier [[Marion Bartoli]] in the first round. Future World No. 1 and three-time Grand Slam finalist [[Dinara Safina]] made her first Grand Slam appearance. She lost in the second round to Serena Williams.
