The following is a list of on-demand [[music streaming]] services. The services offer streaming of full-length content via the Internet as a part of their service,<ref>{{cite web| url=http://stream-recorder.com/forum/showthread.php?p=9 | work=Matthews, Paul | publisher=SRF | title=The best online music service! | accessdate=1 April 2011}}</ref> without the listener necessarily purchasing a file for download.<ref>{{cite web| url=http://aisel.aisnet.org/amcis2010/36/ | work=Doerr, Jonathan; Benlian, Alexander; Vetter, Johannes; Hess, Thomas | title=Pricing of Content Services – An Empirical Investigation of Music as a Service | accessdate=26 February 2011}}</ref> This type of service is comparable to [[Internet radio]]. Many of these sites have advertising and offer [[Non-free software|non-free]] options in the style of an [[online music store]]. For a list of online music stores that provide a means of purchasing and downloading music as files of some sort, see: [[Comparison of online music stores]]. Many of both types of sites offer services similar to an online music database.
