'''The Monster Tour''' was a co-headlining [[concert tour]] by American rapper [[Eminem]] and Barbadian singer [[Rihanna]]. The tour began on August 7, 2014 at the Rose Bowl in Pasadena and concluded on August 23, 2014 at the Comerica Park in Detroit.

==Background==
[[File:Monster Tour Rihanna Eminem.jpg|thumb]]
[[Eminem]] and [[Rihanna]] first collaborated in 2010 with "[[Love the Way You Lie]]". Since then they have released three more collaborations: "[[Love the Way You Lie (Part II)]]" (2010)", "[[Numb (Rihanna song)|Numb]]" (2012) and "[[The Monster (song)|The Monster]]" (2013).

In February 2014, it was announced that they would embark on a mini-tour. On March 19, 2014, it was confirmed that it would be called ''The Monster Tour'' and three dates were announced.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=10151969523390079&set=a.84897990078.93478.45309870078&type=1&stream_ref=10 |title=Eminem - Timeline Photos |work=Facebook.com}}</ref> Tickets went on sale to the general public on March 28, 2014.<ref name="Tour Dates Announced">{{cite web |url=http://www.eminem.com/news/monster-tour-dates-announced-and-ticket-information |title=EMINEM - The Monster Tour - Dates Announced and Ticket Information |work=Eminem.com}}</ref> On March 21, 2014, three further dates were added to the tour due to popular demand.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.facebook.com/events/1465019217060542/?context=create&source=49 |title=IMPORTANT UPDATE...PRESALE NOW + 2nd SHOWS ADDED! |work=Facebook.com}}</ref>

==Commercial performance==
The Monster Tour was the one of the highest-grossing tours of 2014 in North America, with a gross of $36 million in only six shows. A total of 581,276 people attended (including outside the stadiums).

The two shows at [[MetLife Stadium]] in [[New Jersey]] grossed $12.4 million with 101, 941 people attending each concert. It was the sixth highest-grossing box office of the year in North America, only behind [[Billy Joel]], [[George Strait]], [[One Direction]] and [[Garth Brooks]].<ref name="pollstarpro.com">http://www.pollstarpro.com/files/charts2014/2014YearEndTop200ConcertGrosses.pdf</ref>
