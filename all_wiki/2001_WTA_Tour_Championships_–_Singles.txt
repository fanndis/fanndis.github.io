{{main|2001 WTA Tour Championships}}

[[Martina Hingis]] was the defending champion, but pulled out due to ankle surgery.

[[Serena Williams]] won the championship by default after [[Lindsay Davenport]] withdrew from the final.

This was the last professional tournament in former world No. 4 [[Anke Huber]]'s career.
