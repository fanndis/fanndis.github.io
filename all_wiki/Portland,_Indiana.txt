'''Portland''' is a city in and the [[county seat]] of [[Jay County, Indiana|Jay County]], [[Indiana]], United States.<ref name="GR6">{{cite web|url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx|accessdate=2011-06-07|title=Find a County|publisher=National Association of Counties}}</ref> The population was 6,223 at the [[2010 United States Census|2010 census]],<ref name="Census 2010">{{Cite web| url=https://factfinder.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/10_SF1/G001/1600000US1861236| title=Geographic Identifiers: 2010 Census Summary File 1 (G001): Portland city, Indiana| publisher=U.S. Census Bureau| work=American Factfinder| accessdate=May 16, 2018| archive-url=https://archive.today/20200213113125/https://factfinder.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/10_SF1/G001/1600000US1861236| archive-date=February 13, 2020| url-status=dead}}</ref> and in 2018 the estimated population was 6,085.<ref name="USCensusEst2018">{{cite web|url=https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/popest/data/tables.2018.html|title=Population and Housing Unit Estimates|accessdate=September 7, 2019}}</ref>

==History==
Portland was [[plat]]ted in 1837.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.jaycountyhistory.org/2009/incorporated-cities-and-towns#more-1179 | title=Incorporated Cities and Towns | publisher=Jay County Historical Society | accessdate=28 May 2014 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140529085622/http://www.jaycountyhistory.org/2009/incorporated-cities-and-towns#more-1179 | archive-date=29 May 2014 | url-status=dead }}</ref> It was named after [[Portland, Maine]].<ref>{{cite book|last=Baker|first=Ronald L.|title=From Needmore to Prosperity: Hoosier Place Names in Folklore and History|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=SXB5AAAAMAAJ|date=October 1995|publisher=Indiana University Press|isbn=978-0-253-32866-3|page=269|quote=...named for his hometown, Portland, Maine.}}</ref>

The [[Jay County Courthouse]], [[Portland Commercial Historic District]], and [[Jonas Votaw House]] are listed on the [[National Register of Historic Places]].<ref name="nris">{{NRISref|version=2010a}}</ref>

==Geography==
Portland is located in central Jay County at {{Coord|40|26|2|N|84|58|48|W|type:city}} (40.433884, -84.979914),<ref name="GR1">{{cite web|url=https://www.census.gov/geographies/reference-files/time-series/geo/gazetteer-files.html|publisher=[[United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2011-04-23|date=2011-02-12|title=US Gazetteer files: 2010, 2000, and 1990}}</ref> along the [[Salamonie River]].<ref name=DeLorme>[[DeLorme]] (1998).  ''Indiana Atlas & Gazetteer''.  Yarmouth, Maine: DeLorme.  {{ISBN|0-89933-211-0}}</ref>  [[U.S. Route 27]] (Meridian Street) runs through the center of the city, leading north {{convert|49|mi}} to [[Fort Wayne, Indiana|Fort Wayne]] and south {{convert|43|mi}} to [[Richmond, Indiana|Richmond]]. [[Indiana State Road 26]] enters Portland from the west on Votaw Street and from the east on Water Street; SR-26 leads west {{convert|21|mi}} to [[Hartford City, Indiana|Hartford City]] and east {{convert|10|mi|0}} to the [[Ohio]] border near [[Fort Recovery, Ohio|Fort Recovery]]. [[Indiana State Road 67]] follows SR-26 west out of Portland, then leads southwest {{convert|29|mi}} to [[Muncie, Indiana|Muncie]].

According to the U.S. Census Bureau, Portland has a total area of {{convert|12.88|sqkm|order=flip|2}}, of which {{convert|6524|sqm|acre|order=flip|1}}, or 0.05%, are water.<ref name="CenPopGazetteer2016">{{cite web|title=2016 U.S. Gazetteer Files|url=https://www2.census.gov/geo/docs/maps-data/data/gazetteer/2016_Gazetteer/2016_gaz_place_18.txt|publisher=United States Census Bureau|accessdate=Jul 28, 2017}}</ref> The [[Salamonie River]] runs through the city just south of its center. The Salamonie is a west-flowing tributary of the [[Wabash River]].

===2010 census===
At the [[2010 United States Census|2010 census]] there were 6,223 people, 2,607 households, and 1,620 families living in the city. The population density was {{convert|1338.3|PD/sqmi|PD/km2|1}}. There were 3,005 housing units at an average density of {{convert|646.2|/sqmi|/km2|1}}. The [[Race and ethnicity in the United States Census#2010 census|racial makup]] of the city was 94.5% White, 0.4% African American, 0.5% Asian, 3.1% from other races, and 1.5% from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino of any race were 5.8%.<ref name="wwwcensusgov">{{cite web|url=https://www.census.gov|title=U.S. Census website|publisher=[[United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2017-02-08}}</ref>

Of the 2,607 households 30.4% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 42.7% were married couples living together, 14.0% single female householder, 5.4% single male householder, and 37.9% were non-families. 32.1% of households were one person and 14.2% were one person aged 65 or older. The average household size was 2.32 and the average family size was 2.89.

The median age was 39.4 years. 23.7% of residents were under the age of 18; 8.6% were between the ages of 18 and 24; 25.1% were from 25 to 44; 24.7% were from 45 to 64; and 17.9% were 65 or older. The gender makeup of the city was 46.8% male and 53.2% female.

===2000 census===
At the [[2000 United States Census|2000 census]] there were 6,437 people, 2,739 households, and 1,750 families living in the city. The population density was 1,566.8 people per square mile (604.7/km{{sup|2}}). There were 2,928 housing units at an average density of 712.7 per square mile (275.1/km{{sup|2}}).  The [[Race and ethnicity in the United States Census#2000 census|racial makup]] of the city was 96.49% White, 0.37% African American, 0.11% Native American, 0.34% Asian, 0.05% Pacific Islander 1.80% from other races, and 0.84% from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino of any race were 3.36%.<ref name="GR2" />

Of the 2,739 households 27.2% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 47.9% were married couples living together, 12.1% had a female householder with no husband present, and 36.1% were non-families. 32.3% of households were one person and 16.4% were one person aged 65 or older. The average household size was 2.28 and the average family size was 2.86.

The age distribution was 23.1% under the age of 18, 9.0% from 18 to 24, 26.8% from 25 to 44, 22.2% from 45 to 64, and 18.8% 65 or older. The median age was 38 years. For every 100 females, there were 87.9 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 83.3 males.

The median household income was $31,045 and the median income for a family was $41,329. Males had a median income of $29,728 versus $21,134 for females. The per capita income for the city was $18,375. About 5.1% of families and 9.6% of the population were below the [[poverty line]], including 11.9% of those under age 18 and 9.0% of those age 65 or over.
