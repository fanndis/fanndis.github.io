'''KonLive Distribution''' (or simply '''KonLive''') was a [[record label]] founded by R&B singer [[Akon]].{{cn|date=August 2020}} The label served as a [[joint-venture]] deal between [[Jimmy Iovine]] and [[Interscope Records]]. Notable artists briefly signed to the label include [[Lady Gaga]], [[Kardinal Offishall]], [[Colby O'Donis]], [[Brick & Lace]], [[Mali Music (singer)|Mali Music]], [[Natalia Kills]], [[Jeffree Star]] and [[R. City]].<ref>https://www.discogs.com/label/755634-KonLive-Distribution</ref>
