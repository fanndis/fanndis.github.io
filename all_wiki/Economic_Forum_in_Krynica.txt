'''Krynica Economic Forum''' is an annual international meeting of [[economy|economic]] and [[political elite]]s held in the first half of September<sup>[[Economic Forum in Krynica#cite note-1|[1]]]</sup> at the [[ski resort|ski]] and [[spa resort]] of [[Krynica-Zdrój]] in southern [[Poland]]. The Forum was founded by Zygmunt Berdychowski<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://emerging-europe.com/special-reports/economic-forum-2016/krynica-mapping-the-road-to-central-and-eastern-europe-26-years-on/|title=Krynica: Mapping the Road to Central and Eastern Europe — 26 Years On - Emerging-Europe.com|date=2016-09-12|newspaper=Emerging-Europe.com|language=en-US|access-date=2016-10-24}}</ref> in 1992 and it is organised by the Foundation Institute for Eastern Studies in [[Warsaw]].
Within 25 years of its history the Krynica Economic Forum has evolved from a small conference, with about 100 participants, to one of the biggest and most recognisable meetings of the political and business leaders of [[Central and Eastern Europe]],<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://innpoland.pl/129177,od-spotkania-w-malej-salce-po-miano-davos-europy-srodkowo-wschodniej-jak-forum-ekonomiczne-w-krynicy-podbil-europe|title=Od spotkania w małej salce, po miano "Davos Europy Środkowo-Wschodniej". Jak Forum Ekonomiczne w Krynicy podbiło Europę|newspaper=INNPoland.pl|access-date=2016-10-24}}</ref> and is therefore  sometimes referred to as the "Eastern [[Davos]]".<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.worldbank.org/en/news/feature/2014/09/17/world-bank-in-the-krynica-economic-forum---the-davos-for-eastern-europe |title=World Bank in the Krynica Economic Forum – the “Davos for Eastern Europe” |access-date=2018-06-16}}</ref>

According to the organisers, the mission of the Economic Forum is to create a favorable climate for development of political and economic cooperation in Europe. The opinions of the Forum's speakers are often quoted by the international media and discussed by leading analysts.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.wrotapodlasia.pl/pl/region_i_gospodarka/wiadomosci/gospodarka/rozpoczyna-sie-xxvi-forum-ekonomiczne-w-krynicy.html|title=Trwa XXVI Forum Ekonomiczne w Krynicy {{!}} Wrota Podlasia|website=www.wrotapodlasia.pl|access-date=2016-10-24}}</ref>

== Guests ==
The Forum engages political, business and third sector leaders, i.a.: presidents, prime ministers,<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.ukrinform.net/rubric-economics/2079065-ukraine-hungary-agree-to-enhance-cooperation.html|title=Ukraine, Hungary agree to enhance cooperation  - 07.09.2016 15:14 — Ukrinform News|access-date=2016-10-24}}</ref> ministers, [[EU Commissioner|EU commissioners]], parliamentarians, presidents of large companies, experts, as well as representatives of cultural institutions and media. The 26th edition of the Economic Forum<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.warsawvoice.pl/WVpage/pages/article.php/28523/article|title=The Warsaw Voice|website=www.warsawvoice.pl|access-date=2016-10-24}}</ref> in 2016 was attended by over 3,000 visitors<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.thenews.pl/1/6/Artykul/269946,European-politics-discussed-in-Krynica|title=European politics discussed in Krynica|newspaper=Polskie Radio dla Zagranicy|access-date=2016-10-24}}</ref> from 60 countries in Europe, Asia and the Americas.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.thenews.pl/1/6/Artykul/270266,Record-for-Krynica-Economic-Forum|title=Record for Krynica Economic Forum|newspaper=Polskie Radio dla Zagranicy|access-date=2016-10-24}}</ref>
