The '''''Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology''''' is a bimonthly [[peer-reviewed]] [[scientific journal]] that was established in 1980 by [[Jiri Zidek (paleontologist)|Jiri Zidek]] ([[University of Oklahoma]]). It covers all aspects of [[vertebrate paleontology]], including [[Vertebrate#Evolutionary history|vertebrate origins]], [[evolution]], [[Morphology (biology)|functional morphology]], [[Taxonomy (biology)|taxonomy]], [[biostratigraphy]], [[paleoecology]], [[paleobiogeography]], and [[paleoanthropology]]. The journal is published by [[Taylor & Francis]] on behalf of the [[Society of Vertebrate Paleontology]]. According to the ''[[Journal Citation Reports]]'', the journal has a 2017 [[impact factor]] of 2.190.<ref name=WoS>{{cite book |year=2018 |chapter=Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology |title=2017 Journal Citation Reports |publisher=[[Clarivate Analytics]] |edition=Science |series=[[Web of Science]]|title-link=Journal Citation Reports }}</ref>
