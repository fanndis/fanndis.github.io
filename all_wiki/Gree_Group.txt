'''Zhuhai Gree Group Co., Ltd.''' is a Chinese [[state-owned enterprise]] based in [[Zhuhai]], Guangdong Province. The enterprise was owned and supervised by the State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission (SASAC) of {{ill|Zhuhai Municipal People's Government|zh|珠海市人民政府|lt=Zhuhai local government}}.

Gree Group was the parent company of publicly traded companies [[Gree Electric]] and [[Gree Real Estate]] before they were transferred in the late 2010s.
==History==
Gree Group was an investment vehicle of Zhuhai local government, which [[Gree Electric]] and [[Gree Real Estate]] were the notable assets of the company. However, in the past, multiple managers of the company was arrested for corruption.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.people.com.cn/GB/jingji/1039/2722533.html|script-title=zh:格力集团两副总被刑拘　股价昨跌4.58个百分点|date=19 August 2004|access-date=29 April 2018|work=北京娱乐信报|publication-place=Beijing|language=zh-cn|via=''[[People's Daily]]'' website}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.people.com.cn/GB/jingji/1039/3083851.html|script-title=zh:格力集团一高管再入牢狱　格力电器仍是国企身|date=28 December 2004|access-date=29 April 2018|work=第一财经日报|publication-place=Shanghai|language=zh-cn|via=''People's Daily'' website}}</ref>

The stake of Gree Real Estate that held by the group was transferred to another entity of Zhuhai Municipal People's Government in mid-2015 (along with others assets, such as a management company of Zhuhai checkpoint of [[Hong Kong–Zhuhai–Macau Bridge]]).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.shclearing.com/xxpl/zdsxjqt/201505/t20150522_88451.html|script-title=zh:珠海格力集团有限公司关于出资人拟无偿划转资产及转让部分股权事项后续进展情况的公告|trans-title=News update regarding shareholder transferring some assets of our company|publisher=Gree Group|date=22 May 2015|access-date=25 December 2016|via=Shanghai Clearing House website|language=zh-cn}}</ref>  As at 31 December 2015, Gree Group owned just 18.22% stake of Gree Electric,<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://disclosure.szse.cn/finalpage/2016-04-29/1202265764.PDF|title=2015 Nián nián dù bào gào|script-title=zh:2015年年度报告|trans-title=2015 Annual Report|date=29 April 2016|access-date=10 December 2016|publisher=Gree Electric|via=Shenzhen Stock Exchange|language=zh-cn|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161220194804/http://disclosure.szse.cn/finalpage/2016-04-29/1202265764.PDF|archive-date=20 December 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref> but able to control their board of directors by nominating them for election. Gree Group and Gree Electric also shared the same chairwoman [[Dong Mingzhu]] until November 2016. She was replaced by [[Zhou Lewei]] ({{zh|s=周乐伟|labels=no}}). Zhou also became the [[Party Committee Secretary]] of the group, replacing [[Meng Xiangkai]] ({{zh|s=孟祥凯|labels=no}}).<ref>{{cite press release|url=http://www.shclearing.com/xxpl/zdsxjqt/201612/t20161202_207425.html|script-title=zh:珠海格力集团有限公司关于董事、董事长、法定代表人及党委委员、书记变更的提示性公告|trans-title=Announcement on changes in director, chairman, legal representative, member and secretary of party committee of our company |publisher=Gree Group|date=2 December 2016|access-date=25 December 2016|via=Shanghai Clearing House website|language=zh-cn}}</ref>

In December 2019, the group sold 15% shares of Gree Electric to a private equity fund for [[Renminbi|CN¥]]41.7&nbsp;[[billion (short scale)|billion]], only retaining 3.22%. The private equity fund became the largest shareholder of Gree Electric.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://industry.people.com.cn/n1/2019/1203/c415803-31487589.html|script-title=zh:珠海明骏签署股份转让协议  格力开启国企混改现代化治理新局面|department=产经频道|website=people.com.cn|language=zh-cn|author=牛广文|date=3 December 2019|accessdate=15 December 2019}}</ref>
