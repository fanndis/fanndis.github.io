The '''2015 Western & Southern Open'''  was a men's and women's [[tennis]] tournament played on [[Tennis court#Hard courts|outdoor hard courts]] August 15–23, 2015. It was part of the [[ATP World Tour Masters 1000]] of the [[2015 ATP World Tour]] and of the [[WTA Premier tournaments|WTA Premier 5 tournaments]] of the [[2015 WTA Tour]], as well as a [[2015 US Open Series]] event. The 2015 tournament was the men's 114th edition and the women's 87th edition of the [[Cincinnati Masters]]. The tournament is held annually at the [[Lindner Family Tennis Center]] in [[Mason, Ohio|Mason]] (a northern suburb of [[Cincinnati]]), [[Ohio]],  United States.

[[Roger Federer]] and [[Serena Williams]] were the defending champions and both successfully defended their titles.
