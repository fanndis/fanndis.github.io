'''Draper James''' is a clothing line launched by actress [[Reese Witherspoon]], founded in 2015.<ref name="duboff">{{cite web|last1=Duboff|first1=Josh|title=Will Reese Witherspoon's Draper James Unite Blake Lively and Gwyneth Paltrow?|url=http://www.vanityfair.com/hollywood/2015/05/reese-witherspoon-draper-james-lifestyle-site|website=Vanity Fair|publisher=Vanity Fair|accessdate=27 January 2016}}</ref> A nod to Witherspoon’s Southern upbringing, the line aims to “emphasize (Witherspoon’s) southern roots and personal style.”<ref>{{cite web|last1=Malkin|first1=Marc|last2=Rosenbaum|first2=Claudia|title=Reese Witherspoon's Lifestyle Company Gets a Name, Plans to Open a Retail Store|url=http://www.eonline.com/news/535325/reese-witherspoon-s-lifestyle-company-gets-a-name-plans-to-open-a-retail-store|website=E! Style|publisher=E! Entertainment Television|accessdate=28 January 2016}}</ref> The name is in honor of Witherspoon’s grandparents, Dorothea Draper and William James Witherspoon.<ref name="duboff"/>

==History==
Draper James launched with products available in May 2015<ref>{{cite web|last1=O'Connor|first1=Claire|title=Reese Witherspoon Scores $10 Million Investment For Lifestyle Brand Draper James|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/clareoconnor/2015/10/02/reese-witherspoon-scores-10-million-investment-for-lifestyle-brand-draper-james/#5e48e0227d7a|website=Forbes.com|publisher=Forbes|accessdate=28 January 2016}}</ref> under the supervision of former president of Burch Creative Capital, Andrea Hyde<ref>{{cite web|url=http://wwd.com/fashion-news/fashion-scoops/reeses-new-hire-7587801/|title=Reese Witherspoon Hires C. Wonder President Andrea Hyde|website=Women's Wear Daily|publisher=Penske Media Corporation|last1=Medina|first1=Marcy|accessdate=28 January 2016}}</ref> and an initial $7 million in seed investment.<ref name="paresh">{{cite web|last1=Dave|first1=Paresh|title=Reese Witherspoon has a startup, and it just got a $10-million check|url=http://www.latimes.com/business/technology/la-fi-tn-reese-witherspoon-draper-james-20151005-story.html|website=LA Times|publisher=LA Times|accessdate=28 January 2016}}</ref> In October 2015, Draper James announced a $10 million round of investment funding from early-stage investment firm, Forerunner Ventures.<ref name="paresh"/>

In October 2015, Draper James launched its first brick and mortar store in Witherspoon’s hometown of [[Nashville]], [[Tennessee]].<ref name="mitchell">{{cite web|last1=Mitchell|first1=Heidi|title=Reese Witherspoon's Draper James To Open Brick And Mortar Locations|url=http://www.fastcompany.com/3051900/behind-the-brand/reese-witherspoons-draper-james-to-open-brick-and-mortar-locations|website=Fast Company|publisher=Fast Company|accessdate=28 January 2016}}</ref> The second store opened in Dallas, Texas in September 2016.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://people.com/style/reese-witherspoons-dallas-draper-james-most-instagrammable-day/|title=Reese Witherspoon Had the Most Instagrammable Day Ever Opening Her Dallas Store!|date=2016-10-12|work=PEOPLE.com|access-date=2017-05-10|language=en-US}}</ref> The Dallas location was moved to a popup in Southlake, Texas in September 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bizjournals.com/dallas/news/2019/09/13/draper-james-southlake.html|website=www.bizjournals.com|access-date=2019-09-30}}</ref> In May 2017, the Lexington, Kentucky location opened at The Summit at Fritz Farm.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.kentucky.com/news/business/article148659424.html|title=Reese Witherspoon's retail store Draper James opens at The Summit|work=kentucky|access-date=2017-05-10|language=en}}</ref> In December 2017, a opened in Atlanta Georgia at the Shops Around Lenox in Buckhead. 

Draper James has been featured in PEOPLE,<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://people.com/home/inside-reese-witherspoons-classic-chic-dallas-draper-james-store-tour-photos/in-the-heart-of-texas|title=PHOTOS: See Inside Reese Witherspoon's Classic-Chic Dallas Draper James Store!|date=2016-10-10|work=PEOPLE.com|access-date=2017-05-10|language=en-US}}</ref> Vanity Fair,<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.vanityfair.com/style/photos/2016/11/reese-witherspoons-draper-james|title=Step Inside the Charming, Star-Studded Dinner for Feed and Reese Witherspoon's Draper James|website=Vanities|access-date=2017-05-10}}</ref> Oprah Magazine,<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.oprah.com/food/how-to-throw-a-classic-southern-party-according-to-reese-witherspoon|title=Reese Witherspoon Showed Us How to Throw a Classic Southern Party|work=Oprah.com|access-date=2017-05-10|language=en-us}}</ref> and Coveteur,<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://coveteur.com/2016/04/12/reese-witherspoon-draper-james/|title=Reese Witherspoon|website=Coveteur|access-date=2017-05-10}}</ref> among others, and the signature "totes y'all" bag was seen in the Gilmore Girls Revival.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.glamour.com/story/7-most-fantastic-fashion-moments-from-the-gilmore-girls-reboot|title=The 7 Most Fantastic Fashion Moments from the *Gilmore Girls* Reboot|last=Silverstein|first=Noah|work=Glamour|access-date=2017-05-10|language=en}}</ref>

In March 2017, Draper James was voted "Best Shop in Tennessee" by Southern Living.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.southernliving.com/souths-best/draper-james|title=Why Y'all Love Draper James So Much|work=Southern Living|access-date=2017-05-10}}</ref> That same month, Draper James launched a partnership with beauty-subscription service Birchbox.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://people.com/style/reese-witherspoon-draper-james-birchbox/|title=Reese Witherspoon Teams with Birchbox to Curate a Limited Draper James Inspired Beauty Box|date=2017-02-22|work=PEOPLE.com|access-date=2017-05-10|language=en-US}}</ref>

In April 2017, Draper James partnered with sandal brand [[Jack Rogers (Footwear Retailer)]] to launch three different styles of the classic Jack Rogers sandal.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://footwearnews.com/2017/fashion/collaborations/reese-witherspoon-brand-draper-james-jack-rogers-sandals-collaboration-341310/|title=Reese Witherspoon's Brand Draper James Is Collaborating With This Iconic Sandal Brand|last=Allen|first=Rachael|date=2017-04-25|work=Footwear News|access-date=2017-05-10|language=en-US}}</ref> Draper James and Net-a-Porter announced a partnership in May 2017.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://people.com/style/draper-james-net-a-porter-exclusive-capsule-collection-launch/|title=Reese Witherspoon and Net-a-Porter Launch Exclusive Draper James Capsule Collection (Start Shopping Y'all!)|date=2017-05-10|work=PEOPLE.com|access-date=2017-05-10|language=en-US}}</ref>

In September 2017, Taylor Rettig replaced Hyde as the company's CEO.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://wwd.com/business-news/human-resources/draper-james-ceo-andrea-hyde-exits-taylor-rettig-named-interim-chief-10983586/|title=Draper James CEO Andrea Hyde to Exit; Taylor Rettig Named Interim Chief|last1=Medina|first1=Marcy|last2=Medina|first2=Marcy|date=2017-09-12|website=WWD|language=en|access-date=2019-09-30}}</ref> 

Draper James hired Kathryn Sukey as the new head of design, whose first collection debuted<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://magid.com/mentions/reese-witherspoons-draper-james-announces-new-designer-and-expanded-assortment/|title=Reese Witherspoon's Draper James announces new designer and expanded assortment|date=2019-01-28|website=Magid|language=en-US|access-date=2019-09-30}}</ref> in Spring of 2019. Sukey also designed the first in-house plus size collection for the brand. <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20190201005057/en/Draper-James-Introduces-Inclusive-Shopping-Experience-Launch|title=Draper James Introduces a More Inclusive Shopping Experience with the Launch of Its New Extended-Size Offerings|date=2019-02-01|website=www.businesswire.com|language=en|access-date=2019-09-30}}</ref>

==Products==
The line features a wide range of clothing, accessories and home decor designed to “embrace the beauty and style and excitement that embodies what is happening in the South today,”<ref name="murray">{{cite web|last1=Murray|first1=Rheana|title=Check out Reese Witherspoon's new Southern-inspired fashion line|url=http://www.today.com/style/draper-james-reese-witherspoons-fashion-line-launches-t19716|website=Today|accessdate=28 January 2016}}</ref> with prices geared more toward high-end shoppers.
 
Products often feature slang common to the Southeast United States, such as a tote bag with the phrase “totes y’all” imprinted on the bag.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Schmidt|first1=Ingrid|title=Reese Witherspoon's Draper James collection has Southern charm|url=http://www.latimes.com/home/la-ig-draper-james-20150913-story.html|website=LA Times|publisher=LA Times|accessdate=28 January 2016}}</ref>

==Controversy==
In March 2016, Florida-based jeweler Jordann Weingartner sued Draper James for copyright and [[trademark infringement]], due to the similarity between its trademark and [[logo]] and Weingartner's design.
