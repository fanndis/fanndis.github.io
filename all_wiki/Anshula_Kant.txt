'''Anshula Kant''' (born 7 September 1960) is the chief financial officer and managing director of the [[World Bank Group]], appointed on 12 July 2019.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://indianexpress.com/article/explained/anshula-kants-journey-from-sbi-to-world-bank-5827473/|title=Explained: Anshula Kant’s journey from SBI to World Bank|date=13 July 2019|website=The Indian Express|language=en-IN|access-date=13 July 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.businesstoday.in/sectors/banks/sbi-md-anshula-kant-appointed-cfo-and-md-of-world-bank/story/364011.html|title=SBI MD Anshula Kant appointed World Bank CFO and MD|website=www.businesstoday.in|access-date=13 July 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://bfsi.eletsonline.com/world-bank-appoints-anshula-kant-as-md-cfo/|title=World Bank appoints Anshula Kant as MD, CFO|last=Kumar|first=Sanjay|date=13 July 2019|website=BFSI|language=en-US|access-date=13 July 2019}}</ref> She is from [[Roorkee]], India.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/industry/banking/finance/banking/meet-sbi-cfo-anshula-kant-the-woman-who-busted-myths-and-ran-a-bank-like-a-start-up/articleshow/49475339.cms?from=mdr|title=Meet SBI CFO Anshula Kant, the woman who busted myths and ran a bank like a start up|last=Bhakta|first=Pratik|date=21 October 2015|work=The Economic Times|access-date=13 July 2019}}</ref>

== Education ==
In 1981, Kant completed her masters' in Economics from [[Delhi School of Economics]]. She earned her bachelors' in Economics from [[Lady Shri Ram College for Women|Lady Sri Ram College for Women]] in 1979 <ref name=":0" /> At the [[Indian Institute of Banking and Finance|Indian Institute of Bankers]], she is a Certified Associate.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.zeebiz.com/companies/news-who-is-anshula-kant-the-new-sbi-md-62854|title=Who is Anshula Kant, the new State Bank of India (SBI) MD?|date=11 September 2018|website=Zee Business|language=en|access-date=13 July 2019}}</ref>

== Career ==

In 1983, Kant joined the [[State Bank of India]] (SBI) as a probationary officer. She became the chief general manager of SBI ([[Maharashtra]] and [[Goa]]), deputy managing director of operations for National Banking Group, and chief executive officer of SBI ([[Singapore]]).<ref name=":1" /> In September 2018, she became the managing director of SBI for a period of two years, and a member of the bank's board.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.businesstoday.in/sectors/banks/anshula-kant-appointed-new-sbi-md/story/282141.html|title=Anshula Kant appointed new SBI MD; to serve till September 2020|website=www.businesstoday.in|access-date=13 July 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.financialexpress.com/industry/banking-finance/anshula-kant-appointed-sbi-managing-director-heres-her-journey-from-probationary-officer-to-md/1305656/|title=Anshula Kant appointed SBI Managing Director: Here’s her journey from Probationary Officer to MD|date=7 September 2018|website=The Financial Express|language=en-US|access-date=13 July 2019}}</ref> 

On 12 July 2019, she was appointed as the chief financial officer and managing director of the World Bank Group, and will be responsible for the balance sheet and financial and risk management.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.financialexpress.com/industry/banking-finance/who-is-anshula-kant-indian-banker-appointed-as-md-and-cfo-of-world-bank/1642426/|title=Who is Anshula Kant — Indian banker appointed as MD and CFO of World Bank|date=12 July 2019|website=The Financial Express|language=en-US|access-date=13 July 2019}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Kant is married to Sanjay Kant, a chartered accountant from [[Varanasi]], Uttar Pradesh. She has a son, Siddharth, and a daughter, Nupur.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hindustantimes.com/lucknow/fond-of-malai-pua-world-bank-s-new-cfo-anshula-kant-has-a-strong-varanasi-connection/story-Ahwbbx1DIeKqs9cE0I9aaI.html|title=Fond of malai pua, World Bank’s new CFO Anshula Kant has a strong Varanasi connection|date=15 July 2019|website=Hindustan Times|language=en|access-date=15 July 2019}}</ref>
