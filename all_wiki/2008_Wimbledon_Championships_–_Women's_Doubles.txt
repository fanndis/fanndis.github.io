{{main|2008 Wimbledon Championships}}
[[Cara Black]] and [[Liezel Huber]] were the defending champions but lost in the semifinals to [[Lisa Raymond]] and [[Samantha Stosur]].

[[Serena Williams|Serena]] and [[Venus Williams]] defeated Raymond and Stosur in the final, 6–2, 6–2 to win the Ladies' Doubles tennis title at the 2008 Wimbledon Championships.<ref name="Barrett">{{cite book|last=Barrett|first=John|title=Wimbledon: The Official History|date=2014|publisher=Vision Sports Publishing|isbn=9-781909-534230|edition=4th}}</ref> The [[Williams sisters]] won the title without dropping a set.
