The '''2000 US Open''' was held between August 28 – September 10, 2000. It was the final Grand Slam event of [[2000 in tennis|2000]].

Both [[Andre Agassi]] and [[Serena Williams]] were unsuccessful in their title defences; Agassi being upset in the second round by [[Arnaud Clément]] and Williams losing in the quarter-finals to [[Lindsay Davenport]]. [[Marat Safin]] won his first US Open title and first of two Grand Slams, defeating [[Pete Sampras]] in the final, and [[Venus Williams]] defeated Davenport to win the women's title.

==Seniors==

===Men's singles===
{{Main|2000 US Open – Men's Singles}}
{{flagicon|RUS}} '''[[Marat Safin]]''' defeated {{flagicon|USA}} [[Pete Sampras]], 6–4, 6–3, 6–3
:<small>• It was Safin's 1st career Grand Slam singles title and his 1st and only title at the US Open. It was Safin's 4th title of the year, and his 5th overall.</small>

===Women's singles===
{{Main|2000 US Open – Women's Singles}}
{{flagicon|USA}} '''[[Venus Williams]]''' defeated {{flagicon|USA}} [[Lindsay Davenport]], 6–4, 7–5
:<small>• It was Williams' 2nd career Grand Slam singles title and her 1st title at the US Open. It was Williams' 5th title of the year, and her 14th overall.</small>

===Men's doubles===
{{Main|2000 US Open – Men's Doubles}}
{{flagicon|AUS}} '''[[Lleyton Hewitt]]''' / {{flagicon|BLR}} '''[[Max Mirnyi]]'''<ref>First-ever unseeded doubles team to take the title.</ref> defeated {{flagicon|RSA}} [[Ellis Ferreira]] / {{flagicon|USA}} [[Rick Leach]], 6–4, 5–7, 7–6
:<small>• It was Hewitt's 1st and only career Grand Slam doubles title.</small>
:<small>• It was Mirnyi's 1st career Grand Slam doubles title.</small>

===Women's doubles===
{{Main|2000 US Open – Women's Doubles}}
{{flagicon|FRA}} '''[[Julie Halard-Decugis]]''' / {{flagicon|JPN}} '''[[Ai Sugiyama]]''' defeated {{flagicon|ZIM}} [[Cara Black]] / {{flagicon|RUS}} [[Elena Likhovtseva]], 6–0, 1–6, 6–1
:<small>• It was Halard-Decugis' 1st and only career Grand Slam doubles title.</small>
:<small>• It was Sugiyama's 1st career Grand Slam doubles title and her 1st and only title at the US Open.</small>

===Mixed doubles===
{{Main|2000 US Open – Mixed Doubles}}
{{flagicon|ESP}} '''[[Arantxa Sánchez Vicario]]''' / {{flagicon|USA}} '''[[Jared Palmer]]''' defeated {{flagicon|RUS}} [[Anna Kournikova]] / {{flagicon|BLR}} [[Max Mirnyi]], 6–4, 6–3
:<small>• It was Sánchez Vicario's 4th and last career Grand Slam mixed doubles title and her 1st and only title at the US Open.</small>
:<small>• It was Palmer's 2nd and last career Grand Slam mixed doubles title and his 1st and only title at the US Open.</small>
