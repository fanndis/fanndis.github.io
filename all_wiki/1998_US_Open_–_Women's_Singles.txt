[[Lindsay Davenport]] defeated defending champion [[Martina Hingis]], 6–3, 7–5, to win the Women's Singles tennis title at the [[1998 US Open (tennis)|1998 US Open]]. This was a rematch of the previous year's semifinal. Davenport did not drop a set throughout the entire tournament.

The tournament was notable for the final US Open appearance of five-time champion [[Steffi Graf]] in the main draw, who lost to [[Patty Schnyder]] in the fourth round. It was also the first US Open appearance of six-time champion [[Serena Williams]] (who would go on to win her first US Open title (and first Grand Slam title) the following year). She was defeated by [[Irina Spîrlea]] in the third round. As of 2020, it remains the only time Williams has failed to reach at least the fourth round of the US Open.
