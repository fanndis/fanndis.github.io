'''Ahn Hye-jin''' (born July 23, 1995),<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tenasia.hankyung.com/archives/283703|title=My Name, 마마무 (1)|publisher=Ten Asia|language=ko|date=July 15, 2014}}</ref> better known by her stage name '''Hwasa''', is a South Korean singer, rapper, songwriter, composer and television personality signed under [[RBW (company)|RBW]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Searching Works {{!}} KOMCA|url=https://www.komca.or.kr/foreign2/eng/S01.jsp|access-date=2020-09-19|website=www.komca.or.kr}}</ref> She debuted as a member of the girl group [[Mamamoo]] in June 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wowtv.co.kr/newscenter/news/view.asp?bcode=T30001000&artid=A201406270517|title=마마무 그룹명 무슨뜻? 아이유 존경하는 '4인조 걸그룹'|publisher=Wow TV|language=ko}}</ref> She made her solo debut with the digital single "[[Twit (song)|Twit]]" on February 13, 2019. She released her first EP, ''[[María (EP)|María]]'', on June 29, 2020.

==Early life==
Ahn Hye-jin was born on July 23, 1995, in [[Jeonju]], [[North Jeolla Province|Jeollabuk-do]], South Korea,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://people.search.naver.com/search.naver?where=nexearch&query=%ED%99%94%EC%82%AC&sm=ppl_cts&ie=utf8&key=PeopleService&os=1727126|title=화사 프로필|publisher=Naver|language=ko}}</ref> where she lived with her parents and two elder sisters. She graduated from Wonkwang Information Arts High School.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://khmusic.co.kr/about/notice.php?page=1&mode=view&PGn=16&iKeyNum=1&strSkin=notice&pgCode=&SVALUE=YToyOntzOjg6ImtleWZpZWxkIjtzOjc6InN1YmplY3QiO3M6Mzoia2V5IjtzOjA6IiI7fQ==|title=경희실용음악학원 합격생 명단은 신뢰할 수 있습니다.|publisher=KH Music|language=ko}}</ref>

Even before her K-pop debut, a number of South Korean artists – including [[Solbi]] and the band [[Standing Egg]] – recognised her talent and featured her on their tracks when she was 16.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zlEdKi8MYBs|title=Standing egg - 넌 이별 난 아직|last=|first=|date=|website=|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=}}</ref>

== Career ==

Hwasa debuted as a member of the girl group [[Mamamoo]] on June 19, 2014, being one of the leading voices with the release of the lead single "Mr. Ambiguous" from their first extended play (EP) ''Hello''. She wrote and composed her first solo song, "My Heart/I Do Me," from ''Hello''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://vibe.naver.com/track/4284580|title=NAVER VIBE(바이브)|last=|first=|date=|website=vibe.naver.com|language=ko|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=March 21, 2020}}</ref>

In April 2018 she joined the cast of [[Korean Broadcasting System|KBS]] reality television show ''[[Hyena on the Keyboard]]''; the appearance spawned a collaboration with rapper [[Loco (rapper)|Loco]] titled "Don't". Hwasa took part in writing and composing the song and it successfully topped the South Korean's [[Gaon Digital Chart]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.melon.com/song/detail.htm?songId=31035061&ref=etc&snsGate=Y|title=주지마|website=www.melon.com|language=ko|access-date=March 21, 2020}}</ref> The song was later certified Platinum.<ref name="Don't&quot;download&quot;" />

In February 2019, Hwasa made her debut as a solo artist with the digital single, "Twit" which she participated in writing and composing it.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.melon.com/song/detail.htm?songId=31608765|title=멍청이(twit)|website=www.melon.com|language=ko|access-date=March 21, 2020}}</ref> The song successfully topped the Gaon Digital Chart and scored a "Triple Crown", topping the Gaon Digital, Gaon Download and Gaon Streaming charts in Korea.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://mosen.mt.co.kr/article/G1111090329|title=마마무 화사, 솔로 데뷔곡 '멍청이'로 가온차트 3관왕|last=OSEN|date=February 28, 2019|website=mosen.mt.co.kr|language=ko|access-date=March 21, 2020}}</ref> "Twit" also peaked at #3 on Billboard World Digital Song Sales Chart on the week of March 2, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|title=World Digital Song Sales Chart|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/world-digital-song-sales/2019-03-02|access-date=2020-09-06|website=Billboard}}</ref> Hwasa also composed and wrote the song "In The Fall" and was released in October 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://m2.melon.com/song/detail.htm?songId=32090955|title=가을속에서|website=m2.melon.com|language=ko|access-date=March 21, 2020}}</ref>

In March 2020, Hwasa teamed up with an English singer [[Dua Lipa]] for a new remix version of "Physical" which includes Korean lyrics.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/international/9335384/dua-lipa-mamamoo-hwa-sa-physical-kpop-remix?utm_medium=social&utm_source=twitter|title=Dua Lipa & Mamamoo's Hwasa's 'Physical' Remix: Listen|date=March 18, 2020|website=Billboard|access-date=March 21, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/jeffbenjamin/2020/03/18/dua-lipa-physical-remix-hwasa-mamamoo-kpop/|title=Dua Lipa Taps Into The K-Pop World Again With 'Physical' Remix Featuring Hwa Sa Of MAMAMOO|last=Benjamin|first=Jeff|website=Forbes|language=en|access-date=March 21, 2020}}</ref> In April, it was reported that Hwasa has joined the original soundtrack lineup of the Korean drama ''[[The King: Eternal Monarch]]'' and would be releasing her first solo OST titled "Orbit".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=018&aid=0004622623|title=자이언티·화사가 부른 '더 킹' OST 1·2번째 OST 정식 발매|trans-title=Official release of ‘The King’ OST 1st and 2nd OST by Giants and Hwasa|last=Kim|first=Eun-gu|date=April 18, 2020|work=Edaily|language=ko|access-date=April 21, 2020|via=[[Naver]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=469&aid=0000488602|title=자이언티·화사, '더 킹' OST 원투펀치부터 화려한 라인업|last=Lee|first=Ho-yeon|date=April 18, 2020|work=[[Hankook Ilbo]]|language=ko|access-date=April 21, 2020|via=[[Naver]]}}</ref> The song debuted at #16 on Billboard World Digital Song Sales Chart on the week of May 2, 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|title=World Digital Song Sales Chart|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/world-digital-song-sales/2020-05-02|access-date=2020-09-06|website=Billboard}}</ref>

On June 29, Hwasa released her EP ''[[María (EP)|Maria]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|title=마마무 화사, 신곡 'Maria' 음원차트 1위 등극…솔로 2연속 흥행|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/215/0000882303|access-date=July 1, 2020|website=n.news.naver.com|language=ko}}</ref> The lead single of the same name was composed by Hwasa and producer Park Woo-sang and seek to show her growth as a singer-songwriter. Hwasa also wrote the lyrics for "LMM" and composed "Why."<ref>{{Cite web|title=마마무 화사, 첫 미니앨범 'Maria' 트랙리스트 공개|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/215/0000880265|access-date=July 1, 2020|website=n.news.naver.com|language=ko}}</ref> "[[María (EP)|''Maria'']]" debuted at number six on the ''Billboard'' World Digital Song Sales chart, while the EP debuted at number seven on the ''Billboard'' World Albums and 81 on ''Billboard'' Top Current Albums Sales charts for the week of July 11, 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|title=World Digital Song Sales Chart|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/world-digital-song-sales/2020-07-11|access-date=2020-09-06|website=Billboard}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=World Albums Chart|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/world-albums/2020-07-11|access-date=2020-09-06|website=Billboard}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Top Current Album Sales Chart|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/current-albums/2020-07-11|access-date=2020-09-06|website=Billboard}}</ref> On the week of  September 26, 2020, "[[María (EP)|''Maria'']]" peaked at number 2 on ''Billboard'' KPOP Hot 100 and on the week of October 3, 2020, "[[María (EP)|''Maria'']]" peaked at number 79 on ''Billboard'' Japan Hot 100.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Billboard K-Pop 100 Chart|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/billboard-korea-100/2020-09-26|access-date=2020-10-01|website=Billboard}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Japan Hot 100 Chart|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/japan-hot-100|access-date=2020-10-01|website=Billboard}}</ref>

On JTBC's entertainment show "[[Hidden Singer (South Korean TV series)|''Hidden Singer'']] Season 6", Hwasa was one of the contestants for an episode which aired on September 11, 2020. In the final round, Hwasa won by an overwhelming 79 votes which ended the four consecutive losing streaks from previous contestants and recorded her second win as an original singer of the season.<ref>{{Cite web|title="역시 '퀸' 화사" '히든싱어6' 화사, 압도적 79표로 우승…전현무 "JTBC 적자, 이겨달라" [종합]|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/ranking/article/076/0003628936|access-date=2020-10-01|website=n.news.naver.com|language=ko}}</ref>

In August, 2020, [[Yoo Jae-suk|Yoo Jae Suk]] becomes the producer of the special 4-member girl group [[Refund Sisters]] (환불 원정대), who will make their debut on October 10 for an entertainment show "[[Hangout with Yoo]]".<ref>{{Cite web|last=NEWSIS|date=2020-09-27|title=환불원정대, '돈 터치 미'…10월10일 음반 내고 출격|url=https://www.newsis.com/view/?id=NISX20200927_0001180836|access-date=2020-10-01|website=newsis|language=ko}}</ref> This is a spin-off from [[Lee Hyori|Lee Hyo-ri]] naming [[Uhm Jung-hwa]], [[Jessi (musician)|Jessi]] and Hwasa as the members she want in her dream girl group during the [[SSAK3]] segment.

=== Public image ===

Hwasa's first appearance on ''I Live Alone'' has earned her the titles "Gopchang Goddess" and "Hwasa Effect".<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20180621000814|title=[Trending] K-pop star sparks 'cow intestines' craze in Korea|last=Herald|first=The Korea|date=June 21, 2018|website=www.koreaherald.com|language=en|access-date=March 21, 2020}}</ref> It started a national "Gopchang craze", causing the delicacy to sell out across the country.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.scmp.com/magazines/style/people-events/article/2157952/how-k-pop-star-hwasa-turned-goddess-after-eating-beef|title=K-pop star Hwasa turns into a 'goddess' – after eating beef intestines|date=August 2, 2018|website=South China Morning Post|language=en|access-date=February 25, 2019}}</ref> Since then, Hwasa has managed to sell out every item she uses in her screen time, including recorder music sheets, Gopchang, even her fried tofu rice ball for dieting.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://m.insight.co.kr/news/272641|title='나혼산'서 리코더 부는 모습으로 악보까지 '주문 폭주' 시켜버린 화사|date=March 9, 2020|website=인사이트|language=ko|access-date=March 18, 2020}}</ref>

Hwasa also appeared on Forbes Korea Power Celebrity in 2019 (#26).<ref>{{Cite web|date=April 23, 2019|title=포브스코리아 선정 2019 파워 셀러브리티 40|url=http://jmagazine.joins.com/forbes/view/325767|access-date=July 15, 2020|website=중앙시사매거진}}</ref>
