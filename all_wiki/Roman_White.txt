'''Roman White''' is a music video and film director who has directed most of [[Carrie Underwood]]'s videos.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.forbes.com/feeds/prnewswire/2010/05/05/prnewswire201005051633PR_NEWS_USPR_____LA99850.html|title=Eleventh Records Artist Tino Coury's 'Diary'....|date=May 5, 2010|work=[[Forbes]]|accessdate=July 28, 2010}} {{Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot}}</ref> He has also directed music videos for [[Taylor Swift]], [[Kelly Clarkson]], [[Florida Georgia Line]], [[Kelsea Ballerini]], [[Cassadee Pope]], [[Josh Groban]], [[Thomas Rhett]], [[Lauren Alaina]], [[Lady Antebellum]], [[Brett Young (singer)|Brett Young]], [[Danielle Bradbery]], [[Kellie Pickler]], [[Carly Pearce]], [[Hunter Hayes]], [[Maddie & Tae]], [[Maren Morris]], [[Justin Bieber]] and more.

White directed his motion picture debut film ''[[Summer Forever (film)|Summer Forever]]''.
