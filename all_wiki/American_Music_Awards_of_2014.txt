The '''42nd Annual [[American Music Awards]]''' ceremony was held on November 23, 2014, at the [[Microsoft Theater|Nokia Theatre L.A. Live]] in Los Angeles, California.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nokiatheatrelalive.com/events/detail/american-music-awards-2 |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2014-09-15 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141008071134/http://www.nokiatheatrelalive.com/events/detail/american-music-awards-2 |archivedate=2014-10-08 }}</ref> The awards recognized the most popular artists and albums of 2014. [[Iggy Azalea]] led the nominees with six categories.<ref name="Iggy Azalea leads">{{cite web|title=AMA's 2014: Iggy Azalea Has Most Nominations|url=
https://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/amas-2014-iggy-azalea-leads-american-music-awards/story?id=26146303|date=October 13, 2014|publisher=ABC News}}</ref>
It was broadcast live on ABC.<ref name="dickclark">{{cite web|url=http://www.dickclark.com/shows/american-music-awards/|title=American Music Awards - dick clark productions|publisher=dickclark.com|accessdate=2014-09-05}}</ref> [[Pitbull (rapper)|Pitbull]] was announced as a host on October 20, 2014. [[NSYNC]]'s [[Lance Bass]], Chelsea Briggs, [[Kandi Burruss]], [[Gavin DeGraw]], [[Frankie Grande]], [[Taryn Manning]], [[Jordin Sparks]], Morgan Stewart and Ted Stryker were all announced co-hosts of the pre-show on November 7, 2014.<ref name="billboard.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/6312059/american-music-awards-2014-pre-show-hosts-lineup |title=American Music Awards Announce 2014 Pre-Show Hosts & Performers|date=November 7, 2014 |accessdate=November 7, 2014 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url = https://www.theguardian.com/music/live/2014/nov/23/the-2014-american-music-awards-live|title=The 2014 American Music awards – as it happened|date=24 November 2014|work=BBC Sport|accessdate=25 November 2014}}</ref>

The special received 11.61 million viewers, the fourth-most that night.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2014/11/25/sunday-final-ratings-madam-secretary-the-simpsons-60-minutes-americas-funniest-home-videos-adjusted-down/331606/|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141127171317/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2014/11/25/sunday-final-ratings-madam-secretary-the-simpsons-60-minutes-americas-funniest-home-videos-adjusted-down/331606/|url-status=dead|archive-date=November 27, 2014|title=Sunday Final Ratings: 'Madam Secretary', 'The Simpsons', '60 Minutes' & 'America's Funniest Home Videos' Adjusted Down & Final Football Numbers|last=Bibel|first=Sara|publisher=[[TV by the Numbers]]|date=November 25, 2014|accessdate=November 25, 2014}}</ref>
