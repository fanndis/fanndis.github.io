'''Alfredo Nascimento''' (born May 5, 1952) is Brazil's minister of Transport.<ref>[https://www.bloomberg.com/apps/news?pid=20601086&sid=avLKej6tg6j0&refer=latin_america Bloomberg]</ref><ref>[http://www.brasilemb.org/index.php?option=com_content&task=blogcategory&id=15&Itemid=28 Brazilian embassy]</ref> He is a member of the [[Republic Party]],<ref>[http://www.ihsglobalinsight.com/SDA/SDADetail8817.htm IHS global insight]</ref> and was the party's president until he resigned in 2016 in support of the [[Impeachment process against Dilma Rousseff]].<ref>http://zh.clicrbs.com.br/rs/noticias/noticia/2016/04/deputado-alfredo-nascimento-renuncia-a-presidencia-do-pr-e-vota-pelo-impeachment-5780250.html</ref> He has occasionally been at odds with environmental minister [[Carlos Minc]] over projects in [[Amazonas (Brazilian state)|Amazonas]] and [[Rondônia]].<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/americas/8314407.stm BBC]</ref> Nascimento was mayor of Manaus in the year 1988 and between 1997 until 2004, when he resigned to take over the Ministry of Transport of Brazil
