This is a '''list of international prime ministerial trips made by Theresa May''', who served as the 54th [[Prime Minister of the United Kingdom]] from 13 July 2016 until her resignation on 24 July 2019. [[Theresa May]] has made 73 trips to 33 countries during her [[Premiership of Theresa May|premiership]].

The number of visits per country: 
* One visit to [[Argentina]], [[Bahrain]], [[Bulgaria]], [[Estonia]], [[Egypt]],  [[India]], [[Iraq]], [[Kenya]], [[North Macedonia]], [[Malta]], [[Nigeria]], [[Norway]], [[Slovakia]], [[South Africa]], [[Spain]] and [[Turkey]]
* Two visits to [[Austria]], [[Canada]], [[China]], [[Denmark]], [[Republic of Ireland|Ireland]], [[Japan]], [[Jordan]], [[Saudi Arabia]], [[Sweden]] and [[Switzerland]]
* Three visits to [[Netherlands]] and [[Poland]]
* Four visits to [[Italy]] and the [[United States]]
* Nine visits to [[Germany]]
* Ten visits to  [[France]]  
* Twenty-seven visits to [[Belgium]]
