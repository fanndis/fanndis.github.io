The '''2019 [[Dissolution Honours List]]''' was issued on 31 July 2020<ref>{{Cite web|title=Queen confers Peerages: 31 July 2020|url=https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/queen-confers-peerages-31-july-2020|access-date=2020-07-31|website=GOV.UK|language=en}}</ref> following the [[2019 United Kingdom general election]] the previous December. The [[Prime Minister of the United Kingdom|Prime Minister]], [[Boris Johnson]], was re-elected.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Results of the 2019 General Election|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/election/2019/results|access-date=2020-07-31|website=BBC News|language=en-GB}}</ref> 
This list was released concurrently with the '''[[2020 Political Honours]]''' list.
