'''Marc Albrecht''' (born 1964, [[Hanover]]) is a German conductor.

==Biography==
Albrecht is the son of the conductor [[George Alexander Albrecht]] and Corinne Albrecht, formerly a ballet dancer who became a physiotherapist.  Albrecht studied music with his father.  Albrecht has served as an assistant to [[Claudio Abbado]] with the [[Gustav Mahler Jugendorchester]], and an assistant conductor to [[Gerd Albrecht]] (no relation) at the [[Hamburg State Opera]].

From 1995 to 2001, Albrecht was music director of the [[Staatstheater Darmstadt]].  From 2001 to 2004, he was first guest conductor with the [[Deutsche Oper Berlin]].  He became artistic adviser of the [[Orchestre philharmonique de Strasbourg]] (Strasbourg Philharmonic Orchestra) in 2005, and music director in 2008.  With the Strasbourg Philharmonic, he has conducted commercial recordings for the Pentatone label, including orchestral lieder of [[Alban Berg]] and piano concertos by [[Robert Schumann]] and [[Antonín Dvořák]].<ref>{{cite news | author=Andrew Clements | url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2009/dec/04/schumann-dvorak-piano-concerto | title=Schumann: Piano Concerto; Dvorák: Piano Concerto: Helmchen/Strasbourg PO/Albrecht | work=The Guardian | date=2009-12-04 | accessdate=2009-12-06}}</ref>  He concluded his Strasbourg tenure in 2011.

Albrecht made his first conducting appearance at the [[Dutch National Opera]] in September 2008 with ''[[Die Frau ohne Schatten]]''. In March 2009, he was named chief conductor of the [[Netherlands Chamber Orchestra]], [[Netherlands Philharmonic Orchestra]], and Dutch National Opera, effective with the 2011-2012 season.  His initial contract was for four years.<ref>{{cite news | author=Merlijn Schooneboom | url=http://www.volkskrant.nl/kunst/article1170825.ece/Albrecht_nieuwe_dirigent_opera_en_NedPhO | title=Albrecht nieuwe dirigent opera en NedPhO | work=de Volksrant | date=2009-03-26 | accessdate=2009-04-04}}</ref><ref>Charlotte Smith, "Marc Albrecht named chief conductor of Netherlands Phil and De Nederlandse Opera".  ''Gramophone'' 31 March 2009.</ref>  In May 2016, the orchestra announced the extension of Albrecht's contract through the 2019-2020 season.<ref>{{cite press release | url=https://www.orkest.nl/nieuws/marc-albrecht-verlengt-contract-tot-en-met-seizoen-2019-2020 | title=Marc Albrecht verlengt contract tot en met seizoen 2019-2020 | publisher=Netherlands Philharmonic Orchestra | date=9 May 2016 | accessdate=2016-06-01 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170510175814/https://www.orkest.nl/nieuws/marc-albrecht-verlengt-contract-tot-en-met-seizoen-2019-2020 | archive-date=10 May 2017 | url-status=dead }}</ref>  He stood down from all three of these chief conductorships at the end of the 2019-2020 season.<ref>{{cite press release | url=https://www.orkest.nl/nieuws/marc-albrecht-verlaat-amsterdam-2020 | title=Marc Albrecht verlaat Amsterdam in 2020 | publisher=Netherlands Philharmonic Orchestra | date=9 May 2018 | accessdate=2018-05-10}}</ref>  He has led commercial recordings with the Netherlands Philharmonic and the Netherlands Opera for the Pentatone label.<ref>{{cite news | author=Andrew Clements | url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2013/may/22/mahler-lied-erde-alice-coote | title=Gustav Mahler: Das Lied von der Erde – review | work=The Guardian | date=2013-05-22 | accessdate=2016-06-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | author=Andrew Clements | url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2013/dec/11/schreker-der-schatzgraber-review | title=Schreker: Der Schatzgräber – review | work=The Guardian | date=2013-12-11 | accessdate=2016-06-01}}</ref>

Albrecht maintains a residence in Amsterdam.  His wife is an opera singer, and the couple have a son.<ref>{{cite news | author=Rinskje Koelewijn | url=http://www.nrcreader.nl/artikel/3312/marc-albrecht-ik-moest-mezelf-waarmaken | title=Marc Albrecht: "Ik moest mezelf waarmaken" | work=NRC Handelsblad | date=2013-12-01 | accessdate=2016-06-01}}</ref>
