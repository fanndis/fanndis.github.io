'''Michael Redstone''' (born '''Michael Rothstein'''; April 11, 1902 - April 4, 1987) was the founder of the ''Northeast Theater Corporation'' which is now [[National Amusements]], Inc.<ref>http://www.cnn.com/2013/05/16/us/sumner-redstone-fast-facts/index.html</ref>

==Biography==
Redstone was born in [[Boston]].<ref name=BostonGlobeObit>[https://archive.is/20130125035659/http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P2-8005517.html Boston Globe: "MICHAEL REDSTONE, OWNED THEATERS, LATIN QUARTER NIGHTCLUB; AT 85] April 6, 1987</ref> In 1940, at the behest of his son{{clarify|reason=The source doesn't say it, and autobiography says the opposite.|post-text=Not in the source|date=August 2020}} Sumner Redstone, he agreed to change their surname from "Rothstein" to "Redstone" ("Red stone" is a literal translation of the [[German-Jewish]]{{clarify|reason=The source doesn't say German-Jewish. Could well be, works both in German and Yiddish, but is it just a guess or source got lost?|post-text=Not in the source|date=August 2020}} name, "Rothstein").<ref>[http://www.cnn.com/2013/05/16/us/sumner-redstone-fast-facts/ CNN: "Sumner Redstone Fast Facts"] May 16, 2013</ref> He started a trucking firm as a young man and then he worked as an usher at the Scollay Square Olympia theater before starting the Boston branch of the [[Latin Quarter (nightclub)|Latin Quarter nightclub]]. Thereafter he founded the movie theater ownership company Northeast Theater Corporation<ref name=BostonGlobeObit/> which became National Amusements, Inc.

His son, [[Sumner Redstone]], grew National Amusements into one of the largest media conglomerates in the United States, owning one of the three broadcast networks in [[CBS Corporation|CBS]], the cable television and content provider [[Viacom (2005â€“present)|Viacom]] (which includes [[MTV Networks]] and [[Black Entertainment Television|BET]]), and the movie studio [[Paramount Pictures]]. In addition, they are equal partners in [[MovieTickets.com]].
