'''Poshmark''' is a social commerce marketplace where people in the United States can buy and sell new or used clothing, shoes, and accessories. The company is headquartered in [[Redwood City, California]].

== History ==
The company was launched in 2011 by Manish Chandra.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://footwearnews.com/2018/business/retail/best-online-consignment-shops-the-real-real-thred-up-480648/|title=The 6 Best Online Consignment Shops You Need to Know|first1=Nikara|last1=Johns|first2=Nikara|last2=Johns|date=January 16, 2018}}</ref>

In November 2017, Poshmark announced that it had raised $87.5 million in venture backed funding led by [[Singapore]]-based [[Temasek Holdings|Temasek]]. [[Menlo Ventures]], [[GGV Capital]], [[Mayfield Fund|Mayfield]] and others also participated. They have raised a total of $160 million to date.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://social.techcrunch.com/2017/11/14/poshmark-raises-87-5-million-for-fashion-resale-marketplace/|title=Poshmark raises $87.5 million for fashion resale marketplace}}</ref>

In a May 22, 2018, interview with Reuters, Poshmark's CEO and founder, Manish Chandra, stated since Poshmark's launch sellers on the website had earned $1 billion selling clothing and accessories. Reuters reported that "much" of these earnings were "concentrated in a group of sellers making five, six and even seven figures"<ref name="Reuters: Poshmark sellers earn $1b">{{cite web | url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-startups-poshmark/online-fashion-marketplace-poshmark-says-its-sellers-have-made-1-billion-idUSKCN1IO2C0 | title=Online fashion marketplace Poshmark says its sellers have made $1 billion | publisher=Reuters | work=Internet News | date=23 May 2018 | accessdate=10 February 2019 | author=Somerville , Heather}}</ref> though the company noted there were 4 million sellers on the service.

Previously, Reuters reported Poshmark had raised $87.5 million "at a valuation of nearly $600 million."<ref name="Reuters: Poshmark raises $87.5 million">{{cite web | url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-poshmark-funding/online-fashion-startup-poshmark-raises-87-5-million-from-temasek-idUSKBN1DE2C5 | title=Online fashion startup Poshmark raises $87.5 million from Temasek | publisher=Reuters | date=14 November 2017 | accessdate=10 February 2019 | author=Somerville, Heather}}</ref>

On August 1, 2019, the company announced that it had been subject to a [[data breach]] impacting the personal and login information of its customers.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.vice.com/en_us/article/8xw4n4/poshmark-hacked|title=Clothing Resale Marketplace Poshmark Announces Data Breach|last=Cox|first=Joseph|date=1 August 2019|work=[[Vice Media]]|access-date=1 August 2019}}</ref>
