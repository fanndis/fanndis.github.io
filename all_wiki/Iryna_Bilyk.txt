'''Iryna Mykolayivna Bilyk''' ({{lang-ua|Ірина Миколаївна Білик}}, born 1970 in [[Kyiv]]) is a Ukrainian singer, songwriter, and [[People's Artist of Ukraine]].

She wrote her first song at the age of ten. In 1995, Bilyk performed for the United States President [[Bill Clinton]]. She has produced twelve musical albums (including several in Russian and one in Polish),<ref>[http://www.irynabilyk.com/discography.php Discography from official site]</ref> many video clips, and continues to be active in the music industry.

On 27 October 2007 Iryna Bilyk married her dancing partner from the TV show "Dances with the Stars-2", a 22-year-old [[Dmytro Dykusar]]. The wedding ceremony took place in [[Rio de Janeiro]].<ref>[http://www.segodnya.ua/news/606049.html Билык и Дикусар таки поженились], ''[[Segodnya]]'', 30 October 2007</ref><ref>[http://www.korrespondent.net/main/214194 Сегодня: Ирина Билык вышла замуж], ''[[korrespondent.net]]'', 30 October 2007</ref>
