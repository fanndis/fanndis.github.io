'''eleveneleven''' was a record label founded in 2010 by Mike Hamlin, [[Ellen DeGeneres]] and her production company, [[A Very Good Production]], in association with longtime affiliate [[Warner Bros.]] DeGeneres announced it on her [[The Ellen DeGeneres Show|talk show]], saying that the label would concentrate on lesser known artists and that she had been looking for videos of performances on [[YouTube]]. DeGeneres explained her choice of name, claiming  that she often sees the number [[11:11 (numerology)|11:11]] when looking at her clocks, that she found [[Greyson Chance]] on the 11th, and that the singer's soccer jersey has the number 11.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://ellen.warnerbros.com/2010/05/ellen_explains_her_new_eleveneleven_record_label_0528.php|title=Ellen Explains Her New eleveneleven Record Label|work=EllenTV|access-date=2010-05-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100531154322/http://ellen.warnerbros.com/2010/05/ellen_explains_her_new_eleveneleven_record_label_0528.php|archive-date=2010-05-31|url-status=dead}}</ref> All of the artists on the label have been distributed via [[Interscope Geffen A&M]].

==Artists==
*The first act to be signed to the label was [[Greyson Chance]], who gained fame after his cover of [[Lady Gaga]]'s "[[Paparazzi (Lady Gaga song)|Paparazzi]]" performed at a school in [[Edmond, Oklahoma]] went [[Viral video|viral]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://new.music.yahoo.com/ellen-degeneres/news/ellen-degeneres-starts-own-record-label--62002061 |archive-url=https://archive.today/20120630165309/http://new.music.yahoo.com/ellen-degeneres/news/ellen-degeneres-starts-own-record-label--62002061 |url-status=dead |archive-date=2012-06-30 |title=Ellen Degeneres Starts Own Record Label |publisher=[[Yahoo Music]] |date=2010-05-26 |accessdate=2010-05-26 }}</ref> 
*On September 16, 2010, DeGeneres announced her label's second signed artist, [[Tom Andrews (singer)|Tom Andrews]], from the United Kingdom.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://ellen.warnerbros.com/topics/?s=Tom+Andrews|title=TomAndrews|work=EllenTV}}</ref>
* In the fall of 2010, the label signed [[Jessica Simpson]]. She released her first album under the label called [[Happy Christmas (Jessica Simpson album)|''Happy Christmas'']] in November 2010. 
*In March 2011, DeGeneres announced that she had signed [[Savannah Robinson]] to her label and had her on the show to perform a duet with [[Jennifer Hudson]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://ellen.warnerbros.com/2011/03/jennifer_hudson_sings_with_savannah_robinson_0330.php |title=Jennifer Hudson sings with Savannah Robinson |access-date=2011-03-30 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110402070049/http://ellen.warnerbros.com/2011/03/jennifer_hudson_sings_with_savannah_robinson_0330.php |archive-date=2011-04-02 |url-status=dead }}</ref>
*In October 2011, DeGeneres announced that she had signed [[Charlie Puth]] and Emily Luther to her label after seeing their cover of [[Adele]]'s "[[Someone Like You (Adele song)|Someone Like You]]". Puth & Luther performed the song on the show. On January 25, 2012, they came back to ''The Ellen DeGeneres Show'' and performed [[Lady Antebellum]]'s "[[Need You Now (Lady Antebellum song)|Need You Now]]" and an original written by Puth titled "Break Again" (co-written with Robert Gillies).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://repertoire.bmi.com/title.asp?june2013ellensignedAbbieabbytheyoutubechannelblnWriter=True&blnPublisher=True&blnArtist=True&page=1&keyid=13764231&ShowNbr=0&ShowSeqNbr=0&querytype=WorkID |archive-url=https://archive.is/20160210135646/http://repertoire.bmi.com/title.asp?june2013ellensignedAbbieabbytheyoutubechannelblnWriter=True&blnPublisher=True&blnArtist=True&page=1&keyid=13764231&ShowNbr=0&ShowSeqNbr=0&querytype=WorkID |url-status=dead |archive-date=2016-02-10 |title=Break Again (Legal Title) |publisher=[[Broadcast Music, Inc.]] |accessdate=2012-05-26 }}</ref> Puth is no longer a part of the label.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Anderson|first1=Trevor|title=Charlie Puth Talks Meghan Trainor Duet 'Marvin Gaye' & His Plea to James Taylor|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop-shop/6502317/charlie-puth-tonight-show-interview|website=Billboard.com|publisher=Billboard|accessdate=18 October 2016}}</ref>

The eleveneleven section on her official website shortlisted the four most popular acts from the web and asked for a democratic voting from her community to help her make an informed choice on the next artist to be signed to the label.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://wowelle.com/2010/09/20/ellen-degeneres-prime-times-top-earning-woman-is-on-a-roll-with-her-new-enterprise-eleveneleven-and-its-shining-stars-greyson-chance-and-tom-andrews/|title=Ellen DeGeneres is on a roll, with her new enterprise, eleveneleven and its shining stars, Greyson Chance and Tom Andrews|author=Ebenezer|date=September 20, 2010|work=wowelle.com|access-date=2011-03-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718115331/http://wowelle.com/2010/09/20/ellen-degeneres-prime-times-top-earning-woman-is-on-a-roll-with-her-new-enterprise-eleveneleven-and-its-shining-stars-greyson-chance-and-tom-andrews/|archive-date=2011-07-18|url-status=dead}}</ref>
