'''Viktoriya Konstantinova Tomova''' ({{lang-bg|Виктория Константинова Томова}}, born 25 February 1995) is a professional [[Bulgaria]]n tennis player. On 16 July 2018, she reached her highest [[WTA Rankings|WTA]] singles ranking of world No. 128 whilst her best doubles ranking was No. 254 on 11 August 2014.<ref>{{cite news
  | title = Viktoriya Tomova's Biography
  | url = http://www.itftennis.com/procircuit/players/player/profile.aspx?playerid=100134489
  | publisher = [[International Tennis Federation]]
  | accessdate= 2010-11-12
  }}</ref> She also plays for the [[Bulgaria Fed Cup team]] with a current win/loss record of 9–10.

==Professional career==
===2016===
In July 2016, Tomova reached the biggest final of her career so far at [[2016 Europe Tennis Center Ladies Open|Europe Tennis Center Ladies Open]], losing to fellow Bulgarian [[Elitsa Kostova]]. The following week she failed to qualify for [[2016 BRD Bucharest Open|Bucharest Open]], losing in the final qualifying round to [[Nadia Podoroska]]. Tomova made her debut at [[2016 US Open – Women's Singles Qualifying|US Open]] qualifying draw reaching qualifying competition. Tomova finished the year as No. 152 in the world.

===2017===
Tomova started the season with a loss to Elitsa Kostova at the qualifying draw of the [[2017 Brisbane International|Brisbane International]] and then she lost in the first round of qualifying draw in [[2017 Apia International Sydney|Sydney]]. At her debut at [[2017 Australian Open – Women's Singles Qualifying|Australian open]], she lost in the qualifying competition to [[Eri Hozumi]]. At her [[2017 Wimbledon Championships – Women's Singles Qualifying|Wimbledon]] debut she fell in the first round of the qualifying competition. In July, she scored her biggest win so far defeating [[Julia Görges]] in the first round of [[2017 Swedish Open - Women's Singles|Swedish Open]]. At the [[2017 US Open – Women's Singles Qualifying|US Open]] she lost at the qualifying competition. In October, she managed to qualify for the [[2017 Upper Austria Ladies Linz|Linz Open]], where she lost to last year's finalist [[Viktorija Golubic]] in three sets.

===2018===
Tomova made her debut in the main draw of a [[Grand Slam (tennis)|Grand Slam tournament]], coming through qualifying rounds at the [[2018 Australian Open – Women's Singles|Australian Open]] as a lucky loser, but then lost in straight sets to [[Nicole Gibbs]]. At the [[2018 French Open - Women's Singles Qualifying|French Open]], she lost in the second round of qualifying competition. She made her main-draw debut at [[2018 Wimbledon Championships – Women's Singles|Wimbledon]], going through qualifying and defeating [[Tereza Smitková]]. She lost to [[Serena Williams]] in the second round, in straight sets.

===2020===
Tomova tested positive for [[coronavirus disease 2019|COVID-19]] while staying in [[Internazionali Femminili di Palermo|Palermo]], Italy, for a prospective tournament.<ref>https://www.straitstimes.com/sport/tennis/tennis-player-tests-positive-for-coronavirus-at-wta-palermo-open</ref>
