The '''MTV Movie Award for Best Fight''' is an award presented to actors and characters for quality [[fight scene]]s in films at the [[MTV Movie Award]]s, a ceremony established in 1992.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mtv.com/ontv/movieawards/1992/ |title=1992 MTV Movie Awards |publisher=[[MTV]] ([[MTV Networks]]) |accessdate=July 25, 2011}}</ref> Honors in several categories are awarded by [[MTV]] at the annual ceremonies, and are chosen by public vote.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.mtv.com/news/articles/1660408/mtv-movie-awards-2011.jhtml |title=2011 MTV Movie Awards Voting Begins Today! |first=Eric |last=Ditzian |work=[[MTV News]] |publisher=MTV Networks |date=March 22, 2011 |accessdate=July 25, 2011}}</ref> The MTV Movie Award for Best Fight was first presented in [[1996 MTV Movie Awards|1996]] to [[Adam Sandler]] and [[Bob Barker]] for their fight in ''[[Happy Gilmore]]''.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mtv.com/ontv/movieawards/winners_by_category.jhtml |title=Winners By Category |publisher=MTV (MTV Networks) |accessdate=July 25, 2011}} Note: Click on the 'Best Fight' section.</ref> [[Uma Thurman]] won the award in [[2004 MTV Movie Awards|2004]] and [[2005 MTV Movie Awards|2005]] for her fights against [[Chiaki Kuriyama]] and [[Daryl Hannah]] in ''[[Kill Bill: Volume 1]]'' and ''[[Kill Bill: Volume 2|Volume 2]]'', respectively. In [[2008 MTV Movie Awards|2008]] and [[2009 MTV Movie Awards|2009]], [[Cam Gigandet]] was presented with the honor for his fights in ''[[Never Back Down]]'' and ''[[Twilight (2008 film)|Twilight]]''. [[Robert Pattinson]] has also won the award twice, for his appearances in ''[[The Twilight Saga (film series)|The Twilight Saga]]'' films: ''Twilight'' in 2009, and ''[[The Twilight Saga: Eclipse]]'' in [[2011 MTV Movie Awards|2011]]. [[Jackie Chan]] has won the Best Fight honor once from four nominations. [[Jet Li]] and [[Chris Tucker]] have each received three nominations, and [[Brad Pitt]] and [[Hugh Jackman]] have each been nominated twice.
