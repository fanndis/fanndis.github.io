'''Camilla''' "'''Milly'''" '''Rosso''' and '''Rebecca''' "'''Becky'''" '''Rosso''' (born 6 July 1994) are British identical twin former actresses and singers from London. They are best known for their roles as Janice (Milly) and Jessica (Becky) Ellis on ''[[The Suite Life of Zack & Cody]]'', and for their roles as Annabelle "Annie" (Milly) and Isabelle "Izzy" (Becky) Woods in ''[[Legally Blondes]]'' (2009).

==Early life==
Milly is a minute younger than Becky. They are half British by her mom's side and half Latina by her dad's side.
They have lived in Latin American countries such as [[Colombia]], [[Argentina]], and [[Venezuela]]. They have three other sisters, Bianca, Georgina, and Lola.{{citation needed|date=January 2016}}

==Careers==
Milly and Becky were discovered when they were picked out of the audience by one of the executive producers of ''[[The Suite Life of Zack & Cody]]'' at a live taping of the show.<ref name="kw">[http://www.kidzworld.com/article/17473-milly-and-becky-rosso-intervi "Milly and Becky Rosso Interview"]. Retrieved 14 May 2009.</ref><ref>[https://archive.is/20120910205925/http://www.portraitmagazine.net/interviews/rossotwins.html "Milly and Becky Rosso Interview"]. Portrait Magazine. March 2009. Retrieved 13 April 2012.</ref> They appeared in a total seven episodes of the show and then appeared in one episode of the spin-off ''[[The Suite Life on Deck]]'' in March 2010.

The twins starred as the main roles in the film ''[[Legally Blondes]]'', which was released direct-to-DVD on 28 April 2009 as a sequel to ''[[Legally Blonde]]''.<ref>Magazine, Seventeen. (17 July 2009) Since then Legally Blonde 2 starring Reese Whitherspoon has been released. Legally Blondes was a spinoff to the Legally Blonde movies. [http://www.seventeen.com/cosmogirl/legally-blondes "Legally Blonde Goes To High School with Legally Blondes!"]. Seventeen.com. Retrieved 13 April 2012.</ref> They sang "Lucky Girl" for the film.<ref name="kw"/> ''Legally Blondes'' was produced by [[Reese Witherspoon]], the star of the original ''Legally Blonde''.

Milly and Becky were part of an all-girl group called [[The Rosso Sisters]], along with their sisters, Georgina and Lola. Their eldest sister, Bianca Rosso did not join the group. They were signed by [[Virgin Records]] on the spot by Steve Barnett<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://oceanup.com/2013/11/24/introducing-the-flawless-rosso-sisters/ |title=Archived copy |access-date=25 November 2013 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304112131/http://oceanup.com/2013/11/24/introducing-the-flawless-rosso-sisters/ |archive-date=4 March 2016 |url-status=dead }}</ref> and were managed by Paul Kevin Jonas, Sr., the father of the [[Jonas Brothers]]. In 2015, they announced on their Facebook and Instagram accounts the disbandment of their group due to the death of their mother.<ref>https://www.instagram.com/p/zAgC0uOWwj/</ref> They haven't performed since.
