{{main|2000 Australian Open}}
[[Lindsay Davenport]] defeated three-time defending champion [[Martina Hingis]] 6–1, 7–5 in the final to win the Women's Singles tennis title at the 2000 Australian Open. Davenport did not lose a set in the entire tournament. The loss ended Hingis's run of 27 consecutive matches won at this tournament, dating back to [[1997 Australian Open – Women's Singles|1997]].
