The '''China Open''' is an annual men's and women's professional [[tennis]] [[tournament]] held in [[Beijing]], [[China]]. The women's event is the third highest tier [[WTA Premier tournaments#Premier Mandatory|Premier Mandatory]] series on the WTA tour, while the men's is a fourth tier [[ATP Tour 500]] series event. The tournament was first held in 2004 as a back-to-back event for 4 years. In 2008, it became a combined event for the first time.

In 2006, the China Open became the first tournament outside the United States to use the [[Hawk-Eye]] system in match play.<ref>[http://www.chineseathlete.net/press/articles/article.php?article_id=20 Tennis stars support Hawk-Eye decision]  {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070929070404/http://www.chineseathlete.net/press/articles/article.php?article_id=20 |date=September 29, 2007 }}</ref>

[[Novak Djokovic]] is the only player to have won the title six times and holds the distinction of having never lost a match at the China Open. Djokovic holds the record for consecutive wins with four titles.  In doubles, the [[Bryan Brothers]] are the only doubles pair to have won consecutive titles.

[[Svetlana Kuznetsova]], [[Serena Williams]], [[Agnieszka Radwańska]] and [[Caroline Wozniacki]] hold the record for most titles won in the women's tournament, with two titles each.
