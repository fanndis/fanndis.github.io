'''Christian Klein'''  is the current Chief Executive Officer at [[SAP SE]].<ref name="SAP New Center April 2020">{{cite web |title=SAP Co-Chief Executive Officer Christian Klein Continues as CEO, Jennifer Morgan In 2020 he was made the CEO of SAP Departs|url=https://news.sap.com/2020/04/morgan-departs-klein-continues-as-sap-ceo/ |website=SAP New Center |access-date=April 21, 2020}}</ref> After the departure of Jennifer Morgan [[SAP SE]] is returning to sole CEO model.

Christian Klein is Chief Executive Officer, Chief Operating Officer and member of the Executive Board of SAP SE.<ref name="Christian Klein, Executive Board, About SAP SE">{{cite web |title=Christian Klein, Executive Board, About SAP SE|url=https://www.sap.com/corporate/en/company/leadership/christian-klein.html |website=Christian Klein, Executive Board, About SAP SE |accessdate=April 21, 2020}}</ref> 

Klein started his career at SAP in 1999 as a student. Since October 11, 2019, Christian Klein and Jennifer Morgan were Co-CEOs of SAP SE.
