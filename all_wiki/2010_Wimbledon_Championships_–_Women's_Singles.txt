{{main|2010 Wimbledon Championships}}
[[Serena Williams]] successfully defended her title, defeating [[Vera Zvonareva]] in the final, 6–3, 6–2 to win the Ladies' Singles tennis title at the 2010 Wimbledon Championships.<ref name="Barrett">{{cite book|last=Barrett|first=John|title=Wimbledon: The Official History|date=2014|publisher=Vision Sports Publishing|isbn=9-781909-534230|edition=4th}}</ref> She did not [[List of Grand Slam related tennis records#Won a title without losing a set|lose a set throughout the entire tournament]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/sport/2010/jul/03/wimbledon-serena-williams-queen|title=Wimbledon salutes Queen Serena the fourth|date=2010-07-03|work=The Guardian|accessdate=2010-11-18}}</ref> Zvonareva reached her first ever Grand Slam final in singles competition, and became the second lowest-ranked woman to play in a Wimbledon singles final.

The [[2010 French Open]] women's singles champion [[Francesca Schiavone]] and the women's singles runner-up [[Samantha Stosur]] both lost in the first round, marking the first time that the two French Open finalists had lost in the opening round of Wimbledon.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/tennis/8753867.stm|title=Wimbledon 2010: Sixth seed Sam Stosur makes shock exit|date=22 June 2010|work=BBC Sport|accessdate=28 June 2017}}</ref>

This year's women's singles event also saw two surprise semifinalists in [[Petra Kvitová]] (ranked 62nd) and [[Tsvetana Pironkova]] (ranked 82nd). This was the first time two unseeded players had made it to that stage since [[1999 Wimbledon Championships – Women's Singles|1999]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tennis.com/pro-game/2010/06/pironkova-upsets-venus-in-wimbledon-quarterfinals/23165/|title=Pironkova upsets Venus in Wimbledon quarterfinals|date=29 June 2010|accessdate=28 June 2017}}</ref> Furthermore, [[Kaia Kanepi]] (ranked 80th) became the first qualifier to reach the quarterfinals of Wimbledon since [[Séverine Beltrame]] did so in [[2006 Wimbledon Championships – Women's Singles|2006]].{{fact|date=August 2020}}
