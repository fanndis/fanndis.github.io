'''Law360''' is a subscription-based, legal news service operated by the Portfolio Media company, a subsidiary of [[LexisNexis]].<ref name="Online">Sabroski, Suzxanne (May 1, 2012) [[LexisNexis]] goes 360, ''Onliline''</ref><ref name="Official">{{cite web|url=http://www.law360.com/about#single|title=About Law360|work=Law360 Corporate Website|accessdate=6 Nov 2013}}</ref>

In 2003 Law360 parent Portfolio Media began publishing a daily online newsletter on [[intellectual property law]].<ref name="E24">{{cite web|url=http://www.e24.se/entreprenor/svensk-modell-slar-i-usa_2920886.e24|title=Svensk modell slår i USA|last=Kederstedt|first=Daniel|date=12 July 2011|work=E24 Entreprenör|language=Swedish|accessdate=15 July 2011}}</ref><ref name="Talking Biz">{{cite web|url=http://www.talkingbiznews.com/biz-news-help-wanted/law360-seeking-to-hire-reporters-in-new-york/|title=Law360 seeking to hire reports in New York|date=7 Oct 2013}}</ref> In 2012 the company was acquired by LexisNexis.<ref name="LexisNexis">{{cite web|url=https://www.lexisnexis.com/en-us/about-us/media/press-release.page?id=1331917568327157|title=LexisNexis Acquires Law360|date=20 March 2012|work=LexisNexis}}</ref> The publication now has news bureaus across the U.S.<ref name="Dewey B. Strategic">{{cite web|url=http://deweybstrategic.blogspot.com/2013/08/the-improbable-rise-of-law360-what.html|title=The Improbable Rise of Law360: What Lawyers Could Learn And Its Not Just the News!|last=Law360|date=14 Aug 2013|work=Dewey B. Strategic|accessdate=6 Nov 2013}}</ref>{{unreliable source?|date=June 2014}}
