'''''Milk Famous''''' is the third [[album]] by American [[indie rock]] band the [[White Rabbits (band)|White Rabbits]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2012/03/11/arts/television/new-work-from-white-rabbits-and-magic-youth.html|title=From Parables to Pointillism|first=Jon|last=Pareles|date=March 9, 2012|via=NYTimes.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.npr.org/2012/02/26/147346228/first-listen-white-rabbits-milk-famous|title=First Listen: White Rabbits, 'Milk Famous'|website=NPR.org}}</ref>  It was released on March 6, 2012, on [[TBD Records]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.popmatters.com/156920-white-rabbits-milk-famous-2495865211.html|title=White Rabbits: Milk Famous|date=April 22, 2012|website=PopMatters}}</ref>
