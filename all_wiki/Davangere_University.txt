'''Davangere University''' is a [[Public university|public]] [[State university (India)|state university]] located in [[Davangere]], [[Karnataka]], [[India]]. The university was established in the year 2008 by the [[Government of Karnataka]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.ugc.ac.in/oldpdf/alluniversity.pdf |title=UGC List |access-date=21 September 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190617093548/https://www.ugc.ac.in/oldpdf/alluniversity.pdf |archive-date=17 June 2019 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

==Location==
The university is located at Shivagangothri Campus Tolahunase, 9&nbsp;km from [[Davangere]] city. It houses the post-graduate departments of Commerce, Biochemistry, Microbiology, Economics, Food Technology, Accounting & Finance, M.Ed., and MSW. The ''Institute of Management studies'' is also housed at the P.G. Centre. The campus of 73 acres has attractive buildings as well as hostel and boarding facilities.<ref name=autogenerated1 />
Davangere University is one of the youngest affiliating type of universities in Karnataka. It has [[Davangere]] and [[Chitradurga]] districts under its jurisdiction and is headquartered at [[Davangere]]. The city is located on National Highway-4 about 265&nbsp;km from Bangalore, the state capital of Karnataka. Davangere became a separate district from 15 August 1997; before then, it was part of Chitradurga District.

==Overview==
Davangere University was a post-graduate centre of the University of Mysore from 1979 to 1987 and functioned as a post-graduate centre of Kuvempu University from 1987 to 2009. Davangere University was established on 18 August 2009 out of Kuvempu University with a vision to meet the education aspirations of the people of the region. The main campus "Shivagangotri" is about 12&nbsp;km from Davangere and 60&nbsp;km from Chitadurga. Davangere University has revealed a distinctive academic profile, integrating excellence in higher education to fulfill the needs of the contemporary modern society and social ethnos of the region. It has two constituent colleges, one autonomous college and 110 affiliated colleges offering graduate and post-graduate course in disciplines catering to the education needs of nearly 400000 students.

The university has five faculties: Arts, Science and Technology, Commerce and Management, Engineering and Education. It has seven post-graduate departments of studies and research on the main campus offering eight post-graduate and one P.G. diploma courses. The constituent university college of (Fine) Visual Arts (established in the year 1964) has two departments and offers Bachelor of Visual Arts & Master of Visual Arts courses.<ref name=autogenerated1>[http://www.davangereuniversity.org/Davangere%20University.html Davangere University<!-- Bot generated title -->] {{webarchive|url=https://archive.is/20120729014702/http://www.davangereuniversity.org/Davangere%20University.html |date=2012-07-29 }}</ref>
