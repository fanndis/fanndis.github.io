'''Miu Miu''' is an [[Italian fashion|Italian]] [[high fashion]] women's clothing and accessory brand and a fully owned [[subsidiary]] of [[Prada]]. It is headed by [[Miuccia Prada]] and headquartered in [[Paris]], [[France]].<ref name=history>{{cite web|url=http://www.pradagroup.com/en.html|title=PRADA GROUP|work=A History Of Innovation|accessdate=2011-05-06}}</ref>

==History==
[[File:11 East 57th Street.jpg|thumb|Miu Miu store in [[New York City]].]]
Miu Miu was established in 1992 by [[Miuccia Prada]].<ref name=history/> The name was conceived from Miuccia Prada's family nickname.<ref name="nyt">{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1998/09/15/style/once-a-private-club-prada-opens-its-doors.html|title=Once a Private Club, Prada Opens Its Doors|author=Constance C. R. White|date=1998-09-15|work=The New York Times|accessdate=2009-07-28}}</ref>

In 2011, Miu Miu launched the ''[[Women's Tales]]'' series. The campaign consisted of short films that were produced in conjunction with high-profile female directors. The outcome was a list of short, silent films that featured Miu Miu's collections. The first four short films were directed by [[Zoe Cassavetes]], [[Lucrecia Martel]], [[Giada Colagrande]] and [[Massy Tadjedin]] and were screened at the [[69th Venice International Film Festival]].<ref>{{cite news| url=http://fashion.telegraph.co.uk/article/TMG9511446/Venice-Film-Festival-Miu-Miu-showcases-Womens-Tales.html | location=London | work=The Daily Telegraph | first=Bibby | last=Sowray | title=<strong> Fashion shoots</strong> | date=2012-08-31}}</ref> A fifth film that debut in 2013, was written and directed by [[Ava DuVernay]] and also starred.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.miumiu.com/en/women_tales/5/film|title=Miu Miu|publisher=}}</ref>

On March 14, 2013, a counter report of [[Prada]] in violating women's rights was reported to [[Office of the United Nations High Commissioner for Human Rights]] [[Committee on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www2.ohchr.org/English/bodies/cescr/docs/ngos/WorkingWomensNetwork_Japan_CESCR50.pdf |title=Working Womens Network letter |date=14 March 2013 |website=www2.ohchr.org |format=PDF |access-date=2019-06-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www2.ohchr.org/english/bodies/cescr/cescrs50.htm |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2013-09-16 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130917112505/http://www2.ohchr.org/english/bodies/cescr/cescrs50.htm |archive-date=2013-09-17 |df= }}</ref> On May 17, 2013, a statement against [[sexual harassment]] and gender discrimination at workplace was released by the UN Committee.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mofa.go.jp/mofaj/gaiko/kiyaku/pdfs/kenkai_130517_en.pdf |title=GE.13 Committee on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights |date=17 May 2013 |website=www.mofa.go.jp |format=PDF |access-date=2019-06-01}}</ref> In April 2013, an American [[Change.org]] petition against [[Miuccia Prada]], collected over 200K signatures around the world. This petition was against [[Miuccia Prada]] for countersuing a fired employee for making false statements while voicing her opinion against [[sexual harassment]]. This petition damaged the brand image.<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/04/24/prada-employee-lawsuit-discrimination-un_n_3147281.html | work=Huffington Post | first=Lauren | last=Leibowitz | title=Prada Employee Fought Back, And So Did Prada | date=2013-04-24}}</ref> On May 28, 2013, The UN has backed former Prada employee Bovrisse over the [[sexual harassment]] and [[discrimination]]  case of [[Prada]] Japan.  Vogue launched a news article, "Prada Vs The UN" quoting Bovrisse claiming, "Anyone who buys from the Prada and Miu Miu brands are supporting a culture of [[discrimination]] and [[power harassment]]."<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vogue.co.uk/news/2013/05/28/prada-rina-bovrisse-lawsuit---un-statement-released|title=Prada Rina Bovrisse Lawsuit - UN Statement Released (Vogue.co.uk)|work=Vogue UK}}</ref>

Set to launch its first fragrance in 2015, Miu Miu's new venture is backed by the signed agreement between [[Coty Inc.]] and Prada. This is the first time that Miu Miu has expanded outside the fashion and accessories markets.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vogue.co.uk/beauty/2013/10/30/miu-miu-to-launch-first-fragrance-with-coty-inc|title=The Scent Of Miu Miu|author=Lisa Niven|date=2013-10-30|work=British Vogue}}</ref>

==Boutiques==
[[File:HK TST night One Peking Road shop Miu Miu Nov-2013.JPG|thumb|Miu Miu store in [[Hong Kong]].]]

There are Miu Miu boutiques located on a global scale.

Miu Miu opened its first independent store in [[China]] in The Mixc in [[Shenzhen]] in 2009.<ref name=GlassMagazine>{{cite web|url=http://www.glassmagazine.co.uk/forum/feature.asp?tid=182|title=miu miu – welcome to Shenzhen|work=Glass Magazine|year=2009|accessdate=2009-07-27|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090830044723/http://www.glassmagazine.co.uk/forum/feature.asp?tid=182|archive-date=2009-08-30|df=}}</ref> A new North American store was launched in [[Houston, Texas]], in [[The Galleria]] during the summer of 2011 and in [[Short Hills, New Jersey]], in the fall of 2011. Miu Miu also opened their first [[Australia]]n boutique at [[Chadstone Shopping Centre]] in [[Melbourne]].<ref name=chadstone>{{cite web|url=http://www.ragtrader.com.au/news/luxury-brands-make-chadstone-home|title=Luxury brands make Chadstone home|date=2009-08-17|work=Ragtrader|accessdate=2009-08-28}}</ref> A second opened at [[Westfield Sydney]] in 2011. A branch opened in [[Glasgow]], [[Scotland]], in 2010 and is situated in the [[House of Fraser|Fraser]]'s department store.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://insidelife.mondoraro.org/2011/02/prada-e-miu-miu-alla-conquista-di-glasgow/|title=MondoRaro Foundation - BlogZine Indipendente|publisher=|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110409174430/http://insidelife.mondoraro.org/2011/02/prada-e-miu-miu-alla-conquista-di-glasgow/|archive-date=2011-04-09|df=}}</ref>

In 2017 the Miu Miu [[Sloane Street]] store in [[London]] reopened after a refurbishment, and announced that they would be starting a customisation service to allow customers to design their own heels. It is the only Miu Miu store in the world to offer this service.<ref name=AGLAIAMagazine>{{cite web|url=https://www.aglaiamagazine.com/miu-miu-launches-customisation-service/|title=MIU MIU CHELSEA REOPENS WITH CUSTOMISATION SERVICE|work=AGLAIA Magazine|year=2017|accessdate=2017-07-29}}</ref>
