The United States have appeared at the [[Hopman Cup]] from the 2nd staging of the event in 1990. The United States have appeared in eleven Hopman Cup finals and have a record six victories. They are also the only country to successfully defend the title and hold the record for consecutive final appearances at four.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://hopmancup.com/Results/Nations-Records.html |title=Nations Records |publisher=www.hopmancup.com |accessdate=3 Jan 2012}}</ref>
