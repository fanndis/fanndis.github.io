The '''United States Fed Cup team''' is the most successful national team in [[Fed Cup]] competition.  The team has won 18 titles and finished second a further 11 times, out of 55 participations.

==History==
The United States won the inaugural Fed Cup in 1963.  They hold the record for most ties won, with 149, including 37 in a row.  They won seven straight titles between [[1976 Federation Cup|1976]] and [[1982 Federation Cup|1982]]
