'''Donatella Francesca Versace''' ({{IPA-it|donaˈtɛlla franˈtʃeska verˈsaːtʃe|lang}};{{Efn|According to a January 2018 ''[[Vogue (magazine)|Vogue]]'' interview with Versace, her name is correctly pronounced {{IPAc-en|v|ər|ˈ|s|ɑː|tʃ|eɪ}} {{respell|vər|SAH|chay}} in English as opposed to the popular pronunciation of {{IPAc-en|v|ər|ˈ|s|ɑː|tʃ|i}} {{respell|vər|SAH|chee}}.<ref>{{Cite AV media | title=73 Questions With Donatella Versace | work=[[Vogue (magazine)|Vogue]]| url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IkV5VpEROW0 | date=31 January 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news | url=https://www.harpersbazaar.com/uk/fashion/fashion-news/a16023857/youve-probably-been-pronouncing-versace-wrong-according-to-donatella/ | title=You've probably been pronouncing 'Versace' wrong, according to Donatella | work=[[Harper's Bazaar]] | date=2 February 2018}}</ref>}} born 2 May 1955) is an Italian fashion designer and businesswoman. She is the sister of [[Gianni Versace]], founder of the company [[Versace]] which she inherited upon her brother's death in 1997. She sold the company to [[Capri Holdings]] (Michael Kors) in 2018, but remained its [[chief creative officer]].<ref>{{Cite press release | url=https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20181231005037/en/Capri-Holdings-Limited-Completes-Acquisition-Versace | title=Capri Holdings Limited Completes Acquisition of Versace | publisher=[[Business Wire]] | date=31 December 2018}}</ref>

==Biography==
Donatella Versace was born in [[Reggio di Calabria]], [[Southern Italy]], the youngest of four children. Her father, Antonio, was a salesman and her mother, Francesca, was a dressmaker.<ref>{{cite news | url=https://www.italymagazine.com/featured-story/italian-families-versace | title=Italian Families: Versace | first=Carol | last=King | work=Italy Magazine | date=15 April 2013}}</ref> Her older sister, Tina, died at the age of 12 from an improperly treated [[tetanus]] infection.<ref>{{Cite news | last=Collins | first=Lauren | title=Mondo Donatella | work=[[The New Yorker]] | page=158 | date=24 September 2007}}</ref> Donatella was persuaded to dye her hair blonde at just 11 years old by Gianni, because he was a big fan of Italian singer [[Patty Pravo]].<ref>{{cite news | url=https://torontosun.com/2016/11/02/donatella-versace-pressured-to-dye-hair-blond-at-age-11-by-brother-gianni/wcm/9b9de551-28cc-4eae-a622-29898542a8d3 | title=Donatella Versace pressured to dye hair blond at age 11 by brother Gianni | work=[[Toronto Sun]] | date=2 November 2016}}</ref><ref name="nytimes gay">Jacob Bernstein, [https://www.nytimes.com/2019/06/29/style/donatella-versace-gay-pride-stonewall.html Donatella Versace Has Long Been a Gay Icon. Now It’s Official.], ''Nytimes.com'', 29 June 2019</ref>

In the mid-1970s, Donatella studied literature and languages in [[Florence, Italy]] but would commute to Milan every weeked to spend time with her brother [[Gianni Versace]].<ref name="vanityfair bella">Cathy Horyn, [https://www.vanityfair.com/style/1997/06/donatella-versace-profile-cathy-horyn La Bella Donatella], ''Vanityfair.com'', 1 June 1997</ref><ref name="nymag sunqueen">Ariel Levy, [https://nymag.com/fashion/06/fall/19388/ Summer for the Sun Queen], ''Nymag.com'', 17 August 2006</ref> She had planned to work for her brother in the [[public relations]] department, but, according to her, was more valuable to him as a "[[muse]] and critic".<ref>{{cite news | url=https://www.vanityfair.com/style/2018/01/versace-fashion-gianni-donatella-american-crime-story | title=Inside Gianni Versace’s Final Fashion Show | first=JULIE | last=MILLER | work=[[Vanity Fair (magazine)|Vanity Fair]] | date=25 January 2018}}</ref> Through her closeness to her brother's enterprises, she entered the fashion world. In 1989, Gianni launched a perfume dedicated to her, ''Blonde'', and gave her her own label, ''[[Versus (Versace)|Versus]]'', which Gianni formerly ran for her until his death.<ref name="theguardian strong">Jess Cartner-Morley, [https://www.theguardian.com/fashion/2017/sep/14/interview-donatella-versace-my-brothers-death-made-me-strong Donatella Versace: "My brother was the king, and my whole world had crashed around me”], ''Theguardian.com'', 14 September 2017</ref> In the media, she was widely known as her brother's muse.<ref name="vanityfair bella"/> During the 1990s when the luxury war was between Versace and Armani, she brought the louche glamour that characterized the brand's appeal.<ref name="nymag sunqueen"/>

Her brother [[Gianni Versace]] was murdered on 15 July 1997, outside the re-constructed [[Casa Casuarina]], also known as Versace Mansion, in [[Miami]], [[Florida]].<ref>{{Cite news | url=https://abcnews.go.com/US/inside-mind-serial-killer-murdered-fashion-icon-gianni/story?id=48459029 | title=Inside the mind of the serial killer who murdered Gianni Versace | first1=MURIEL | last1=PEARSON | first2=JIM | last2=DUBREUIL | first3=ALEXA | last3=VALIENTE | work=[[ABC News]] | date=7 July 2017}}</ref> In the aftermath of the highly publicized crime and manhunt, most of the Versace family, including Donatella, moved temporarily to a secluded private resort in the [[Caribbean]]. Her brother, [[Santo Versace]], inherited 30% of Versace upon Gianni's death and her daughter, [[Allegra Versace]], inherited 50%.<ref>{{cite news | url=https://www.businessinsider.com/gianni-versace-donatella-versace-family-life-story-2019-4 | title=Inside the iconic Versace family's journey through fame, tragedy, and resilience | first=Rachel | last=Askinasi | work=[[Business Insider]] | date=23 April 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news | url=https://www.nytimes.com/2004/06/15/nyregion/a-new-half-owner-for-versace-and-she-s-almost-18.html | title=A New Half-Owner for Versace, and She's Almost 18 | last=Horyn | first=Cathy | work=[[The New York Times]] | date=15 June 2004 | issn=0362-4331 | url-access=subscription}}</ref>

On 18 July 1998, one year and three days after Gianni's death, Donatella Versace mounted her first [[Haute couture]] show for the Versace Atelier at the [[Hôtel Ritz Paris]]. She built her runway over the hotel's swimming pool, as her brother had done every season, though this time using sheer glass. From October 2002 to January 2003, Gianni and Donatella's best known Versace clothing was displayed in a special exhibit of the [[Victoria and Albert Museum]] in London.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.vam.ac.uk/content/articles/g/gianni-versace/ | title=Gianni Versace | publisher=[[Victoria and Albert Museum]]}}</ref> Her first years as head of the company were unstable, until she defined a new feminist proposition for the brand in 2005.<ref name="theguardian strong"/>

Donatella has designed advertising campaigns for Versace that included [[Jennifer Lopez]], [[Madonna (entertainer)|Madonna]],<ref>[https://todayinmadonnahistory.com/2020/04/07/today-in-madonna-history-april-7-2005-2/ Today in Madonna History: April 7, 2005], ''Todayinmadonnahistory.com'', 7 April 2005</ref> [[Courtney Love]], [[Christina Aguilera]], [[Jonathan Rhys Meyers]], [[Demi Moore]], [[Nicki Minaj]], and [[Lady Gaga]] and made [[Beyoncé]] and [[January Jones]]. She designed the [[Green Versace dress of Jennifer Lopez]], also known as the "Jungle-Dress", which was worn by [[Jennifer Lopez]] at the [[42nd Grammy Awards]] in 2000.<ref>{{cite news | url=https://www.oprahmag.com/style/a27254465/jennifer-lopez-2000-grammys-versace-dress/ | title=Jennifer Lopez Reveals Why She Almost Didn't Wear That Infamous Versace Dress to the 2000 Grammys | first=Celia | last=Fernandez | work=[[O, The Oprah Magazine]] | date=20 June 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | url=https://www.today.com/style/jennifer-lopez-shares-story-iconic-green-versace-dress-t152647 | title=Jennifer Lopez shares the story of that iconic green Versace dress | first=Alyssa | last=Newcomb | work=[[Today (American TV program)|Today]] | date=22 April 2019}}</ref> In 2015, she appeared in the AW15 ad campaign of its direct competitor [[Givenchy]].<ref>Morwenna Ferrier, [https://www.theguardian.com/fashion/2015/apr/29/donatella-versace-the-new-face-of-givenchy Donatella Versace – the new face of Givenchy?], ''Theguardian.com'', 29 April 2015</ref><ref name="theguardian strong"/>

Donatella designed the [[Palazzo Versace Australia]] resort on the [[Gold Coast, Queensland]], [[Australia]], which opened in September 2000, and played a major role in the design of the [[Palazzo Versace Dubai]], the second Palazzo Versace hotel, which opened in November 2016.<ref>{{cite news | url=https://www.vogue.com/article/donatella-versace-alessandra-ambrosio-palazzo-versace-dubai-opening | title=Go Big or Go Home! Palazzo Versace’s Grand Opening in Dubai | work=[[Vogue (magazine)|Vogue]] | date=16 November 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/travel/destinations/middle-east/united-arab-emirates/dubai/articles/donatella-palazzo-versace-dubai-hotel-launch/ | title=Donatella Versace’s Dubai dream – inside the emirate's most glamorous hotel | first=Lara | last=Brunt | work=[[The Daily Telegraph]] | date=28 November 2016}}</ref> She also designed a [[Mini (marque)|Mini Cooper]] for the [[Life Ball]].<ref>[https://www.chicagotribune.com/hoy/ct-hoy-8005176-un-mini-mas-aventurero-story.html Un Mini Cooper más aventurero], ''Chicagotribune.com'', 2 June 2011</ref> In September 2017, Donatella Versace set up a tribute show dedicated to her brother.<ref>Lake Como, [https://www.vogue.co.uk/shows/spring-summer-2018-ready-to-wear/versace Versace], ''Vogue.co.uk'', 22 September 2017</ref>

==In popular culture==
Versace has made a [[cameo appearance]] in ''[[Zoolander]]''. In ''[[The Devil Wears Prada (film)|The Devil Wears Prada]]'', her name was mentioned sporadically.<ref>{{cite news | url=https://www.elitedaily.com/p/donatella-versaces-net-worth-is-even-higher-than-you-think-it-is-thats-a-lot-7937486 | title=Donatella Versace's Net Worth Is Even Higher Than You Think It Is & That's A Lot | first=ANI | last=BUNDEL | work=Elite Daily | date=17 January 2018}}</ref>

At the [[42nd Grammy Awards]] (2000), [[Jennifer Lopez]] wore the [[Green Versace dress of Jennifer Lopez|Green Jungle Dress]] which became an international point of attention, being highly publicised in the media. It was designed by Donatella herself and Lopez has the original dress currently with her at her estate. The dress worn by Lopez remains the reason for the creation of [[Google Images]], and is known to have been downloaded over 600,000 times within 24 hours of the Grammy's night.<ref>{{Cite book|last=Lee|first=Michelle|url=http://archive.org/details/fashionvictim00mich|title=Fashion victim : our love-hate relationship with dressing, shopping, and the cost of style|date=2003|publisher=New York : Broadway Books|others=Internet Archive}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.gq.com/story/jennifer-lopez-versace-google-images|title=How Jennifer Lopez’s Versace Dress Created Google Images|last=Tashjian|first=Rachel|website=GQ|language=en|access-date=2020-03-14}}</ref> In 2019, Lopez wore the dress for the first time since 2001 for the Versace Milan Fashion Show.<ref>{{Cite news|last=Jones|first=Jo|url=https://www.theguardian.com/fashion/gallery/2019/sep/23/milan-fashion-week-springsummer-2020-14-key-shows-in-pictures|title=Milan fashion week spring/summer 2020: 14 key shows – in pictures|date=2019-09-23|work=The Guardian|access-date=2020-03-14|last2=Conlon|first2=Scarlett|language=en-GB|issn=0261-3077}}</ref>

Versace was parodied several times by [[Maya Rudolph]] on ''[[Saturday Night Live]]''.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.nbc.com/saturday-night-live/cast/maya-rudolph-14896/impersonation/donatella-versace-192131 | title=DONATELLA VERSACE SKETCHES | publisher=[[NBC]]}}</ref>

In the [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] series ''[[Ugly Betty]]'', a fictional character called "Fabia", played by actress [[Gina Gershon]], is a parody of Donatella.<ref>{{cite news | url=https://www.today.com/entertainment/double-take-time-gershon-uncanny-twin-donatella-versace-8C11080334 | title=Double-take! Gina Gershon becomes an uncanny twin of Donatella Versace | first=Gael Fashingbauer | last=Cooper | work=[[Today (American TV program)|Today]] |date=5 September 2013}}</ref> Gershon also played Versace as the lead role in the [[Lifetime (TV network)|Lifetime]] television movie ''[[House of Versace]]'', released in 2013.

In 1999, director [[William Friedkin]] considered [[Angelina Jolie]] to portray Donatella, in a small role, in a film which never materialized entitled ''The Man Who Killed Versace'' written by [[Frederic Raphael]].{{citation needed|date=November 2018}}

[[Lady Gaga]] wrote the song "Donatella" from her 2013 album ''[[Artpop]]'' for her.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://fashionista.com/2013/08/donatella-versace-approves-of-the-love-song-lady-gaga-wrote-for-her |title=Donatella Versace Approves of the Love Song Lady Gaga Wrote for Her |publisher=Fashionista.com |accessdate=19 May 2014}}</ref> The plot of season 2 of the television series ''[[American Crime Story]]'' recounts the murder of Donatella's brother, [[Gianni Versace]].<ref>{{Cite news | url=https://variety.com/2017/tv/news/lady-gaga-donatella-versace-american-crime-story-season-3-1201910108/ | title=Lady Gaga Will Not Play Donatella Versace in FX’s 'American Crime Story' | last=Wagmeister | first=Elizabeth | work=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]] | date=12 January 2017}}</ref> She is portrayed by [[Penélope Cruz]].<ref>{{cite news | url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/penelope-cruz-star-versace-american-crime-story-987324 | title=Penelope Cruz to Star in 'Versace: American Crime Story' | last=Goldberg | first=Lesley | work=[[The Hollywood Reporter]]| date=20 March 2017}}</ref> She criticized the authenticity of the TV series, stating that she was with [[Madonna]] when the murder occurred, a moment that is not in the pictures.<ref>Kyle Munzenrieder, [https://www.wmagazine.com/story/gianni-versace-donatella-madonna-american-crime-story/ Donatella Versace Reveals a Stunning Detail About Madonna and the Aftermath of Her Brother’s Death], ''Wmagazine.com'', 10 April 2018</ref>

==Personal life==
Versace and her former husband, American [[model (person)|model]] Paul Beck have two children: daughter [[Allegra Versace|Allegra Versace Beck]] (born 30 June 1986), and son Daniel Versace Beck (born 1989). Her marriage with Paul Beck ended in 2000.<ref name="theguardian strong"/>

She fought a cocaine addiction for 18 years,<ref>Silvia Castillo, [https://www.abc.es/estilo/gente/abci-polemica-supuesta-adiccion-drogas-donatella-versace-200407290300-9622808754144_noticia.html Polémica por la supuesta adicción a las drogas de Donatella Versace], ''Abc.es'', 29 July 2004</ref> until 2005.<ref name="theguardian strong"/> A heavy smoker, she quit in 2014.<ref name="nytimes gay"/> She supposedly abused tanning sessions to the point of being affected by premature skin aging.<ref>{{in lang|es}} [http://www.teinteresa.es/belleza/disenadora-Donatella-Versace-moreno-tostado_1_528557762.html La diseñadora Donatella Versace ha abusado del bronceado y presenta un envejecimiento de piel prematuro], Teinteresa.it'', 2 August 2011</ref>

She is a patron of the [[Elton John AIDS Foundation]].<ref>{{cite web | url=http://ejaf.com/about/our-patrons/ | title=Our Patrons | publisher=[[Elton John AIDS Foundation]]}}</ref>
