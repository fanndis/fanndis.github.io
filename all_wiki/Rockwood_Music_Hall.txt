'''Rockwood Music Hall''' is a music venue at 196 [[Allen Street]] on the [[Lower East Side]] of [[Manhattan]], [[New York City]]. Owner Ken Rockwood opened the establishment in 2005 as a small bar and music venue. The venue features three stages (2 larger rooms upstairs and one smaller room downstairs) and a record label of the same name. 
[[File:Rockwood Approved 1 3000x2000.jpg|left|thumb]]
