The '''2015 [[Bank of the West Classic]]''' was a professional [[tennis]] tournament played on [[hard court (tennis)|hard courts]]. It was the 44th edition of the tournament, which was part of the [[WTA Premier tournaments]] of the [[2015 WTA Tour]], making it the longest-running women's only tennis tournament.<ref>{{cite news|title=2015 Tournament Preview: Bank of the West Classic|url=http://www.usta.com/news/2015_tournament_preview_bank_of_the_west_classic/|accessdate=May 19, 2015|publisher=United States Tennis Association|date=January 29, 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150521022959/http://www.usta.com/news/2015_tournament_preview_bank_of_the_west_classic/|archive-date=2015-05-21|url-status=dead}}</ref> It took place in [[Stanford, California|Stanford]], United States between 3 and 9 August 2015. It was the first women's event on the [[2015 US Open Series]].
