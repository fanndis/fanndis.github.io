The '''Interfax-Ukraine News Agency''' ({{lang-uk|Інтерфакс-Україна}}) is a [[Kyiv]]-based<ref>[http://www.interfax.com.ua/eng/info/contacts/ Contacts], Official website</ref> [[Ukraine|Ukrainian]] [[news agency]] founded in 1992.<ref>[http://www.mondotimes.com/1/world/ua/126/4150/10209 Interfax (Ukraine News Agency)], [[Mondo Times]]</ref> The company belongs to the Russian news group [[Interfax]] Information Services.<ref name=about>[http://www.interfax.com.ua/eng/info/agency/ About agency] Official website</ref> The company publishes in Ukrainian, Russian and English.<ref name=about/>

The company owns a 50-seat press centre.<ref>[http://www.interfax.com.ua/eng/info/presscenter/ Press-center], Official website</ref>

== History ==
Interfax was formed on 24 November 1992, the year following [[Modern history of Ukraine#Independent Ukraine (1991 to present)|Ukraine's 1991 independence]], by a team of 10 people in [[Kharkiv]].<ref name="Interfax-Ukraine - 25 years">[http://en.interfax.com.ua/news/general/464226.html Interfax-Ukraine - 25 years] Official website (24 November 2017)</ref> In 1993 the agency moved to [[Kyiv]].<ref name="Interfax-Ukraine - 25 years"/>
