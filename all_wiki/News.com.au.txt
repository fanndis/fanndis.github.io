'''News.com.au''' is an [[Australia]]n news and entertainment [[website]] owned by [[News Corp Australia]]. It had 9.6 million unique readers in April 2019<ref>{{cite web |title=Nielsen Digital Content Ratings April 2019 Tagged Rankings |url=https://www.nielsen.com/au/en/press-room/2019/nielsen-digital-content-ratings-april-2019-tagged-rankings.html |website=The Nielsen Company |accessdate=25 May 2019}}</ref> and specialises in breaking national and international news as well as entertainment, sport, lifestyle, travel, technology and finance.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.news.com.au/more-information|title=About us|publisher=News.com.au|accessdate=18 January 2019|date=24 March 2015}}</ref>

==Web analytics==
According to third-party [[web analytics]] providers, [[Alexa Internet|Alexa]] and [[SimilarWeb]], News.com.au was the 19th and 27th most visited website in Australia respectively, as of July 2015.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.alexa.com/siteinfo/news.com.au|title=News.com.au Site Overview|publisher=Alexa|accessdate=18 January 2019}}</ref><ref name=similar>{{cite web|url=https://www.similarweb.com/website/news.com.au|title=News.com.au Analytics|publisher=SimilarWeb|accessdate=18 January 2019}}</ref> SimilarWeb rates the site as the third most visited news website in Australia, attracting more than 18 million visitors per month.<ref name=similar/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.similarweb.com/country_category/australia/news_and_media|title=Top 50 sites in Australia for News And Media|publisher=SimilarWeb|accessdate=30 July 2015|url-status = dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150825042518/http://www.similarweb.com/country_category/australia/news_and_media|archivedate=25 August 2015|df=dmy-all}}</ref> [[Nielsen Online]] Ratings rated news.com.au as Australia's most popular news website as of January 2015.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mumbrella.com.au/january-online-news-ratings-remain-steady-news-com-au-winning-275553#|title=News site audiences dive but News.com.au increases lead at the top of online ratings in January|website=[[Mumbrella]]|date=11 February 2015|accessdate=18 January 2019}}</ref>

Whilst frequently ranked as the number-one visited Australian news website in Australia during 2019, as of June, 2020, News.com.au has slipped to the  third most visited news website in Australia after [[ABC Online|ABC Australia News Online]] and [[MailOnline|''Daily Mail'' Australian Edition]].  <ref>{{cite web|url=https://mumbrella.com.au/news-com-au-tumbles-to-sixth-in-ranking-of-australias-most-popular-websites-as-abc-maintains-lead-630211|title=News.com.au tumbles to sixth in ranking of Australiaâ€™s most popular websites, as ABC maintains lead|website=[[Mumbrella]]|date=4 June 2020|accessdate=24 June 2020}}</ref>
