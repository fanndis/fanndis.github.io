'''Alceu de Deus Collares''' (born September 12, 1927 in [[Bagé]]) is a Brazilian politician and lawyer. He was the [[Governor]] of [[Rio Grande do Sul]] state (1991–95), and was also a member of the [[Chamber of Deputies (Brazil)|Chamber of Deputies]] and [[Mayor]] of [[Porto Alegre]] (1986–89).<ref>http://www.sul21.com.br/jornal/alceu-collares-de-quitandeiro-a-governador/</ref><ref>http://www.camarapoa.rs.gov.br/noticias/alceu-collares-recebera-trofeu-camara-municipal</ref>

He is a member of the [[Democratic Labour Party (Brazil)|Democratic Labor Party of Brazil]].
