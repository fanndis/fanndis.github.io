The '''49th Annual Grammy Awards''' was a ceremony honoring the best in music for the recording year beginning October 1, 2005 and ending September 30, 2006 in the United States. The awards were handed out on Sunday, February 11, 2007 at the [[Staples Center]] in [[Los Angeles]]. The [[Dixie Chicks]] were the night's biggest winners winning a total of five awards. [[Mary J. Blige]] received the most nominations, with eight. [[Don Henley]] was honored as [[MusiCares Person of the Year]] two nights prior to the show on February 9, 2007. The show won an [[59th Primetime Emmy Awards|Emmy]] for Outstanding Lighting Direction (electronic, multicamera) for VMC Programming.
