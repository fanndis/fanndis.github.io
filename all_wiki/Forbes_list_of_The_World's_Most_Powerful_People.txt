{{DISPLAYTITLE:''Forbes'' list of The World's Most Powerful People}}
Since 2009 (with absences in 2017, 2019 and 2020), the business magazine, ''[[Forbes]]''  had compiled an annual '''list of the world's most powerful people'''. The list has one slot [[World population|for every 100 million people]], meaning in 2009 there were 67 people on the list and by 2018 there were 75. Slots are allocated based on the amount of human and financial resources that they have sway over, as well as their influence on world events.<ref>{{cite news |author= Michael Noer and Nicole Perlroth|title=The World's Most Powerful People |work=[[Forbes]] |date=11 November 2009 |access-date=2009-11-16 |url=https://www.forbes.com/2009/11/09/world-most-powerful-leadership-power-09-intro.html}}</ref>
