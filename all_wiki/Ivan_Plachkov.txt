'''Ivan Plachkov''' ({{lang-ua|Іван Васильович Плачков}}; b. 23 November 1957, [[Krynychne]], [[Bolhrad Raion]], [[Odessa Oblast]]) is a [[Ukrain]]ian politician, [[Bulgarians in Ukraine|Ukrainian Bulgarian]]. Plachkov was Ukraine's Minister of Energy in 1999 and again [[Ministry of Fuel and Energy (Ukraine)|Minister of Fuel and Energy of Ukraine]] between 2005 and 2007.<ref name="659497Plachkov">{{cite web|url=https://www.epravda.com.ua/publications/2020/04/17/659497/|title=How much does a minister's chair cost: new head of the Ministry of Energy as an irritant for Kolomoisky|work=[[Ukrayinska Pravda]]|date=April 17, 2020|accessdate=April 17, 2020|language=uk}}</ref>
