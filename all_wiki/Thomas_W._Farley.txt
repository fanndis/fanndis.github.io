'''Thomas W. Farley''' (born December 10, 1975) is an American banker. He serves as the CEO of Far Point Acquisition Corp. He previously served as the president of the [[NYSE Group]], including the [[New York Stock Exchange]].

==Early life==
Thomas W. Farley was born December 10, 1975.<ref name="bloombergfarley">{{cite web|title=Thomas W. Farley|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/people/person.asp?personId=566238&privcapId=1081952|website=Bloomberg Business|accessdate=February 6, 2016}}</ref> He graduated from [[Gonzaga College High School]] as a member of the class of 1993. He graduated from [[Georgetown University]], where he received a bachelor of arts degree in political science.<ref name="bloombergfarley"/><ref name="icemanagementthomasfarley">{{cite web|title=Management: Thomas Farley|url=http://ir.theice.com/governance/management|website=InterContinental Exchange|accessdate=February 7, 2016}}</ref> He received a [[Chartered Financial Analyst]] certification from the [[CFA Institute]].<ref name="icemanagementthomasfarley"/>

==Career==
Farley worked for [[Montgomery Securities]], an investment bank, and Gryphon Investors, a private equity firm.<ref name="icemanagementthomasfarley"/> He served as the chief financial officer, chief operating officer and later president of SunGard Kiodex, the [[Derivative (finance)|derivatives]] advisory subsidiary of [[SunGard]].<ref name="icemanagementthomasfarley"/>

Farley became the president of the [[Intercontinental Exchange]] (ICE) and chief operating officer of the ICE Futures U.S. in 2007.<ref name="icemanagementthomasfarley"/> He later served as senior vice president of financial markets at the ICE.<ref name="icemanagementthomasfarley"/>

Farley served as the chief operating officer of the NYSE Group from November 2013 to May 2014.<ref name="icemanagementthomasfarley"/> He served as its president from 2014 to 2018.<ref name="icemanagementthomasfarley"/> He is a proponent of keeping its equity trading floor.<ref name="fttradingfloor">{{cite news|last1=Bullock|first1=Nicole|last2=Meyer|first2=Gregory|title=NYSE boss Thomas Farley sees value in trading floor|url=http://www.ft.com/cms/s/0/17611f84-6372-11e4-8a63-00144feabdc0.html#axzz3zPzWy0ln|accessdate=February 6, 2016|work=Financial Times|date=November 4, 2014}}</ref>
