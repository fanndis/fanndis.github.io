'''Vitaliy Moskalenko''' ({{lang-ru|Виталий Москаленко}}; born 30 June 1974) is a [[Russia]]n [[triple jump]]er.

He competed at the [[2003 World Championships in Athletics|2003 World Championships]] and the [[Athletics at the 2004 Summer Olympics – Men's triple jump|2004 Olympic Games]] without reaching the final.

His personal best jump is 17.17 metres, achieved in August 2003 in [[Tula, Russia|Tula]].
