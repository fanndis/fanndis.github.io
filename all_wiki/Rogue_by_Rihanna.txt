'''Rogue''' is the fourth fragrance released by [[Barbadians|Barbadian]] singer [[Rihanna]]. The fragrance was released in two installments; firstly a women's fragrance and finally a men's cologne.<ref name="BET">{{cite web|url=http://www.bet.com/news/fashion-and-beauty/2013/05/17/rihanna-set-to-release-new-fragrance-rogue.html |title= Rihanna Set to Release New Fragrance |publisher=bet.com |date=May 17, 2013 |accessdate=June 4, 2014}}</ref> The women's fragrance was officially released on September 4, 2013 under the perfume line, Parlux Fragrances and was made available for purchase on Rihanna's official perfume website and also at [[Macy's]], Dillards and Belk stores.<ref name="MTV Style">{{cite web|url=http://style.mtv.com/2013/09/04/rihanna-rogue-perfume/ |title= Rihanna's Rogue Fragrance is Available Now |publisher=MTV |date=September 4, 2013 |accessdate=June 4, 2014}}</ref>

==Background==
In light of her success with her three previous fragrances, [[Reb'l Fleur]], [[Rebelle (fragrance)|Rebelle]] and [[Nude by Rihanna|Nude]], Rihanna decided to embark on her fourth fragrance Rogue. The fragrance bottle abandons the previous designs of Rihanna's first three fragrances. Of the fragrance, Rihanna said "People are always changing and evolving, and with Rogue, my new fragrance, I wanted to switch it up to reflect who I am today. It's a personal fragrance that's more self-aware. It's Rogue, it's me."<ref name="BET9-19">{{cite web|url=http://www.bet.com/news/fashion-and-beauty/2013/09/19/rihanna-goes-rogue-with-new-fragrance.html |title= Rihanna Goes Rogue With New Fragrance |publisher=bet.com |date=September 19, 2013 |accessdate=June 4, 2014}}</ref>

==Information==
Rihanna described the fragrance as being "for all women for every mood".<ref name="MTV">{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.co.uk/music/urban/10730-rihanna-to-release-new-fragrance-called-rogue |title= Rihanna To Release New Fragrance Called 'Rogue' |publisher=MTV |date=July 18, 2013 |accessdate=June 4, 2014}}</ref> The top notes of the fragrance include lemon blossom, cyclamen, bergamot and pink peppercorn. The middle notes consist of Jasmine, rose, muget, plum and suede while the base notes are sandalwood, patchouli, golden amber and vanilla.<ref name="Perfumes by Rihanna">{{cite web|url=http://www.perfumesbyrihanna.com |title= Perfumes by Rihanna |publisher=perfumesbyrihanna.com |accessdate=June 4, 2014}}</ref>

'''Rogue Man''' is the fifth overall fragrance and first men's [[fragrance]] released by [[Barbadians|Barbadian]] singer [[Rihanna]]. The fragrance was first available to purchase from [[Macy's]] on October 12, 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www1.macys.com/shop/product/rihanna-rogue-man-eau-de-toilette-34-oz?ID=1746307&LinkshareID=J84DHJLQkR4-JDeixJg_LG9fqeooR2I0aQ&PartnerID=LINKSHARE&cm_mmc=LINKSHARE-_-5-_-63-_-MP563#pdpTabs|title=Rihanna Rogue Man Eau de Toilette 3.4 oz|publisher=[[Macy's]]}}</ref>

===Background===
The perfume was developed by Frank Voelkl as an oriental – woody. In the top notes there are bergamot, clementine, black pepper and rosemary. The heart consists of cedar, jasmine, vanilla orchid and labdanum, leaning on the base of sandalwood, patchouli, amber, musk and tonka bean.

===Promotion===
The advertising campaign for Rogue Man was shot by photographer Mario Sorrenti and shows Rihanna and tattooed model Daniel Ness. In black and white shots, the singer is seen with her face pressed sensually into Ness' neck as she wraps her arms around him from behind. She also posted on Twitter: ""LADIES!!! This September wake up next to your man smelling like this!!! Makes you wanna steal his Tshirts #ROGUEMAN."<ref>{{cite web|url=https://mobile.twitter.com/rihanna/status/495267684192628736 |title=Rihanna on Twitter "LADIES!!! This September wake up next to your man smelling like this!!! Makes you wanna steal his Tshirts #ROGUEMAN" |publisher=Twitter}}</ref>

Rihanna made an appearance at Macy's at Lenox Square Mall on October 25, 2014. The first 500 customers who purchased the fragrance from the store had the opportunity to meet and receive a photo with her.
