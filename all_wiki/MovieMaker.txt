'''''MovieMaker''''' is an American publication focused on the art and business of [[filmmaking]] with a special emphasis on independent film. It was founded by Timothy Rhys in 1993<ref name="MMAbout"/> as a small, regional publication based in [[Seattle]].<ref>{{cite book|title=American Independent Cinema: Indie, Indiewood and Beyond|author1=Geoff|author2=Claire Molloy|author3=Yannis Tzioumakis|publisher=Routledge|year=2013|isbn=9780415684286|page=34|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=7ibUEeJMNCYC&pg=PA34}}</ref> The magazine is published on a quarterly basis.<ref name=MMAbout/> Its headquarters is in Los Angeles, California.<ref name="MMAbout">{{cite web |url=https://www.moviemaker.com/about/|title= About|author=<!--Not stated--> |date= |website=MovieMaker.com |access-date= 17 May 2017}}</ref>
