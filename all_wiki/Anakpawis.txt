'''Anakpawis''' is a [[political party|party-list]] in the [[Philippines]]. The party-list is the electoral wing of the radical [[trade union]] movement [[Kilusang Mayo Uno]] and the peasant group Kilusang Mangbubukid ng Pilipinas.

Known for its radical pro-labor and peasant stand, Anakpawis had been known for its 125 across the board wage increase for the workers, as well as the genuine agrarian reform bill that involves direct redistribution of land to the landless peasants.

In the [[2004 Philippine general election|2004 elections]] for the [[House of Representatives of the Philippines|House of Representatives]] the party-list obtained 538,396 votes (4.2320% of the nationwide vote)<ref>[http://www.comelec.gov.ph/results/2004partylist.html COMELEC Election Results<!-- Bot generated title -->] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070427161552/http://www.comelec.gov.ph/results/2004partylist.html |date=2007-04-27 }}</ref> and two seats ([[Crispin Beltran|Crispin B. Beltran]] and [[Rafael V. Mariano]]).<ref>[http://www.congress.gov.ph/members/search.php?id=beltran], [http://www.congress.gov.ph/members/search.php?id=mariano]</ref> In the May 14, 2007 [[2007 Philippine general election|election]], the party won 1 seat in the nationwide party-list vote.
