This is a list of currently enthroned [[monarch]]s sorted by length of service. It includes monarchs who do not reign over entire nations, such as [[Muhammad V of Kelantan]], but does not include [[List of living former sovereign monarchs|former monarchs]] and [[List of current pretenders|pretenders]], such as [[Simeon Saxe-Coburg-Gotha|Simeon II of Bulgaria]], ''ex officio'' monarchs such as [[Emmanuel Macron]] who in his capacity as [[President of France]] is also [[Co-Prince of Andorra]], monarchs without physical territories such as the [[List of Grand Masters of the Knights Hospitaller|Prince and Grand Master]] of the [[Sovereign Military Order of Malta]], [[List of current constituent monarchs|constituent monarchs]] such as the traditional African rulers, or monarchs whose position is unofficial, such as [[14th Dalai Lama|Tenzin Gyatso, 14th Dalai Lama]].

[[Elizabeth II]] is currently the longest-reigning monarch, having been Queen of the [[United Kingdom]], [[Canada]], [[Australia]] and [[New Zealand]] since 6 February 1952. She is also the monarch of 12 other [[Commonwealth realm]]s which have become independent states since 1952; she is therefore listed multiple times in the table below.
