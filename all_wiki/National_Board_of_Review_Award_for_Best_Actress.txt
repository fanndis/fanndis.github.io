The '''National Board of Review Award for Best Actress''' is one of the annual film awards given (since 1945) by the [[National Board of Review|National Board of Review of Motion Pictures]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nationalboardofreview.org/award-names/best-actress/|title=Best Actress Archives – National Board of Review|work=[[National Board of Review]]|accessdate=February 2, 2015}}</ref>
