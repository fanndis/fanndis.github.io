The '''2014 [[Bank of the West Classic]]''' was a professional [[tennis]] tournament played on [[hard court (tennis)|hard courts]]. It was the 43rd edition of the tournament, which was part of the [[WTA Premier tournaments]] of the [[2014 WTA Tour]]. It took place in [[Stanford, California|Stanford]], United States between 28 July and 3 August 2014. It was the first women's event on the [[2014 US Open Series]].
