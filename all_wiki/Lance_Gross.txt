'''Lance Darnell Gross''' (born July 8, 1981)<ref name=cbi>{{cite web|url=https://www.californiabirthindex.org/birth/lance_darnell_gross_born_1981_14899293|work=California Birth Index|publisher=California Office of Health Information and Research|title=Lance Darnell Gross was born on July 8, 1981 in Alameda County, California|accessdate=November 4, 2018}}</ref> is an Ghanaian American actor, model and photographer. He is best known for his role as Calvin Payne on the [[TBS (TV network)|TBS]] sitcom ''[[Tyler Perry's House of Payne]]'' and [[Oprah Winfrey Network|OWN]] sitcom ''[[The Paynes|Tyler Perry's The Paynes]]''.,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.familytreelegends.com/records/calbirths?c=search&first=Lance&last=Gross&spelling=Exact&4_year=1981&4_month=7&4_day=8&5=Male&7=Alameda&SubmitSearch.x=0&SubmitSearch.y=0 |title=California Birth Index, 1905-1995 |website=Family Tree Legends |publisher=Center for Health Statistics, California Department of Health Services, Sacramento, California, USA. |accessdate=April 19, 2014 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140421050621/http://www.familytreelegends.com/records/calbirths?c=search&first=Lance&last=Gross&spelling=Exact&4_year=1981&4_month=7&4_day=8&5=Male&7=Alameda&SubmitSearch.x=0&SubmitSearch.y=0 |archivedate=April 21, 2014 }}</ref> as well as appearing in other Tyler Perry productions such as the ''[[Meet the Browns (film)|Meet the Browns]]'' (2008) film and ''[[Temptation: Confessions of a Marriage Counselor|Tyler Perry's Temptations: Confessions of a Marriage Counselor]]'' (2013). He also co-starred in 2010's ''[[Our Family Wedding]]'' alongside [[Forest Whitaker]], [[Carlos Mencia]], [[America Ferrera]], and [[Regina King]]. He also starred as Secret Service Agent Marcus Finley in NBC's political drama ''[[Crisis (TV series)|Crisis]]''.

==Early life and career==
Gross was born in [[Oakland, California]], the child of a Ghanaian father and African American mother, and later moved to Las Vegas, Nevada, to attend and graduate from Bonanza High School.<ref>https://www.reviewjournal.com/entertainment/ex-bonanza-high-track-star-gross-stays-on-the-run-with-role-in-hostage-thriller/</ref> He went on to attend [[Howard University]], where he received a Bachelor of Arts in theater.{{citation needed|date=January 2018}}  He then trained at The Ivanna Chubbuck Studio as well as the Tasha Smith Acting Studio.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://independentfilmquarterly.com/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=1056&Itemid=125|title=Lance Gross: The Last Fall & Tyler Perry|accessdate=August 12, 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130903064009/http://independentfilmquarterly.com/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=1056&Itemid=125|archive-date=September 3, 2013|url-status=dead|df=mdy-all}}</ref>

Gross began his career by modeling for [[Karl Kani]] and appearing in music videos for artists such as [[Mary J. Blige]], [[Rihanna]] and [[Mariah Carey]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tbs.com/stories/story/0,,107834,00.html |title=Tyler Perry's House of Payne - Lance Gross As Calvin Payne |accessdate=August 12, 2013 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130705213632/http://www.tbs.com/stories/story/0%2C%2C107834%2C00.html |archivedate=July 5, 2013 }}</ref> In 2006, he had minor roles in ''[[Eve (U.S. TV series)|Eve]]'' and ''[[The Bernie Mac Show]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/name/nm2342614/bio |title= Biography for Lance Gross}}</ref> It was while he was performing in the Tasha Smith Acting Studio that he was discovered by [[Tyler Perry]].{{citation needed|date=January 2018}} He was consequently cast as Calvin Payne on Perry's new sitcom ''[[House of Payne]]''. In the show Gross plays Calvin, a young professional who has attended college for seven years. He has been awarded four [[NAACP Image Award]]s for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series for this role.<ref>{{cite web|title= Meet The Brown's Man Of The House-Lance Gross|url= http://clients.digipage.co.uk/?userpath=00000001/00003838/00019163/&page=25|access-date= 2019-04-30|archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20150626121357/http://clients.digipage.co.uk/?userpath=00000001%2F00003838%2F00019163%2F&page=25|archive-date= 2015-06-26|url-status= dead}}</ref>

2008 saw Gross make his film debut in the romantic comedy ''[[Meet the Browns (film)|Meet the Browns]]''.{{citation needed|date=January 2018}}  His next big screen appearance was in ''[[Our Family Wedding]]'' in 2010 alongside [[America Ferrera]], in which they portrayed a recently engaged young couple whose wedding preparations are disrupted by their families.<ref>{{cite web|title=Lance Gross Preps For Wedlock In 'Our Family Wedding|url=http://www.essence.com/2010/03/12/lance-gross-our-family-wedding/}}</ref> In 2012 he starred in the independent film ''The Last Fall'', the story of a retired [[NFL]] player dealing with financial and family difficulties.<ref>{{cite web|title=Lance Gross Talks ‘The Last Fall’ and His Most Vulnerable Role |url=http://www.vibe.com/article/lance-gross-talks-%E2%80%98-last-fall%E2%80%99-and-his-most-vulnerable-role |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131115092400/http://www.vibe.com/article/lance-gross-talks-%E2%80%98-last-fall%E2%80%99-and-his-most-vulnerable-role |archivedate=2013-11-15 }}</ref> Currently Gross plays a major role on the Fox musical drama ''[[Star (TV series)|Star]]''.

In 2018, on OWN's ''The Paynes'', he makes an appearance as a recurring character in the episodes "[[The Paynes#ep1|A Surprise for the Paynes]]", "[[The Paynes#ep2|Revelations of Payne]]" and "[[The Paynes#ep3|A Confrontation of Payne]]", along with [[Allen Payne]], [[Demetria McKinney]], [[Doc Shaw|Larramie "Doc" Shaw]] and [[China Anne McClain]].

==Personal life==
Gross was previously engaged to [[Eva Marcille]]. Their engagement ended in March 2010.<ref>{{cite magazine|url=http://www.essence.com/2010/03/25/eva-marcille-lance-gross-breakup|title=Eva Marcille and Lance Gross Break Up Exclusive|magazine=Essence.com|date=March 24, 2010|accessdate=December 18, 2015}}</ref> Gross married fashion stylist Rebecca Jefferson on May 23, 2015.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.eonline.com/news/659623/lance-gross-is-married-actor-says-i-do-to-fiancee-rebecca-jefferson-in-los-angeles|title=Lance Gross Says I Do To Rebecca Jefferson|author1=Vulpo, Mike  |author2=Cooper, Jennifer|publisher=Eonline.com|date=May 23, 2015|accessdate=December 18, 2015}}</ref> The couple are parents to two kids, one daughter, Berkeley Brynn Gross (born November 20, 2014),<ref>{{cite magazine|url=http://www.essence.com/2015/01/11/lance-gross-reveals-first-photo-newborn-daughter-berkeley|title=Lance Gross Reveals Newborn Daughter|author=Penn, Charlie|magazine=Essence.com|date=January 11, 2015|accessdate=December 18, 2015}}</ref> and one son, Lennon Lorin Gross (born July 10, 2018).
