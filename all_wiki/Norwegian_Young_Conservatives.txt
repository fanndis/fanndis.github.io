'''Norwegian Young Conservatives''' (Norwegian: '''Unge Høyres Landsforbund''', '''UHL''', normally referred to as '''Unge Høyre''') is a [[Norway|Norwegian]] [[youth wing|youth party]]. Its ideology is [[liberal conservatism]]. 
 
The party has 5567 members as of 2013.<ref>
