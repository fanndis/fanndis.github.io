The '''[[Golden Globe Award]] for Best Original Song''' was awarded for the first time in 1962 and has been awarded annually since [[1965 in film|1965]] by the [[Hollywood Foreign Press Association]]. The award is presented to the songwriters of a song written specifically for a motion picture.<ref>[http://www.goldenglobes.com/winners-nominees/best-original-song-motion-picture Winners & Nominees Best Original Song – Motion Picture at the Golden Globe Awards website]</ref> The performers of the song are not credited, unless they also have a writing or co-writing credit.
