'''Hello Sunshine''' is an American [[media company]] founded by American actress [[Reese Witherspoon]] and Strand Equity Founder and Managing Partner Seth Rodsky in 2016. '''Pacific Standard''', the production company Witherspoon co-founded with [[Bruna Papandrea]] in 2012, now serves as a subsidiary for Hello Sunshine.<ref name="deadline">{{cite web|last1=Busch|first1=Anita|title=Reese Witherspoon, Otter Media Pact For New Company Hello Sunshine|url=https://deadline.com/2016/11/reese-witherspoon-otter-media-new-company-hello-sunshine-1201858003/|website=Deadline}}</ref>

On July 10, 2018, its co-owner [[AT&T]] through [[AT&T Communications|its communications division]] announced the launch of a Hello Sunshine branded cable and satellite television channel '''Hello Sunshine Channel''' focused on women similar to Oprah Winfrey Network.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://seekingalpha.com/pr/17211694-t-and-reese-witherspoons-hello-sunshine-announce-launch-of-vod-channel-spotlighting-female|title=AT&T and Reese Witherspoon's Hello Sunshine Announce Launch of VOD Channel Spotlighting Female Creators and Their Stories|website=Seeking Alpha|language=en|access-date=2020-01-15}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2018/digital/news/reese-witherspoon-shine-on-with-reese-hello-sunshine-att-directv-1202868192/|title=Reese Witherspoon Interview Series to Kick Off Her Hello Sunshine Channel Debut on AT&T, DirecTV|last=Spangler|first=Todd|date=2018-07-10|website=Variety|language=en|access-date=2020-01-15}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2018/07/reese-witherspoon-shine-on-series-hello-sunshine-directv-u-verse-channel-1202423660/|title=Reese Witherspoon To Host 'Shine On' Female Profile Series For Hello Sunshine VOD Channel On DirecTV With AT&T|last=Patten|first=Dominic|date=2018-07-10|website=Deadline|language=en|access-date=2020-01-15}}</ref><ref>http://start.att.net/exclusive/hello-sunshine/shine-on?partner=LinkShare&siteId=TnL5HPStwNw-YqaEOa6CaElEt0SdrjfLFw&source=ECay0000000CEL00O</ref>

==History==
In 2000, Witherspoon founded production company Type A Films, which she merged with [[Bruna Papandrea]]'s Make Movies banner to create a new production company entitled Pacific Standard in 2012.<ref>{{cite news|date=March 18, 2012 |last=Kit |first=Borys |title=Reese Witherspoon, Bruna Panadrea Merge Production Companies to Create Pacific Standard |work=Hollywood Reporter |url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/reese-witherspoon-papandrea-pacific-standard-297776 }}</ref> Together, they produced the Oscar-nominated films ''[[Gone Girl (film)|Gone Girl]]'' and ''[[Wild (2014 film)|Wild]]'', and the [[HBO]] drama series ''[[Big Little Lies (miniseries)|Big Little Lies]]''. In 2016, Witherspoon and Papandrea split up, with Witherspoon gaining full control over the company.<ref name="parting">{{cite web|last1=Fleming|first1=Mike, Jr.|title='Gone Girl' Producers Reese Witherspoon & Bruna Papandrea Parting|url=https://deadline.com/2016/09/reese-witherspoon-bruna-papandrea-split-pacific-standard-gone-girl-1201812345/|website=Deadline}}</ref>

In November 2016, Witherspoon, Seth Rodsky and [[Otter Media]] formed Hello Sunshine, a joint venture focused on telling female-driven stories on film, TV and digital platforms. Pacific Standard has become a subsidiary of the newer company.<ref name="deadline" /> The company's CEO is media and tech industry executive Kerry Tucker.<ref name="forbes">{{cite web|last1=Robehmed|first1=Natalie|title=Reese Witherspoon Launches New Multimedia Venture With Peter Chernin And AT&T|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/natalierobehmed/2016/11/21/reese-witherspoon-launches-new-production-venture-with-peter-chernin-at-att/#44c3ca8d7fb9|website=Forbes}}</ref>
 
In addition to producing content, Hello Sunshine also curates a book club called ''Reese's Book Club x Hello Sunshine''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://hello-sunshine.com/book-club|title=Hello Sunshine|website=Hello Sunshine|language=en|access-date=2018-07-20}}</ref> Witherspoon announces a new book pick every month on her Instagram and the book club is aimed to bring women's stories forward. The book club has over 500,000 followers on Instagram, and more than 70,000 followers on Facebook. Hello Sunshine also had a Filmmaker Lab to teach 20 girls aged 13â€“18 the art of film making.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://fresh-films.com/ATThellosunshine/index.php|title=Fresh Films {{!}} Programs|website=fresh-films.com|access-date=2018-07-20}}</ref>

On social media platforms [[Facebook Watch]] and [[IGTV]], Hello Sunshine has released three short-form unscripted series focussing on female successes. In April 2018, Hello Sunshine launched the podcast ''How It Is'', hosted by American actress [[Diane Guerrero]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ew.com/podcasts/2018/04/25/reese-witherspoon-producing-podcast-how-it-is/|title=Reese Witherspoon to produce new podcast 'How It Is', hosted by Diane Guerrero|website=EW.com|language=en|access-date=2020-01-15}}</ref> A second season of the podcast launched in October 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/reese-witherspoons-hello-sunshine-launch-female-podcast-network-1139898|title=Reese Witherspoon's Hello Sunshine to Launch Female-Centric Podcast Network|website=The Hollywood Reporter|language=en|access-date=2020-01-15}}</ref> In November 2018, the third season of ''How It Is'' premiered with [[Kelly McCreary]] taking over as host.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://art19.com/shows/how-it-is|title=How It Is|website=ART19|access-date=2019-02-22}}</ref>

In July 2018, Hello Sunshine launched the talk show [[Shine On with Reese]] on [[DirecTV]] hosted by Witherspoon in her first unscripted series. The show presents one-on-one interviews between Witherspoon and various female guests focussing on how they achieved their dreams.

In October 2018, Hello Sunshine launched a new podcast titled ''My Best Break-Up'' which is hosted by Irish comedian [[Maeve Higgins]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://art19.com/shows/my-best-break-up|title=My Best Break-Up|website=ART19|access-date=2019-02-22}}</ref>

In November 2018, Hello Sunshine partnered with ''Together Live'' to create the tour ''Hello Sunshine x Together Live.'' The tour went to ten different cities across the United States and featured multiple guest speakers telling their story including [[Halima Aden]], [[MILCK]], [[Yara Shahidi]], [[Uma Thurman]], [[Nicole Byer]], [[Cameron Esposito]], [[Sonequa Martin-Green]] and [[Sophia Bush]]. Highlights from the tour will be featured in the third forthcoming podcast ''And Especially You'' which will be hosted by guest speaker Sophia Bush.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://hello-sunshine.com/and-especially-you|title=And Especially You {{!}} Hello Sunshine|website=Hello Sunshine|language=en|access-date=2019-02-22}}</ref>

In February 2019, Hello Sunshine received an investment from [[Laurene Powell Jobs]]' [[Emerson Collective]] with Otter Media has also increased its stake in the company.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2019/02/laurene-powell-jobs-emerson-collective-reese-witherspoon-hello-sunshine-otter-media-funding-infusion-1202560936/|title=Laurene Powell Jobs' Emerson Collective Buys Stake In Reese Witherspoon's Hello Sunshine; Otter Media Ups Its Stake|last1=Fleming|first1=Mike, Jr.|date=2019-02-20|website=Deadline|language=en|access-date=2019-02-22}}</ref>

===Upcoming projects===
Witherspoon and [[Bruna Papandrea]] will continue to produce the projects they have in development together including ''[[Luckiest Girl Alive]]'' at Lionsgate/Summit, ''All Is Not Forgotten'' at Warner Bros, and ''Ashley's War'' at Fox 2000. They also will continue to work together under Pacific Standard's overall deal at ABC.<ref name="parting" />

In May 2017, it was reported Hello Sunshine has set up to produce two novels, ''[[Eleanor Oliphant is Completely Fine]]'' by [[Gail Honeyman]] and ''Something in the Water'' by [[Catherine Steadman]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2017/05/reese-witherspoon-hello-sunshine-eleanor-oliphant-is-completely-fine-something-in-the-water-1202090140/|title=Reese Witherspoon's Hello Sunshine Sets Two Novels For Screen|last1=Fleming|first1=Mike, Jr.|website=Deadline}}</ref> Furthermore, it was announced that Hello Sunshine was filming a documentary about [[Martina Navratilova]], one of the best tennis players of all time.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://deadline.com/2018/05/reese-witherspoon-documentary-martina-navratilova-1202391557/|title=Reese Witherspoon Producing Documentary On Tennis Legend Martina Navratilova|last=N'Duka|first=Amanda|date=2018-05-15|work=Deadline|access-date=2018-07-20|language=en-US}}</ref>

Hello Sunshine has acquired film rights to the novel The Gilded Years. [[Zendaya]] has signed to star in the adaption which will be called ''A White Lie'' and Witherspoon will star and produce ''Legally Blonde 3'' which will premiere on Valentine's Day 2020''.''<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://deadline.com/2017/11/zendaya-a-white-lie-reese-witherspoons-karin-tanabe-hello-sunshine-anita-hemmings-monica-beletsky-1202207512/|title=Zendaya To Star In 'A White Lie,' Pitch Package From Reese Witherspoon's Hello Sunshine|last1=Fleming|first1=Mike, Jr.|date=2017-11-14|work=Deadline|access-date=2018-07-20|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2018/film/news/legally-blonde-3-release-date-valentines-day-2020-reese-witherspoon-1202836355/|title=Reese Witherspoon's 'Legally Blonde 3' to Open Valentine's Day 2020|last=McNary|first=Dave|date=2018-06-07|website=Variety|language=en|access-date=2019-02-22}}</ref> In November 2018, it was announced that Hello Sunshine will produce the novel League of Wives as a feature film with [[Fox 2000 Pictures|Fox 2000]] but due to the dissolvement of Fox 2000 following the [[acquisition of 21st Century Fox by Disney]], the fate of the project is unknown as of February 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2018/11/fox-2000-reese-witherspoon-hello-sunshine-league-of-wives-movie-1202497732/|title=Fox 2000 & Reese Witherspoon's Hello Sunshine Team On 'League Of Wives' Adaptation|last=N'Duka|first=Amanda|date=2018-11-07|website=Deadline|language=en|access-date=2019-02-22}}</ref>
