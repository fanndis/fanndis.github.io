'''Horizons Ventures Limited''' is a [[venture capital]] firm based in [[Hong Kong]] with a focus on disruptive and technology-focused start-ups.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.ejinsight.com/20141010-horizons-ventures-financial-technology/|title=Horizons Ventures eyes investment in financial technology|date=2014-10-10|work=EJ Insight|access-date=2017-07-13|language=en-US}}</ref>  It manages the private investment of Sir [[Li Ka-shing]] in the technology, media and telecommunications sector (TMT sector).<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.chinamoneynetwork.com/2014/10/15/li-ka-shings-horizons-ventures-invests-in-impossible-foods|title=Li Ka-Shing's Horizons Ventures Invests In Impossible Foods|date=2014-10-15|website=China Money Network|access-date=2017-07-13}}</ref>  Investments include [[DeepMind]], (later purchased by [[Google]]), [[Dollar Shave Club]] (purchased by [[Unilever]]), [[Siri]] (purchased by [[Apple Inc.|Apple]]), [[Facebook]], [[Spotify]], [[Impossible Foods]] and [[Zoom Video Communications]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Zoom investment shines light on woman behind Li Ka-shingâ€™s tech bets |url=https://www.ft.com/content/63eb4916-7fd2-4a87-a18d-010533241a75 |work=Financial Times |date=2020-06-20 |access-date=2020-07-24 }}</ref>
