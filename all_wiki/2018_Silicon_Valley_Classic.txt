The '''2018 Silicon Valley Classic''' (also known as the '''Mubadala Silicon Valley Classic''' for sponsorship reasons) was a professional women's [[tennis]] tournament played on [[Tennis court#Hard courts|hard courts]]. It was the 47th edition of the [[Silicon Valley Classic|tournament]], and part of the [[WTA Premier tournaments]] of the [[2018 WTA Tour]]. It took place between 30 July and 5 August 2018 and was the first time the tournament is held in [[San Jose, California]], following a move from [[Stanford, California|Stanford]].<ref>{{cite web | url=http://tennis.life/2018/01/29/stanford-moves-to-san-jose-for-2018/ | title=Stanford moves to San Jose for 2018 | accessdate=14 March 2018}}</ref> It is the first women's event on the [[2018 US Open Series]].
