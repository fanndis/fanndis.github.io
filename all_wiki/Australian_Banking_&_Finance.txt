'''''Australian Banking & Finance Magazine''''' is a [[trade magazine]] of the Australian banking and finance community, providing coverage of the financial services sector, with a 30,000-strong readership.{{Citation needed|date=February 2011}}

It is produced by [[Australian Financial Publications]], an Australian financial industry media company that produce, manage and deliver magazines, conferences, forums, briefings and custom and online communications.{{Citation needed|date=February 2011}}

The magazine is published monthly in print format, with eleven editions a year. A daily online newsletter was established in 2010 providing coverage of day-to-day events in the banking and finance industry.
