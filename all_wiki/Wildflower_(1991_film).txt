'''''Wildflower''''' is a 1991 television film directed by [[Diane Keaton]] and starring [[Beau Bridges]], [[Susan Blakely]], [[Patricia Arquette]] and [[Reese Witherspoon]]. It is based on Sara Flanigan's book ''Alice''.<ref>[http://www.allrovi.com/movies/movie/wildflower-v54626 Allrovi.com]</ref>

==Cast==
*[[Beau Bridges]] as Jack Perkins
*[[Susan Blakely]] as Ada Guthrie
*[[William McNamara]] as Sammy Perkins
*[[Reese Witherspoon]] as Ellie Perkins
*[[Patricia Arquette]] as Alice Guthrie
*[[Bessie Morgan]] as Collin Wilcox
