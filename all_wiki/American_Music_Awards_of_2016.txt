The '''44th Annual [[American Music Awards]]''' took place on November 20, 2016, at the [[Microsoft Theater]] in [[Los Angeles]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://edition.cnn.com/2016/11/21/entertainment/american-music-awards-winners-2016-list/index.html|title= 2016 American Music Awards top moments and winners|date=21 November 2016|work=CNN|accessdate=21 November 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2016/nov/21/american-music-awards-anti-trump-sentiment-peppers-pops-timid-party|title=American Music Awards 2016: anti-Trump sentiment peppers pop's timid party|date=21 November 2016|work=Guardian|accessdate=21 November 2016}}</ref> It was broadcast on [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] and was hosted by [[Gigi Hadid]] and [[Jay Pharoah]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/7565611/gigi-hadid-jay-pharoah-to-host-2016-american-music-awards|title=Gigi Hadid & Jay Pharoah to Host 2016 American Music Awards|date=November 4, 2016|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|accessdate=November 27, 2017}}</ref> The 2016 AMAs fell 31% from 2015 among adults 18-49 and 26% among viewers, to 8.2 million watching the show, which was an all-time low in the show's history.<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://deadline.com/2016/11/packers-redskins-sunday-night-football-ratings-fall-american-music-awards-down-drake-nbc-nfl-abc-1201858012/ |title=American Music Awards Ratings Hit All-Time Low, ‘Sunday Night Football’ Falls |date=November 21, 2016 |work=Variety}}</ref>
