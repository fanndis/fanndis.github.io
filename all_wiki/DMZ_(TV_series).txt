'''''DMZ''''' is an upcoming American [[Dystopia|dystopian]] television miniseries, based on the [[DMZ (comics)|comic book of the same name]].

==Premise==
Alma, a medic becomes a symbol of hope in a demilitarized [[Manhattan island]] while trying to also find her lost son during the [[Second American Civil War]].

==Cast==
* [[Rosario Dawson]] as Alma
* [[Benjamin Bratt]] as Parco Delgado
* [[Freddy Miyares]] as Skel
* [[Hoon Lee]]
* Jordan Preston Carter

==Production==
===Development===
In February 2014, it was announced that [[Syfy]] is planning on making a TV series adaptation of the comic with former ''[[Mad Men]]'' writers and executive producers Andre and [[Maria Jacquemetton]], with ''[[Harry Potter]]'' and ''[[Gravity (2013 film)|Gravity]]'' producer [[David Heyman]] as executive producer for the potential series.<ref>{{cite web|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|title=Series Adaptation Of DC Comic 'DMZ' From 'Mad Men's Andre & Maria Jacquemetton, 'Gravity's David Heyman Set Up At Syfy|url=http://www.deadline.com/2014/02/dmz-syfy-series-dc-comics-david-hayman-jacquemettons/|date=February 5, 2014|work=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|access-date=January 15, 2020}}</ref> On October 1, 2019, it was announced that [[HBO Max]] is developing a TV series adaptation of the comic with [[Ava DuVernay]] directing the pilot with [[Roberto Patino]] writing the pilot and as showrunner the series.<ref name="HBOmaxseries">{{cite web|last=Patten|first=Dominic|title=Ava DuVernay Teams With HBO Max For 'DMZ' Pilot; DC Comic Depicts New American Civil War|url=https://deadline.com/2019/10/ava-duvernay-hbo-max-pilot-dmz-roberto-patino-1202749125/|date=October 1, 2019|work=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|access-date=January 15, 2020}}</ref>
On November 19, 2020, HBO Max gave ''DMZ'' a limited series order. The limited series will consist of 4 episodes. Patino is set to write all of the episodes.<ref name="SeriesOrder">{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2020/11/dmz-hbo-max-series-order-ava-duvernay-and-roberto-patino-dc-comics-1234618836/|title='DMZ': HBO Max Hands Series Order To Ava DuVernay & Roberto Patino's Futuristic Civil War Drama, Based On DC Comic|last=White|first=Peter|work=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|date=November 19, 2020|access-date=November 19, 2020}}</ref>

===Casting===
On January 22, 2020, [[Rosario Dawson]] was cast as Alma.<ref>{{cite web|last=Porter|first=Rick|title=Rosario Dawson to Star in Ava DuVernay's 'DMZ' at HBO Max|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/rosario-dawson-star-ava-duvernays-hbo-max-pilot-1272012|date=January 22, 2020|work=[[The Hollywood Reporter]]|access-date=January 22, 2020}}</ref> On February 6, 2020, [[Benjamin Bratt]] was cast as Parco Delgado.<ref>{{cite web|last=D'Alessandro|first=Anthony|title=DC Comics 'DMZ': Benjamin Bratt Joins HBO Max Pilot From Ava DuVernay|url=https://deadline.com/2020/02/dc-comics-hbo-max-dmz-benjamin-bratt-joins-ava-duvernay-series-1202853205/|date=February 6, 2020|work=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|access-date=February 10, 2020}}</ref> On February 18, 2020, [[Freddy Miyares]] was cast as Skel.<ref>{{cite web|last=Ramos|first=Dino-Ray|title=HBO Max Pilot 'DMZ' Adds 'When They See Us' Star Freddy Miyares|url=https://deadline.com/2020/02/hbo-max-pilot-dmz-when-they-see-us-star-freddy-miyares-rosario-dawson-1202862291/|date=February 18, 2020|work=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|access-date=March 25, 2020}}</ref> Upon the limited series order announcement, [[Hoon Lee]] and Jordan Preston Carter joined the main cast.<ref name="SeriesOrder"/>

===Filming===
On October 1, 2019, the pilot was scheduled to go into production in early 2020,<ref name="HBOmaxseries"/> with filming on the pilot wrapping on March 16, 2020.<ref>{{cite tweet|title=That's a wrap. Thanks to the mighty crew and cast of #DMZ, led by the magnificent @RosarioDawson. Had an absolute blast!|number=1239434299885191169|user=ava|first=Ava|last=DuVernay|date=March 15, 2020|access-date=November 19, 2020|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200316061911if_/https://twitter.com/ava/status/1239434299885191169|archive-date=March 16, 2020}}</ref> Filming for the rest of the episodes is scheduled to begin in 2021.<ref name="SeriesOrder"/>
