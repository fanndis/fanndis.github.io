The '''Committee of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine on issues of European integration''' ({{lang-uk|Комітет Верховної Ради України з питань європейської інтеграції}}, {{lang|uk|Komitet Verkhovnoi Rady Ukrainy z pytan’ yevropeis’koi intehratsii}}) is a [[Committees of the Verkhovna Rada|standing committee]] of the [[Verkhovna Rada]], [[Ukraine]]'s [[unicameral]] parliament. The committee consists of 14 [[People's Deputy of Ukraine|people's deputies]] in the parliament's [[8th Ukrainian Verkhovna Rada|8th convocation]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Committees of the Verkhovna Rada of the VIII convocation|url=http://w1.c1.rada.gov.ua/pls/site2/p_komitis|website=Official portal|publisher=[[Verkhovna Rada|Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine]]|accessdate=December 10, 2014|language=Ukrainian}}</ref> Its entire composition was approved on December 4, 2014.

It was created on September 8, 2003 during the fourth convocation of the Verkhovna Rada.<ref>{{Cite Ukrainian law|type=[[Verkhovna Rada|Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine]] decree|number=867|law=867/03-рг|name=Про предмети відання комітетів Верховної Ради України (About the competence of parliamentary committees of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine)|date=September 8, 2003}}</ref> The committee was created to coordinate [[Ukraine–European Union relations|cooperation between Ukraine and the European Union]] in terms of the country's ongoing [[European integration]] as part of the [[Ukraine–European Union Association Agreement]].

==Scope==
The Committee of the Verkhovna Rada on issues of European integration's scope is recognized as follows:<ref>{{Cite Ukrainian law|type=[[Verkhovna Rada|Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine]] decree|number=2-VIII|law=22-19|name=Про перелік, кількісний склад і предмети відання комітетів Верховної Ради України восьмого скликання (On the list, the number of members, and competence of the committees of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine of the eighth convocation)|date=December 4, 2014}}</ref>

{{col-begin}}
{{col-break|width=50%}}

* Ukraine's participation in international integration processes related to the activities of the EU;
* adaptation of Ukrainian legislation to EU standards to ensure its compliance with the obligations of Ukraine within the [[Council of Europe]];
* assessment of the legislation with international legal obligations of Ukraine in European integration;
* state policy in the field of European integration;

{{col-break|width=50%}}

* parliamentary affairs in cooperation Ukraine, the EU, and the [[Western European Union]];
* coordination of technical assistance of the EU to the Parliament of Ukraine and special education programs;
* consent to be bound by international treaties of Ukraine and the EU and its [[Member state of the European Union|member states]] (ratification, accession, preparation of an international treaty), and denouncing of these treaties;
* cross-border and interregional cooperation with the EU;

{{col-end}}

==Subcommittees==
The Committee of the Verkhovna Rada on issues of European integration consists of the following subcommittees:<ref>{{cite web|title=Information on the activities of the committee|url=http://comeuroint.rada.gov.ua/komevroint/control/uk/publish/article?art_id=57262&cat_id=49366|website=Committee of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine on issues of European integration|publisher=[[Verkhovna Rada|Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine]]|accessdate=10 December 2014|language=Ukrainian|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141211020451/http://comeuroint.rada.gov.ua/komevroint/control/uk/publish/article?art_id=57262&cat_id=49366|archive-date=11 December 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref>

{{col-begin}}
{{col-break|width=50%}}

* Subcommittee on information support of integration processes;
* Subcommittee on adaptation of the Ukrainian legislation to the EU to ensure its compliance with the obligations of Ukraine within the Council of Europe and conformity assessment laws with international legal obligations of Ukraine in European integration;
* Subcommittee on migrant workers and national cultural cooperation;
* Subcommittee on coordination of cooperation between Ukraine and the EU in the field of agriculture;
* Subcommittee on cooperation with [[NATO]] and the Inter-Parliamentary Conference on the EU's [[Common Security and Defence Policy|Common Security and Defense Policy]] on the [[Eastern Partnership]] and [[Euronest Parliamentary Assembly|Euronest PA]];

{{col-break|width=50%}}

* Subcommittee on political dialogue, human contacts, and cooperation between Ukraine and the EU in justice and home affairs, and the coordination of technical assistance;
* Subcommittee on economic and sectoral cooperation between Ukraine and the EU;
* Subcommittee on subregional and cross-border cooperation between Ukraine and the EU;
* Subcommittee on coordination of cooperation between Ukraine and the EU in preventing and combating transnational crime;
* Subcommittee on [[Deep and Comprehensive Free Trade Area|DCFTA]] between Ukraine and the EU cooperation in the field of investment policy.

{{col-end}}
