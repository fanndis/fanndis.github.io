'''Philippe Goffin''' (born 1 April 1967) is a Belgian politician who served as Minister of Foreign Affairs and Minister of Defence from 30 November 2019 to 1 October 2020.<ref name="RTBF 30-11-2019">{{cite web|url=https://www.rtbf.be/info/belgique/detail_philippe-goffin-succede-a-didier-reynders-aux-affaires-etrangeres-et-a-la-defense?id=10377853|title=Philippe Goffin succède à Didier Reynders aux Affaires étrangères et à la Défense|publisher=RTBF.be|date=30 November 2019|language=fr}}</ref>

==Political career==
In the [[Belgian federal elections of 26 May 2019|federal elections of 26 May 2019]], he ran in the [[Province of Liège]], in 3rd place on the [[Reformist Movement]] (MR) list led by [[Daniel Bacquelaine]]. He was re-elected to the House with 14,717 votes.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://elections2019.belgium.be/fr/votes-nominatifs?el=CK&id=CKC62063&party=328|title=Votes nominatifs|work=elections2019.belgium.be|date=24 June 2019|language=fr}}</ref> On 12 November 2019, following the departure of [[Charles Michel]] from the presidency of the MR, he stood in the internal elections of the party but failed in the first round.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rtbf.be/info/belgique/detail_presidence-du-mr-denis-ducarme-affrontera-jean-louis-bouchez-lors-d-un-deuxieme-tour?id=10364081|title=Présidence du MR: Philippe Goffin soutiendra Georges-Louis Bouchez au deuxième tour|publisher=RTBF.be|date=12 November 2019|language=fr}}</ref>

On 30 November 2019, he entered the federal government in current affairs as Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defence, replacing [[Didier Reynders]], who became a member of the European Commission headed by [[Ursula von der Leyen]], holding the Justice portfolio.<ref name="RTBF 30-11-2019"/>

In August 2020, Goffin said he welcomed the [[Israel–United Arab Emirates peace agreement|peace agreement]] between [[Israel]] and the [[United Arab Emirates]] as a step toward a peaceful Middle East and added that the suspension of Israel's [[Proposed Israeli annexation of the West Bank|annexation plans]] must be followed up with the [[two-state solution]].<ref>{{Cite news|title= Belgium welcomes normalisation of relations between UAE, Israel|url= https://www.wam.ae/en/details/1395302862321|date=14 August 2020|agency=Emirati News Agency}}</ref>
