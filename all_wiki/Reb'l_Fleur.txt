'''Reb'l Fleur''' is the first [[perfume|fragrance]] for women endorsed by [[Barbadians|Barbadian]] singer [[Rihanna]]. The fragrance was promoted with the accompanying [[tagline]] "Bad feels so good". Reb'l Fleur was released in the United States on January 25, 2011, and in the United Kingdom on August 19, 2011. The product was positively received upon its release,<ref name="Metro"/> and it became highly successful.<ref name="RS"/>

==Background==
In 2009, [[Jay-Z]] signed a deal with Parlux Fragrances that involved launching scents for [[Kanye West]] and Rihanna.<ref name="MTV2">{{cite web |last=Alwill |first=Cara |date=April 8, 2009 |title=Kanye West, Rihanna Releasing Fragrances Via Jay-Z Deal |url=http://www.mtv.com/news/articles/1608803/kanye-west-rihanna-releasing-fragrances.jhtml |work=[[MTV]] |accessdate=April 14, 2012}}</ref> In reference to the deal, Jay-Z stated "We are excited for the opportunity to partner with Parlux and their management team in the launch of these dynamic new brands".<ref name="MTV2"/>

According to Frederick E. Purches, the founder of Parlux Fragrances, when perfume designers first met with Rihanna, they commented on her "unique scent".<ref name="NYTimes">{{cite news |title=For Rihanna’s Perfume, a Video Meant for Rewatching |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2011/02/10/business/media/10adco.html |work=[[The New York Times]] |accessdate=July 16, 2012 |date=February 9, 2011}}</ref> Rihanna disclosed to the designers that she had created it by mixing and layering five different scents.<ref name="NYTimes"/> The perfume designers then analyzed the five fragrances and incorporated them into the final formula for the Reb'l Fleur fragrance.<ref name="NYTimes"/>

==Promotion==
The official promotional and interactive video for Reb'l Fleur was directed by Anthony Mandler and released on February 11, 2011.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rihannareblfleur.com/ |title=Reb'l Fleur by Rihanna |work=Rihannareblfleur.com |publisher=Parlux Fragrances |accessdate=February 26, 2011}}</ref> The commercial features Rihanna taking a walk through a garden maze and interacting with a "tuxedo-clad seducer."<ref name="MTV">{{cite web |last=Dinh |first=James |date=February 11, 2011 |title=Rihanna's Reb'l Fleur Spot: How Does It Compare To Other Pop-Star Fragrance Ads? |url=http://www.mtv.com/news/articles/1657745/rihanna-rebl-fleur-ad.jhtml |work=[[MTV]] |accessdate=April 14, 2012}}</ref> James Dinh from ''[[MTV]]'' reacted positively to the commercial, which had a duration of two minutes, lauding Rihanna for "creating an ultra-sexy and sleek atmosphere".<ref name="MTV"/> The British daily newspaper [[Metro (British newspaper)|Metro]] felt the ad showcased Rihanna "showing off both her saucy and innocent sides."<ref name="Metro">{{cite web |title=Writhing Rihanna stars in sexy Reb'l Fleur perfume advert |url=http://www.metro.co.uk/lifestyle/855300-writhing-rihanna-stars-in-sexy-rebl-fleur-perfume-advert |work=[[Metro (British newspaper)|Metro]] |accessdate=April 14, 2012}}</ref>

Billboards advertising the fragrance were scheduled for month-long runs; one began on February 7, 2011, on [[Sunset Boulevard]] in [[Los Angeles]], while another began running in [[Times Square]] on February 14, 2011.<ref name="NYTimes"/> Beginning in April, 2011, an edited 30-second version of the promotional video for Reb'l Fleur was scheduled to be screened in 363 movie theaters for three months.<ref name="NYTimes"/> Representatives for the fragrance had planned to hand moviegoers scented cards as they exited the theaters.<ref name="NYTimes"/>

Rihanna made an in-store appearance at the Macy's Department Store in [[Lakewood, California]], for the official launch of Reb'l Fleur on February 18, 2011.<ref>{{cite news |title=Rihanna's Style Evolution |url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2012/07/16/rihanna-river-island_n_1677755.html#slide=705535 |work=[[The Huffington Post]] |accessdate=July 16, 2012 |first=Julee |last=Wilson |date=July 16, 2012}}</ref> The fragrance was also heavily marketed via social media platforms such as [[Facebook]]<ref>{{cite web |title=Rihanna Fragrances on Facebook |url=http://www.facebook.com/FragrancesbyRiRi/ |work=[[Facebook]] |accessdate=February 26, 2011}}</ref> and [[Twitter]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Rihanna Fragrances on Twitter |url=https://twitter.com/perfumesbyriri |work=[[Twitter]] |accessdate=February 26, 2011}}</ref> On August 19, 2011, Rihanna officially launched the perfume in the [[United Kingdom]] at the [[House of Fraser]].<ref>{{cite web |last=Lazarine |first=Devine |date=August 19, 2011 |title=Rihanna Launches Fragrance in the U.K. |url=http://www.rap-up.com/2011/08/19/rihanna-launches-fragrance-in-the-uk/ |work=[[Rap-Up]] |accessdate=August 19, 2011}}</ref>

Reb'l Fleur was officially released in the United States on January 25, 2011.<ref name="People"/> It was exclusively released at [[Macy's]] Department Stores.<ref>{{cite web |title=Reb'l Fleur: Rihanna to Release Signature Scent |url=http://www.thehollywoodgossip.com/2011/01/rebl-fleur-rihanna-to-release-signature-scent/ |work=The Hollywood Gossip |accessdate=July 16, 2012}}</ref> The product is sold in 1.7 and 3.4 fluid ounce bottles at a retail price of $49.00 and $59.00 respectively.<ref name="Parlux">{{cite web |title=Parlux Fragrances, Inc |url=http://www.parlux.com/ |work=Parlux.com |accessdate=February 26, 2011}}</ref> The collection also features a bottle of body lotion, which is sold at a retail price of $30.00.<ref name="Parlux"/> Reb'l Fleur products are also available online through [[Macy's]] official website.<ref>{{cite web |title=Reb'l Fleur by Rihanna Fragrance Collection for Women |url=http://www1.macys.com/shop/product/rebl-fleur-by-rihanna-fragrance-collection-for-women?ID=531396 |work=[[Macy's]] |accessdate=July 16, 2012}}</ref>

===Packaging and scent===
Reb'l Fleur's scent is fresh with ripe fruits and sumptuous florals.<ref name="People"/> It also includes a "musky mix of vanilla, patchouli and amber".<ref name="People"/> The bottle, which was designed to be "as dramatically unique as a Rihanna performance", was inspired by the shape of a [[stiletto heel]] due to Rihanna's fondness for them.<ref name="NYTimes"/><ref name="About"/> The side of the bottle features a twisting matte black "ribbon".<ref name="About"/>

==Reception==
Reb'l Fleur was positively received by critics upon its release and distribution.<ref name="Metro"/> Catherine Helbig from [[About.com]] called the fragrance "sexy and memorable," commenting that "Reb’l Fleur is as much in tune with Barbados-born Rihanna’s roots as it is with the glamour of her present life in New York City."<ref name="About">{{cite web |last=Helbig |first=Catherine |title=Review of Rihanna's Perfume Reb’l Fleur |url=http://beauty.about.com/od/celebrityfragrances/fr/Review-Of-Rihannas-Perfume-Reb-L-Fleur.htm |work=[[About.com]] |accessdate=April 14, 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111007002836/http://beauty.about.com/od/celebrityfragrances/fr/Review-Of-Rihannas-Perfume-Reb-L-Fleur.htm |archive-date=October 7, 2011 |url-status=dead }}</ref> Helbig concluded her review by praising the fragrance bottle, describing it as "edgy" with "an extra touch of glam."<ref name="About"/> Melanie Dee from [[Yahoo!]] called the fragrance "a complete winner in [her] book" and concluded that if "Rihanna keeps pumping out scents this good, I will have a new favorite in the celebrity scent department."<ref name="Yahoo!">{{cite web |last=Dee |first=Melanie |title=Review of Reb'l Fleur by Rihanna, Perfume for Women |url=http://voices.yahoo.com/review-rebl-fleur-rihanna-perfume-7704238.html |work=[[Yahoo!]] |accessdate=April 29, 2012 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140619185910/http://voices.yahoo.com/review-rebl-fleur-rihanna-perfume-7704238.html |archivedate=June 19, 2014 }}</ref> According to ''[[Rolling Stone]]'', Reb'l Fleur was a financial success, expecting to gross a total of US$80 million at retail by the end of 2011. The fragrance has grossed over US$1.4 billion as of 2016.<ref name="RS">{{cite web |last=Nika |first=Colleen |date=November 21, 2011 |title=Rihanna Rebels With New Fragrance, Shocking Shoes |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/blogs/thread-count/rihanna-rebels-with-new-fragrance-shocking-shoes-20111121 |work=[[Rolling Stone]] |accessdate=July 16, 2012}}</ref>
