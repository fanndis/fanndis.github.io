The '''Cabinet Secretary for Infrastructure, Investment and Cities''' was a position in the [[Scottish Government]] [[Cabinet (government)|Cabinet]]. The Cabinet Secretary had responsibilities for [[infrastructure]], [[procurement]], [[transport]], [[Structural Funds and Cohesion Funds|European Structural Funds]], [[Scottish Water]] and [[city|cities]]. The Cabinet Secretary was assisted by the [[Minister for Transport and Islands]].

==History==
The position was created in May 2011 as the '''Cabinet Secretary for Infrastructure and Capital Investment'''.<ref>{{cite press release |url=http://www.scotland.gov.uk/News/Releases/2011/05/19155402 |title=New Scottish Cabinet |publisher=Scottish Government |date=19 May 2011|accessdate=2 September 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-scotland-13461467 |title=At-a-glance: Scottish government cabinet |work=[[BBC News]] |publisher=[[BBC]] |last=Black |first=Andrew |date=20 May 2011|accessdate=3 September 2014}}</ref> The position was renamed as the '''Cabinet Secretary for Infrastructure, Capital Investment and Cities''' in September 2012 then as the '''Cabinet Secretary for Infrastructure, Investment and Cities''' in November 2014. The position was abolished in May 2016, with transport and infrastructure matters moving to the [[Cabinet Secretary for the Rural Economy and Connectivity]].

==Overview==

===Responsibilities===
The responsibilities of the Cabinet Secretary for Infrastructure, Investment and Cities include:
* [[infrastructure]] and [[investment|capital investment]]
* [[Structural Funds and Cohesion Fund|European structural funds]]
* government procurement
* [[water]]
* [[city|cities]]
* [[transport|transport policy]]
* [[public transport]]
* [[air travel]]
* [[rail transport]]
* [[ferry]] services
* [[road]]s
* military veterans
* cross government co-ordination on Scotland's islands

===Public bodies===
The following public bodies report to the Cabinet Secretary for Infrastructure, Investment and Cities:
* [[Caledonian Maritime Assets]]
* [[David MacBrayne|David MacBrayne Ltd]]
* [[Glasgow Prestwick Airport]]
* [[Highlands and Islands Airports|Highlands and Islands Airports Ltd]]
* [[Scottish Futures Trust]]
* [[Scottish Water]]
* [[Transport Scotland]]
* [[Water Industry Commission for Scotland]]
