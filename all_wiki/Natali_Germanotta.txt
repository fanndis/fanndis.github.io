'''Natali Veronica Germanotta''' (born March 10, 1992) is an American fashion designer, stylist, and the younger sister of singer [[Lady Gaga]].

== Early life ==
Germanotta was born on March 10, 1992 in New York City to business executives Joseph Germanotta and [[Cynthia Germanotta|Cynthia Bissett]] and grew up on the [[Upper West Side]] of [[Manhattan]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.teenvogue.com/gallery/natali-germanotta|title=Lady Gaga's Little Sister, Natali Germanotta Tells All|first=Condé|last=Nast|website=Teen Vogue}}</ref> She was raised in the Catholic faith. Germanotta is a younger sister of actress and singer [[Lady Gaga]]. As a teenager, she worked at Joanne Trattoria, an Italian restaurant owned by her parents.<ref name= diply>{{Cite web|url=https://crafty.diply.com/1716/12-facts-about-lady-gagas-mysterious-sister-natali-germanotta|title=12 Facts About Lady Gaga's Mysterious Sister, Natali Germanotta|date=July 3, 2018|website=Diply}}</ref> She attended the [[Convent of the Sacred Heart (New York City)|Convent of the Sacred Heart]], a private all-girls Roman Catholic school located on the [[Upper East Side]].<ref name= diply/><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thelist.com/98064/dont-know-lady-gagas-sister/|title=What you don't know about Lady Gaga's sister|first=Cailyn|last=Cox|website=TheList.com}}</ref> After graduating, she studied fashion at [[Parsons School of Design]], a constituent college of [[The New School]].<ref name= refinery/><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://newyork.cbslocal.com/2011/09/21/gotham-gossipist-lady-gagas-sister-not-always-a-fan-of-singers-wardrobe-choices/|title=Gotham Gossipist: Lady Gaga's Sister Not Always A Fan Of Singer's Wardrobe Choices|date=September 21, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.popsugar.com/celebrity/photo-gallery/36093166/image/36100312/Lady-Gaga-Natali-Germanotta|title=Lady Gaga and Natali Germanotta|first=Britt|last=Stephens|date=August 30, 2019|website=POPSUGAR Celebrity}}</ref>

== Career ==
[[Image:Ashley Biden, Joe Biden, Lady Gaga and Jill Biden.jpg|thumb|right|upright=1.2|Natali Germanotta (second from left) with [[Ashley Biden]], Vice President [[Joe Biden]], [[Lady Gaga]], and Second Lady [[Jill Biden]] in February 2016]]

In February 2016 Germanotta and her sister partnered with [[Mattel]]'s ''[[Monster High]]'', in conjunction with the [[Born This Way Foundation]] as part of the #KindMonsters campaign, to create a doll for the franchise.<ref name= refinery>{{Cite web|url=https://www.refinery29.com/en-us/2016/02/103102/lady-gaga-monster-mattel-doll|title=How Lady Gaga & Her Sister Designed A Monster Doll & Sparked A Kindness Campaign|first=Morgan|last=Baila|website=www.refinery29.com}}</ref>

Germanotta works as a fashion designer, and has designed for and styled her sister, including for a spread in ''[[Harper's Bazaar]]'' in 2014.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.refinery29.com/en-us/2014/08/72140/lady-gaga-wears-sister-natali-germanotta-coat|title=Lady Gaga Wears Sister Natali Germanotta Coat|first=Hilary|last=Moss|website=www.refinery29.com}}</ref> In 2018 she worked as a stylist on set for the film ''[[A Star Is Born (2018 film)|A Star Is Born]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.womenshealthmag.com/life/a28801276/lady-gaga-sister/|title=FYI: Lady Gaga's Sister Appeared In A Music Video With Beyonce|first=Jennifer|last=Nied|date=August 23, 2019|website=Women's Health}}</ref> In 2019 she accompanied her sister to the [[91st Academy Awards]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.oprahmag.com/entertainment/tv-movies/a26470362/lady-gaga-2019-oscars-red-carpet-date/|title=Lady Gaga’s 2019 Oscars Date Might Surprise You|first=Jonathan|last=Borge|date=February 25, 2019|website=Oprah Magazine}}</ref>
