'''Interfax Ltd.''' ({{lang-ru|Интерфакс}}) is a [[Private limited company|privately-held]] independent major [[news agency]] in [[Russia]] (along with state-operated [[Russian News Agency TASS|TASS]] and [[RIA Novosti]]) and information services company [[headquarter]]ed in [[Moscow]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.kommersant.ru/doc.aspx?DocsID=636458 |script-title=ru:''Интеллектуальная собственность'' |author=Воронов А. |date=2005-12-17 |publisher=— [[Kommersant]] |accessdate=2011-01-29 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/684DBaXfH?url=http://www.kommersant.ru/doc/636458 |archivedate=2012-05-31 |url-status=dead |language=ru}}</ref>

== History ==
As the first non-governmental channel of political and economic information about the USSR,<ref>{{Cite web|title=группа "Интерфакс"|url=http://group.interfax.ru/default.asp|access-date=2020-07-09|website=group.interfax.ru}}</ref> Interfax was formed in September 1989, during [[Mikhail Gorbachev]]’s ''[[perestroika]] and [[glasnost]]'' period, by [[Mikhail Komissar]] and his colleagues from [[international broadcasting]] station '[[Radio Moscow]]', a part of Soviet [[Gosteleradio]] system. Interfax originally used [[Fax|fax machines]] for text transmission, hence the company name.<ref name="vestiru">''[http://www.vesti.ru/doc.html?id=314295 Михаил Комиссар: задача «Интерфакса» — быть номером один]''. — TV-channel '[[Russia-24]]', 9 September 2009. {{in lang|ru}}</ref>

By 1990, Interfax had 100 subscribers and the agency quickly began to attract the attention of conservatives within the government, who attempted to shut down the agency. This saw the agency gain prominence in major western media, a position strengthened by its coverage of the [[1991 Soviet coup d'état attempt|1991 August Putsch]] and the [[Dissolution of the Soviet Union|collapse of the Soviet Union]].<ref name="bulletin">Корпоративный бюллетень «[http://www.interfax.ru/ftproot/files/Vash_INT_10.pdf Ваш Интерфакс]». — #10/2009. {{in lang|ru}}</ref>
[[File:Interfax-presscentreMoscow2016.jpg|left|thumb|190px|Interfax Press Center in Moscow (2016)]]
Interfax continued to expand in the 1990s, adding [[subsidiary]] agencies for [[Financial market|financial]], [[Metallurgical industry|metallurgical]], [[Energy industry|oil and gas]] markets, information products on [[agriculture]], [[business law]], [[transport]], [[telecommunications]],<ref name="vestiru" /> and the market data terminal 'EFiR information system' for [[stock market]] players to its general news coverage.<ref name="efir">''[http://www.ideal.ru/text.asp?Rbr=1 About]''. — Official site of EFiR system. {{in lang|ru}}</ref> Interfax also opened subsidiaries across the [[post-Soviet states]], first in Ukraine (1992), Belarus (1993) and Kazakhstan (1996), and later in Azerbaijan (2002),<ref name="history">''[http://group.interfax.ru/history.asp?rbr=9 Our history]''. — Official site of Interfax Group. {{in lang|ru}}</ref> during this time bringing the number of local offices across the [[Subdivisions of Russia|regions of Russian Federation]] to 50.<ref name="politcom">''[http://politcom.ru/3331.html Михаил Комиссар: «Надеюсь, в России установится баланс между либеральным и государственным»]''. — Politcom.ru, 1 September 2006. {{in lang|ru}}</ref>

To promote its information products abroad, Interfax opened its first company outside the former USSR in 1991, when 'Interfax America' was opened in [[Denver]], CO. This was followed by the opening of [[London]]-based 'Interfax Europe Ltd.' (1992), 'Interfax Germany GmbH', based in [[Frankfurt]] (1993), and the 'Interfax News Service Ltd.' in [[Hong Kong]] (1998).<ref name="history" />

In 2004, Interfax created the 'SPARK system' designed to check [[Business manager|business agents]] in Russia, Ukraine and Kazakhstan,<ref name="sparc">''[http://group.interfax.ru/rbr.asp?rbr=34 Информация по юридическим лицам РФ, Украины и Казахстана]''. — Official site of Interfax Group. {{in lang|ru}}</ref> Interfax also launched [[media monitoring]] services through its 'SCAN system'.<ref name="ucb">''[http://www.bki-okb.ru/about?lang=en About]''. — Official site of United Credit Bureau.</ref> In 2011, the company added London-based project, 'Interfax Global Energy', which reports on the [[energy market]]s worldwide.<ref name="ige">''[http://interfaxenergy.com/about Interfax Global Energy]''. — Official site of Interfax Global Energy.</ref>

== Partnerships ==

In 1999, Interfax launched a [[rating agency]] in partnership with '[[Moody's Investors Service]]' to provide rating services on the [[Russian market]].<ref name="history" /> SPARK system became the basis for a [[joint venture]] with a provider of [[Credit risk|business credit information]] and [[credit report]]s '[[Dun & Bradstreet]]'.<ref name="dandb">''[http://group.interfax.ru/rbr.asp?rbr=35 Информация по компаниям всего мира]''. — Official site of Interfax Group. {{in lang|ru}}</ref>

Also in 1999, Interfax and Reuters signed a cooperation agreement.<ref>{{Cite web|date=1999-04-18|title=Новости|url=https://www.kommersant.ru/doc/2286574|access-date=2020-07-09|website=www.kommersant.ru|language=ru}}</ref>

Since 2001 Interfax has collaborated with '[[Business Wire]]', a global leader in the distribution of [[Prospectus (finance)|corporate regulatory disclosures]] and [[press release]]s.<ref name="history" /> Interfax now also offers consumer [[credit report]]s via a joint venture with '[[Experian]]' and '[[Sberbank of Russia]]'.<ref name="ucb">''[http://www.bki-okb.ru/about?lang=en About]''. — Official site of United Credit Bureau.</ref>

== Management == 
'Interfax Group' [[senior management]] includes [[Mikhail Komissar]] as [[Chairman of the Board]] and [[Chief Executive Officer|CEO]], Vladimir Gerasimov, [[Executive Director]] and General Director of Financial and Business Information Service, Georgy Gulia, Executive Director of General Information Service.<ref name="persons">''[http://www.group.interfax.ru/persons.asp?rbr=8&tp=1 Management]''. — Official site of Interfax Group. {{in lang|ru}}</ref>

== Content ==
Interfax provides general and political news, business [[credit information]], industry analysis, [[market data]] and [[Business service provider|business solutions]] for [[Risk management|risk]], compliance and [[Credit manager|credit]] management. The company employs over 1,000 staff in over 70 bureaus worldwide and publishes over 3,000 stories each day.<ref name="about">{{cite web|author=|date=|title=''About Interfax''|url=http://www.interfax.com/txt.asp?rbr=1|accessdate=2016-07-20|publisher=— Interfax Group official site.}}</ref>

== Dissemination ==
Interfax controls around 50% of the Russian [[Organizational memory|corporate data market]].<ref name="rbc">[http://marketing.rbc.ru/reviews/it-business/chapter_3_4.shtml Информационно-справочные системы] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160916101903/http://marketing.rbc.ru/reviews/it-business/chapter_3_4.shtml|date=2016-09-16}}. - [[RBC Information Systems|RBC]].Marketing. {{in lang|ru}}</ref> [[Subscription business model|Subscription-based]] business and [[financial intelligence]] contribute to the bulk of the revenues generated by Interfax with [[Information technology|IT]]-analytical business solutions accounting for approximately 75% of revenue.<ref name="yourinterfax">Комиссар М. ''[http://group.interfax.ru/ftproot/files/Vash_INT_16.pdf Успеть первыми]''. - Корпоративный бюллетень «Ваш Интерфакс», #16/2014. {{in lang|ru}}</ref>

'Interfax [[Holding company|Group]]' comprises more than 30 news producing and marketing companies in [[Russia]], [[Ukraine]], [[Kazakhstan]], [[Belarus]], [[Azerbaijan]], the [[United Kingdom]], the [[United States]], [[Germany]] and [[China]].<ref name="beneficiary">Гавшина О., Товкайло М. ''[https://www.vedomosti.ru/business/articles/2012/01/19/lgota_dlya_smi Из списка подлежащих раскрытию бенефициаров сделали исключения]''. — [[Vedomosti]], January 19, 2012. {{in lang|ru}}</ref>

In 2015, Interfax was awarded the Runet Prize in the category "Culture, Media and Mass Communications".<ref>{{Cite web|title=Итоги «Премии Рунета 2015»|url=https://www.shopolog.ru/news/itogi-premii-runeta-2015/|access-date=2020-07-14|website=SHOPOLOG.RU}}</ref>

In 2016, Interfax was awarded the Runet Prize in the category “Economy, Business and Investment".<ref>{{Cite web|title=SPARK project wins Runet Prize in "Economy, business and investment" nomination|url=https://interfax.com/newsroom/press-releases/1198/|access-date=2020-07-14|website=interfax.com}}</ref>
