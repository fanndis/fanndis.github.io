'''Maria da Conceição de Almeida Tavares''' ([[Anadia, Portugal|Anadia]], April 24, 1930) is a [[Portuguese people|Portuguese]] naturalized [[Brazilians|Brazilian]] economist.<ref>[http://en.mercopress.com/2010/05/11/current-development-model-can-t-continue-warns-one-of-brazil-s-top-economists Current Development Model Can’t Continue, Warns One of Brazil’s top Economists] published by the news agency "MercoPress" on May 11, 2010</ref><ref name=CNPq1>{{cite web|last1=Pereira de Melo e Ligia|first1=Hildete|last2=Rodrigues|first2=M.C.S|title=Pioneiras da Ciência no Brasil|url=http://cnpq.br/pioneiras-da-ciencia-do-brasil|website=cnpq.br|publisher=[[National Council for Scientific and Technological Development]]|accessdate=January 4, 2018|language=pt}}</ref> She is also a titular professor at the [[University of Campinas|State University of Campinas]] (Unicamp) and [[professor emeritus|professor emerita]] of the [[Federal University of Rio de Janeiro]] (UFRJ).

Maria da Conceição is affiliated with the [[Workers' Party (Brazil)|Workers' Party]],<ref>[http://brasildamudanca.com.br/en/bolsa-familia/maria-da-conceicao-tavares-economist-full-professor-unicamp Maria da Conceição Tavares, economist, full professor at Unicamp] published by the "Lula Institute"</ref> and she has been [[Chamber of Deputies|Federal Deputy]] state of Rio de Janeiro between 1995 and 1999, and is the author of several books on economic development.<ref>[http://www.envplan.com/abstract.cgi?id=d030223 Brazil: a difficult present, an urgent future—an interview with Maria da Conceição Tavares] by R Bueno on April 30, 1984(Environment and Planning D: Society and Space 3(2) 223 – 230)</ref>
