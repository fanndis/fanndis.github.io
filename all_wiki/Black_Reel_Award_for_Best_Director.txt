This page lists the winners and nominees for the '''[[Black Reel Award]] for Best Director'''. [[Lee Daniels]] and [[Steve McQueen (director)|Steve McQueen]] are the only directors nominated for Academy Awards. Daniels earned a nomination for ''[[Precious (film)|Precious]]'' and McQueen for ''[[12 Years a Slave (film)|12 Years a Slave]]''.

==Winners and nominees==
Winners are listed first and highlighted in '''bold'''.
