'''''Shine On with Reese''''' is an American [[talk show]] hosted by actress [[Reese Witherspoon]]. The show was announced on July 10, 2018 and had its premiere one week later on [[DirecTV]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://deadline.com/2018/07/reese-witherspoon-shine-on-series-hello-sunshine-directv-u-verse-channel-1202423660/|title=Reese Witherspoon To Host ‘Shine On’ Female Profile Series For Hello Sunshine VOD Channel On DirecTV With AT&T|last=Patten|first=Dominic|date=2018-07-10|work=Deadline|access-date=2018-07-20|language=en-US}}</ref>

''Shine On'' also marks Witherspoon's first unscripted role in her career.

== Premise ==
The show is a one-on-one interview between Witherspoon and a female guest focusing on how she achieved her dreams.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://eu.usatoday.com/story/life/tv/2018/07/20/shine-reese-witherspoon-directv-talk-show-host/801721002/|title=Is Reese Witherspoon our next big talk-show host? She tries it out in|work=USA TODAY|access-date=2018-07-20|language=en}}</ref>

== Background ==
In November 2016, Witherspoon and [[Otter Media]] formed [[Hello Sunshine (company)|Hello Sunshine]], a joint venture focused on telling female-driven stories on film, TV and digital platforms. The company has multiple series and films in development including ''[[Big Little Lies (TV series)|Big Little Lies]]''.

Before the formal announcement, the series was teased by Witherspoon in an interview on ''[[Good Morning America]]'' while promoting [[A Wrinkle in Time (2018 film)|''A Wrinkle in Time'']].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D_ay3yY3BJc|title=Reese Witherspoon says diverse crew for ‘A Wrinkle in Time’ infused the film with love|date=March 8, 2018|work=Good Morning America|accessdate=July 27, 2018}}</ref> The show was announced on July 10, 2018 on Witherspoon's Instagram. The first season will have nine episodes and will air on [[DirecTV]]'s Hello Sunshine channel. All guests on the show were also announced in a press release.
