'''''A Very Gaga Thanksgiving''''' is a [[Thanksgiving]] [[television special]] that originally aired on November 24, 2011, in the United States on the [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] network. Conceived and directed by [[Lady Gaga]], it discusses her personal life and the inspiration behind her music. A 30-second preview of the special premiered on November 20, 2011 on Lady Gaga's [[YouTube]] channel. [[Principal photography]] took place at the [[Convent of the Sacred Heart (New York City)|Convent of the Sacred Heart]] in New York City.

The program features guest appearances by [[Katie Couric]], [[Art Smith (chef)|Art Smith]], and [[Tony Bennett]]. Gaga performed stripped down, acoustic versions of four songs from her second [[studio album]], ''[[Born This Way (album)|Born This Way]]'', along with two Christmas songs and a duet with Bennett. The special received critical acclaim and gave ABC its best ratings in four years for its respective time slot on Thanksgiving Day. At the 2012 [[Dorian Awards]], the special was nominated in the category of Best TV Musical Program of the Year but did not win the award. A live [[extended play|EP]] of performances from the special, titled ''[[A Very Gaga Holiday]]'', was made available for purchase from the [[iTunes Store]]. The EP debuted on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]] albums chart in the United States at number&nbsp;52.

==Reception==

===Ratings===
''A Very Gaga Thanksgiving'' aired on November 24, 2011, in the United States on ABC. It attracted 5.749 million viewers and garnered a 1.8/5 rating in the 18–49 demographic in its first hour. Gaga's special aired simultaneously with reruns of ''[[The Simpsons]]'' on [[Fox Broadcasting Company|FOX]], ''[[Person of Interest (TV series)|Person of Interest]]'' on [[CBS]], and ''[[The Secret Circle (TV series)|The Secret Circle]]'' on [[The CW]].<ref name=ratings2/> In its second hour, total viewership and ratings declined to 5.388 million viewers and a 1.6/5 rating in the 18–49 demographic, despite airing at the same time as a recap of the [[Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade]] on [[NBC]] and a rerun of ''[[The Mentalist]]'' on [[CBS]].<ref name=ratings2/> ''A Very Gaga Thanksgiving'' failed to beat other Thanksgiving specials in the ratings, including ''[[A Charlie Brown Thanksgiving]]''.<ref name="mtv">{{Cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/1675000/lady-gaga-thanksgiving-ratings-charlie-brown/|first=James|last=Montgomery|title=Lady Gaga Bested By Charlie Brown In Thanksgiving Ratings|publisher=[[MTV News]]|date=November 28, 2011|accessdate=November 28, 2011|url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140725174742/http://www.mtv.com/news/1675000/lady-gaga-thanksgiving-ratings-charlie-brown/|archivedate=July 25, 2014}}</ref> The program had the highest rating in the 18–49 demographic in its time slot. Total viewership increased by 23% (1.3 million viewers) from the previous year's Thanksgiving special, singer [[Beyoncé]]'s 2009 concert ''[[I Am... World Tour]]''. It gave ABC its best ratings in four years for the Thanksgiving Day time slot.<ref name=ratings2>{{cite web|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2011/11/25/tv-ratings-thursday-cbs-tops-thanksgiving-night-of-repeats-and-specials-but/111748/|title='A Very Gaga Thanksgiving' Ranks #1 And Improves Its Slot By Wide Margins|first=Bill|last=Gorman|work=[[TV by the Numbers]]|date=November 25, 2011|accessdate=November 25, 2011|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160305075318/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2011/11/25/tv-ratings-thursday-cbs-tops-thanksgiving-night-of-repeats-and-specials-but/111748/|archivedate=March 5, 2016}}</ref><ref name="#1timeslot">{{cite web|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2011/11/25/a-very-gaga-thanksgiving-ranks-1-and-improves-its-slot-by-wide-margins/111777/|title=TV Ratings Thursday: CBS Tops Thanksgiving Night Of Repeats And Specials, But..|first=Bill|last=Gorman|work=TV by the Numbers|date=November 25, 2011|accessdate=November 25, 2011|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111128232619/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2011/11/25/a-very-gaga-thanksgiving-ranks-1-and-improves-its-slot-by-wide-margins/111777/|archivedate=November 28, 2011}}</ref>

On November 22, 2011, [[iTunes Store]] began selling an exclusive EP of the live performances from the special, which included "White Christmas", "Orange Colored Sky", "You and I", and "The Edge of Glory". The EP was made available for purchase only. Titled ''[[A Very Gaga Holiday]]'', it entered the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]] chart at number&nbsp;52, selling 22,000&nbsp;copies, and number nine on the Top Holiday Albums chart.<ref name="moveup">{{cite news|url=http://www.billboard.com/biz/articles/news/1158965/chart-moves-lady-gagas-marry-moves-up-michael-jackson-misses-with-immortal|title=Chart Moves: Lady Gaga's 'Marry' Moves Up, Michael Jackson Misses With 'Immortal', Divas Duke It Out|last=Trust|first=Gary|date=December 1, 2011|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|accessdate=April 24, 2013|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141031112224/http://www.billboard.com/biz/articles/news/1158965/chart-moves-lady-gagas-marry-moves-up-michael-jackson-misses-with-immortal|archivedate=October 31, 2014}}</ref> The same week, the special also increased sales of Gaga's second studio album, ''[[Born This Way (album)|Born This Way]]'', which moved up the ''Billboard'' 200 chart from 72 to 21, with sales of 47,000&nbsp;copies (up 416% from the previous week).<ref name="moveup"/>
