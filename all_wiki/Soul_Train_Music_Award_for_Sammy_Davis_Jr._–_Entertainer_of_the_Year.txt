This page lists the winners and nominees for the '''[[Soul Train Music Award|Soul Train Music]] [[Sammy Davis Jr.]] Award for Entertainer of the Year'''. At times the award was given out twice in a year, honoring male and female artists separately. [[Beyoncé]] was the only artist to have won this award twice in the show's history (once solo and once with [[Destiny's Child]]) until 2009, when [[Michael Jackson]] was posthumously awarded his second Entertainer of the Year.
