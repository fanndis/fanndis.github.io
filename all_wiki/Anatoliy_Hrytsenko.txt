'''Anatoliy Stepanovych Hrytsenko''' ({{lang-uk|Анатолій Степанович Гриценко}}; born 25 October 1957) is a [[Ukraine|Ukrainian]] politician, [[Independent (politician)|independent]]{{#tag:ref|In the Ukrainian context, an MP is considered independent if he/she does not belong to any faction represented in the parliament, hence, not necessarily non-partisan. The [[Ukrainian language|Ukrainian]] term is ''позафракційний'' which literally means ''out of a faction''.|group=nb}} [[People's Deputy of Ukraine|member]] of the [[7th Ukrainian Verkhovna Rada|current]] [[Verkhovna Rada|Ukrainian parliament]], former [[Defence minister|Minister of Defence]], member of the [[Our Ukraine (political party)|Our Ukraine]] political party and leader of the [[Civil Position]] party.<ref name=DATACV>{{in lang|uk}} [http://da-ta.com.ua/mon_mainnews/918.htm Політична партія „Громадянська позиція“], Database DA-TA</ref><ref>[http://www.interfax.com.ua/eng/main/21256/ Western Ukrainian intelligentsia calls on candidates for president to withdraw in favor of Tymoshenko] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120314005853/http://www.interfax.com.ua/eng/main/21256/ |date=March 14, 2012 }}, [[Interfax-Ukraine]] (October 1, 2009)</ref><ref>[http://www.nrcu.gov.ua/index.php?id=148&listid=80493 Civil Position organization has chance to become 160th political party] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101205233049/http://www.nrcu.gov.ua/index.php?id=148&listid=80493 |date=December 5, 2010 }}, [[Radio Ukraine]] (December 11, 2008)</ref>

==Education==
Hrytsenko graduated with honours from [[Kyiv Military Aviation Engineering Academy|Kyiv Higher Military Aviation Engineering School]] on 23 June 1979.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mil.gov.ua/images/event/minob_vvnz.jpg |title=Diploma of Kyiv Military Aviation School |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070321030156/http://www.mil.gov.ua/images/event/minob_vvnz.jpg |archivedate=2007-03-21 }}</ref> On 10 December 1984 he was awarded a [[Candidate of Sciences]] (Ph.D.) degree from Kyiv Higher Military Aviation Engineering School.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mil.gov.ua/images/event/minob_candidat.jpg |title=PhD from Kyiv Military Aviation School |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070321030236/http://www.mil.gov.ua/images/event/minob_candidat.jpg |archivedate=2007-03-21 }}</ref> And in 1993 Hrytsenko graduated from the [[Defense Language Institute]] of the [[United States Department of Defense]]. On 6 June 1994 he graduated from the Resident Program of the [[Air War College|U.S. Air War College]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mil.gov.ua/images/event/minob_usa.jpg |title=Diploma of Air University |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070321030132/http://www.mil.gov.ua/images/event/minob_usa.jpg |archivedate=2007-03-21 }}</ref> And on 30 October 1995 Hrytsenko graduated from the Academy of the [[Armed Forces of Ukraine]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mil.gov.ua/images/event/minob_stnauk.jpg |title=Diploma of Academy of the Armed Forces of Ukraine |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070321030206/http://www.mil.gov.ua/images/event/minob_stnauk.jpg |archivedate=2007-03-21 }}</ref>

Hrytsenko is author of more than 100 scientific papers published in [[Ukraine]], [[Belgium]], the [[Netherlands]], [[United States]], [[Germany]] and [[Switzerland]].<ref name=LIGA>{{in lang|ru}} [http://file.liga.net/person/330-anatolii-gricenko.html Short bio], LIGA</ref>

==Army career==
Hrytsenko served 25 years in the [[Armed Forces of Ukraine]], in combat units, as teacher at the military college and in staff positions in the [[Ministry of Defense of Ukraine]].<ref name=LIGA/>

Hrytsenko's rank was colonel.<ref name=LIGA/>

From December 1999 till 2004 he worked as a military consultant in various firms.<ref name=LIGA/>

In February 2000 Hrytsenko served as Freelance Consultant on the Committee on National Security and Defence of the [[Verkhovna Rada]] (Ukraine's parliament) and in November 2000 as a member of the Public Council of Experts on domestic issues of the [[President of Ukraine]].<ref name=LIGA/>

==Political career==
In 2004 Hrytsenko worked in the election headquarters (for the [[2004 Ukrainian presidential election]]) of [[Viktor Yushchenko]] and took an active part in the writing his election program.<ref name=LIGA/>

===Minister of Defence===
Hrytsenko became [[Defence minister|Minister of Defence]] in the [[First Tymoshenko Government]] in February 2005, he continued this position in the [[Yekhanurov Government]].<ref name=LIGA/> On 4 August 2006 he was appointed again as head of the Ministry of Defence in the [[Alliance of National Unity|government of]] [[Viktor Yanukovych]] on the quota of President Yushchenko.<ref name=LIGA/>

As Minister of Defense, Hrytsenko took up the issues of financing and reforming the [[Armed Forces of Ukraine|Armed Forces]]. In 2005, for the first time in recent years, the army was fully funded. In 2006, its budget grew by 51% and reached 8.9 billion hryvnias. Compared to 2004, in 2005 combat training indicators increased by an average of 40-50%.<ref>{{Cite web | url = https://zn.ua/ARCHIVE/belaya_kniga_s_chernoy_otmetinoy.html | title = «Белая книга» с «чёрной отметиной» | publisher = [[Dzerkalo Tyzhnia]] | author = Volodymyr Kravchenko | date = 24 February 2006 | accessdate = 21 November 2019 | language = ru}}</ref>

In 2007, when Hrytsenko was the Minister of Defense, a military hospital in [[Dnipropetrovsk]] was sold to a private company. After that, the former military facility was given to the Russian [[VTB Bank]], which later received direct control over this hospital. [[Petro Poroshenko]]'s commissioner for the peaceful settlement of the conflict in Donetsk and Lugansk Oblasts stated thet "Today, in fact, the hospital where our guys are being treated belongs to the invader country." In October 2014, this information was confirmed by [[Valeriy Heletey]] who served as Minister of Defense at that moment.<ref>{{Cite web | url = https://ukranews.com/news/276037-prodannyy-vo-vremena-grycenko-voennyy-gospytal-okazalsya-v-rukakh-russkykh-5-kanal | title = Проданный во времена Гриценко военный госпиталь оказался в руках русских – «5 канал» | publisher = [[Ukrainian News Agency]] | date = 3 October 2014 | accessdate = 21 November 2019 | language = ru}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web | url = https://ru.tsn.ua/politika/prodannyy-voennyy-gospital-okazalsya-v-rukah-rossiyan-smi-389923.html | title = Проданный военный госпиталь оказался в руках россиян - СМИ | publisher = [[1+1 (TV channel)|1+1]] / TSN | date = 3 October 2014 | accessdate = 21 November 2019 | language = ru}}</ref>

===Since 2014 unsuccessful attempts to get elected===
Hrytsenko was one of the candidates for presidency in the [[2014 Ukrainian presidential election]]. According to the opinion poll ordered by [[1+1 (channel)|1+1]] TV channel and conducted by [[GfK|GfK Ukraine]] on 6–8 May, 5.6 per cent of the surveyed would cast their votes for him.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://news.finance.ua/ua/~/1/0/all/2014/05/12/325540 |title=Порошенко тотально лідирує в електоральних симпатіях українців, Тігіпко трохи випереджає Тимошенко - опитування GFK |publisher=Finance.ua |language=uk |accessdate=16 May 2014 |date=12 May 2014}}</ref> In the election he received 5.48% of the vote, ranking him in 4th place.<ref name="CEC election results">{{cite web|url=http://www.nrcu.gov.ua/en/148/566632/ |title=Poroshenko wins presidential election with 54.7% of vote - CEC |publisher=[[Radio Ukraine International]] |date=29 May 2014 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140529212731/http://www.nrcu.gov.ua/en/148/566632/ |archivedate=29 May 2014 }}<br />{{in lang|ru}} [http://telegraf.com.ua/ukraina/politika/1300294-rezultatyi-vyiborov-prezidenta-ukrainyi-2014-tsik-obrabotala-51-99.html Results election of Ukrainian president], Телеграф (29 May 2014)</ref>

About a UAH 100 million was allocated for the implementation of the project.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://kp.ua/life/564934-kto-meshaet-rabote-antykorruptsyonnoho-buiro|script-title=ru:Кто мешает работе Антикоррупционного бюро?|publisher=[[KP (newspaper)|KP]]|author=|date=25 January 2017|accessdate=12 December 2018|language=ru}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://from-ua.com/articles/247259-ministr-bez-diploma.html|script-title=ru:Министр без диплома|publisher=From-UA|author=|date=19 October 2012|accessdate=12 December 2018|language=ru}}</ref>
** The eldest daughter Svetlana (born 1982) graduated from school with a gold medal, received a bachelor's degree in international economics from the [[Kyiv National Economic University]] and a master's degree in international business from [[University of Stirling]] in [[Scotland]]. Svetlana worked as an expert at the Ukrainian Centre for Economic and Political Studies (UCEPS) and in the election headquarters of [[Viktor Yushchenko]], then became the press secretary of the Berkut Hockey Club and the project manager for the Kyiv office of ''The PBN Company''.<ref name="fakty"/>
* Anatoliy Hrytsenko is married to [[Yulia Mostova|Yulia Volodymyrivna Mostova]], a chief editor of well known Ukrainian newspaper ''[[Dzerkalo Tyzhnia]]'' and a widow of [[Oleksandr Razumkov]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://lb.ua/file/person/4310_mostovaya_yuliya_vladimirovna.html|script-title=ru:Мостовая Юлия Владимировна|publisher=Livyy bereh|date=4 May 2018|accessdate=14 December 2018|language=ru}}</ref> Hrytsenko and Mostova had to celebrate the wedding seven times, most recently in June 2003, together with the then-future Ukrainian President [[Viktor Yushchenko]] and his wife [[Kateryna Yushchenko|Kateryna Chumachenko]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tv.ua/news/showbiz/47982-anatolijj-gricenko-politik-s-plamennym-serdcem.html|script-title=ru:Анатолий Гриценко: Политик с пламенным сердцем|publisher=tv.ua|date=7 May 2014|accessdate=14 December 2018|language=ru}}</ref> On 6 March 2016, the couple [[Marriage in the Eastern Orthodox Church|married]] in the [[Eastern Orthodox Church|church]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://gazeta.ua/ru/articles/people-newspaper/_anatolij-gricenko-obvenchalsya-s-uliej-mostovoj-cherez-13-let-v-brake/683905|script-title=ru:Анатолий Гриценко обвенчался с Юлией Мостовой через 13 лет в браке|publisher=gazeta.ua|date=10 March 2016|accessdate=14 December 2018|language=ru}}</ref>
** Hrytsenko and Mostova have a daughter Anna (born 2004), also Mostova have a son Hlib (born 1999) - the youngest son of Razumkov.<ref name="fakty"/>
