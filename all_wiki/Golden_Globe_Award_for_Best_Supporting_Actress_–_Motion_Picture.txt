'''The [[Golden Globe Award]] for Best Supporting Actress – Motion Picture''' was first awarded by the [[Hollywood Foreign Press Association]] in [[1944 in film|1944]] for a performance in a [[film|motion picture]] released in the previous year.

The formal title has varied since its inception; since 2005, the award has officially been called "Best Performance by an Actress in a Supporting Role in a Motion Picture".
