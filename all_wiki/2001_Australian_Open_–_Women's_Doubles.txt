[[Lisa Raymond]] and [[Rennae Stubbs]] were the defending champions but lost in the first round to [[Martina Hingis]] and [[Monica Seles]].

[[Serena Williams|Serena]] and [[Venus Williams]] won in the final 6&ndash;2, 4&ndash;6, 6&ndash;4 against [[Lindsay Davenport]] and [[Corina Morariu]], each achieving a career doubles Golden Slam.
