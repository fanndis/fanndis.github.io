The '''''Journal of Women, Politics & Policy''''' is a [[peer-reviewed]] [[academic journal]] published by [[Routledge]] which covers women's roles in the political process. It was established in 1980 and changed from ''Women & Politics'' to its current name in 2005.<ref>{{cite web|title=Journal of Women, Politics & Policy: Publication History|url=http://www.tandfonline.com/loi/wwap20#.VLLUxZhXuH0|publisher=Taylor and Francis|accessdate=11 January 2015}}</ref> The [[editor-in-chief]] is [[Heidi Hartmann]] ([[Institute for Women's Policy Research]]).

== Abstracting and indexing ==
The journal is abstracted and indexed in the [[Social Sciences Citation Index]] and [[Current Contents]]/Social & Behavioral Sciences.<ref name=ISI>{{cite web |url=http://ip-science.thomsonreuters.com/mjl/ |title=Master Journal List |publisher=[[Thomson Reuters]] |work=Intellectual Property & Science |accessdate=2014-06-04}}</ref> According to the ''[[Journal Citation Reports]]'', the journal has a 2019 [[impact factor]] of 1.500, ranking it 15th out of 45 journals in the category "Women's Studies".<ref name=WoS>{{cite book |year=2020 |chapter=Journals Ranked by Impact: Women's Studies |title=2020 Journal Citation Reports |publisher=[[Thomson Reuters]] |edition=Social Sciences |series=[[Web of Science]]|title-link=Journal Citation Reports }}</ref>
