'''Morrinsville College''' is a state secondary school located in [[Morrinsville]], [[Waikato]], New Zealand. The school has a roll of approximately {{NZ school roll data|126|y}} students from years 9 to 13 (approx ages 13 to 18) as of {{NZ school roll data|||y|y||.}}

The school dates back to 1923 when Morrinsville School (established 1877) opened a secondary department and became Morrinsville District High School. The district high school was split into separate primary and secondary schools in 1950, forming the current Morrinsville School and Morrinsville College.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://morrinsville.school.nz/school-history/|title=School History {{!}} Morrinsville School|language=en-US|access-date=2019-08-02}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.morrcoll.school.nz/schoo-history|title=School History {{!}} Home|website=Morrinsville College|language=en|access-date=2019-08-02}}</ref>
