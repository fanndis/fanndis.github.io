{{main|2009 Australian Open}}

[[Serena Williams]] defeated [[Dinara Safina]] in the final 6–0, 6–3, to win the Women's Singles tennis title at the [[2009 Australian Open]]. It was Williams' fourth career Australian Open singles title, her tenth Grand Slam title and Safina's second career Grand Slam final loss. By winning the title, Williams regained the [[List of WTA number 1 ranked tennis players|World No. 1 ranking]] from [[Jelena Janković]] for the first time since October 2008.

[[Maria Sharapova]] was the defending champion, but withdrew from the tournament, due to a recurring shoulder injury which also ruled her out of the [[2008 US Open (tennis)|2008 US Open]].

This was also the first Australian Open to feature three Russian semifinalists with [[Vera Zvonareva]], [[Dinara Safina]] and [[Elena Dementieva]].
