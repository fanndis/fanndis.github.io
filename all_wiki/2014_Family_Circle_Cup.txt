The '''2014 [[Family Circle Cup]]''' was a women's [[tennis]] event on the [[2014 WTA Tour]]. It took place from March 31 to April 6, 2014. It was the 42nd edition of the tournament and a [[WTA Premier tournaments#Premier|Premier level]] tournament. The event was hosted at the [[Family Circle Tennis Center]], on [[Daniel Island]], [[Charleston, South Carolina|Charleston]], United States. It is the only event of the [[clay court]] season played on green clay.
