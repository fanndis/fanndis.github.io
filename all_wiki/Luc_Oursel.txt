'''Luc Marie Bernard Oursel''', (7 September 1959 – 3 December 2014),<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.lesechos-debats.fr/fr/evenement/luc-oursel-president-directoire-areva,45.html|title=Luc Oursel, Président du Directoire d'AREVA|work=|author=Meissa Tall|date=March 20, 2014|accessdate=|website=lesechos-debats.fr|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141027151158/http://www.lesechos-debats.fr/fr/evenement/luc-oursel-president-directoire-areva,45.html|archive-date=October 27, 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref> was the former chairman of the board of the nuclear company [[Areva]] and member of its Executive Committee. He resigned on 20 October 2014 for health reasons. Until June 2013, he was the President of the French Nuclear Energy Corporation. In 2010, he was made a Knight Commander of the [[Legion of Honour|French Legion of Honour]]<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/affichTexte.do?cidTexte=JORFTEXT000023336519|title=Décret du 31 décembre 2010 portant promotion et nomination|work=|author=|date=December 31, 2010|accessdate=}}</ref>

He died on 3 December 2014.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Areva president and CEO Luc Oursel passes away - World Nuclear News|url=https://www.world-nuclear-news.org/Articles/Areva-president-and-CEO-Luc-Oursel-passes-away|access-date=2020-07-14|website=www.world-nuclear-news.org}}</ref>

== Education==
After having studied at the [[Lycée Janson de Sailly]] in Paris, Luc Oursel graduated from the [[Mines ParisTech|École nationale supérieure des mines de Paris]] as a mining engineer.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://investing.businessweek.com/research/stocks/people/person.asp?personId=28869950&ticker=AREVA:FP|title=Luc Oursel Chairman of the Executive Board, Chief Executive Officer and President |author= |date=December 31, 2010|accessdate=|website=investing.businessweek.com}}</ref>
