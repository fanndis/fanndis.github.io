'''Colmar Brunton''' is a [[market research]] agency founded in [[New Zealand]] in 1981.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.companies.govt.nz/co/110698|title=COLMAR & BRUNTON RESEARCH LIMITED (110698) - New Zealand Companies Office|access-date=2016-09-07}}</ref>

==Services==
There are two Colmar Brunton organisations, which operate in the Asia Pacific Region. The first Colmar Brunton was founded in New Zealand and still operates with its head office in [[Auckland]]. Colmar Brunton Australia is an international market research agency that was split from Colmar Brunton New Zealand in the 1990s.

==New Zealand==
Colmar Brunton was formed by Dick Brunton and Pauline Colmar in 1986.<ref name=WPP2005/> [[Millward Brown]] bought a 20% stake in Colmar Brunton in 2005.<ref name=WPP2005>{{cite news |title=Market research: WPP web spreads out to Colmar Brunton |first=John |last=Drinnan |date=18 March 2005 |newspaper=The National Business Review |via=Newztext}}</ref>

==Australia==
Colmar Brunton Australia is Australia's largest independent market research agency. Its head office is in [[Sydney]], with offices in the Australian cities of Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide and Canberra. It specialises in qualitative and quantitative research, using a wide variety of research methodologies. Its sister companies also provide performance benchmarking and marketing consultancy services.

Colmar Brunton has undertaken work for the Government of Australia through various agencies and departments. In 2011, the firm provided services related to "Evaluation of welfare reform and employment" in the NTER.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tenders.gov.au/?event=public.cn.view&CNUUID=454B83ED-DCF0-F4BB-9860B7909E5CFC63|title=AusTender: Contract Notice View - CN411485|website=www.tenders.gov.au|access-date=2016-11-20}}</ref> In 2015, Colmar Brunton provided services to the government agency [[Austrade|AusTrade]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tenders.gov.au/?event=public.cn.view&CNUUID=3164B79F-928C-41B1-F855C1AB8A29A305|title=AusTender: Contract Notice View - CN2848532-A1|website=www.tenders.gov.au|access-date=2016-11-20}}</ref>
