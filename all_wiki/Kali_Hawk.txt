'''Kali Hawk''' (born October 4, 1986) is an [[United States|American]] actress, comedian and model. She has starred in films such as ''[[Fifty Shades of Black]]'', ''[[Couples Retreat]]'', ''[[Bridesmaids (2011 film)|Bridesmaids]]'' and ''[[Tyler Perry Presents Peeples]]''. She recurred as Shelby in ''[[New Girl]]'' and currently co-starred in the first two seasons of the [[Adult Swim]] series ''[[Black Jesus (TV series)|Black Jesus]].''

==Life and career==
Hawk was raised in Manhattan.<ref name="RL">{{cite web|url=http://www.runwaylive.com/entertainment/3208-kali-hawk-interview-runway-magazine-2010.html|title=Runway ® - Celebrity Fashion Magazine - Kali Instincts|work=Runway ® - Celebrity Fashion Magazine|accessdate=4 November 2014|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141104122953/http://www.runwaylive.com/entertainment/3208-kali-hawk-interview-runway-magazine-2010.html|archivedate=4 November 2014}}</ref> Her father was in merchandising and her mother was a buyer for [[Bloomingdale's]].<ref name="RL" /> She is of African-American, Native American, and German Jewish ancestry.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://becksmithhollywood.com/?p=4776|title=‘Black and Jewish’ Success Surprised Rising Multi-Talent Kali Hawk|publisher=Becksmithhollywood.com|accessdate=4 November 2014}}</ref> She attended [[State University of New York at Purchase]].

She has appeared in several commercials and music videos, and she competed in the fifth season of ''[[Last Comic Standing]]'' in 2007.

Hawk has appeared in numerous comedy films, including ''[[Couples Retreat]]'', ''[[Get Him to the Greek]]'', ''[[Bridesmaids (2011 film)|Bridesmaids]]'', ''[[Peeples (film)|Peeples]]'', ''[[Answer This!]]'' and ''[[Fifty Shades of Black]]''.

In 2012, Hawk was added to the cast of the ''[[In Living Color]]'' reboot pilot, which did not go forward.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2012/04/03/meet-the-new-cast-of-foxs_n_1400813.html |title=Meet The New Cast Of FOX's 'In Living Color' Reboot |accessdate=2012-04-23 |date=April 3, 2012 |publisher=The Huffington Post}}</ref> She also recurred in the sitcom ''[[New Girl]]'' as Shelby.

In 2019, Hawk was added to the cast of the ABC television series ''[[Schooled (TV series)|Schooled]]'' playing Wilma, a science teacher who was poached from an elite school to teach at William Penn Academy.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Andreeva |first1=Nellie |title=‘Schooled’: Kali Hawk Joins ABC Comedy As Series Regular |url=https://deadline.com/2019/08/schooled-kali-hawk-cast-abc-comedy-series-regular-season-2-1202660476/ |website=Deadline |accessdate=3 August 2019 |language=en |date=3 August 2019}}</ref>
