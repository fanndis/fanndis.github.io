'''Elisabeth Mohn''' (née Beckmann, born 21 June 1941)<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.munzinger.de/document/00000024242|title=Liz Mohn|date=2013-07-09|website=Internationales Biographisches Archiv|publisher=Munzinger|language=de|access-date=2017-07-20}}</ref> is a German [[billionaire]] businesswoman and philanthropist.<ref name=":2">{{cite news|title=Mit den Augen einer Mutter|author=|date=2001-01-07|newspaper=Welt am Sonntag|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=30|language=de|isbn=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Das Mädchen aus Rheda-Wiedenbrück|author=Matthias Benirschke|date=2011-06-21|newspaper=Bonner General-Anzeiger|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=2|language=de|isbn=}}</ref>

Mohn, who was married to [[Reinhard Mohn]] until his death in 2009,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.faz.net/aktuell/wirtschaft/reinhard-mohn-gestorben-eine-herausragende-persoenlichkeit-1865820.html|title=Eine herausragende Persönlichkeit: Reinhard Mohn gestorben|author=|date=2009-10-04|website=Frankfurter Allgemeine Zeitung|language=de|access-date=2017-10-04|editor=}}</ref> represents the fifth generation of the family that founded and continues to play a leading role at the [[Bertelsmann]] media group.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bertelsmann-stiftung.de/fileadmin/files/BSt/Publikationen/Infomaterialien/IN_LMFolder_deutsch_2013.pdf|title=Liz Mohn: Entscheidung für ein engagiertes Leben|date=2013|pages=6|language=de|access-date=2017-08-25|editor=Bertelsmann Stiftung}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.handelsblatt.com/unternehmen/it-medien/bertelsmann-medienmonarchie-aus-guetersloh/3818956.html|title=Bertelsmann: Medienmonarchie aus Gütersloh|author=|date=2011-01-30|website=Handelsblatt|language=de|access-date=2017-07-10|editor=}}</ref><ref name=":3" /> She is chairwoman of the shareholders' meeting and of the steering committee of Bertelsmann Verwaltungsgesellschaft,<ref name=":3">{{cite web|url=https://www.faz.net/aktuell/wirtschaft/unternehmen/bertelsmann-liz-mohn-bleibt-an-der-macht-14291543.html|title=Bertelsmann: Liz Mohn bleibt an der Macht|author=Holger Steltzner|date=2016-06-17|website=Frankfurter Allgemeine Zeitung|language=de|access-date=2017-08-01}}</ref> where she serves as family spokesperson.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.t-online.de/regionales/id_78150736/bertelsmann-liz-mohn-spricht-weiter-fuer-die-familie.html|title=Bertelsmann: Liz Mohn spricht weiter für die Familie|author=|date=2016-06-17|website=T-Online|language=de|access-date=2017-10-04|editor=}}</ref> She is also a member of the governance bodies at Bertelsmann.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bertelsmann.de/unternehmen/aufsichtsrat/|title=Mitglieder der Aufsichtsräte von Bertelsmann|website=|language=de|access-date=2017-07-10|editor=}}</ref> In addition, she serves as the vice-chairwoman of [[Bertelsmann Stiftung]]'s executive board and its board of trustees.<ref>{{cite news|title=Personen: Liz Mohn, die mächtige Frau in Gütersloh|author=Annette Becker|date=2009-10-10|newspaper=Börsen-Zeitung|pages=7|language=de}}</ref><ref name="Carsten Heil 4">{{cite news|title=Unsere Wurzeln sind uns wichtig|author=Carsten Heil|date=2017-03-14|newspaper=Neue Westfälische|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=4|language=de|isbn=}}</ref>

Mohn has been recognized with numerous awards for her extensive civic engagement, among them the [[Grand Cross of the Order of Merit of the Federal Republic of Germany]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Eigentum verpflichtet|author=Margaretha Kopeinig|date=2009-10-01|newspaper=Kurier|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=8|language=de|isbn=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bild.de/regional/duesseldorf/bertelsmannerbin-liz-mohn-erhaelt-bundesverdienstorden-13149288.bild.html|title=Bertelsmann-Erbin Liz Mohn erhält Bundesverdienstorden|author=|date=2010-07-01|website=Bild|language=de|access-date=2017-07-10|editor=}}</ref>

== Life==
After completing her schooling, Mohn began an apprenticeship as a [[dental hygienist]]. She later applied to become a telephone operator at Bertelsmann and subsequently worked for the company's book club. At the age of 17 she met Reinhard Mohn.<ref>{{cite news|title=Liz Mohn: Von der Telefonistin zur Medien-Matriarchin|author=Hans-Peter Siebenhaar|date=2012-04-19|newspaper=Handelsblatt|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=6|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> In 1963, she married the editor Joachim Scholz;<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.berliner-zeitung.de/wie-aus-elisabeth-scholz-einst-liz-mohn-wurde---heute-eigentuemerin-des-bertelsmann-konzerns-und-eine-der-maechtigsten-frauen-des-landes-die-stimme-ihres-herrn-15378348|title=Die Stimme ihres Herrn|author=Thomas Schuler|date=2009-10-17|newspaper=Berliner Zeitung|access-date=2017-07-27|pages=3|language=de}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tagesspiegel.de/wirtschaft/die-komplizierte-romanze-der-liz-mohn/472428.html|title=Die komplizierte Romanze der Liz Mohn|author=Matthew Karnitschnig|date=2003-12-08|website=Der Tagesspiegel|language=de|access-date=2017-07-10|editor=}}</ref> the couple separated in 1978.<ref>{{cite news|title=Die starke Frau bei Bertelsmann|author=Matthias Benirschke|date=2011-06-21|newspaper=Westdeutsche Zeitung|language=de}}</ref> In 1982, Reinhard Mohn's first marriage ended in divorce.<ref>{{cite news|title=Die starke Frau bei Bertelsmann|author=Matthias Benirschke|date=2011-06-21|newspaper=Westdeutsche Zeitung|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=|language=de|isbn=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Die Schattenfrau|author=Magdalene Mohn, Minna Wagner|date=2004-04-15|newspaper=Bunte|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=58|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> Liz and Reinhard Mohn married the same year.<ref>{{cite news|title=Probier's mal. Du schaffst das!|author=Hans-Peter Siebenhaar|date=2011-09-16|newspaper=Handelsblatt|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=77|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> He adopted their biological children [[Brigitte Mohn]], {{ill|Christoph Mohn|de}} and {{ill|Andreas Mohn|de}}.<ref>{{cite news|title=Bertelsmann: Szenen einer Ehe|author=Günther Bähr, Tatjana Meier|date=2003-12-08|newspaper=Focus|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=188|language=de|isbn=}}</ref>

[[File:Gorbatschow_in_Gütersloh_1992.jpg|thumb|Liz Mohn, Reinhard Mohn and [[Mikhail Gorbachev]] (1992)]]

In the following years, Liz Mohn gradually began playing a greater role at the company and foundation:<ref>{{cite news|title=Matriarchin, fest im Sattel des Imperiums|author=|date=2011-06-21|newspaper=Wiener Zeitung|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=3|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> In 1986, she joined the Bertelsmann Stiftung's advisory council.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://d-nb.info/016447255|title=Zehn Jahre Bertelsmann Stiftung – Organe und Geschäftsstelle|work=Dritter Tätigkeitsbericht der Bertelsmann Stiftung 1985–1986|pages=127|language=de}}</ref> In 1999, she was asked to become a member of the shareholders' meeting of Bertelsmann Verwaltungsgesellschaft,<ref>{{cite news|title=Reinhard Mohn regelt Besitzverhältnisse neu|author=|date=1999-07-02|newspaper=Der Tagesspiegel|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=19|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> which controls the voting rights at the media group's [[annual general meeting]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Reinhard Mohn verzichtet auf seine Stimmrechte|author=|date=1999-07-02|newspaper=Börsen-Zeitung|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=13|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> In 2000, she also joined the executive committee of the Bertelsmann Stiftung;<ref>{{cite news|title=Bertelsmann Stiftung: Neue Organisation der Führungsstruktur|author=|date=2000-09-30|newspaper=Frankfurter Rundschau|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=18|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> the foundation indirectly holds a majority of shares in the Bertelsmann group.<ref>{{cite news|title=Bertelsmann gehört jetzt mehrheitlich der Stiftung|author=|date=1993-10-14|newspaper=Deutscher Drucker|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=4|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> In 2002, Mohn advanced to the top position at Bertelsmann Verwaltungsgesellschaft,<ref>{{cite news|title=Die starke Frau von Bertelsmann|author=Petra Schäfer, Simone Wermelskirchen|date=2002-07-31|newspaper=Handelsblatt|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=22|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> where she also became the family spokesperson; in addition, she joined the [[supervisory board]] at Bertelsmann.<ref>{{cite news|title=Liz Mohn greift nach der Macht bei Bertelsmann|author=Burkhard Riering|date=2002-07-30|newspaper=Die Welt|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=1|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> With that, she took on a leading role overseeing the company.<ref>{{cite news|title=Die Dame im Hintergrund|author=Lutz Meier|date=2002-07-30|newspaper=Financial Times Deutschland|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=2|language=de|isbn=}}</ref>

Mohn became her husband's successor upon his death in 2009.<ref>{{cite news|title=Liz Mohn tritt die Nachfolge an|author=|date=2009-10-10|newspaper=Neue Zürcher Zeitung|pages=29|language=de}}</ref> Among other rights, he had granted her a veto at Bertelsmann Verwaltungsgesellschaft.<ref>{{cite news|title=Liz Mohn bekommt Vetorecht bei Bertelsmann|date=2009-10-09|newspaper=Handelsblatt|pages=14|language=de}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Liz Mohn übernimmt Vetorecht bei Bertelsmann|date=2009-10-09|newspaper=Berliner Zeitung|pages=30|language=de}}</ref> She was also awarded a majority of the founder's rights at the Bertelsmann Stiftung which allows her,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.faz.net/aktuell/wirtschaft/unternehmen/bertelsmann-alle-macht-fuer-liz-mohn-1578677.html|title=Alle Macht für Liz Mohn|author=Johannes Ritter|date=2009-10-08|website=Frankfurter Allgemeine Zeitung|language=de|access-date=2017-07-10|editor=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.handelsblatt.com/unternehmen/it-medien/bertelsmann-medienmonarchie-aus-guetersloh/3818956.html|title=Bertelsmann: Medienmonarchie aus Gütersloh|date=2011-01-30|website=Handelsblatt|language=de|access-date=2017-08-01}}</ref> for example, to propose members for the board of trustees.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bertelsmann-stiftung.de/fileadmin/files/BSt/Corporate/Organisation/Satzung_BertelsmannStiftung_20140402.pdf|title=Satzung der Bertelsmann Stiftung|author=|date=2014-04-02|website=|language=de|access-date=2017-10-05|editor=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160404132409/http://www.bertelsmann-stiftung.de/fileadmin/files/BSt/Corporate/Organisation/Satzung_BertelsmannStiftung_20140402.pdf|archive-date=April 4, 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref>

As a result of her dual role at the company and the foundation, Mohn has received considerable [[media attention]].<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.spiegel.de/spiegel/print/d-73290134.html|title=Konzerne: Mit Liz und Tücke|date=2010-08-16|newspaper=Der Spiegel|access-date=2017-08-25|language=de}}</ref> One reason for this is the assertion that the Bertelsmann Stiftung does not completely separate its nonprofit activities from the company's commercial interests.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tagesspiegel.de/zeitung/macht-ohne-mandat/755580.html|title=Macht ohne Mandat|author=Harald Schumann|date=2006-09-25|website=Der Tagesspiegel|language=de|access-date=2017-07-10|editor=}}</ref>

== Philanthropy==
In 1987, Mohn launched the [[Neue Stimmen]] International Singing Competition.<ref>{{cite news|title=Musik gehört zu meinem Leben|date=2013-03-13|newspaper=Westfalen-Blatt|language=de}}</ref> She was inspired to do so by [[Herbert von Karajan]],<ref>{{cite news|title=Die Idee hatte Herbert von Karajan|author=Bernhard Hertlein|date=2012-11-28|newspaper=Westfalen-Blatt|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> who noted that too little was being done to promote new opera talent.<ref name=":1">{{cite book|title=Schlüsselmomente: Erfahrungen eines engagierten Lebens|author=Liz Mohn|date=2011|newspaper=|publisher=C. Bertelsmann Verlag|isbn=978-3-641-07123-3|editor=|edition=|location=München|pages=69, 78|language=de|number=}}</ref> Organized by the Bertelsmann Stiftung, the competition has since become an internationally recognized forum for discovering up-and-coming opera singers.<ref>{{cite news|title=Talentschmiede der weltbesten Opernstimmen|author=|date=2008-10-05|newspaper=Neue Westfälische|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=|language=de|isbn=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Talentschmiede gewährt Blick hinter die Kulissen|author=|date=2016-09-29|newspaper=Die Glocke|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=21|language=de|isbn=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bertelsmann-stiftung.de/de/unsere-projekte/neue-stimmen/|title=Neue Stimmen|author=|date=|website=|language=de|access-date=2017-07-10|editor=Bertelsmann Stiftung}}</ref> In addition, Mohn initiated a project in 1999 for promoting music appreciation and education among children, especially in primary schools.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{cite news|title=Liz Mohn will Kinder fordernd fördern|author=Annette Westhoff|date=1999-09-19|newspaper=Welt am Sonntag|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=103|language=de|isbn=}}</ref>

In 1992, she founded the nonprofit {{ill|German Stroke Foundation|de|Stiftung Deutsche Schlaganfall-Hilfe}},<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.aerztezeitung.de/panorama/article/560114/wirksame-strategien-schlaganfall.html|title=Wirksame Strategien gegen den Schlaganfall|author=Pete Smith|date=2009-08-28|website=Ärzte Zeitung|language=de|access-date=2017-07-10|editor=}}</ref> which works to prevent and increase awareness of [[stroke]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Not-Helferin|author=Klaus Ahrens, Hanno Pittner|date=1999-01-01|newspaper=Manager Magazin|publisher=|editor=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=226|language=de|isbn=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Stiftung setzt auf Prävention und Aufklärung|author=|date=1998-06-02|newspaper=Frankfurter Neue Presse|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=4|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> She was motivated to do so by a medical condition affecting one of her sons which caused symptoms that resembled a stroke.<ref>{{cite book|title=Liebe öffnet Herzen|author=Liz Mohn|date=2010|newspaper=Goldmann Verlag|publisher=|isbn=978-3-570-00367-1|editor=|edition=|location=München|pages=243|language=de|number=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Schlaganfall-Stiftung hat in kurzer Zeit viel bewegt|author=|date=1999-05-10|newspaper=Ärzte Zeitung|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=14|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> Mohn is president of the foundation and her daughter Brigitte serves as chairwoman of its board of trustees.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.schlaganfall-hilfe.de/documents/10156/1230381/2016_jahresbericht_schlaganfall-hilfe.pdf|title=Jahresbericht 2016|website=|pages=3, 7|language=de|access-date=2017-07-10|editor=Stiftung Deutsche Schlaganfall-Hilfe|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171209100029/https://www.schlaganfall-hilfe.de//documents/10156/1230381/2016_jahresbericht_schlaganfall-hilfe.pdf|archive-date=December 9, 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref> A key activity in this area is the Roseball, at which Mohn collects donations to be used for fighting the illness.<ref name=":0">{{cite news|title=Lasst uns die Herzen der Menschen öffnen|author=Liz Mohn|date=1999-10-17|newspaper=Welt am Sonntag|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=105|language=de|isbn=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.morgenpost.de/berlin/article207504853/Rosenball-2016-Der-Promi-Tanz-in-den-Mai.html|title=Rosenball 2016: Der Promi-Tanz in den Mai|author=Annika Schönstädt|date=2016-04-30|website=Berliner Morgenpost|language=de|access-date=2017-07-10|editor=}}</ref>

In 2005, Mohn expanded her efforts to promote music appreciation and education by establishing the Liz Mohn Foundation for Culture and Music.<ref name=":1" /> The foundation carries out numerous activities: staging musicals for children and young people, for example, and awarding scholarships to opera singers.<ref>{{cite news|title=Eintauchen in eine neue Welt|author=Martina Helmig|date=2015-04-23|newspaper=Berliner Morgenpost|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=2|language=de|isbn=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Verrückte Reise durch die Zeit|author=|date=2017-03-24|newspaper=Neue Westfälische|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=12|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> To achieve its goals, the foundation partners with the [[Berlin State Opera|Berlin State Opera Unter den Linden]] and other organizations.<ref>{{cite news|title=Seit an Seit mit Daniel Barenboim: Die neue Liz Mohn Kultur- und Musikstiftung fördert ein Internationales Opernstudio in Berlin|date=2007-09-29|newspaper=Neue Westfälische|publisher=|number=|location=|language=de}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.staatsoper-berlin.de/de/staatsoper/internationales-opernstudio/|title=Internationales Opernstudio|publisher=Staatsoper Unter den Linden|language=de|access-date=2017-08-25}}</ref> It currently has an endowment of €16.2 million and Mohn serves as the chairwoman of its executive board.<ref>{{cite news|title=Ideen für interkulturelle Musikprojekte gesucht|date=2017-07-14|newspaper=Gütersloher Zeitung|pages=12|language=de}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.kultur-und-musikstiftung.de/ueber-die-stiftung|title=Über die Stiftung|date=|website=|language=de|access-date=2017-07-10|editor=Liz Mohn Kultur- und Musikstiftung|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170609115114/http://www.kultur-und-musikstiftung.de/ueber-die-stiftung|archive-date=June 9, 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref>

As vice-chairwoman of the Bertelsmann Stiftung executive board, Mohn is responsible for the programs Germany and Asia, Living Values, Discovering Music, and Business in Society, as well as the Competence Center for Leadership and Corporate Culture.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bertelsmann-stiftung.de/fileadmin/files/BSt/Publikationen/Infomaterialien/Bertelsmann_St_Jahresabschluss_2016f.pdf|title=Jahresabschluss 2016: Programme und Projekttätigkeiten|author=|website=|pages=14–31|language=de|access-date=2017-10-05|editor=Bertelsmann Stiftung}}</ref> She is also president of the board of trustees of the [[Fundación Bertelsmann]] and is a member of the board of directors of the [[Bertelsmann Foundation North America]].<ref name="Carsten Heil 4"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bfna.org/page/board|title=Board of Directors|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170206211809/http://www.bfna.org/page/board|archive-date=2017-02-06|access-date=2017-07-25|editor=Bertelsmann Foundation North America}}</ref> She is also member of [[IESE Business School|IESE's]] International Advisory Board (IAB).<ref>[https://www.iese.edu/about/leadership/ ''Members of IESE's International Advisory Board''], iese.edu</ref>

== Awards and honors==
[[File:Liz_Mohn,_Ehrenbürgerin_von_Gütersloh,_2016.jpg|thumb|Liz Mohn, becoming honorary citizen of Gütersloh, with mayor Henning Schulz and [[Wolfgang Schüssel]] (2016)]]

Liz Mohn has been recognized numerous times for her social engagement.<ref name=":2" /> In 1996, she was awarded the {{ill|European Philanthropy Prize|de|Europäischer Kulturpreis#Europäischer Stifterpreis}}, the Grand Cross of the Order of Merit of the Federal Republic of Germany, and the [[Bambi Award|Charity Bambi]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Das Schöne mit dem Guten verbinden|author=Ivette Hillebrecht|date=1999-03-07|newspaper=Welt am Sonntag|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=48|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> In 2000, she received the German Medical Association's Badge of Honor.<ref>{{cite news|title=Ehrenzeichen der Ärzte für Liz Mohn|author=|date=2000-03-13|newspaper=Ärzte Zeitung|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=2|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> In 2006, the [[University of Tel Aviv]] awarded her an [[honorary doctorate]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Talar und Doktorhut für Liz Mohn|author=Ludger Osterkamp|date=2006-05-22|newspaper=Neue Westfälische|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=|language=de|isbn=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Gleichauf mit Reinhard Mohn: Die Universität Tel Aviv verleiht Liz Mohn die Ehrendoktorwürde|author=Stefan Brams|date=2006-03-03|newspaper=Neue Westfälische|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> In 2008, she received [[UNESCO]]'s [[Children in Need]] award.<ref>{{cite news|title=Starauflauf in der Domstadt|author=Monika Salchert|date=2008-09-13|newspaper=Rheinische Post|publisher=|editor=|issue=|number=|location=|edition=|pages=|language=de|isbn=}}</ref> In 2009, she was the first woman to be honored with the {{ill|Karl Winnacker Prize|de|Karl-Winnacker-Preis (Marburg)}},<ref>{{cite news|title=Liz Mohn als erste Frau ausgezeichnet|author=|date=2009-07-31|newspaper=Neue
