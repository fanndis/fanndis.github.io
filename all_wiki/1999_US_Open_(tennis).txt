The '''1999 US Open''' was a [[tennis]] tournament played on [[Hardcourt|outdoor hard courts]] at the [[USTA Billie Jean King National Tennis Center|USTA National Tennis Center]] in [[New York City]] in [[New York (state)|New York]] in the [[United States]]. It was the 119th edition of the US Open and was held from August 30 through September 12, 1999.

==Seniors==
===Men's Singles===
{{main|1999 US Open – Men's Singles}}
{{flagicon|USA}} '''[[Andre Agassi]]''' defeated {{flagicon|USA}} [[Todd Martin]], 6–4, 6–7<sup>(5–7)</sup>, 6–7<sup>(2–7)</sup>, 6–3, 6–2
:<small>• It was Agassi's 5th career Grand Slam singles title and his 2nd and last at the US Open.</small>

===Women's Singles===
{{main|1999 US Open – Women's Singles}}
{{flagicon|USA}} '''[[Serena Williams]]''' defeated {{flagicon|SUI}} [[Martina Hingis]], 6–3, 7–6<sup>(7–4)</sup>
:<small>• It was Williams' 1st career Grand Slam singles title.</small>

===Men's Doubles===
{{main|1999 US Open – Men's Doubles}}
{{flagicon|CAN}} '''[[Sébastien Lareau]]''' / {{flagicon|USA}} '''[[Alex O'Brien]]''' defeated {{flagicon|IND}} [[Mahesh Bhupathi]] / {{flagicon|IND}} [[Leander Paes]], 7–6<sup>(9–7)</sup>, 6–4
:<small>• It was Lareau's 1st and only career Grand Slam doubles title.</small>
:<small>• It was O'Brien's 1st and only career Grand Slam doubles title.</small>

===Women's Doubles===
{{main|1999 US Open – Women's Doubles}}
{{flagicon|USA}} '''[[Serena Williams]]''' / {{flagicon|USA}} '''[[Venus Williams]]''' defeated {{flagicon|USA}} [[Chanda Rubin]] / {{flagicon|FRA}} [[Sandrine Testud]], 4–6, 6–1, 6–4
* It was Serena Williams' 5th career Grand Slam title and her 3rd US Open title. It was Venus Williams' 4th career Grand Slam title and her 1st US Open title.v

===Mixed Doubles===
{{main|1999 US Open – Mixed Doubles}}
{{flagicon|JPN}} '''[[Ai Sugiyama]]''' / {{flagicon|IND}} '''[[Mahesh Bhupathi]]''' defeated {{flagicon|USA}} [[Kimberly Po]] / {{flagicon|USA}} [[Donald Johnson]], 6–4, 6–4
:<small>• It was Sugiyama's 1st and only career Grand Slam mixed doubles title.</small>
:<small>• It was Bhupathi's 2nd career Grand Slam mixed doubles title and his 1st at the US Open.</small>

===Girls' Singles===
{{main|1999 US Open – Girls' Singles}}
{{flagicon|RUS}} '''[[Lina Krasnoroutskaya]]''' defeated {{flagicon|RUS}} [[Nadia Petrova]] 6–3, 6–2

===Boys' Doubles===
{{main|1999 US Open – Boys' Doubles}}
{{flagicon|FRA}} '''[[Julien Benneteau]]''' / {{flagicon|FRA}} '''[[Nicolas Mahut]]''' defeated {{flagicon|USA}} [[Tres Davis]] / {{flagicon|USA}} [[Alberto Francis]] 6–4, 3–6, 6–1

===Girls' Doubles===
{{main|1999 US Open – Girls' Doubles}}
{{flagicon|CZE}} '''[[Dája Bedáňová]]''' / {{flagicon|UZB}} '''[[Iroda Tulyaganova]]''' defeated {{flagicon|RUS}} [[Galina Fokina]] / {{flagicon|RUS}} [[Lina Krasnoroutskaya]] 6–3, 6–4
