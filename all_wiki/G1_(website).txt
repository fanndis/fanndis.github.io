'''G1''' is a [[Brazil|Brazilian]] [[news]] portal maintained by [[Grupo Globo]] and under the guidance of Central Globo de Jornalismo. It was released on September 18, 2006, the year [[Rede Globo]] was 41 years old. The portal provides the [[journalism]] content of the various companies of the Grupo Globo - Rede Globo, [[Globo News]], Radios [[Central Brasileira de Notícias|CBN]] and [[Rádio Globo|Globo]], newspapers [[O Globo]], [[Extra (newspaper)|Extra]], Expresso and [[Valor Econômico]], [[Época (Brazilian magazine)|Época]] and [[Globo Rural]] magazines, among others - besides own reports in format of [[Text (literary theory)|text]], [[Photograph|photos]], [[Sound|audio]] and [[video]].

In addition to five editorial offices in the [[Rio de Janeiro]], [[São Paulo]], [[Brasilia]], [[Belo Horizonte]] and [[Recife]], affiliates of Rede Globo, newspapers, magazines, [[radio station]]s and [[News agency|news agencies]] [[Agência Estado]], [[Agence France Presse]], [[Associated Press]], [[EFE]], [[The New York Times]], [[Lusa News Agency|Lusa]] and [[Reuters]] feed the news portal, which is updated 24 hours a day.

The portal stands out for its multimedia content, taking advantage of the internet's advantages over traditional means of communication.

The G1 versions in [[English language|English]] and [[Spanish language|Spanish]] were released on June 11, 2010 and have videos subtitled in two languages.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://video.globo.com/Videos/Player/Noticias/0,,GIM1280926-7823-G+GANHA+VERSOES+EM+ESPANHOL+E+INGLES,00.html |title=G1 ganha versões em espanhol e inglês|publisher=G1 |date=June 11, 2010|accessdate=2018-06-27}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.advillage.com.br/conteudo_detalhes.asp?id=52168|title=Portal G1 lança versões do site em inglês e espanhol, e vídeos com legendas|publisher=Cidade Biz|date=July 6, 2010|accessdate=2018-06-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130615191805/http://www.advillage.com.br/conteudo_detalhes.asp?id=52168|archive-date=June 15, 2013|url-status=dead}}</ref>

The G1 also has the mobile version and applications for [[Android (operating system)|Android]] and [[iOS]].
