"'''Loyalty'''" (stylized as "'''LOYALTY.'''") is a song by American rapper [[Kendrick Lamar]], from his fourth studio album ''[[Damn (Kendrick Lamar album)|Damn]]'', released on April 14, 2017. The song was later sent to urban and rhythmic radio June 20, 2017 as the second single from the album.<ref name="Rhythmic Radio">{{cite web|title=Top 40 Rhythmic Future Releases|url=https://www.allaccess.com/top40-rhythmic/future-releases|website=All Access|accessdate=June 13, 2017|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170613194735/https://www.allaccess.com/top40-rhythmic/future-releases|archivedate=June 13, 2017|df=}}</ref><ref name="Urban Radio">{{cite web|title=Urban/UAC Future Releases|url=https://www.allaccess.com/urban/future-releases|website=All Access|accessdate=June 13, 2017|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170613194754/https://www.allaccess.com/urban/future-releases|archivedate=June 13, 2017|df=}}</ref> The sixth track on the album (ninth on the ''Collector's Edition'' of ''Damn''<ref name="xxl collector's edition">{{cite web|last1=India|first1=Lindsey|title=Kendrick Lamar Surprises Fans With 'Damn.' Collector's Edition|url=http://www.xxlmag.com/rap-music/new-music/2017/12/kendrick-lamar-surprises-fans-damn-collectors-edition/|website=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]|accessdate=December 9, 2017|date=December 8, 2017}}</ref>), the song features Barbadian singer [[Rihanna]] on vocals and was written by Lamar, [[DJ Dahi]], [[Sounwave]], Rihanna, and [[Terrace Martin]].<ref name="digitalbooklet" /> The song won the [[Grammy Award for Best Rap/Sung Performance]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2017/music/news/2018-grammy-nominations-list-nominees-1202623881/|title=2018 Grammy Nomination List|website=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]]|accessdate=November 28, 2017}}</ref>

==Critical reception==
''[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]'' calls it a "standout track" and one of ''Damn''{{'}}s most "radio-friendly cuts".<ref name="complex">{{cite web|last1=Espinoza|first1=Joshua|title=Here's How Kendrick Lamar and Rihanna's "Loyalty" Came Together|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2017/04/how-kendrick-lamar-rihanna-loyalty-collaboration-went-down|website=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]|publisher=Complex|accessdate=April 19, 2017}}</ref> ''[[The Fader]]'' writer Jason Parham calls "Loyalty", "anxious, berserk, and beautifully intransigent in [its] own right" and "equal parts pop, funk, and brooding rap hymnal".<ref>{{cite web|last1=Parham|first1=Jason|title=The Story Behind The Kendrick Lamar And Rihanna Collaboration "LOYALTY."|url=http://www.thefader.com/2017/04/18/kendrick-lamar-rihanna-terrace-martin-loyalty-damn|website=[[The Fader]]|accessdate=April 19, 2017}}</ref> Frank Guan of ''Vulture'' enjoyed the song, especially Rihanna's part, saying "though only eight bars, Rihanna's sung rap is as fluent as it is brief, a swift and casual tour of her accomplishments."<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.vulture.com/2017/04/kendrick-lamar-featuring-rihanna-loyalty.html|title=On ‘Loyalty’ Rihanna Plays It Casual With Kendrick Lamar|last=Guan|first=Frank|work=Vulture|access-date=April 20, 2017}}</ref> ''[[Rap-Up]]'' called the song a "standout track that allows Kung Fu Kenny and Bad Gal RiRi to address allegiance from a variety of angles."<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.rap-up.com/2017/04/14/new-music-kendrick-lamar-rihanna-loyalty/|title=New Music: Kendrick Lamar feat. Rihanna – 'LOYALTY.'|website=[[Rap-Up]]|date=April 14, 2017|access-date=April 20, 2017}}</ref>

==Composition==
The song contains samples of "[[24K Magic (song)|24K Magic]]" produced by Shampoo Press & Curl and [[The Stereotypes]], and performed by American singer [[Bruno Mars]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://hiphopdx.com/news/id.43113/title.kendrick-lamars-collab-with-rihanna-began-with-bruno-mars-sample#|title=Kendrick Lamar's Collab With Rihanna Began With Bruno Mars Sample|last=http://hiphopdx.com|first=HipHopDX -|work=HipHopDX|access-date=April 20, 2017}}</ref>

==Music video==
The music video for the song was released to Lamar's [[Vevo]] channel on [[YouTube]] on July 28, 2017. It was directed by [[Dave Meyers (director)|Dave Meyers]] and The Little Homies and includes an appearance from featured artist Rihanna.<ref>{{cite web|title=Kendrick Lamar - LOYALTY. ft. Rihanna|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Dlh-dzB2U4Y|website=[[YouTube]]|accessdate=July 28, 2017|date=July 28, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Finkelstein|first1=Sabrina|title=Kendrick Lamar Drops 'Loyalty' Video With Rihanna: Watch|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/7881995/kendrick-lamar-rihanna-loyalty-video-watch|website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|accessdate=October 19, 2017|date=July 28, 2017}}</ref> Jordan Darville of ''[[The Fader]]'' gave the music video a positive review, praising the visuals and called Lamar and Rihanna's adventure together "carefully crafted [and] suitably amorous."<ref>{{cite web|last1=Darville|first1=Jordan|title=Kendrick Lamar And Rihanna Are A Dynamic Duo In The "LOYALTY." Music Video|url=http://www.thefader.com/2017/10/19/gucci-mane-back-on-video|website=[[The Fader]]|accessdate=October 19, 2017|date=July 28, 2017}}</ref>

==Live performances==
Lamar has performed "Loyalty" on [[The Damn Tour|the Damn tour]].<ref>{{cite web|last=Masley|first=Ed|title=Kendrick Lamar launches DAMN.
