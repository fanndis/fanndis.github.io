'''Paddles''' (2016 – 7 November 2017) was a ginger and white [[polydactyl cat]],<ref name="people">{{Cite web | title = New Zealand’s adorable ‘First Cat’ Is taking over Twitter with its opposable thumbs | work = PEOPLE.com | accessdate = 9 November 2017 | url = http://people.com/pets/new-zealands-adorable-first-cat-is-taking-over-twitter-with-its-opposable-thumbs/ | url-status = live | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20171108213814/http://people.com/pets/new-zealands-adorable-first-cat-is-taking-over-twitter-with-its-opposable-thumbs/ | archivedate = 8 November 2017 | df = dmy-all }}</ref> owned by New Zealand Prime Minister [[Jacinda Ardern]] and her partner. Paddles was a rescue cat, adopted by Ardern from a branch of the [[Royal New Zealand Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals|SPCA]]. She became a celebrity as the "First Cat" after Ardern took office, and a [[Twitter]] account was established in her name. Ardern said that she didn't know who created the account.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-41757958|title=New Zealand's 'first cat' and other political pets|last=Chen|first=Heather|date=26 October 2017|work=BBC News|access-date=9 November 2017|language=en-GB|url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171109143048/http://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-41757958|archivedate=9 November 2017|df=dmy-all}}</ref> Paddles interrupted the congratulatory phone call from US President [[Donald Trump]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://news.sky.com/story/paddles-the-new-zealand-pms-famous-cat-dies-in-car-crash-11117994|title=Paddles the New Zealand PM's famous cat dies in car crash|work=Sky News|access-date=9 November 2017|language=en-GB|url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171109075617/http://news.sky.com/story/paddles-the-new-zealand-pms-famous-cat-dies-in-car-crash-11117994|archivedate=9 November 2017|df=dmy-all}}</ref>

Paddles was run over by a car and killed in the [[Auckland]] suburb of [[Point Chevalier]] on 7 November 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.vanityfair.com/style/2017/10/prime-minister-of-new-zealand-has-a-twitter-famous-cat|title=New Zealand’s hip, young Prime Minister comes complete with a tech-savvy cat|website=[[Vanity Fair (magazine)|Vanity Fair]]|first=Hilary |last=Weaver |date=24 October 2017|access-date=25 October 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.newshub.co.nz/home/new-zealand/2017/11/jacinda-ardern-s-cat-paddles-has-died-after-being-hit-by-car.html|title=Tributes flow into SPCA after the death of Jacinda Ardern's cat Paddles|first=Ella|last=Prendergast|work=Newshub|accessdate=7 November 2017|url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171108035053/http://www.newshub.co.nz/home/new-zealand/2017/11/jacinda-ardern-s-cat-paddles-has-died-after-being-hit-by-car.html|archivedate=8 November 2017|df=dmy-all}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nzherald.co.nz/nz/news/article.cfm?c_id=1&objectid=11941627|title=Jacinda Ardern's cat Paddles has died after being hit by a car|date=7 November 2017|work=New Zealand Herald|accessdate=7 November 2017}}</ref>
