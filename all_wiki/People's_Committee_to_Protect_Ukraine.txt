The '''People's Committee to Protect Ukraine''' ({{lang-uk|Народний комітет захисту України}}) was a political movement in [[Ukraine]] formed May 2010. The committee intended to hold a mass [[protest]] [[political campaign|campaign]] in [[Ukraine]] against the policies of [[Ukrainian President|President]] [[Viktor Yanukovych]],<ref name=BBCukr1/><ref>{{in lang|uk}} [http://www.pravda.com.ua/news/2010/05/10/5027134/ Комітет захисту України кличе людей під Раду для "демонстрації"], [[Ukrayinska Pravda]] (May 10, 2010)</ref><ref name=fromground/><ref>[http://www.rferl.org/content/Opposition_Committee_To_Protect_Ukraine_Launched/2038029.html National committee for protection of Ukraine starts to work] [[Radio Free Europe/Radio Liberty|Radio Liberty]], (May 17, 2010)</ref> who ultimately fled amid violence in 2014.

==History==
On May 10, 2010 nine political parties and several non-governmental organizations signed a document on the creation of the committee:<ref name=newmov/>
*[[Ukrainian Social-Democratic Party]]
*[[European Party of Ukraine]]
*[[All-Ukrainian Union "Fatherland"]]
*[[People's Movement of Ukraine]] (Rukh)
*[[Motherland Defenders Party]]
*[[Reforms and Order Party]]
*[[People's Self-Defence Party]]
*[[All-Ukrainian Union "Freedom"]]
*[[Ukrainian Party]]

The parties [[Ukrainian Republican Party Assembly]], [[Our Ukraine (political party)|Our Ukraine]], [[Ukrainian Platform]],<ref name=BBCukr1>{{in lang|uk}} [http://www.bbc.co.uk/ukrainian/news/2010/05/100510_oposition_committee_bo.shtml Опозиція створила Комітет захисту України], [[BBC Ukrainian]] (May 10, 2010)</ref> [[Ukraine Cathedral]] and [[All-Ukrainian Public Organization Civil Position]]<ref>{{in lang|uk}} [http://www.unian.net/ukr/news/news-376143.html Комітет захисту України закликає прийти завтра на мітинг до ВР], [[UNIAN]] (May 10, 2010)</ref> intended to make a decision about joining the committee.

One of the initiators of the committee is writer [[Dmytro Pavlychko]].<ref name=BBCukr2/> Present at the opening signing of the committee were among others: [[Yulia Tymoshenko]], [[Borys Tarasyuk]]<ref name=UkrPravda1/> and [[Levko Lukyanenko]].<ref>{{in lang|uk}} [http://www.pravda.com.ua/news/2010/05/10/5027591/ Яценюк, Гриценко, Кириленко і "Наша Україна" поки осторонь Комітету захисту], [[Ukrayinska Pravda]] (May 10, 2010)</ref> Although they were invited, and also in opposition to [[Ukrainian President|President]] [[Viktor Yanukovych]], former President [[Viktor Yushchenko]] and former [[2010 Ukrainian presidential election|presidential candidate]] [[Arseniy Yatsenyuk]] were not present and did not publicly comment about the committee.<ref name=UkrPravda1>{{in lang|uk}} [http://www.pravda.com.ua/news/2010/05/10/5025903/ Ющенко і Яценюк проігнорували Комітет захисту України], [[Ukrayinska Pravda]] (May 10, 2010)</ref>

After the first rally of the movement (near the [[Verkhovna Rada building]] on May 11, 2010) opposition supporters complained of being hassled by the [[Ukrainian police|police]] in an attempt to limit the number of participants in the rally.<ref name=fromground>[http://www.kyivpost.com/news/politics/detail/66342/ Police move to dampen protest turnout], [[Kyiv Post]] (May 11, 2010)</ref> According to the police buses were only stopped because companies didn't have permits to travel in convoys or if buses were in bad technical shape.<ref>{{in lang|uk}} [http://www.pravda.com.ua/news/2010/05/13/5037290/ Міліція каже, що не зупиняла автобуси опозиції], [[Ukrayinska Pravda]] (May 10, 2010)</ref> [[Ukraine|Ukrainian]] [[Ministry of Internal Affairs (Ukraine)|Minister of Internal Affairs]] [[Anatolii Mohyliov]] stated on May 13, 2010 “[[Ukrainian law|The law]] allows rallies but bans street barricades and loud shouts”.<ref>[http://www.unian.net/eng/news/news-376657.html Interior Minister: rallies permitted, loud shouts banned], [[UNIAN]] (May 13, 2010)</ref>

[[Mykola Tomenko]], member of [[Yulia Tymoshenko Bloc]], predicted on May 11, 2010 opposition rallies would get bigger and louder in the near future.<ref name=fromground/>

On 8 August 2011 [[All-Ukrainian Union "Fatherland"]], [[People's Movement of Ukraine|Rukh]], [[European Party of Ukraine]], [[Civil Movement "People's Self-Defense"|People's Self-Defense]], [[Reforms and Order Party]], [[Motherland Defenders Party]], [[Civil Position]] and [[Front for Change (Ukraine)|Front for Change]] formed the [[Dictatorship Resistance Committee]] "to better coordinate our efforts".<ref>[http://www.kyivpost.com/news/nation/detail/110510/ Dictatorship Resistance Committee to coordinate opposition parties in parliamentary elections], [[Kyiv Post]] (10 August 2011)</ref><ref>[http://www.kyivpost.com/news/nation/detail/110369/ Ukrainian opposition to create Dictatorship Resistance Committee after Tymoshenko's arrest], [[Kyiv Post]] (8 August 2011)</ref><ref>[http://www.kyivpost.com/news/politics/detail/114583/ Ukraine opposition alliance urges mass-scale public action against government], [[Kyiv Post]] (11 October 2011)</ref>

==Goals==
The organisation saw as its main tasks:<ref name=newmov>[http://www.kyivpost.com/news/nation/detail/66234/ Ukrainian political forces create people's committee to protect Ukraine], [[Kyiv Post]] (May 10, 2010)</ref>
*Protection of the territorial integrity of the state
*Protection of the rights and freedoms of citizens
*the cessation of "anti-Ukrainian humanitarian policies
*Rejection of any attempts to establish foreign monitoring over strategic enterprises and sectors of Ukrainian industry
*Preservation of the Euro-Atlantic choice of Ukraine
*Move forward towards civilized European values

===Declaration of the People's Committee to Protect Ukraine===
On the May 10, 2010 the committee released the following opening statement: {{citation needed|date=March 2012}}
<blockquote>
On the May 10, 2010, we, representatives of political
parties and civil organizations have created ''People's Committee to Protect Ukraine''.

Taking into account the danger of losing the statehood and democratic freedoms by Ukrainian population in results of actions of Yanukovych's regime we announce the consolidation of all forces to coordinate the actions in order to protect Ukraine.

In the conditions of widely spread attacks of the Yanukovych's regime onto the
live-important national interest of Ukraine the first task of the Committee is to
organize all-Ukrainian opposition movement to:
* protect the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Ukraine;
* protect the main freedom of speech|freedoms and political rights;
* stop anti-Ukrainian policy in Ukraine and protect Ukrainian identity;
* stop any efforts to impose the foreign control over strategically important factories and sections of Ukrainian industry;
*  hold the Euro-Atlantic choice of Ukraine and keep orientation onto European values.

Our actions will be carried out in accordance with the rights and freedoms defined by
the Constitution of Ukraine, will be open for equal rights partnership to protect Ukraine.
</blockquote>

==Authorities' response==
In early May 2010, [[Prime Minister of Ukraine|Prime Minister]] [[Mykola Azarov]] called the ideas of the committee "hysterical and hopeless".<ref name=BBCukr2>{{in lang|uk}} [http://www.bbc.co.uk/ukrainian/ukraine/2010/05/100506_oposition_ok_is.shtml Народний комітет порятунку: добре чи погано?], [[BBC Ukrainian]] (May 6, 2010)</ref> Meanwhile, one of the members of [[Party of Regions]] [[Valeriy Konovalyuk]] stated that "the committee is unlikely to receive support from the population".<ref name=BBCukr2/>
