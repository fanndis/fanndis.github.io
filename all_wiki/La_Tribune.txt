'''''La Tribune''''' ({{IPA-fr|la tʁiˈbyn}}) is a French weekly financial [[newspaper]] founded in 1985 by Bruno Bertez.<ref>{{cite web|title=La Tribune|url=http://en.kiosko.net/fr/np/tribune.html|work=Kiosko.net}}</ref><ref name=osc>{{cite web|title=France. Media Guide 2008|url=https://fas.org/irp/dni/osc/france-media.pdf|work=Open Source Center|accessdate=28 June 2016|date=16 July 2008}}</ref> Its main competitor is the French newspaper [[Les Échos (France)|Les Échos]], which is currently owned by [[LVMH]].

From 1993 to 2007 ''La Tribune'' was part of [[LVMH]].<ref name="osc"/> In 2010, [[Alain Weill]], the Chairman and CEO of [[NextRadioTV]] sold 80% of ''La Tribune'' to Valérie Decamp for €1 and he still owns 20%.<ref>{{cite news|first=Hélène|last=Reitzaum|first2=Olivia|last2=Derreumaux|url=http://www.lefigaro.fr/medias/2011/12/20/04002-20111220ARTFIG00322-alain-weill-invite-du-buzz-media-orange-le-figaro.php|title=NextRadio TV doit croître face aux chaînes historiques|language=fr|trans-title=NextRadio TV breaks its historical chains|work=[[Le Figaro]]|date=21 December 2011|accessdate=8 January 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.20minutes.fr/medias/405858-Media-Alain-Weill-cede-80-de-La-Tribune.php|archive-url=https://archive.today/20130213082950/http://www.20minutes.fr/medias/405858-Media-Alain-Weill-cede-80-de-La-Tribune.php|url-status=dead|archive-date=13 February 2013|title=Alain Weill cède 80% de "La Tribune"|work=[[20 minutes (France)|20 Minutes]]|date=20 May 2010|accessdate=8 January 2015|language=fr}}</ref>

In 2000 had a circulation of 531,000 copies.<ref>{{cite book|editor1=Mary Kelly|editor2=Gianpietro Mazzoleni|editor3=Denis McQuail|title=The Media in Europe: The Euromedia Handbook|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=yPS8edN_aJUC&pg=PA76|accessdate=24 April 2015|date=17 December 2003|publisher=SAGE Publications|isbn=978-1-4129-3260-8|page=76}}</ref> In 2008 it switched from tabloid to berliner format. It was rescued from bankruptcy in 2011.<ref>{{cite web |title=La Tribune |url=https://www.eurotopics.net/en/148670/la-tribune |website=eurotopics.net |language=en}}</ref> In 2012, the newspaper switched to a weekly.

==State aid==
In 2003 and 2010, the newspaper received state subsidies in a sum of 2.53 million euros.<ref>{{cite web |title=Wikiwix's cache |url=http://www.rue89.com/2010/08/11/lheure-des-fuites-sur-les-subventions-a-la-presse-161807 |archive-url=http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20101026142108/http://www.rue89.com/2010/08/11/lheure-des-fuites-sur-les-subventions-a-la-presse-161807 |url-status=dead |archive-date=2010-10-26 |website=archive.wikiwix.com}}</ref>
