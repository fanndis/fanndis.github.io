[[File: Ariana Grande Grammys Red Carpet 2020.png|upright|thumbnail|[[Ariana Grande]] is the most-followed woman on Instagram, with over 208 million followers.]]

[[File:Kendall & Kylie Jenner interview.png|upright|thumbnail|[[Kylie Jenner]] (''right'') and [[Kendall Jenner]] (''left'') own the fifth and thirteenth most-followed accounts, respectively.]]

This list contains the top 50 accounts with the most followers on the photo and video-sharing social platform [[Instagram]].<ref name="Trackalytics">{{Cite web|url=https://www.trackalytics.com/the-most-followed-instagram-profiles/page/1/|title=The Most Followed Instagram Profiles|website=Trackalytics.com|accessdate=April 9, 2020}}</ref><ref name="SocialBlade" /> {{As of|2020|12}}, the most-followed individual is Portuguese footballer [[Cristiano Ronaldo]] with over 244 million followers, and the most-followed female is American singer [[Ariana Grande]] with over 208 million followers. Instagram has its own brand account on the platform, and with over 380 million followers; it is the most-followed account overall. ''[[National Geographic]]'' is the second most-followed brand account, with over 146 million followers. A total of 22 accounts have exceeded 100 million followers on the site.
