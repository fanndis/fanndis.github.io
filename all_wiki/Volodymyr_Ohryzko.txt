'''Volodymyr Stanislavovych Ohryzko''' ({{lang-uk|Володимир Станiславович Огризко}}; born April 1, 1956) is a [[Ukraine|Ukrainian]] diplomat. He served as the [[Ministry of Foreign Affairs (Ukraine)|Minister of Foreign Affairs]] of [[Ukraine]] from December 18, 2007 to March 3, 2009, when he was fired by the [[Ukrainian Parliament]].<ref>[http://www.ukranews.com/eng/article/183916.html Rada Dismisses Ohryzko] {{webarchive|url=https://archive.is/20130105021153/http://www.ukranews.com/eng/article/183916.html |date=2013-01-05 }} [[Ukrainian News Agency]] (March 3, 2009)</ref> On March 17, 2009 Ohryzko was appointed First Deputy Secretary of the [[National Security and Defense Council]] by [[President of Ukraine|President]] [[Viktor Yushchenko]].<ref>{{cite news|accessdate=May 29, 2014 |date=March 17, 2009 |url=http://www.kyivpost.com/content/ukraine/yushchenko-appoints-ohryzko-as-national-security-a-37621.html |title=Yuschenko Appoints Ohryzko As NSDC First Deputy Secretary |publisher=[[Kyiv Post]] |agency=[[Ukrainian News Agency]] |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131123110224/http://www.kyivpost.com/content/ukraine/yushchenko-appoints-ohryzko-as-national-security-a-37621.html |archivedate=November 23, 2013 |url-status=live }}</ref>

==Resignation==
[[Prime Minister of Ukraine|Prime Minister]] [[Yulia Tymoshenko]] commented on the resignation: "If there were no crisis, he should have been dismissed long ago. I think all questions have been failed. The systematic work on discrediting the government was performed by Mr.Ohryzko". Tymoshenko said she had applied to the faction of the [[Bloc of Yulia Tymoshenko]] (BYuT) at Parliament with a request not to vote for the dismissal of Ohryzko cause that would stir up the political situation in the country. [[President of Ukraine|President]] [[Viktor Yushchenko]] called the dismissal of Ohryzko an "untimely and unfounded move from the [[Verkhovna Rada]]".<ref>{{cite news|date=March 3, 2009|url=http://www.ukranews.com/eng/article/184063.html|title=Tymoshenko: Ohryzko Deserves Dismissal|agency=Ukrainian News Agency|accessdate=March 5, 2009|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120918005459/http://www.ukranews.com/eng/article/184063.html|archivedate=September 18, 2012}} </ref>

On March 4, 2009 the faction of BYuT at the Verkhovna Rada pledged its preparedness to support reinstatement of Volodymyr Ohryzko as Foreign Minister ("For the sake of preservation of the majority coalition and further effective work of the parliament of Ukraine") if he would apologies to Prime Minister Yulia Tymoshenko and the [[Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine|Cabinet of Ministers]] for his actions that, in the opinion of the BYuT caused damage to the reputation of Ukraine. 49 MPs out of 156 from the faction of BYuT voted in favour of the dismissal of Ohryzko a day earlier.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.ukranews.com/eng/article/184414.html|archive-url=https://archive.is/20130205035909/http://www.ukranews.com/eng/article/184414.html|url-status=dead|archive-date=February 5, 2013|title=BYT Pledges Preparedness To Support Reinstatement Of Ohryzko As Foreign Minister|agency=Ukrainian News Agency|date=March 4, 2009}} </ref> Despite this, Ohryzko asked President Viktor Yushchenko not to nominate him for the post of Foreign Affairs Minister in the Cabinet of Ministers on March 5, 2009 and accused BYuT of developing its own foreign policy in parallel to the government's foreign policy.<ref>{{cite news|agency=[[Interfax-Ukraine]] |url=http://www.kyivpost.com/content/ukraine/ohryzko-asks-president-not-to-nominate-him-as-fore.html?flavour=full |title=Ohryzko asks president not to nominate him as foreign minister |publisher=Kyiv Post |date=March 5, 2009 |accessdate=May 29, 2014 |url-status=live |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140529022034/http://www.kyivpost.com/content/ukraine/ohryzko-asks-president-not-to-nominate-him-as-fore.html?flavour=full |archivedate=May 29, 2014 }}</ref>

Ohryzko was placed at number 3 on the electoral list of [[Ukrainian Platform "Sobor"]] during the [[2012 Ukrainian parliamentary election]].<ref>{{cite news|publisher=Kyiv Post |date=August 5, 2012 |url=http://www.kyivpost.com/content/politics/ukrainian-news-ohryzko-kapranov-brothers-lead-sobor-for-parliament-311062.html |title=Ohryzko, Kapranov Brothers lead Sobor for Parliament |accessdate=August 28, 2013 |agency=Ukrainian News |archivedate=November 26, 2013 |url-status=live |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131126165442/http://www.kyivpost.com/content/politics/ukrainian-news-ohryzko-kapranov-brothers-lead-sobor-for-parliament-311062.html }}</ref> But on 15 October 2012 the party withdrew itself from the national list of this election.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://gazeta.ua/articles/politics/_ukrajinska-platforma-sobor-znimaetsya-z-viboriv-na-korist-opozicijnih-sil/460744 |script-title=uk:Українська платформа "Собор" знімається з виборів на користь опозиційних сил |trans-title=Ukrainian platform "Sobor" removed from the election in favor of the opposition |publisher=[[Krayina]] |date=October 13, 2012 |accessdate=August 28, 2013 |archivedate=May 29, 2014 |url-status=live |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140529024350/http://gazeta.ua/articles/politics/_ukrajinska-platforma-sobor-znimaetsya-z-viboriv-na-korist-opozicijnih-sil/460744 |language=uk }}<br>{{cite news |date=October 15, 2012 |accessdate=August 28, 2013 |url=http://tvi.ua/htos_zalezhavsya_na_pechi_a_htos_i_dosi_pich_kolupae___rozsliduvannya_tvi_pro_uzgodzhenih_kandidativ |script-title=uk:Хто і чому змушує кандидатів зніматись з виборів? – розслідування ТВі |trans-title=Who and what makes candidates to withdraw from the election? - TBi investigates |publisher=[[TVi (channel)|TVi]] |language=uk |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121019074354/http://tvi.ua/htos_zalezhavsya_na_pechi_a_htos_i_dosi_pich_kolupae___rozsliduvannya_tvi_pro_uzgodzhenih_kandidativ |archivedate=October 19, 2012 |df=mdy-all }}</ref>

After the resignation, in 2013, Ohryzko have said that it was a big mistake for Ukraine to pause the association agreement with the [[European Union]].<ref>{{cite news|accessdate=August 15, 2014 |url=http://www.kyivpost.com/content/ukraine/ex-foreign-minister-ohryzko-says-pause-in-association-with-eu-is-big-mistake-332316.html |title=Ex-Foreign Minister Ohryzko says pause in association with EU is big mistake |work=Kyiv Post |agency=Interfax-Ukraine |date=November 22, 2013 |archivedate=November 23, 2013 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131123052553/http://www.kyivpost.com/content/ukraine/ex-foreign-minister-ohryzko-says-pause-in-association-with-eu-is-big-mistake-332316.html |url-status=live }}</ref>
