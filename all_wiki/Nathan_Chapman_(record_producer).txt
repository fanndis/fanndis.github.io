'''Nathan Chapman''' is an American [[record producer]] who works in the field of [[country music]]. He is known primarily for working with American singer-songwriter [[Taylor Swift]], having co-produced with Swift for her albums ''[[Taylor Swift (album)|Taylor Swift]]'', ''[[Fearless (Taylor Swift album)|Fearless]]'', ''[[Speak Now]]'', ''[[Red (Taylor Swift album)|Red]]'' and ''[[1989 (Taylor Swift album)|1989]]''. [[Taylor Swift (album)|''Taylor Swift'']] was also the first album that he produced.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cmt.com/news/country-music/1546980/when-she-thinks-tim-mcgraw-taylor-swift-savors-payoff.jhtml|title=When She Thinks "Tim McGraw," Taylor Swift Savors Payoff|last=Morris|first=Edward|date=December 1, 2006|work=[[Country Music Television|CMT]].|accessdate=July 22, 2010}}</ref> He is a 2001 graduate of [[Lee University]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chattanoogan.com/articles/article_164648.asp|title=Lee Alumnus Nathan Chapman Stands Out in the Country Music Business|last=Harbin|first=Beth|date=December 9, 2009|work=The Chattanoogan|accessdate=July 22, 2010|archive-url=https://archive.is/20130102041500/http://www.chattanoogan.com/articles/article_164648.asp|archive-date=January 2, 2013|url-status=dead}}</ref> He was said to be working in a shack before producing music with various artists.

At the [[52nd Grammy Awards]] in 2010 and the [[58th Grammy Awards]] in 2016, Chapman won the [[Grammy Award for Album of the Year]] for his work as a co-producer on [[Taylor Swift]]'s albums ''[[Fearless (Taylor Swift album)|Fearless]]'' and ''[[1989 (Taylor Swift album)|1989]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.grammy.com/nominees?category=67|title=52nd Grammy Awards|work=Grammy.com|accessdate=July 22, 2010}}</ref> He also co-produced the song "This Love" on the album ''1989'', this being his first time co-producing a [[pop music|pop]] song. 

Other acts for whom Chapman has produced include [[Kylie Minogue]], [[Martina McBride]], [[The Band Perry]], [[Jypsi]], [[Point of Grace]], [[Keith Urban]], [[Krystal Keith]], [[Shania Twain]], [[Lady Antebellum]], [[Electra Mustaine]] and [[Jimmy Wayne]].<ref name="credits">{{cite web|url=http://www.nathanchapman.com/credits.html|title=Credits|work=Nathan Chapman website|accessdate=July 22, 2010|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090226225319/http://www.nathanchapman.com/credits.html|archivedate=February 26, 2009}}</ref><ref name="bill">{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/470343/shania-twain-returns-to-country-songs-chart|title=Shania Twain Returns To Country Songs Chart|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|accessdate=2011-06-20}}</ref> In addition to his production work, Chapman is a session musician who plays guitar, [[banjo]] and [[mandolin]].<ref name="credits" />
