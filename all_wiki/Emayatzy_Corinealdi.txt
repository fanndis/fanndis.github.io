'''Emayatzy Evett Corinealdi'''<ref name='KY-Birth'>{{cite web |url= |title=Kentucky, Birth Index, 1911-1999 [database on-line] |last= |first= |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|year=2006 |website=Ancestry.com |series= |publisher= Ancestry.com Operations Inc. |quote=Original data: Kentucky Department for Libraries and Archives. Kentucky Birth, Marriage, and Death Databases: Births 1911-1999. Frankfort, Kentucky: Kentucky Department for Libraries and Archives. |ref= |postscript= }}</ref> ({{IPAc-en|ˌ|ɛ|m|eɪ|ˈ|ɑː|t|s|i|_|ˌ|k|ɒr|ɪ|ˈ|n|ɑː|l|d|i}} {{respell|EM|ay|AHT|see|_|KORR|ih|NAHL|dee}}; born January 14, 1980)<ref name='KY-Birth'/> is an American actress. She is best known for her leading role in the [[Ava DuVernay]] film ''[[Middle of Nowhere (2012 film)|Middle of Nowhere]]'' (2012).

==Early life==
Corinealdi was born in [[Fort Knox|Fort Knox, Kentucky]],<ref name='KY-Birth'/><ref name=BET1>{{cite web| url= http://www.bet.com/topics/e/emayatzy-corinealdi.html |title= Emayatzy Corinealdi | website= BET.com |publisher=BET Interactive, LLC |accessdate= 29 December 2014}}</ref> the daughter of Rosemarie Hilton and Edward Corinealdi. Her father is [[Panamanians|Panamanian]], while her mother is [[African-American]] from [[Ohio]].<ref name='KY-Birth'/><ref>[http://blackactors.net/2011/06/the-cast-of-ava-duvernays-middle-of-nowhere/ THE CAST OF AVA DUVERNAY'S 'MIDDLE OF NOWHERE'] at BlackActors.net</ref><ref name="backstage breakout">{{cite web| url= http://www.backstage.com/interview/middle-nowhere-star-emayatzy-corinealdi-her-breakout-leading-role/ | title= Interview: 'Middle of Nowhere' Star Emayatzy Corinealdi on Her Breakout Leading Role | first= Jenelle |last= Riley |date= 11 September 2012 |accessdate= 29 December 2014}}</ref> As a member of a military family, she was raised between [[Germany]] and the U.S. states of Ohio, [[Kansas]], and [[New Jersey]].<ref name="backstage breakout" /><ref name= "good role">{{cite news| title= Emayatzy Corinealdi finds a good role in 'Middle of Nowhere' | date= 13 October 2012| first= Nicole |last= Sperling |work= Los Angeles Times |url= http://articles.latimes.com/2012/oct/13/entertainment/la-et-mn-emayatzy-corinealdi-20121014 | accessdate= 29 December 2014}}</ref> She studied in New Jersey at the Actor's Training Studio, then at the [[William Esper Studio]] in [[New York City]], then at Playhouse West.<ref name= "good role" />

==Career==
In her early career, Corinealdi co-starred in a number of independent and short films, had the recurring role on the CBS daytime soap opera, ''[[The Young and the Restless]]'', and appeared in the Hallmark television film, ''[[The Nanny Express]]'' (2008). In 2012, she made her feature film debut as the lead in the drama ''[[Middle of Nowhere (2012 film)|Middle of Nowhere]]''. Corinealdi won the 14th Annual [[American Black Film Festival]] Star Project,<ref>{{cite web| url= http://www.clutchmagonline.com/2014/09/look-book-favorite-emayatzy-corinealdi-style-beauty-looks/ | work= Clutch Magazine| publisher= Sutton New Media LLC| title = Look Book: Our Favorite Emayatzy Corinealdi Style and Beauty Looks |author= Clutch |date= September 2014 | accessdate= 29 December 2014}}</ref> an international acting competition for emerging multicultural artists. Later that year, she won the [[Gotham Independent Film Award for Breakthrough Actor]] for the film.<ref>{{cite web| url= http://hellobeautiful.com/2012/11/28/middle-of-nowhere-emayatzy-corinealdi-wins-best-actor-gotham-film-awards/ | title= Emayatzy Corinealdi Of 'Middle Of Nowhere' Wins Gotham Film Awards' Best Actor | date= 28 November 2012 |first= Danielle |last= Young | website= HelloBeautiful.com | publisher= Interactive One| accessdate= 29 December 2014}}</ref>  She was also nominated for an [[Independent Spirit Award for Best Female Lead]].<ref>{{cite news| title= Spirit Awards reaction: Jack Black, Matthew McConaughey, Benh Zeitlin, David O. Russell, more weigh in | work= Entertainment Weekly | first= Lindsey |last= Bahr |date= 27 November 2012}}</ref>

In 2013 Corinealdi appeared in the short film ''The Door'', part of [[Miu Miu]]'s fashion series ''Women's Tales'', which reunited her with her ''Middle of Nowhere'' director [[Ava DuVernay]].<ref>{{cite web| url= http://www.miumiu.com/en/women_tales/5/film | title= "The Door" by Ava DuVernay, Women's Tales #5 | publisher= MiuMiu.com | accessdate= 29 December 2014}}</ref> In 2014, Corinealdi was cast in the drama series ''[[Hand of God (TV series)|Hand of God]]''. Later that year, she won the role of [[Frances Taylor Davis]], the wife of [[Miles Davis]] in the biographical film ''[[Miles Ahead (film)|Miles Ahead]]''.<ref name="BET replace">{{cite web |url=http://www.bet.com/news/celebrities/2014/06/25/middle-of-nowhere-star-to-replace-zoe-saldana-in-miles-davis-biopic.html |title=Middle of Nowhere Star to Replace Zoe Saldana in Miles Davis Biopic |last1=Diaz |first1=Evelyn |last2= |first2= |date=25 June 2014 |website=BET.com |publisher=BET Interactive, LLC |accessdate=16 November 2014}}</ref>
