'''Colleen Martin''' (born December 23, 1975), known professionally as '''Lady Starlight''', is an American DJ and musical performer. Based in [[Lower East Side|New York City's Lower East Side]], she is best known for her numerous collaborations with [[Lady Gaga]]. Starlight released her debut extended play, ''Untitled'', in 2017. The following year, she released her second extended play,  ''Which One of Us Is Me?''. Besides her own performances, she also sometimes performs with [[Surgeon (musician)|Surgeon (Anthony Child)]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.residentadvisor.net/feed-item.aspx?id=82590 |title=Watch Surgeon and Lady Starlight open for Lady Gaga in full |last1=HerrJordan |date=December 3, 2014 |website=Resident Advisor |access-date=April 21, 2016 }}</ref>

==Biography==
Lady Starlight was born Colleen Martin on December 23, 1975, and was raised in [[upstate New York]].<ref name="Source1">{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/lady-starlight-mn0003627419/biography|title=Artist Biography by Rich Wilson|last=Wilson|first=Rich|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|accessdate=February 11, 2019}}</ref> She moved to [[New York City]] in 2001, at the age of 26.<ref name="yahoo1">{{cite web|url=http://au.news.yahoo.com/thewest/entertainment/access-all-areas/a/-/14084262/starlight-shines-on-gaga |title=Starlight shines on Gaga - The West Australian |publisher=Yahoo! News |date=June 29, 2012 |accessdate=July 11, 2012}}</ref>
