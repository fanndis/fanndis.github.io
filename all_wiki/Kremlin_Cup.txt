The '''Kremlin Cup''' ({{lang-ru|Кубок Кремля}}) is a professional [[tennis]] tournament played on [[Tennis court#Indoor courts|indoor hard courts]]. It is currently part of the [[ATP Tour 250]] series of the [[ATP Tour]] and is a [[WTA Premier Tournaments|Premier Tournament]] on the [[WTA Tour]]. It was held annually at the [[Olympic Stadium (Moscow arena)|Olympic Stadium]] in Moscow, Russia, from 1990 to 2018. In 2019, the Olympic Stadium will undergo a major reconstruction which will last two years.<ref>[https://www.championat.com/tennis/article-3575695-karen-hachanov--chempion-turnira-vtb-kubok-kremlja-2018.html «Олимпийская сказка, прощай!»]</ref><ref>[https://tass.ru/ekonomika/4844849 Спорткомплекс "Олимпийский" в Москве закроется на реконструкцию в 2019 году]</ref> The 2019 edition of the tournament will be held at the [[Ice Palace Krylatskoye]].<ref>[https://kremlincup.ru/en/news/531/ VTB Kremlin Cup Jubilee Edition To Be Held At Krylatskoye Ice Palace]</ref>

The Kremlin Cup was created in 1990 by [[Sasson Khakshouri]] becoming the first international professional tennis tournament to be held in Russia. The tournament started as a men's only event during the first years of its run, before it became a joint men's and women's event in 1996.

Until 2007, it was held on a [[carpet court|carpet surface]]. It is now held on a RuKortHard surface.<ref>http://images.kremlincup.ru/kremlincup/2008/08/20/0000000390/field8.pdf</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rukort.com.hr/RukortOutdoor/RuKortHard/tabid/164/Default.aspx |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2010-09-10 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101022145406/http://www.rukort.com.hr/RukortOutdoor/RuKortHard/tabid/164/Default.aspx |archivedate=2010-10-22 }}</ref>
