'''Ideli Salvatti''' is a Brazilian politician. She became the first woman to be elected senator of the state of [[Santa Catarina (state)|Santa Catarina]]. She was the Minister of Fisheries and Aquaculture in the [[Dilma Rousseff]] Government. She later worked in the Secretariat of Institutional Relations and Secretariat of Human Rights.<ref name="Encarnación2016">{{cite book|author=Omar G. Encarnación|title=Out in the Periphery: Latin America's Gay Rights Revolution|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=QLgBCwAAQBAJ&pg=PA152|accessdate=18 November 2017|year=2016|publisher=Oxford University Press|isbn=978-0-19-935665-2|pages=152–}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|title=Key Facts on Brazil: Essential Information on Brazil|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=RNYMTx9d3S0C&pg=PA2002|accessdate=18 November 2017|publisher=The Internationalist|pages=2002–|id=GGKEY:7ZBSSS3L5E5}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://brazzil.com/12601-new-aide-to-brazilian-president-has-a-mission-to-talk-to-friends-and-foes-of-government/ | title=New Aide to Brazilian President Has a Mission: To Talk to Friends and Foes of Government | publisher=Brazzil.com | work=Yara Aquino | date=15 June 2011 | accessdate=18 November 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.brazilcouncil.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/Brazil-Bulletin-1-03-2011.pdf | title=DILMA ROUSSEFF INAUGURATION AND CABINET LINEUP | publisher=Brazil Council | accessdate=18 November 2017}}</ref>

==Biography==
She graduated with a degree in Physics from the [[University of Paraná]] .  She married Eurides Mescolotto and had two children and later she married Jeferson Figueiredo in 2009.
