This page lists the winners and nominees for the '''[[Black Reel Awards|Black Reel Award]] for Outstanding Film'''. This award is presented to the producers of the film. ''[[Black Panther (film)|Black Panther]]'' holds the record with the most nominations with 17 and has the most wins in a single year with 10.

==Winners and nominees==
Winners are listed first and highlighted in '''bold'''.
