'''Populus''' is a [[market research]] company in the [[United Kingdom]] formed in 2003. 

Populus co-founded the [[British Polling Council]] in 2004 and regularly publishes [[opinion poll]]s on voting intention and as well as other political and commercial issues.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.britishpollingcouncil.org/press041101.html|title=On November 15th the British Polling Council (BPC) will be launched.|date=2004-11-01|publisher=British Polling Council|accessdate=2009-08-14|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100413051900/http://www.britishpollingcouncil.org/press041101.html|archive-date=2010-04-13|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://populus.co.uk/politics-category.html|title=Polling Archive > Politics|publisher=Populus|accessdate=2009-08-14|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090831064526/http://populus.co.uk/politics-category.html|archive-date=2009-08-31|url-status=dead}}</ref> It is also a member of the [[Market Research Society]]<ref>https://www.mrs.org.uk/researchcompany/populus-ltd</ref>

Populus conduct telephone research using [[Random digit dialing]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.populusdatasolutions.com/called-by-us/|title=Populus Data Solutions web site.|year=2012|accessdate=2013-06-30}}</ref>
