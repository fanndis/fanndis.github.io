The '''''General-Anzeiger''''' is a regional daily [[newspaper]] based in the city of [[Bonn]],<ref>{{cite web|title=Successful start for series of debates hosted by GIZ and General-Anzeiger|url=https://www.giz.de/en/mediacenter/10884.html|publisher=GIZ|accessdate=9 February 2015|date=2013}}</ref> the former [[West Germany|West German]] capital in the federal state of [[North Rhine-Westphalia]], [[Germany]]. The paper was first published in April 1888.<ref>{{cite news|title=General Anzeiger|url=http://www.drehscheibe.org/tl_files/drehscheibe/Ideen/Deutschlandkarte_2013_neu/General_Anzeiger_Rhauderfehn_2013_neu.pdf|accessdate=9 May 2016|work=Drehscheibe|date=2013}}</ref> In addition to the city and its surroundings, the [[distribution (business)|distribution]] of the newspaper and its local editions extends to the neighboring districts of [[Rhein-Sieg]], [[Ahrweiler (district)|Ahrweiler]] and [[Neuwied (district)|Neuwied]]. It is published daily, except Sundays. In the second [[fiscal year|quarter]] of 2008, the ''General-Anzeiger'' recorded average daily [[circulation (newspaper)|circulation figures]] of 85,176.
