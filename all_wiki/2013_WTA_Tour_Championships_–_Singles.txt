{{main|2013 WTA Tour Championships}}

World No. 1 [[Serena Williams]] successfully defended her title, beating [[Li Na]] in the final, 2–6, 6–3, 6–0.<ref>{{cite web|url = https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/tennis/24694991|title=Serena Williams beats Li Na to win WTA Championships|date=27 October 2013|work=BBC Sport|accessdate=29 October 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url = https://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/tennis/10407986/Serena-Williams-signs-off-a-year-of-dominance-and-7.6million-won-with-victory-over-Li-Na-in-Istanbul.html|title=Serena Williams signs off a year of dominance and £7.6million won with victory over Li Na in Istanbul|date=27 October 2013|work=Daily Telegraph|accessdate=29 October 2013}}</ref>
