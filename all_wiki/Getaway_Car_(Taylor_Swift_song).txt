"'''Getaway Car'''" is a song by American singer-songwriter [[Taylor Swift]], recorded for her sixth studio album, ''[[Reputation (Taylor Swift album)|Reputation]]'' (2017). Swift wrote and produced the song with [[Jack Antonoff]]. It was serviced to Australian and New Zealand radio stations as the album's final single in those countries.

==Writing and composition==

"Getaway Car" was written and produced by Swift and [[Jack Antonoff]]. "Getaway Car" runs for a duration of three minutes and 53 seconds;<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://listen.tidal.com/album/81185009|title=reputation by Taylor Swift on TIDAL|date=November 10, 2017|website=[[Tidal (service)|TIDAL]]|accessdate=March 12, 2018|archive-date=August 22, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180822145644/https://listen.tidal.com/album/81185009|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.theatlantic.com/entertainment/archive/2017/11/reputation-taylor-swift-first-review/545561/|title=The Old Taylor Swift Is Hiding Within Reputation|date=November 10, 2017|first=Spencer|last=Kornhaber|website=[[The Atlantic]]|accessdate=September 12, 2018|archive-date=June 25, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180625050316/https://www.theatlantic.com/entertainment/archive/2017/11/reputation-taylor-swift-first-review/545561/|url-status=live}}</ref> and it has a [[Tempo (music)|tempo]] of 86 [[beats per minute]]. Swift performs it in the [[Key (music)|key]] of [[C major]] in a vocal range span from [[G (musical note)|G<sub>3</sub>]] to [[D (musical note)|D<sub>5</sub>]].<ref name="musicnotes">{{cite web|url=https://www.musicnotes.com/sheetmusic/mtd.asp?ppn=MN0179491|title=Digital Sheet Music â€“ Delicate|work=Musicnotes.com|publisher=[[Universal Music Publishing Group]]|accessdate=September 12, 2018|archive-date=July 19, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180719173730/https://www.musicnotes.com/sheetmusic/mtd.asp?ppn=MN0179491|url-status=live}}</ref> Shortly after the two-minute mark, there is a key change to [[D major]]. Musically, the track is a [[synth-pop]] number, reminiscent of the works of her ''[[1989 (Taylor Swift album)|1989]]'' era.<ref name="rs">{{cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/albumreviews/taylor-swift-reputation-sheffield-review-most-intimate-lp-w511359|title=Taylor Swift 'Reputation' Is the Most Intimate LP of Taylor Swift's Carrer|author=Rob Sheffield|work=[[Rolling Stone]]|date=November 10, 2017|accessdate=November 9, 2017|archive-date=November 10, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171110140315/http://www.rollingstone.com/music/albumreviews/taylor-swift-reputation-sheffield-review-most-intimate-lp-w511359|url-status=live}}</ref>

Lyrically, the song speaks about attempting to leave a "doomed" relationship for someone else, using [[Crime scene getaway|crime scene escape]] metaphors. Swift also mentions the infamous criminal couple [[Bonnie and Clyde]] in the lyrics.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://genius.com/Taylor-swift-getaway-car-lyrics |title=Taylor Swift - Getaway Car lyrics |work=[[Genius (website)|Genius]] |accessdate=December 7, 2018 |archive-date=September 25, 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180925141929/https://genius.com/Taylor-swift-getaway-car-lyrics |url-status=live }}</ref>

==Critical reception==
The song received critical acclaim. Zack Schonfeld of ''[[Newsweek]]'' called the song as an "excellent, radiant song" and claimed that the hook is "massive, in both catchiness and energy".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.newsweek.com/taylor-swift-reputation-album-review-gorgeous-ready-708853|title=Taylor Swift's "reputation" album Review|website=Newsweek|access-date=2018-09-24|archive-date=2020-01-07|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200107155910/https://www.newsweek.com/taylor-swift-reputation-album-review-gorgeous-ready-708853|url-status=live}}</ref> In 2019, [[Rob Sheffield]] of ''[[Rolling Stone]]'' ranked "Getaway Car" as Swift's thirteenth best song.<ref>{{cite magazine|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-lists/taylor-swift-songs-ranked-rob-sheffield-201800/getaway-car-2017-202802/|title=All 153 of Taylor Swift's Songs, Ranked|last=Sheffield|first=Rob|magazine=[[Rolling Stone]]|date=December 12, 2019|accessdate=December 16, 2019|archive-date=December 16, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191216162630/https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-lists/taylor-swift-songs-ranked-rob-sheffield-201800/getaway-car-2017-202802/|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Chart performance==
The song debuted at number 9 on New Zealand Hot Singles, and number 33 on Australian Digital Tracks.

==Live performances==
The song had its debut performance at the [[University of Phoenix Stadium]] in [[Glendale, Arizona]], on May 8, 2018, as the start of the three-song encore for [[Taylor Swift's Reputation Stadium Tour|Swift's Reputation Stadium Tour]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8455216/taylor-swift-reputation-tour-set-list |title=Here Are All the Songs Taylor Swift Played on the Opening Night of the Reputation Tour|work= [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|location=US|accessdate=29 November 2018|archivedate= 22 May 2018|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180522091658/https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8455216/taylor-swift-reputation-tour-set-list|url-status=live}}</ref>
