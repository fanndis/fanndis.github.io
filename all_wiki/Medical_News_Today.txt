'''''Medical News Today''''' is a [[World Wide Web|web-based]] outlet for medical information and news, targeted at both the general public and [[physician]]s. All posted content is available [[online]] (>250,000 articles as of January 2014), and the earliest available article dates from May 2003.

The website has been owned by [[Healthline|Healthline Media]] since 2016.<ref>Healthline Media Grows Digital Reach with Acquisition of #1 Website for Medical News Information http://www.prweb.com/releases/2016/04/prweb13368746.htm</ref> The business office for the site is located in Brighton, East Sussex, UK, and a second office is maintained near [[Manchester]].

As of September 2019 it was the #3 most visited health site in the United States.
<ref>{{cite web |last1=PR Newswire |first1=PR Newswire |title=Healthline Media's Medical News Today Becomes #3 Health Media Site |url=https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/healthline-medias-medicalnewstoday-becomes-3-health-media-site-300913268.html |website=PR Newswire |publisher=PR Newswire |accessdate=24 October 2019}}</ref>
As of October 2019, it had a global ranking by Alexa of 869 and a United States ranking of 440.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Alexa |first1=Alexa |title=medicalnewstoday.com Competitive Analysis |url=https://www.alexa.com/siteinfo/medicalnewstoday.com |website=alexa.com |publisher=Alexa |accessdate=24 October 2019}}</ref>
