The '''Order of the Aztec Eagle''' ({{lang-es|Orden Mexicana del Águila Azteca}}) forms part of the [[Mexican Honours System]] and is the highest [[Mexico|Mexican]] [[Order (honour)|order]] awarded to foreigners in the country.

==History==
It was created by decree on December 29, 1933 by [[President of Mexico|President]] [[Abelardo L. Rodríguez]] as a reward to the services given to Mexico or humankind by foreigners. It corresponds to similar distinctions given to Mexican citizens such as the [[Condecoración Miguel Hidalgo]] or the [[Belisario Domínguez Medal of Honor]]. It is given by the office of the [[Foreign minister of Mexico|foreign minister]] on the instructions of a Council established for this purpose headed by the [[President of Mexico|President]].

==Design==
There is some design similarity of the order with the [[coat of arms of Mexico]], particularly the [[golden eagle]] holding a [[rattlesnake]], which is associated with the Aztec civilization.

==Classes==
=== Since 2011 ===
Since the reform of March 2011,<ref>[http://www.dof.gob.mx/avisos/2035/SG010611_01/SG010611_01.htm Reform text] (Spanish)</ref> the classes are :
#[[File:MEX Orden del Aguila Azteca 2011 Collar BAR.svg|60px]] ''Collar'' ("Collar"), awarded to [[Head of state|heads of state]];
#[[File:MEX Orden del Aguila Azteca 2011 Banda Especial BAR.svg|60px]] ''Sash of Special Category'' ("Banda en Categoría Especial"), awarded to [[prime minister]]s and [[Head of government|heads of government]], hereditary princes(ses), consorts of heads of state, or to people whose category is tantamount to the previous;
#[[File:MEX Orden del Aguila Azteca 2011 Banda BAR.svg|60px]] ''Sash'' ("Banda"), awarded to [[minister (government)|government ministers]], [[Secretary|secretaries]], members of royal families, [[ambassador]]s, or individuals whose category is tantamount to the previous;
#[[File:MEX Orden del Aguila Azteca 2011 Placa BAR.svg|60px]] ''Plaque'' ("Placa"), awarded to government [[undersecretary|undersecretaries]], [[plenipotentiary]] ministers, consuls general, brigadier generals, rear admirals, as well to those whose category is tantamount to the previous;
#[[File:MEX Orden del Aguila Azteca 2011 Venera BAR.svg|60px]] ''Venera'' (a type of insignia),  awarded to ''[[ad hoc]]'' business representatives, colonels and lieutenant colonels, captains of a ship, equivalent servants of [[Embassy|embassies]] in Mexico, as well to those whose category is tantamount to the previous;
#[[File:MEX Orden del Aguila Azteca 2011 Insignia BAR.svg|60px]] ''Insignia'', awarded to ad interim business representatives and other members of diplomatic missions; captain, navy lieutenant, as well to those whose category is tantamount to the previous and to those cases the Council considers pertinent.

All grades except the collar may be awarded, under the Council's discretion, to distinguished foreigners.

=== Before 2011 ===
Prior to the 2011 reform, the classes were, in descending order:
#[[File:MEX Order of the Aztec Eagle 1Class BAR.png|60px]] ''Collar'' ("Collar"), awarded to [[Head of state|heads of state]];
#[[File:MEX Order of the Aztec Eagle 1Class BAR.png|60px]] ''Grand Cross'' ("Cruz"), awarded to [[prime minister]]s and [[Head of government|heads of government]]
#[[File:MEX Order of the Aztec Eagle 1Class BAR.png|60px]] ''Sash'' ("Banda"), awarded to [[minister (government)|government ministers]], [[Secretary|secretaries]] and [[ambassador]]s
#[[File:MEX Order of the Aztec Eagle 2Class BAR.png|60px]] ''Medal'' ("Medalla"), awarded to government [[undersecretary|undersecretaries]], [[plenipotentiary]] ministers, as well to those whose category is tantamount to the previous;
#[[File:MEX Order of the Aztec Eagle 3Class BAR.png|60px]] ''Placard'' ("Placa"), awarded to ''[[ad hoc]]'' business representatives, colonels and lieutenant colonels, captains of a ship, equivalent servants of [[Embassy|embassies]] in Mexico), as well to those whose category is tantamount to the previous;
#[[File:MEX Order of the Aztec Eagle 4Class BAR.png|60px]] ''Venera'' (a type of insignia), awarded to ad interim business representatives and other members of diplomatic missions;
#[[File:MEX Order of the Aztec Eagle 5Class BAR.png|60px]] ''Insignia proper'', awarded under the Council's discretion; and
#[[File:MEX Order of the Aztec Eagle 5Class BAR.png|60px]] ''Honorable Mention'' ("Mención Honorífica").
