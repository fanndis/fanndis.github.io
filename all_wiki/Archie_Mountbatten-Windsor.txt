'''Archie Harrison Mountbatten-Windsor''' (born 6 May 2019) is a member of the [[British royal family]]. He is the son of [[Prince Harry, Duke of Sussex]], and [[Meghan, Duchess of Sussex]], and seventh in the [[line of succession to the British throne]].

==Title and succession==
As a great-grandchild of [[Queen Elizabeth II]],<ref name="guard">{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/uk-news/2019/may/06/meghan-duchess-of-sussex-gives-birth-to-boy|title=Royal baby: Meghan, Although not a [[royal prince]] nor entitled to the style of [[royal highness]], as [[heir apparent]] to his father's [[Dukedom of Sussex]], [[Earldom of Dumbarton]], and [[Barony of Kilkeel]], he would customarily have been entitled to use Prince Harry's senior [[subsidiary title]] Earl of Dumbarton as a [[Courtesy titles in the United Kingdom|courtesy]].<ref name="guard" /><ref>{{cite Kingdom|Master]] Archie Harrison Mountbatten-Windsor,<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-48204592|title=Archie Harrison: The meaning behind the royal baby's name|work=[[BBC News]]|date=8
