The '''2015 Swedish Open''' was a [[tennis]] tournament played on [[Tennis court#Clay courts|outdoor clay courts]] as part of the ATP World Tour 250 Series of the [[2015 ATP World Tour]] and as part of the International Series on the [[2015 WTA Tour]]. It took place in [[Båstad]], [[Sweden]], from 13 through 19 July 2015 for the women's tournament, and from 20 through 26 July 2015 for the men's tournament. It was also known as the '''2015 SkiStar Swedish Open''' for the men and the '''2015 Collector Swedish Open''' for the women for sponsorship reasons. It was the 68th edition of the event for the men and the 7th edition for the women.
