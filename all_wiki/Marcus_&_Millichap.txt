'''Marcus & Millichap, Inc.''' provides [[real estate broker]]age, [[joint venture broker]]age, [[mortgage broker]]age, research, and advisory services in the U.S. and Canada in the field of [[commercial property]]. It popularized the practice of listing properties exclusively with one [[brokerage firm]].

==History==
The company was founded in [[Palo Alto]] in 1971 by [[George M. Marcus]].<ref>{{cite web | title=Profile: Marcus & Millichap, Inc. | url=https://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=4249042 | publisher=[[Bloomberg L.P.]]}}</ref><ref name=expanding>{{Cite news | url=https://www.bizjournals.com/sanjose/news/2015/12/03/marcus-millichap-aims-for-bigger-piece-of-property.html | title=Marcus & Millichap expanding Institutional Property Advisors into office/industrial | first=Nathan | last=Donato-Weinstein | work=[[American City Business Journals]] | date=December 3, 2015}}</ref>

William Millichap was one of the first salespeople Marcus hired, and was made a partner in 1976.<ref name=bayarea/>

In the early 1990s, it put together [[Essex Property Trust]], also controlled by George M. Marcus.<ref>{{cite news | last=Zuckerman | first=Laurence | title=Questioning the Realty-Trust King | url=https://www.nytimes.com/1994/08/04/business/questioning-the-realty-trust-king.html | work=[[The New York Times]] | date=August 4, 1994 |url-access=subscription }}</ref><ref name=bayarea>{{cite news | last1=Metcalf | first1=Tom | last2=Brandt | first2=Nadja | title=Bay Area Broker Becomes Billionaire on Sub $10 Million Listings | url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2015-06-01/bay-area-broker-becomes-billionaire-on-sub-10-million-listings | work=[[Bloomberg News]] | date=June 1, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | last=Berry | first=Jahna | title=George Marcus's Marcus & Millichap closed most commercial real estate deals in U.S. in 2014 | url=https://www.bizjournals.com/sanfrancisco/morning_call/2015/06/george-marcus-millichap-essex-mmi-cre-profile.html | work=[[American City Business Journals]] | date=June 4, 2015}}</ref>

In 2013, it became a [[public company]] via an [[initial public offering]].<ref name=expanding/>

In February 2020, the company announced the promotion of James Shiebler to VP of investments in the company's Tampa, FL office.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cpexecutive.com/post/marcus-millichap-names-vice-president-in-florida/|title=Marcus & Millichap Names Vice President in Florida|website=www.cpexecutive.com|access-date=2020-02-28}}</ref>
