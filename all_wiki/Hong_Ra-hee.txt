'''Hong Ra-hee''' (born 15 July 1945) is a South Korean billionaire businesswoman and philanthropist, who is the director of the [[Leeum, Samsung Museum of Art]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20110329000654|title=Hong Ra-hee makes comeback as Leeum's director|website=[[The Korea Herald]]|accessdate=|date=2011-03-29}}</ref> She is the widow of [[Lee Kun-hee]], who was the richest citizen in South Korea. She is known as the most powerful art collector in South Korea.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.kookje.co.kr/news2011/asp/newsbody.asp?code=0300&key=20170307.99002001034|title=홍라희 관장은 누구?...미술계 영향력 1위, `행복한 눈물` 사건 겪기도|website=국제신문|access-date=2020-03-01}}</ref>

== Career ==
Hong graduated from Seoul National University. She majored in Applied Arts.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://news.joins.com/article/2556065|title=30대 그룹 회장 부인, 여성 회장 이화여대 출신 13명으로 최다|last=지면보기|first=입력 2007 01 04 05:31 수정 2007 01 04 08:34 {{!}} 종합 13면|date=2007-01-04|website=중앙일보|language=ko|access-date=2020-03-01}}</ref> She is the co-founder of [[Leeum, Samsung Museum of Art]], which she has built with her husband in 2004. Hong's collection includes [[Lee Ufan]], [[Do-ho Suh]], [[Whanki Kim]], [[Jackson Pollock]], [[Mark Rothko]], and [[Andy Warhol]].      

Hong began her career at JoongAng Ilbo [[Publishing]] from 1975 to 1980.    

She served as a [[Chairperson]] of Samsung Arts and Cultural Foundation since 1995.       

==Family==
Her daughters are joint presidents of [[Samsung C&T Corporation]], while her daughter Lee Seo-hyun oversees [[Samsung C&T Corporation#Fashion Group|Samsung's fashion division]], and her other daughter Lee Boo-jin oversees the [[Samsung C&T Corporation#Resort .26 Construction Group|resort division]], which included the [[Everland Resort]]. Her father [[Hong Jin-ki]] was chairman of [[JoongAng Ilbo]], which is now run by her brother, and also an identified [[Chinilpa]]. 

Her brother [[Hong Seok-hyun]] is the ex-CEO of JoongAng Media Group. It is now run by [[Jeongdo Hong|Hong Jeongdo]], Hong's nephew.  

Her ex-daughter-in-law was Lim Se-ryung, the daughter of Daesang Group's chairman Lim Chang-Wook. Lim was married to Hong's son [[Lee Jae-yong (businessman)|Lee Jae-yong]] in 1998 and divorced in 2009.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://woman.donga.com/3/all/12/140537/1|title=삼성이재용 전무 임세령씨 파경 내막 ＆ 임씨 친정어머니 단독 인터뷰|date=2009-03-24|website=woman.donga.com|language=ko|access-date=2020-03-01}}</ref>
