==Career==
In 2004, Jameel trained at the [[Toyota Motor Corporation]] in Japan, in their domestic [[kaizen]] department.<ref name=PresidentsCouncilWhosWho/><ref name=FBN2014/>

Following his period at Toyota, he returned to Saudi Arabia to work in the family-owned business Abdul Latif Jameel.<ref name=PresidentsCouncilWhosWho/><ref name=FBN2014/><ref name=UTokyoGABbio>[https://www.u-tokyo.ac.jp/en/about/hassan-jameel.html "Mr. Hassan Jameel Profile"]. Jameel is currently Deputy President and Vice Chairman of domestic Saudi Arabia operations of Abdul Latif Jameel, with responsibility for operations such as automotive, land and real estate, and machinery.<ref name=UTokyoGABMembersNov2018>[https://www.u-tokyo.ac.jp/content/400103699.pdf#page=9 "Biographies of UTokyo Global Advisory Board Members"].
