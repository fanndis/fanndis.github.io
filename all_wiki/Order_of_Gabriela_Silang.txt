The '''Order of Gabriela Silang''' ({{lang-fil|Orden ni Gabriela Silang}}) is an all-female order of the [[Philippines]] founded on September 19, 2003.<ref name="eo236">[https://www.officialgazette.gov.ph/2003/09/19/executive-order-no-236/ Executive Order No. 236, s. 2003] Official Gazette of the Republic of the Philippines. Retrieved 20 May 2020.</ref>

== Award and Rank ==
The Order of Gabriela Silang is a single-class Order which may be conferred upon the spouses of heads of state and/or of government, both Filipino and foreign.<ref name="eo236" />

== Insignia ==
The ''ribbon'' of the order is red with yellow and blue borders.<ref>[http://www.medals.org.uk/philippines/philippines043.htm The Philippines: Order of Gabriela Silang] World Medals Index. Retrieved 13 April 2013.</ref>
