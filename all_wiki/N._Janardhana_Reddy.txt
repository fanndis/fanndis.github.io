'''Nedurumalli Janardhana Reddy''' (20 February 1935 – 9 May 2014) was a member of the 12th, 13th and 14th [[Lok Sabha]] of [[India]]. He represented the [[Visakhapatnam (Lok Sabha Constituency)|Visakhapatnam]] constituency of [[Andhra Pradesh]] and was a member of the [[Indian National Congress]]. He was also a [[List of Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh|Chief Minister]] of Andhra Pradesh from 1990 to 1992.<ref name="Hindu10514"/> His wife, Nedurumalli Rajyalakshmi, has been a minister in the Andhra Pradesh government since 2004.

In September 2007, Reddy escaped an assassination attempt near [[Nellore]] by [[naxalite|naxal]] groups. Janardhana Reddy and his wife both escaped the event unhurt.<ref name="Hindu10514"/><ref>{{cite web|last1=Rangarajan|first1=D.|title=Janardhana Reddy escapes bid on life|url=http://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/janardhana-reddy-escapes-bid-on-life/article1907659.ece|website=The Hindu|accessdate=22 April 2016|date=8 September 2007}}</ref>

== Political history ==
{{Expand section|date=October 2012}}
* 1972–1978: Rajya sabha,
* 1978–1984: MLC
* 1978–1983: Andhra Pradesh Cabinet Minister (Revenue, Industries, Power and Agriculture),
* 1988–1989: P.C.C. President,
* 1989–1994: MLA,
* 1989–1990: Andhra Pradesh Cabinet Minister (Agriculture, Forests and Higher Education),
* 1990–1992: Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh,
* 1998–1999: MP (Bapatla&nbsp;– 12th Lok Sabha),
* 1999–2004: MP (Narasaraopet&nbsp;– 13th Lok Sabha&nbsp;– 2nd term),
* 2004–2009: MP (Visakhapatnam&nbsp;– 14th Lok Sabha&nbsp;– 3rd term),
* 2009: Elected to Rajya Sabha,
* 2010: Re-elected to Rajya Sabha
