The [[WTA Tour]] is the elite tour for professional women's [[tennis]] organised by the [[Women's Tennis Association]] (WTA). The WTA Tour includes the four [[Grand Slam (tennis)|Grand Slam]] tournaments, the [[WTA Tour Championships]] and the [[WTA Tier I]], Tier II, Tier III and Tier IV events. ITF tournaments are not part of the WTA Tour, although they award points for the WTA World Ranking.
