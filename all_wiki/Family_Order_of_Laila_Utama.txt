'''The Most Esteemed Family Order of Laila Utama'''<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.adat-istiadat.gov.bn/SiteCollectionDocuments/Publishing/SENARAI%20BINTANG-BINTANG%20KEBESARAN%20DAN%20PINGAT-PINGAT%20KEHORMATAN%20NEGARA%20BRUNEI%20DARUSSALAM.pdf|title=Senarai Bintang-Bintang Kebesaran dan Pingat-Pingat Kehormatan Negara Brunei Darussalam|last=|first=|date=|website=www.adat-istiadat.gov.bn|language=Malay|page=1|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=22 July 2019}}</ref> ({{lang-ms|Darjah Kerabat Laila Utama Yang Amat Dihormati}}) is an [[Order (decoration)|order]] of [[Brunei]]. It was established on 1 March 1954 by [[Sultan Omar Ali Saifuddien III]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.medals.org.uk/brunei/brunei003.htm|title=ODM of Brunei: Family Order of Brunei|website=www.medals.org.uk|access-date=22 July 2019}}</ref> The order carries the [[post-nominal letters]] "DK" as well as the [[Malay styles and titles|title]] "Dato Laila Utama".
