'''Jake Nava''' is a British director, mostly known for his work in music videos and TV commercials.

== Career ==
Nava graduated with a degree in film from the University of Westminster.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Photography and Film - Westminster School of Media Arts and Design - University of Westminster, London|url = http://www.westminster.ac.uk/about-us/faculties/media/departments/photography|website = www.westminster.ac.uk|accessdate = 2015-11-27|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20151208070309/http://www.westminster.ac.uk/about-us/faculties/media/departments/photography|archive-date = 2015-12-08|url-status = dead}}</ref> After college, he began his career directing music videos and films for [[MTV Sports]].<ref>{{Cite web|title = Jake Nava {{!}} Cherry|url = http://www.cherrystudio.net/jake-nava.html|website = www.cherrystudio.net|accessdate = 2015-11-27}}</ref> Nava has created videos for various artists, including [[Britney Spears]], [[Beyoncé]], [[Arctic Monkeys]] and [[Adele]].<ref>{{Cite web|title = Jake Nava {{!}} IMVDb|url = http://imvdb.com/n/jake-nava|website = IMVDb|accessdate = 2015-11-27}}</ref> Nava has also directed advertising campaigns for [[Armani]], [[Puma SE|Puma]], [[Bacardi]], and more. In 2013, Nava directed three music videos for [[Beyoncé]]’s [[Beyoncé (album)|visual album]] (“[[Partition (song)|Partition]],” “[[Beyoncé (album)|Grown Woman]],” and “[[Flawless (Beyoncé song)|Flawless]]”).

In 2014, Nava collaborated with [[Lana Del Rey]] on '[[Shades of Cool]]'. In 2015, Nava directed his first commercial for Guinness, titled 'Intolerant Champion'. In 2016, Nava directed Martini's 'Play With Time', and a second spot for Guinness, titled "We Are Made of Football". In 2017, Nava directed campaigns for Bacardi, Mastercard, Vauxhall "Pyjama Mamas" for Mother and Levi's "Circles".
