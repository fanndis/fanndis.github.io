'''''Little Big Shots'''''  is an American [[variety show|variety]] television series co-created and produced by [[Steve Harvey]] and [[Ellen DeGeneres]]. The series features children demonstrating [[talent show|talents]] and engaging in conversation with the host.

The series was ordered by [[NBC]] & [[Citytv]] in May 2015 for an eight-episode first season,<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thewrap.com/ellen-degeneres-steve-harvey-variety-show-little-big-shots-ordered-by-nbc/|title=Ellen DeGeneres-Steve Harvey Variety Show 'Little Big Shots' Ordered by NBC|date=May 9, 2015|work=TheWrap|access-date=August 5, 2017|language=en-US}}</ref> which premiered on March 8, 2016 with Steve Harvey as host. On March 14, 2016, NBC renewed the series for a second season, which premiered on March 5, 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2016/tv/news/little-big-shots-steve-harvey-renewed-season-2-nbc-1201729986/|title='Little Big Shots' Renewed for Season 2 at NBC|last=Littleton|first=Cynthia|work=Variety|date=March 14, 2016|accessdate=March 14, 2016}}</ref><ref name="Season 2">{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2016/12/nbc-midseason-schedule-trial-error-great-news-succeed-this-is-us-shades-of-blue-moves-sunday-dc-powerless-thursday-1201866933/|title=NBC Midseason Schedule: 'Trial & Error' & 'Great News' Succeed 'This Is Us', 'Shades Of Blue' Moves, DC's 'Powerless' Slotted|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=Deadline Hollywood|date=December 8, 2016|accessdate=December 8, 2016}}</ref> The series was renewed for a third season, which premiered on March 18, 2018.<ref name="NBC Spring 2018">{{cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2018/01/05/nbc-sets-sunday-lineup-with-little-big-shots-genius-junior-561212/12380/|title=NBC Sets Sunday Lineup with "Little Big Shots," "Genius Junior"|work=[[The Futon Critic]]|accessdate=January 5, 2018}}</ref> A special holiday episode aired in [[Citytv]] December 2018. In May 2019, nearly a year after the third season finished airing, it was announced that the series had been renewed for a fourth season and that [[Melissa McCarthy]] would be replacing Harvey as the new host.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2019/05/melissa-mccarthy-nbc-little-big-shots-1202612992/|title=Melissa McCarthy To Replace Steve Harvey As Host Of 'Little Big Shots' As Variety Show Scores Fourth Season On NBC|work=Deadline Hollywood|first=Peter|last=White|date=May 12, 2019|accessdate=December 17, 2019}}</ref> The fourth season premiered on February 24, 2020.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Mitovich |first1=Matt Webb |title=NBC Sets Return Dates for The Wall (Finally!), "Reimagined" ''Little Big Shots'' |url=https://tvline.com/2020/02/12/the-wall-season-3-premiere-date-nbc/ |website=TVLine |accessdate=12 February 2020}}</ref>
