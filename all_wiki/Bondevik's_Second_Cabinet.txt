'''Bondevik's Second Cabinet''' governed [[Norway]] between 19 October 2001 and 17 October 2005. It was led by [[Kjell Magne Bondevik]] and consisted of the [[Conservative Party of Norway|Conservative Party]], the [[Christian Democratic Party (Norway)|Christian Democratic Party]] and the [[Liberal Party (Norway)|Liberal Party]].
