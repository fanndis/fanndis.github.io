The '''{{lang|fr|Lycée Guébré-Mariam|italic=no}}''' ('''LGM''') or '''{{lang|fr|Lycée franco-éthiopien Guébré-Mariam|italic=no}}''' ({{lang-am|ገብረ ማርያም ትምህርት ቤት}}) is a French international school in [[Addis Ababa]], [[Ethiopia]]. It was established in 1947 and has integrated the [[Mission laïque française]] in 1947.<ref>{{cite book

== Namesake ==
The school's name commemorates [[Dedjazmatch]] Guebre Mariam Gari (or, without his military title: Guebre Mariam Gari), who was an anti-fascist resistance fighter during the [[Second Italo-Abyssinian War]] in 1935, when Italy invade [[Abyssinia]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Présentation d’une biographie de Guebre Mariam Gari |url=https://et.ambafrance.org/Presentation-d-une-biographie-de-Guebre-Mariam-Gari |website=Ambassade de France à Addis Abeba |language=fr |date=2016 |quote=les petits-enfants du Dedjazmatch Guebre Mariam Gari, qui a donné son nom au lycée français d’Addis Abeba, ont présenté une biographie de leur grand-père, héros de la résistance éthiopienne contre l’invasion fasciste en 1935.}}</ref> His name, Guebre Mariam, literally means “servant of Mary”.<ref>{{cite journal |last1=Berlan |first1=Édouard |title=L'installation humaine au Choa |journal=Revue de Géographie Alpine |date=1952 |volume=40 |issue=4 |pages=615–623 |doi=10.3406/rga.1952.1071 |url=https://www.persee.fr/doc/rga_0035-1121_1952_num_40_4_1071 |language=fr |quote= [Guebre Mariam] « Serviteur de Marie », nom très commun au Choa.}} Translation: "a very common name in [[Shewa]]", a region of Ethiopia.</ref>

The hyphen in the school's name is due to a French punctuation convention. (See [[French orthography#Hyphens]])
