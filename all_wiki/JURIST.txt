A '''jurist''' is a person with expert knowledge of [[law]];  someone who analyses and comments on law.<ref>{{cite book |author=Vieto Piergiovanni|title=Comparative Studies in Continental and Anglo-American Legal History|location=Germany|publisher=Duncker & Humblot|page=236 |date=2000 |isbn=978-3428097562}}</ref><ref>"One who professes or treats of law;  one versed in the science of law;  a legal writer":  {{cite book|title=[[Oxford English Dictionary]] online|location=Oxford|publisher=Oxford University Press|chapter=Jurist<!--entry needing update-->}}</ref>  This person is usually a specialist '''legal scholar'''{{mdash}}not necessarily with a formal qualification in law or a [[lawyer|legal practitioner]], although in the United States the term "jurist" may be applied to a judge.<ref>{{cite book|editor-last=Garner|editor-first=Bryan A.|title=Black's Law Dictionary|year=2019|location=St. Paul, Minn.|publisher=West|edition=11|chapter=Jurist}}</ref> With reference to [[Roman law]], a "jurist" (in English) is a jurisconsult (''jurisconsulta'').<ref>{{cite web |title=Definition of Jurisconsult|url=https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/jurisconsult |website=www.merriam-webster.com |language=en}}</ref>

The English term ''jurist'' is to be distinguished from similar terms in other European languages, where it may be synonymous with ''[[legal profession]]al'', i.e. anyone with a professional law degree that qualifies for legal work.{{cn|date=November 2020}}<!--This is not a reference, and does not establish the claim made in the statement it follows; it merely gives translations of the term in selected European languages: <ref>For example: [[German language|German]] ''Jurist'', [[Norwegian language|Norwegian]]/[[Danish language|Danish]]/[[Swedish language|Swedish]]/[[Dutch language|Dutch]] ''jurist'', [[French language|French]] ''juriste'', [[Italian language|Italian]] ''giurista'', [[Spanish language|Spanish]]/[[Catalan language|Catalan]]/[[Portuguese language|Portuguese]] ''jurista'', [[Galician language|Galician]] ''xurista'', [[Russian language|Russian]] {{lang|ru|юрист}}.</ref> -->
