'''Ivan Vasyunik''' is a [[Ukrainians|Ukrainian]] politician, who served in different posts, including vice prime minister.

==Career==
Vasyunik served as first deputy secretary of [[Presidential Administration of Ukraine]] until 2005.<ref name=ria6sep>{{cite news|title=Yushchenko appoints acting secretary of state|url=http://en.rian.ru/world/20050906/41313582.html|accessdate=11 June 2013|newspaper=Ria Novosti|date=6 September 2005|location=Kiev}}</ref> He was appointed acting presidential secretary on 6 September 2005.<ref>{{cite web|title=President appoints Ivan Vasyunik acting Secretary of State|url=http://www.kmu.gov.ua/control/en/publish/article?art_id=20796215&cat_id=32598|publisher=Government portal|accessdate=11 June 2013|date=6 September 2005}}</ref> He replaced [[Oleksandr Zinchenko (politician)|Oleksandr Zinchenko]] in the post.<ref name=ria6sep/> 

In late 2007, Vasyunik was appointed vice prime minister to [[Second Tymoshenko Government|the cabinet]] led by prime minister [[Yulia Tymoshenko]].<ref name=cep12dec>{{cite news|title=Yushchenko to submit Timoshenko candidature for PM 2nd time|url=http://cep.ut.ac.ir/en/pages/news/623.htm|accessdate=11 June 2013|work=CEP|date=12 December 2007|agency=Itar Tass|location=Kiev|archive-url=https://archive.is/20130620211600/http://cep.ut.ac.ir/en/pages/news/623.htm#|archive-date=20 June 2013|url-status=dead|df=dmy-all}}</ref><ref name=cee2007>{{cite journal|title=Tymoshenko|journal=CEE Biweekly|date=20 December 2007|url=http://mediaserver.fxstreet.com/Reports/17049c9e-669f-4a64-ad9c-a4d78ab619f0/e1243e31-eec5-40fb-94f4-dfaf7bc91193.pdf|accessdate=11 June 2013}}</ref> At that position he was put in charge for the preparation to the [[Euro 2012]].<ref>[http://www.ukrinform.ua/eng/news/vice_prime_minister_ivan_vasyunyk_leaves_for_working_visit_to_lviv_118953 Vice Prime Minister Ivan Vasyunyk Leaves for Working Visit to Lviv]. [[Ukrinform]]</ref>
