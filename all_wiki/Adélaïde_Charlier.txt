'''Adélaïde Charlier''' (born {{birth based on age as of date |18 |2019|03|07|noage=1}})<ref>{{cite tweet |user= RTLFrance|number=1103911110581772288 |date=March 7, 2019|title=Climat : "Les vieux ont agi mais pas suffisamment, ils n'ont pas compris l'urgence dans laquelle on se trouve. She co-edited a statement for the [[The Guardian|Guardian]].<ref>{{Cite news|last1=Thunberg|first1=Greta|url=https://www.theguardian.com/commentisfree/2019/mar/15/school-climate-strike-greta-thunberg|title=Think we should be at school? ==Life==
She is studying at Collège Notre-Dame de la Paix in [[Namur]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.opengovpartnership.org/people/adelaide-charlier/|title=Adélaïde Charlier|website=Open Government Partnership|language=en|access-date=2020-03-20}}</ref> In 2019, with [[Anuna De Wever]], she co-created the Belgian ‘Youth for Climate’ movement.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rtbf.be/info/societe/detail_les-belges-anuna-de-wever-et-adelaide-charlier-ne-seront-pas-presentes-a-la-cop25-a-madrid?id=10367985|title=Les Belges Anuna De Wever et Adélaïde Charlier ne seront pas présentes à la COP25 à Madrid|date=2019-11-18|website=RTBF Info|language=fr|access-date=2020-03-20}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.brusselstimes.com/belgium/101724/flemish-opera-singer-gives-impromptu-performance-amid-coronavirus-shutdown/|title=Belgian climate leaders Anuna De Wever and Adélaïde Charlier will not make it to Madrid|last=|first=|date=2020-03-20|website=The Brussels Times|language=en|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2020-03-20}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rtbf.be/info/societe/detail_anuna-de-wever-et-adelaide-charlier-sont-de-retour-en-belgique-apres-plus-de-trois-mois-en-amerique-du-sud?id=10411570|title=Anuna De Wever et Adélaïde Charlier sont de retour en Belgique, après plus de trois mois en Amérique du Sud|date=2020-01-20|website=RTBF Info|language=fr|access-date=2020-03-20}}</ref> She organized [[School strike for climate|climate marches]] in Belgium.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://magazine.areweeurope.com/stories/elections-issue/melissa-vida-belgium-anuna-de-weaver-adelaide-charlier-climate-activism|title=Skipping School to Save the World|website=Are We Europe Magazine|language=en-US|access-date=2020-03-20}}</ref>
