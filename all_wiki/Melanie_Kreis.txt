'''Melanie Kreis''' (born 1971) is a German businesswoman, and the [[chief financial officer]] (CFO) of [[Deutsche Post]], the world's largest logistics company, since October 2016.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.dpdhl.com/de/ueber-uns/dpdhl-management/vorstand/melanie-kreis.html|title=Melanie Kreis|website=DPDHL|language=de|access-date=2018-07-06}}</ref><ref name="forbes.com">{{cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/profile/melanie-kreis/?list=power-women|title=Melanie Kreis|author=|date=|website=forbes.com|accessdate=2 November 2017}}</ref>

== Education ==
Kreis attended the [[Stony Brook University|State University of New York at Stony Brook]], US, and graduated in 1994 with a master's in physics and obtained another degree in physics in 1997 from the [[University of Bonn]]. In 2000 she graduated with a [[Master of Business Administration]] at [[INSEAD]], France.<ref name=":0" />

Kreis began her career in 1997 at the management consultancy [[McKinsey & Company]] in [[Cologne]]. In 2000 she then moved to [[Apax Partners]], based in [[Munich]] and [[London]].<ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url=https://www.finance-magazin.de/finance-koepfe/cfos/melanie-kreis-deutsche-post-dhl-ag/|title=Melanie Kreis, Deutsche Post DHL AG|access-date=2018-07-10|language=de}}</ref> She has been working for [[Deutsche Post DHL]] since 2004 and was appointed to the board in October 2014.<ref name=":0" />

== Career ==
At Deutsche Post, Kreis began her career in the corporate development department in [[Bonn]]. Later she worked in [[England]], advancing the integration of [[Exel]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.sueddeutsche.de/wirtschaft/talente-melanie-kreis-jung-weiblich-ehrgeizig-1.587554|title=Jung, weiblich, ehrgeizig|last=Dohmen|first=Caspar|date=2010|work=sueddeutsche.de|access-date=2018-07-10|language=de|issn=0174-4917}}</ref> In 2006 Kreis was appointed executive vice president corporate office, corporate organization. Since 2009 she then served as leader of group controlling.<ref name=":0" /> In April 2013 she was appointed as [[chief financial officer]] (CFO) of [[DHL Express]].<ref name=":1" /> She has been a member of the executive board of Deutsche Post since 2014 as [[chief human resources officer]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.humanresourcesmanager.de/personalien/melanie-kreis-wird-personalvorstand-der-deutschen-post-dhl.html|title=Melanie Kreis wird Personalvorstand der Deutschen Post DHL|website=www.humanresourcesmanager.de|language=de|access-date=2018-07-11}}</ref> Since October 2016 Kreis is CFO of Deutsche Post.<ref name=":1" />

== Awards ==
Kreis was named as one of the "75 most influential women in German Business" and as "woman of the year" by ''[[Manager Magazin]]'' and [[Boston Consulting Group]] (BCG) in 2016.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.manager-magazin.de/magazin/artikel/manager-magazin-prima-inter-pares-2016-melanie-kreis-a-1130976.html|title=manager magazin: Prima inter pares 2016 - Melanie Kreis - manager magazin|work=manager magazin|access-date=2018-07-11}}</ref> In November 2017, she was assessed by ''[[Forbes]]'' as being the 67th most powerful woman in the world.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/power-women/list/2/#tab:overall|title=World's Most Powerful Women|author=|date=|website=forbes.com|accessdate=2 November 2017}}</ref><ref name="forbes.com" />
