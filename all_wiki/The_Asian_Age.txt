'''''The Asian Age''''' is an English-language [[India]]n daily [[newspaper]] with editions published in [[Delhi]], [[Mumbai]] and [[Kolkata]]. It also prints an "international edition" in [[London]]. It was launched in February 1994.<ref name="aboutus">{{cite web |url= http://www.asianage.com/content/about-us |title=About Us |work=The Asian Age |year=2011}}</ref>

The same publishing company also produces the ''[[Deccan Chronicle]]''.<ref name="aboutus"/>
