The '''2015 [[Indian Wells Masters|BNP Paribas Open]]''' (also known as the 2015 [[Indian Wells Masters]]) was a professional [[tennis]] tournament played at [[Indian Wells, California]],<ref>{{Cite web
| url = http://www.wtatennis.com/tournaments/tournamentId/543/title/indian-wells--bnp-paribas-open
| title = BNP Paribas Open
| website = Women's Tennis Association
| access-date = 2016-02-28
}}</ref> in March 2015. It was the 42nd edition of the men's event (27th for the women), known as the [[BNP Paribas Open]], and was classified as an [[ATP World Tour Masters 1000]] event on the [[2015 ATP World Tour]] and a [[WTA Premier tournaments|Premier Mandatory]] event on the [[2015 WTA Tour]]. Both the men's and the women's events took place at the [[Indian Wells Tennis Garden]] in Indian Wells, United States, from March 11 through March 22, 2015, on outdoor [[hard court]]s.
