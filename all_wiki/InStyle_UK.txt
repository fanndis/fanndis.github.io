'''''InStyle''''', published by [[IPC Media#Southbank|IPC Media]], was a monthly glossy [[magazine]] focusing on [[celebrities]] and their [[fashion|style]]. The British edition launched in 2001, following the success of the magazine in the US. The current editor is Trish Halpin, who commenced the position in September 2006.<ref name="Press">{{cite web|url=http://www.pressgazette.co.uk/node/42655 |title=In Style editor Trish Halpin takes helm at Marie Claire |work=Press Gazette |date=12 December 2008 |accessdate=7 February 2014 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140225203227/http://www.pressgazette.co.uk/node/42655 |archivedate=25 February 2014 |df=dmy-all }}</ref> The circulation of ''In Style'' was currently more than 145,000 in 2013.<ref name=Circ/> In October 2016 the print version was cancelled and it became a web-only publication.<ref>{{cite news|author1=Jasper Jackson|title=InStyle UK magazine to shut print edition|url=https://www.theguardian.com/media/2016/oct/19/instyle-uk-magazine-digital-only-time-inc?subid=1254639&CMP=ema_546|accessdate=6 December 2016|work=The Guardian|date=19 October 2016}}</ref>

With the strapline "Your Own Personal Stylist", the magazine offers [[fashion]] and [[beauty]] tips to its readers.{{Citation needed|date=February 2011}} It also focuses on [[celebrity]], with stars instead of models as subjects of the [[fashion]] and [[lifestyle (sociology)|lifestyle]] shoots.

== Brand extension ==
In May 2007 ''In Style'' launched its website.  The daily updated{{citation needed|date=February 2014}} website publishes red carpet photos, A-list party features, editors' [[fashion]] and [[beauty]] picks, blogs written by the magazine team and behind-the-scenes videos of the magazine's celebrity photo shoots.{{citation needed|date=February 2014}} On average, the site achieves 2 million page impressions a month and 240,000 unique users a month.{{citation needed|date=February 2014}} The most searched for celebrities on the site are [[Jennifer Aniston]], [[Sienna Miller]], [[Victoria Beckham]], [[Kate Moss]], [[Mischa Barton]], [[Nicole Richie]], [[Lindsay Lohan]], [[Jennifer Lopez]], [[Cameron Diaz]], [[Mary-Kate Olsen]] and [[Ashley Olsen]].{{Citation needed|date=November 2007}}
