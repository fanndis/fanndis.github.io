{{main|2015 Australian Open}}

[[Serena Williams]] defeated [[Maria Sharapova]] in the final, 6–3, 7–6<sup>(7–5)</sup>, to win the Women's Singles tennis title at the [[2015 Australian Open]]. This was her sixth Australian Open title and her 19th Grand Slam singles title overall.<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.bbc.com/sport/0/tennis/31063354|title= Serena Williams beats Maria Sharapova to win Australian Open|date=31 January 2015|work=BBC Sport|accessdate=2 February 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.theguardian.com/sport/live/2015/jan/31/serena-williams-v-maria-sharapova-australian-open-womens-final-live|title= Serena Williams beats Maria Sharapova to win Australian Open – as it happened|date=31 January 2015|work=Guardian|accessdate=2 February 2015}}</ref>	

[[Li Na]] was the defending champion, but she retired from professional tennis on 19 September 2014.

All of the top four seeds (Williams, Sharapova, [[Simona Halep]], and [[Petra Kvitová]]) were in contention for the [[Women's Tennis Association#WTA Rankings|world no. 1 ranking]] at the start of the tournament. Williams retained the top position by reaching the final.
