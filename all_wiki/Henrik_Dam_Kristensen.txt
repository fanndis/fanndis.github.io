'''Henrik Dam Kristensen''' (born 31 January 1957 in [[Vorbasse]], [[Denmark]]) is a [[Politics of Denmark|Danish politician]] and the current speaker of the [[Folketinget|Danish parliament]]. He has been a member of the Danish parliament for the [[Social Democrats (Denmark)|Social Democrats]] from 1990–2004 and again from 2007, during which he served as Minister for Agriculture and Fisheries (1994–1996), Minister for Food (1996–2000), Minister for Social Affairs (2000–2001), Minister for Transport (2011–2013) and Minister for Employment (2014–2015). He served as President of the [[Nordic Council]] in 2011 and 2016. 

From 1978 Kristensen worked as a postman until he became an MP in 1990.

From 2004–2006 Kristensen served as a [[member of the European Parliament]] and sat on the [[European Parliament]]'s [[European Parliament Committee on Fisheries|Committee on Fisheries]], the [[European Parliament Committee on the Internal Market and Consumer Protection|Committee on the Internal Market and Consumer Protection]] and the Delegation for Relations with the Countries of South-East Europe.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.europarl.europa.eu/meps/en/28152/HENRIK+DAM_KRISTENSEN/history/6|title=6th parliamentary term {{!}} Henrik Dam KRISTENSEN {{!}} MEPs {{!}} European Parliament|website=www.europarl.europa.eu|language=en|access-date=2019-06-21}}</ref>

On 3 October 2011 Kristensen was appointed to the post of [[Danish Ministry of Transport|Minister for Transport]] in the [[Cabinet of Helle Thorning-Schmidt]], and left office on 9 August 2013.<ref>{{cite news|title=The cabinet reshuffle: Who’s in and who’s out|url=http://cphpost.dk/politics/cabinet-reshuffle-who%E2%80%99s-and-who%E2%80%99s-out|accessdate=10 September 2013|newspaper=The Copenhagen Post|date=9 August 2013}}</ref> On 10 October 2014 he again entered the cabinet, when he was appointed the position of Minister for Employment.<ref>{{cite news|title=Changes in government |url=http://stm.dk/_p_14087.html |accessdate=16 October 2014 |newspaper=The Prime Ministers Office |date=10 October 2014 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141013030031/http://stm.dk/_p_14087.html |archivedate=13 October 2014 }}</ref>

On 21 June 2019 Kristensen was appointed speaker of the Danish parliament.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ft.dk/da/aktuelt/nyheder/2019/06/henrik-dam-ny-formand-for-folketinget|title=Henrik Dam Kristensen (S) valgt som Folketingets formand|date=21 June 2019|website=Folketinget.dk}}</ref>

==Political career==
*Member of the Folketing (1990–2004) and (2007–present)
* Minister for Agriculture and Fisheries (1994–1996)
* Minister for Food (1996–2000)
* Minister for Social Affairs (2000–2001)
* Member of the European Parliament (2004–2006)
* Secretary general of the Social Democrats (2006–2007)
*Minister for Transport (2011–2013)
*Minister for Employment (2014–2015)
*Speaker of the Folketing (2019–present)
