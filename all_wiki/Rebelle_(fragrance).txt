'''Rebelle''' is the second [[fragrance]] for women by Barbadian singer [[Rihanna]]. The fragrance was released in spring 2012 under the perfume line Parlux Fragrances.<ref name="billboard">{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/the-hook/465780/meet-rebelle-rihannas-new-fragrance |title=Meet Rebelle, Rihanna's New Fragrance - The Hook |publisher=Billboard.com |date= |accessdate=2012-05-29}}</ref> The fragrance was made available for purchase  on Belk.com, on February 15, 2012.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://plus.ultgate.com/rebelle-by-rihanna-now-available-at-belk-com/ |title=Rebelle by Rihanna now available at Belk.com |publisher=Plus.ultgate.com |accessdate=2012-05-29 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303221727/http://plus.ultgate.com/rebelle-by-rihanna-now-available-at-belk-com/ |archivedate=2016-03-03 }}</ref>  in the United States. It was also available in a special pack at [[Macy's]] for a limited time. The pack included the singer's previous fragrance, [[Reb'l Fleur]], her sixth studio album, ''[[Talk That Talk (Rihanna album)|Talk That Talk]]'' and Rebelle.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.macys.com/shop/product/rebl-fleur-rebelle-by-rihanna-deluxe-gift-set?ID=637072&cm_mmc=Facebook-_-pdpshare-_-n-_-n|title=Reb'l Fleur and Rebelle by Rihanna Deluxe Gift Set - Fragrance - Beauty - Macy's|website=Macys}}</ref>

==Background==
In a poll on the official website for Rihanna's first fragrance Reb'l Fleur, Rihanna asked fans to state whether they were a Rebelle or a Fleur. After an overwhelming 68% of Rihanna's fans voted Rebelle, the singer decided to embark on the release of her second fragrance, Rebelle, having achieved great success with her debut fragrance, Reb'l Fleur.<ref name="billboard" />

==Information==
Rihanna described the fragrance, as having a "feminine romantic element," while maintaining a "defiant quality." She added, "my new fragrance is about taking control but still being a lady." <ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://news.instyle.com/2011/11/18/rihanna-new-perfume-rebelle/ |title=Rihanna’s New Rebelle Perfume Comes Out Soon! |publisher=News.instyle.com |date=2011-11-18 |accessdate=2012-05-29}}</ref> The top notes of the fragrance include; [[plum]], [[strawberry]] and [[ginger]], the middle notes include [[orchid]], [[cocoa bean|cacao]] and [[heliotropium|heliotrope]], and the base notes include [[musk]], [[patchouli]], [[amber]] and [[coffee]]. The fragrance is packaged in a flacon shape, created of red and gold surfaces, similar to that of Rihanna's previous fragrance.<ref name=fragrantica>{{cite web|url=http://www.fragrantica.com/perfume/Rihanna/Rebelle-13442.html |title=Rebelle Rihanna perfume - a new fragrance for women 2012 |publisher=Fragrantica.com |date= |accessdate=2012-05-29}}</ref>

==Promotion==
The promotional campaign for Rebelle, was shot by renowned music video director, [[Anthony Mandler]], who also shot the promotional campaign for Reb'l Fleur.<ref name="billboard" /> The fragrance was also marketed on the [[social networking service]], [[Facebook]] and on [[Twitter]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/FragrancesbyRiRi |title=Reb'l Fleur by Rihanna |publisher=Facebook |date= |accessdate=2012-05-29}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/perfumesbyriri|title=Perfumes by Rihanna (@PerfumesbyRiRi) - Twitter|website=twitter.com}}</ref> Additionally, a large billboard, displayed in [[Times Square]], [[New York City]], features Rihanna, promoting the fragrance.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://abcnews.go.com/blogs/entertainment/2012/06/rihanna-poses-nude-in-times-square/ |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2012-06-13 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120615161905/https://abcnews.go.com/blogs/entertainment/2012/06/rihanna-poses-nude-in-times-square/ |archivedate=2012-06-15 }}</ref>

==Reception==
Since the release of Rebelle in the [[United Kingdom]] in February 2012, it has sold over 2 million bottles. Rihanna's second fragrance has beaten other celebrity scents, in terms of bottles sold in the country, including [[Heat (perfume)|Heat]] by [[Beyoncé Knowles|Beyoncé]], [[Glow by J.Lo|Glow]] by [[Jennifer Lopez]] and [[Fantasy (fragrance)#Midnight Fantasy|Midnight Fantasy]] by [[Britney Spears]]. Rebelle sold a record-breaking, 4 million bottles, in the UK, at the end of 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.capitalfm.com/artists/rihanna/news/rebelle-uk-sales|title=Rihanna's Fragrance Rebelle Becomes Fastest Selling Celebrity Perfume In The UK|website=capitalfm.com}}</ref>
