The '''Golden Globe Award for Best Television Series – Drama''' is one of the annual [[Golden Globe Award]]s, given to the best drama television series. Documentary series and mini-series are also eligible for this award. From 1962 to 1968, the category was '''Golden Globe Award for Best Television Series''', and grouped musical, comedy and drama series in a single category. {{crossref|(See [[Golden Globe Award for Best Television Series – Musical or Comedy]].)}}
