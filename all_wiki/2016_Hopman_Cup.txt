The '''Hopman Cup XXVIII''' was the 28th edition of the [[Hopman Cup]] tournament between nations in men's and women's tennis. It took place at the [[Perth Arena]] in [[Perth, Western Australia|Perth]], Western Australia.

Eight teams competed for the title, with two [[Round-robin tournament|round robin]] groups of four, from which the top team of each group advanced to the final. For the first time, there were two Australian teams, Australia Green (representing 25 and under) and Australia Gold (representing the veterans). The original full line-up of entrants was announced in October.<ref name="Hewitt-Murray">{{cite web|url=http://hopmancup.com/news/2015/09/16/hewitt-murray-set-for-hopman-cup|title=Hewitt & Murray set for Hopman Cup|publisher=Hopman Cup|date=16 September 2015|accessdate=16 September 2015}}</ref><ref name="schedule">{{cite web|url=http://hopmancup.com/schedules-draws/order-of-play|title=Order of play|publisher=Hopman Cup|accessdate=16 September 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151206013435/http://hopmancup.com/schedules-draws/order-of-play|archive-date=2015-12-06|url-status=dead}}</ref> Originally, [[Lleyton Hewitt]] and [[Casey Dellacqua]] were supposed to represent Australia Gold, but Dellacqua was replaced by [[Jarmila Wolfe]].<ref>http://hopmancup.com/countries/australia-goldfor health reasons {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160104021352/http://hopmancup.com/countries/australia-gold |date=4 January 2016 }}</ref> Other replacements are [[Kenny de Schepper]] for [[Gaël Monfils]] in the French team, [[Karolína Plíšková]] for [[Lucie Šafářová]] in the Czech team.

The [[2015 Hopman Cup|2015]] champions [[Poland at the Hopman Cup|Poland]] did not return to defend their title.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theroar.com.au/2015/09/17/hopman-cup-part-of-hewitts-swansong/|title=Hopman Cup part of Hewitt's swansong|first=Justin|last=Chadwick|publisher=The Roar|date=17 September 2015|accessdate=17 September 2015}}</ref>
The competition was won by Australia Green.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/tennis/35271371|title=Nick Kyrgios secures victory for Australia Green|date=9 January 2016|work=BBC Sport|accessdate=22 June 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theaustralian.com.au/sport/tennis/hopman-cup-kyrgios-gavrilova-win-for-australia/news-story/443719b8ecaddc18d2eb3112124db8dd|title=Hopman Cup: Kyrgios, Gavrilova win for Australia|date=9 January 2016|work=The Australian|accessdate=22 June 2016}}</ref>
