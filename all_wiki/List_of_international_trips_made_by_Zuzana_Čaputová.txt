This is a '''list of international presidential trips made by [[Zuzana Čaputová]]''', the fifth [[President of Slovakia|president of the Slovak republic]], since her [[Inauguration of Zuzana Čaputová|inauguration]] on 15 June 2019.

Since June 2019, she has visited these countries:

* 1 visit: [[Austria]], [[Belgium]], [[France]], [[Hungary]], [[Israel]], [[Japan]], [[Switzerland]], [[Ukraine]], [[United Kingdom]], [[United States]]
* 3 visits: [[Germany]], [[Poland]]
* 4 visits: [[Czech Republic]]
