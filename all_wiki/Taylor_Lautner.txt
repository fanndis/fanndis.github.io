'''Taylor Daniel Lautner'''<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.boomsbeat.com/articles/454/20140219/50-things-you-probably-didn-t-know-about-taylor-lautner.htm |title=50 Things You Probably Didn't Know About Taylor Lautner |publisher=Boombsbeat |date=February 19, 2014 |access-date=January 8, 2019 |archive-date=January 8, 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190108145544/https://www.boomsbeat.com/articles/454/20140219/50-things-you-probably-didn-t-know-about-taylor-lautner.htm |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.mirror.co.uk/3am/celebrity-news/10-things-you-need-to-know-431641 |title=10 things you need to know about Taylor Lautner |publisher=Mirror |date=November 22, 2009 |access-date=January 8, 2019 |archive-date=January 8, 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190108145549/https://www.mirror.co.uk/3am/celebrity-news/10-things-you-need-to-know-431641 |url-status=live }}</ref> ({{IPAc-en|Ëˆ|l|aÊŠ|t|n|É™r|}}; born February 11, 1992) is an American actor, voice actor, and model. He is known for playing [[Jacob Black]] in ''[[The Twilight Saga (film series)|The Twilight Saga]]'' film series based on the [[Twilight (novel series)|novels of the same name]] by [[Stephenie Meyer]].

Lautner began his acting career playing bit roles in comedy series such as ''[[The Bernie Mac Show]]'' (2003) and ''[[My Wife and Kids]]'' (2004), before having voice roles in television series like ''[[What's New, Scooby-Doo?]]'' (2005) and ''[[Danny Phantom]]'' (2005). In 2005, he appeared in the film ''[[Cheaper by the Dozen 2]]'' and starred in ''[[The Adventures of Sharkboy and Lavagirl in 3-D]]''. He also starred in the 2011 action film ''[[Abduction (2011 film)|Abduction]]''.

From 2014 to 2018, Lautner starred in the [[BBC]] sitcom [[Cuckoo (TV series)|''Cuckoo'']] as the son of the titular character. In 2016, he played a leading role, Dr. Cassidy Cascade, in the second season of [[Fox Broadcasting Company|FOX]] [[black comedy]] series ''[[Scream Queens (2015 TV series)|Scream Queens]]''.

The late 2000s saw Lautner become a [[teen idol]] and [[sex symbol]], after extensively changing his physique to keep the role of Jacob Black in further ''Twilight'' installments, and generating media attention for his looks. In 2010, he was ranked second on ''[[Glamour (magazine)|Glamour]]''<nowiki>'</nowiki>s "The 50 Sexiest Men of 2010" list, and fourth on ''[[People (magazine)|People]]''<nowiki>'</nowiki>s "Most Amazing Bodies" list. Also in the same year, Lautner was named the highest-paid teenage actor in Hollywood.

===2001â€“07: Career beginnings===
In his first months after moving to Los Angeles, Lautner appeared in small television roles, small film roles, and ads and commercials.
