The '''Yale Child Study Center''' is a department at the [[Yale University School of Medicine]]. The center conducts research and provides clinical services and medical training related to children and families. Topics of investigation include [[autism]] and related disorders,<ref>[http://www.medicalnewstoday.com/articles/61708.php Yale Child Study Center Receives Over $3.5 Million NIH Grant For Autism Research], ''Medical News Today'', Jan 27, 2007</ref> [[Tourette syndrome]], other pediatric [[mental health]] concerns, parenting, and [[neurobiology]].<ref>[http://medicine.yale.edu/childstudy/about/chair.aspx Welcome from Director, Fred R. Volkmar, MD], Yale Child Study Center website, accessed April 15, 2012</ref>

== Mission ==
The center conducts research and provides clinical services and medical training related to children and families.  Topics of investigation include [[autism spectrum disorder]]s,<ref>[http://www.medicalnewstoday.com/articles/61708.php Yale Child Study Center Receives Over $3.5&nbsp;Million NIH Grant For Autism Research], ''Medical News Today'', January 27, 2007.</ref> [[Tourette syndrome]], other pediatric [[mental health]] concerns, parenting, and [[neurobiology]].<ref>[http://medicine.yale.edu/childstudy/about/chair.aspx Welcome from Director, Fred R. Volkmar, MD], Yale Child Study Center website. Retrieved April 15, 2012.</ref>

== History ==
The center was started in 1911 as the '''Yale Clinic of Child Development''' by [[Arnold Gesell]]. Dr. Gesell, who is considered the father of [[child development]] in the United States, led the center until 1948.<ref name=History>[http://medicine.yale.edu/childstudy/about/history.aspx Yale Child Study Center History]. Retrieved April 15, 2012.</ref> Subsequent directors were:<ref name=History/>
* Milton J.E. Senn,  1948–1966
* [[Albert J. Solnit]], 1966–1983
* [[Donald J. Cohen]], 1983–2001
* John E. Schowalter, (interim) 2001–2002
* [[Alan E. Kazdin]], 2002–2006
* [[Fred R. Volkmar]], 2006–2014<ref>{{cite press release|url= http://medicineatyale.org/julyaug2006/people/peoplearticles/60950 |title= Expert on autism is named new director of Child Study Center |publisher= Medicine@Yale.org |date= July–August 2006 |accessdate= April 15, 2012}}</ref>
* Linda C. Mayes, (interim) 2014-2016; Director 2016-present<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.ynhh.org/vSiteManager/Upload/Docs/msb/msb_1214.pdf |format= PDF |publisher= Yale-New Haven Hospital |work= I Am Yale-New Haven |title= Medical Staff Recognition |date= December 2014 |accessdate= March 5, 2015 |url-status= dead |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20150402124955/http://www.ynhh.org/vSiteManager/Upload/Docs/msb/msb_1214.pdf |archivedate= April 2, 2015 |df= mdy-all }}</ref>
