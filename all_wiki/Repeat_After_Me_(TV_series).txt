'''''Repeat After Me''''' is an American [[Reality television#Hidden cameras|hidden camera]] television series on [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] based on one of the segments from ''[[The Ellen DeGeneres Show]]''. The series premiered on February 17, 2015.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Wagmeister|first1=Elizabeth|title=Ellen DeGeneres' ABC Hidden-Camera Show Gets Premiere Date|url=https://variety.com/2015/tv/news/repeat-after-me-abc-hidden-camera-reality-show-wendy-mclendon-covey-1201405064/|accessdate=February 19, 2015|work=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]]|publisher=Penske Business Media|date=January 14, 2015}}</ref> The series is executive produced by talk show host [[Ellen DeGeneres]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/ellen-degeneres-produce-abc-hidden-763597|title=Ellen DeGeneres to Produce ABC Hidden-Camera Prank Show|first=Lesley|last=Goldberg|accessdate=March 9, 2015|date=January 14, 2015|work=[[The Hollywood Reporter]]|publisher=Prometheus Global Media}}</ref>

==Format==
The series features host [[Wendi McLendon-Covey]] guiding celebrities via a remote ear piece to interact with everyday people who do not know they are being filmed. Each episode features different celebrities. The most memorable moment of the night is crowned in front of a live studio audience at the end of each episode.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://abc.go.com/shows/repeat-after-me/about-the-show|title=''Repeat After Me'' – About The Show|publisher=[[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]]|accessdate=March 9, 2015}}</ref>
