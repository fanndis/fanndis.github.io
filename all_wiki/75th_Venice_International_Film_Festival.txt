The '''75th [[Venice Film Festival|Venice International Film Festival]]''' was held from 29 August to 8 September 2018. Mexican film director [[Guillermo del Toro]] was named as the President of the Jury.<ref>{{cite web|title=Guillermo del Toro President of the Venezia 75 International Jury|url=http://www.labiennale.org/en/news/guillermo-del-toro-president-venezia-75-international-jury|accessdate=12 February 2018|work=La Biennale di Venezia|date=12 February 2018}}</ref> ''[[First Man (film)|First Man]]'', directed by [[Damien Chazelle]], was selected to open the festival.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/entertainment-arts-44884023 |title=Ryan Gosling's Neil Armstrong movie to open Venice Film Festival |work=BBC News |accessdate=19 July 2018}}</ref> [[Guillermo del Toro]] was named as the Jury President for the main competition section,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2018/film/news/guillermo-del-toro-preside-venice-film-festival-jury-1202694985/ |title=Guillermo Del Toro to Head Venice Film Festival Jury |work=Variety |accessdate=23 July 2018}}</ref> with [[Michele Riondino]] hosting the festival.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ansa.it/english/news/lifestyle/arts/2018/06/06/riondino-to-host-venice-opening-closing_cd9c2c74-2366-44ef-898b-9ad56ba00df5.html |title=Riondino to host Venice opening, closing |work=ANSA |accessdate=23 July 2018}}</ref>

The festival poster is made by Italian artist [[Lorenzo Mattotti]], who designed it in the way "that attracts the eye, that attracts thought, but without revealing too much."<ref>{{cite web|title=The 75th Venice International Film Festival|url=https://fg-arttravelint.com/the-75th-venice-international-film-festival|accessdate=9 September 2018|work=fg-arttravelint.com|date=27 August 2018}}</ref> The [[Golden Lion]] was awarded to ''[[Roma (2018 film)|Roma]]'', directed by [[Alfonso Cuarón]].<ref name="winners">{{cite web |url=https://variety.com/2018/film/news/venice-film-festival-complete-list-of-winners-updated-live-1202933206/ |title=Venice Film Festival: Alfonso Cuaron’s ‘Roma’ Wins Golden Lion (Complete Winners List)|work=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]] |accessdate=9 September 2018}}</ref>
