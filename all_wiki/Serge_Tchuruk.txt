'''Serge Tchurukdichian''' (born 13 November 1937), known as '''Serge Tchuruk''', is a French businessman of [[Armenians|Armenian]] descent. He was the chief executive officer and chairman of [[Alcatel-Lucent|Alcatel]] (a global telecommunications [[company (law)|company]]) until the end of November 2006 and was the chairman of [[Alcatel-Lucent]] until his resignation in October 2008.

Serge Tchuruk was graduate of the [[École Polytechnique]] in 1962 and a member of the [[Corps de l'armement]].
