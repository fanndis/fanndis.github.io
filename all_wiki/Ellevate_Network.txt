'''Ellevate Network''' (formerly 85 Broads) is a global community of women committed fostering and promoting gender equality in the workplace.  The organization provides women and femme-identifying non-binary individuals with a community to lean on and learn from through networking, online and offline education, inspiration, and opportunity. Founded in 1997<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://worklife.wharton.upenn.edu/2014/06/connecting-best-brightest-women-janet-hanson-85-broads/|title=Connecting Best and Brightest Women — Janet Hanson 85 Broads|website=Wharton Work/Life|language=en-US|access-date=2016-03-24}}</ref> to bring high-achieving women in finance together, the network now serves women across industries and around the world. Ellevate Network is chaired by [[Sallie Krawcheck]]. Its CEO is Kristy Wallace.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ellevatenetwork.com/team|title=Our Team {{!}} Ellevate|website=www.ellevatenetwork.com|access-date=2016-03-24}}</ref> 

As of December, 2019 the organization boasts a community of over 150,000 women across the globe<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.womanaroundtown.com/sections/living-around/ellevate-network-a-network-for-women-that-works/|title=Ellevate Network—A Network for Women that Works|last=Sheils|first=Merry|date=2019-06-19|website=Woman Around Town|language=en-US|access-date=2019-12-06}}</ref> and has over 40 chapters primarily in the United States, with international locations in [[Dubai]], [[London]], [[Madrid]], and [[Toronto]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ellevatenetwork.com/chapters|title=Chapters {{!}} Ellevate|website=www.ellevatenetwork.com|language=en|access-date=2019-12-06}}</ref>  

== History ==
Ellevate was founded in 1997 as 85 Broads by [[Janet Hanson]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://archive.nytimes.com/www.nytimes.com/library/financial/102799manage-85broad.html|title=Management: A Corporate Alumni Network Just for Women|website=archive.nytimes.com|access-date=2019-12-06}}</ref> Hanson had formerly worked at [[Goldman Sachs]] and formed the organization as an alumnae network for women who had left the firm. The name came from the address of New York's Goldman Sachs office.<ref name=":0" />

== Services ==
Ellevate Network hosts networking events around the world: online webinars, online networking, and career advice on topics like creating a personal brand, growing a business, finding a mentor, reinventing your career, and finding balance and fulfillment. Ellevate Network also provides Squads to professional women, an innovative online program that uses technology to help professional women get ahead in business by leveraging Ellevate’s community to lean on and learn from. Special events, opportunities and advice are all available for young professionals, working mothers, entrepreneurs, and senior executives.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.crainsnewyork.com/article/20140619/TECHNOLOGY/140619836/women-s-networking-groups-find-life-offline|title=Women's networking groups find life offline|date=2014-06-19|website=Crain's New York Business|language=en|access-date=2019-12-06}}</ref>

== Partnerships ==
On March 21, 2016 Ellevate announced a partnership with American Corporate Partners, a women's veteran mentoring program.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://acp-usa.org/ACP-Launches-New-Initiative-For-Transitioning-Women-Veterans|title=American Corporate Partners Launches Mentoring Initiative for Transitioning Women Veterans|website=acp-usa.org|access-date=2016-03-24}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ellevatenetwork.com/articles/7200-help-a-transitioning-female-veteran-find-her-next-career|title=Help a Transitioning Female Veteran Find Her Next Career|website=www.ellevatenetwork.com|access-date=2016-03-24}}</ref> In 2014, Ellevate partnered with Pax World Management to form the Pax Ellevate Global Women's Index Fund, a fund focused on companies with significant representation of women in director and officer positions.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://dealbook.nytimes.com/2014/06/04/sallie-krawcheck-opens-an-index-fund-focused-on-women/|title=Sallie Krawcheck Opens an Index Fund Focused on Women|last=Alden|first=William|work=DealBook|access-date=2018-01-11|language=en}}</ref>
