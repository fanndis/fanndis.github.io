The '''Satellite Award for Best Supporting Actress in a Motion Picture''' is one of the [[Satellite Awards]] presented annually by the [[International Press Academy]]. In 2011, the IPA pared down its Satellite nominations in the motion picture categories from 22 to 19 classifications; the change reflects the merger of drama and comedy under a general Best Picture heading, including the Best Actor/Actress headings and the Best Supporting Actor/Actress headings.<ref>[http://www.pressacademy.com/award_cat/current-nominees/ 2011 Satellite Winners], December 2011.</ref>
