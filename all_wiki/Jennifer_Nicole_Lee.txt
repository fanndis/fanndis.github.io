'''Jennifer Nicole Lee''' (born '''Jennifer Nicole Siciliano''' on June 13, 1975) is an American fitness model, motivational speaker, and [[author]]. She is known for losing 70 pounds and launching a career as a fitness guru after bearing two children.

==Early life and education==
Jennifer Nicole Siciliano was born in [[Rochester, New York]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bsnonline.net/teambsn/team_bio.php?id=21|title=Team BSN:Jennifer Nicole Lee|publisher=Bio-Engineered Supplements & Nutrition|accessdate=2009-10-21}}</ref> to [[Italy|Italian]] immigrants; the family later moved to [[Tennessee]].<ref name=oconnell>{{cite journal|journal=[[Muscle & Fitness]] |title=Editorial: Ms. Muscle & Fitness |volume=67 |issue=2 |page=24 |author=O'Connell, Jeff |date=February 2006 |url=http://findarticles.com/p/articles/mi_m0801/is_2_67/ai_n16029919/ |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090512192045/http://findarticles.com/p/articles/mi_m0801/is_2_67/ai_n16029919/ |archive-date=2009-05-12 }}</ref> She earned a [[Bachelor's degree]] in [[international relations]] from the [[University of Tennessee at Knoxville]].<ref name=m&f>{{cite journal |journal=[[Muscle & Fitness]] |title=Ms. Muscle & Fitness |volume=67 |issue=2 |date=February 2006}}</ref>

== Career==
Lee describes herself as having "no athletic background whatsoever," and says she has struggled with her weight her entire life.  She weighed 170 pounds when she married, and continued to gain weight during both of her pregnancies, eventually reaching 200 pounds after the birth of her second child.<ref>{{cite web|last=Raterman|first=David|title=JNL Fusion creator: From 'fat and frumpy housewife to super-mom'|url=http://www.sun-sentinel.com/health/fl-hk-fitness-profile-lee-20110629,0,2405392.story?page=1|publisher=SunSentinel.com|accessdate=5 January 2012}}</ref> Lee lost 70 pounds, was crowned "Miss Bikini America" in 1996, and launched a career as a "fitness guru".<ref name="cbs">{{cite news|url=http://www.cbsnews.com/stories/2006/01/04/earlyshow/series/main1176846.shtml|title=Kids Give Bikini Mom An 'Edge'|last=Schorn|first=Daniel|date=January 4, 2006|publisher=[[CBS News]]|accessdate=2009-11-04}}</ref><ref name="fitguru">{{cite news|url=http://southflorida.bizjournals.com/southflorida/stories/2006/10/02/focus10.html|title=In Depth: Guide to Health Care:Moms can be buff babes, too, guru says|last=Trapps|first=Tynisa|date=September 29, 2006|publisher=[[South Florida Business Journal]]|accessdate=2009-10-19}}</ref> JNL, Inc. (now JNL, Inc. Worldwide) was founded in January 2004.<ref name="Johnson">{{cite web|last=Johnson|first=Daniel|title=Jennifer Nicole Lee, Inc Hits Its 8 Year Milestone in Business, CEO Jennifer Nicole Lee Shares Her Successful Business Tips in Latest Online Interview|url=http://missourisportsmag.com/?p=49824|work=Online article|publisher=Missouri Sports Magazine|accessdate=9 February 2012}}</ref> Her books have been published both electronically and in print.  She also launched a line of swimwear.<ref name="fitguru"/>

Lee has two clothing lines. The Jennifer Nicole Lee Collection through Rogiani is designed by Elisabetta Rogiani for fitness model photo shoots.{{cn|date=May 2020}} Lee's own line is called the JNL Clothing Line, which includes gym and [[streetwear]].<ref>{{cite web|title=JNL Clothing Line Blazing the Fitness and Fashion Industry|url=https://news.yahoo.com/jnl-clothing-line-blazing-fitness-fashion-industry-intl-050407466.html|work=Press release|publisher=Yahoo|accessdate=22 December 2011}}</ref>

In March 2011, Lee opened the Fitness Model Factory in [[Miami, Florida]], a production studio containing photography and videography studios, hair and makeup styling, and coaching for fitness models.<ref>{{cite web|last=Wilson|first=Alexandra|title=Inside JNL's Fitness Model Factory|url=http://viewer.zmags.com/publication/d8e38af9#/d8e38af9/50|work=Magazine article July 2011|publisher=Fit and Firm magazine|accessdate=21 December 2011}}</ref>

Lee is the creator of a fitness program called JNL Fusion which emphasizes "super spiking," or the introduction of cardio between [[weight training]] exercises.<ref>{{cite news|title=The World of Celebrity Fitness with Jennifer Nicole Lee|newspaper=Sports and Fitness Magazine|date=Winter 2012}}</ref> Lee states that JNL Fusion is designed after her own workout routine:  4 training sessions of 45 minutes each per week.  The workout method is targeted to women.<ref>{{cite news|last=Raterman|first=David|title=JNL Fusion Creator: From 'fat and frumpy housewife to supermom|url=http://www.sun-sentinel.com/health/fl-hk-fitness-profile-lee-20110629,0,2405392.story?page=1|accessdate=10 January 2012|newspaper=South Florida Sun-Sentinel|date=July 5, 2011}}</ref> JNL Fusion is a home exercise system and DVD set.<ref>{{cite web|last=Nunez|first=Alanna|title=Fitness Guru Jennifer Nicole Lee Releases New Workout Routine|url=http://www.shape.com/blogs/fit-famous/fitness-guru-jennifer-nicole-lee-releases-new-workout-routine|work=Blog post|publisher=Shape.com|accessdate=10 January 2012|date=2011-10-27}}</ref> It requires several small pieces of equipment.<ref name="missourisportsmag.com">{{cite web|last=Johnson|first=Daniel|title=Jennifer Nicole Lee's JNL Fusion Training Method Is Predicted to Be Fitness Industry and Coaching Trend for 2012|url=http://missourisportsmag.com/?p=45326|work=Article|publisher=Missouri Sports Magazine|accessdate=10 January 2012}}</ref> JNL Fusion has a certification program for personal trainers. Lee recruits and educates the trainers.<ref name="missourisportsmag.com"/> She also holds an annual wellness retreat called the JNL Fusion World Conference & Fitness Revival.<ref name="Brown 2011"/>

==Published work==
Each of her e-books is sold through a dedicated web site: "Crack the Code", "The Fitness Model Program", "The Sexy Body Diet", "101 Things Not To Do If You Want to Lose Weight", "Get Fit with JNL", "Get Fit Now With JNL", and "The Bikini Model Diet."

Lee stars in the "Fabulously Fit Moms" [[Physical fitness|fitness]] DVD series.<ref name=reu07>{{cite news|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/entertainmentNews/idUSN1528923520070816|title=Busy moms can get physical with new DVD series|last=Arnold|first=Thomas|date=Aug 15, 2007|publisher=Reuters|accessdate=2009-10-19}}</ref> The series, started in 2007 with "The New Mom Workout", "Total Body Workout", "Lower Body Burn" and "Super Energized Workout."<ref name=reu07 /><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.homemediamagazine.com/news/koch-promotes-fit-moms-with-parents-11363|title=Koch Promotes 'Fit Moms' With 'Parents' |last=Flores|first=Angelique|date=13 Oct 2007 |publisher=[[Home Media Magazine]]|accessdate=2009-10-19}}</ref> Two more titles were released in 2008,<ref name=reu07 /> "Upper Body Blast"<ref>{{cite video|people = Jennifer Nicole Lee|date = 2008|title = Fabulously Fit Moms: Upper Body Blast|format = Color, Dolby, DVD, NTSC|medium = DVD|publisher = Koch Vision}}</ref> and "Sleek and Sexy Workout".<ref>{{cite video|people = Jennifer Nicole Lee|date = 2008|title = Fabulously Fit Moms: Sleek and Sexy Workout|format = Color, Dolby, DVD, NTSC|medium = DVD|publisher = Koch Vision}}</ref>

==Honors and awards==
Lee was named [[Muscle & Fitness]] Magazine's 2006 ''Ms. Muscle and Fitness'', a title she shared with [[Alicia Marie]].<ref name=m&f/><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/lifestyle/health/2007/06/30/2007-06-30_toning_tips_for_new_moms.html|title=Toning tips for new moms|last=Schultz|first=Heather|date=June 30, 2007|work=[[Daily News (New York)|Daily News]]|accessdate=2009-10-19}}</ref> Lee is a brand ambassador for nutritional supplement company Bio-Engineered Supplements and Nutrition (BSN).<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.bsnonline.net/biography/jennifer_nicole.html|accessdate=2011-12-16}}</ref> In 2011, Lee was mentioned as one of the "Top Three Females in the Fitness Industry" on [[Yahoo]] Sports.<ref>{{cite web|last=Johnson-Emerson|first=Heather M.|title=Top Three Females in the Fitness Industry|url=https://sports.yahoo.com/top/news?slug=ycn-10435231|work=Online article 11/15/11|publisher=Yahoo Sports|accessdate=20 December 2011}}</ref> Lee was also named one of Splash Online's "Best Celebrity Bikini Babes of 2011."<ref>{{cite web|title=Best Celebrity Bikini Babes of 2011|url=http://www.splashnewsonline.com/2011-12-26/best-celebrity-bikini-babes-of-2011/|access-date=5 January 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120108002730/http://www.splashnewsonline.com/2011-12-26/best-celebrity-bikini-babes-of-2011/|archive-date=2012-01-08|url-status=dead}}</ref> In 2012, CBS News named Lee as "America's Sexiest Fitness Mom."<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.cbsnews.com/2300-204_162-10004981.html|title= Jennifer Nicole Lee: America's Sexiest Fitness Mom=|publisher=cbsnews.com|accessdate=2012-01-12}}</ref>
