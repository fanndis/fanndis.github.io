The '''28th [[Independent Spirit Awards]]''' announced its nominees on November 27, 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/2013-spirit-award-nominations-being-announced-in-progress|title='Silver Linings,' 'Moonrise Kingdom' Lead Spirit Award Nominations|date=2012-11-27|work=indieWire|accessdate=2012-11-27}}</ref> The winners were announced on February 23, 2013 during an awards ceremony hosted by [[Andy Samberg]].<ref name="HollywoodReporter">{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/independent-spirit-awards-2013-winners-423644|title=Independent Spirit Awards 2013: Winners List|author=|date=23 February 2013|accessdate=23 February 2013|work=[[The Hollywood Reporter]]}}</ref>
