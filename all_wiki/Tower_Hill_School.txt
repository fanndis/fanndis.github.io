'''Tower Hill School''' is a [[private school|private]] [[university-preparatory school|college preparatory school]] in [[Wilmington, Delaware]], offering instruction for [[pre-school]] through [[12th grade]].

==History==
Tower Hill was founded in 1919.<ref name="History" /><ref name="Home">{{cite web|url=http://www.towerhill.org/|title=Tower Hill School Homepage|publisher=Tower Hill School|accessdate=October 25, 2013}}</ref> The school is situated at the high point of Wilmington's neighborhood, The Highlands, where the dominant landmark is Rockford Tower, erected in 1901 to control the city's water supply. [[Tower Hill]], in London, is the birthplace of [[William Penn]], the founder of Pennsylvania, who landed in Delaware, in 1682.

==Recognition==
The school is a member of the [[Delaware Independent School Conference]]<ref name="Athletics">{{cite web|url=http://www.towerhill.org/podium/default.aspx?t=167464|title=Athletics at Tower Hill|publisher=Tower Hill School|accessdate=October 25, 2013}}</ref> and fields 23 competitive varsity sports.<ref name="Athletics" /> The school offered {{USD}}2,000,000 a year of need-based financial aid to 28% of its students for the 2016–2017 school year.<ref name="Glance">{{cite web|url=http://www.towerhill.org/glance|title=Tower Hill at a Glance|publisher=Tower Hill School|accessdate=October 25, 2013}}</ref> Elizabeth “Bessie” Cromwell Speers currently serves as the Tower Hill's head of school, having been selected to be headmaster starting the 2015–2016 school year.<ref name="towerhill.org">"Meet Our Head of School." Towerhill.org. Tower Hill School, n.d. Web. 09 Sept. 2015. <http://www.towerhill.org/Page/About/Meet-Our-Head-of-School>.</ref>''The Wall Street Journal'' ranked Tower Hill 24th in the nation and 1st in Delaware, in percentage of students attending eight top colleges.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://online.wsj.com/public/resources/documents/info-COLLEGE0711-sort.html|title=How the Schools Stack Up|publisher=[[Wall Street Journal]]|date=December 28, 2007|accessdate=October 25, 2013}}</ref> Tower Hill School is ranked 47th in the nation for best private day schools.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thebestschools.org/blog/2013/04/30/50-private-day-schools-united-states|title= Top 50 Private day schools/accessdate=October 25, 2015}}</ref>

== Controversy ==
In 2014, former President of the school Christopher Wheeler was found guilty and sentenced to 50 years in prison for dealing in child pornography.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://nypost.com/2015/04/24/headmaster-at-elite-prep-school-gets-50-years-in-jail-for-child-porn/|title=Headmaster at elite prep school gets 50 years in jail for child porn|last=Press|first=Associated|date=2015-04-24|website=New York Post|language=en|access-date=2019-02-05}}</ref> However, the sentence was overturned in 2016 after the [[Delaware Supreme Court]] found that the [[search warrant]]s used to search his residence and office were unconstitutionally broad.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.delawareonline.com/story/news/crime/2016/03/02/ex-headmasters-child-porn-conviction-overturned/81230142/|title=Ex-headmaster's child porn conviction overturned|last=Masulli Reyes|first=Jessica|date=March 2, 2016|website=delawareonline|language=en|access-date=2019-02-05}}</ref>
