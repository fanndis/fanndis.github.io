'''John Weeks''' (1941 – 26 July 2020) was an American  economist.

==Life==
Weeks, born in [[Austin, Texas]], was Professor Emeritus at the [[SOAS|School of Oriental and African Studies]] of the [[University of London]]. His research interests were in theoretical and policy-applied [[macroeconomics]] and [[economic development]]. He published academic papers, books and policy reports in these areas.<ref>[http://www.soas.ac.uk/staff/staff60157.php SOAS staff listing] Retrieved 14 September 2010</ref><ref name="Weeks' personal home page">[http://jweeks.org Weeks' personal home page]</ref> 

He is credited with coining the phrase "quantity theory of competition" to reflect a proposition that more competition in various aspects of the markets (producers, consumers, and workers) will create a more efficient economy.<ref name="FineSaad-Filho2012">{{cite book|last1=Fine|first1=Ben|last2=Saad-Filho|first2=Alfredo|last3=Boffo|first3=Marco|title=The Elgar Companion to Marxist Economics|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=xe0VQArHt8sC&pg=PR12|accessdate=23 November 2013|date=2012-01-01|publisher=Edward Elgar Publishing|isbn=9781781001226|pages=12–}}</ref>

In August 2015, Weeks endorsed [[Jeremy Corbyn]]'s [[2015 Jeremy Corbyn Labour Party leadership campaign|campaign]] in the [[2015 Labour Party (UK) leadership election|Labour Party leadership election]].<ref name="theguardian">{{cite news |url=https://www.theguardian.com/politics/2015/aug/14/the-labour-party-stands-at-a-crossroads|title=The Labour party stands at a crossroads|newspaper=[[The Guardian]]|date=14 August 2015|accessdate=15 July 2017}}</ref>

He died on 26 July 2020.<ref name=counter>{{cite web |url=https://countercurrents.org/2020/07/prof-john-weeks-humanised-economics-as-a-discipline/|title=Prof John Weeks Humanised Economics as a Discipline|last1=Shankar Nayak|first1=Bhabani |publisher=|date=27 July 2020 |website=Counter Currents |access-date=27 July 2020}}</ref><ref name=sad>{{cite web |url=https://braveneweurope.com/we-are-greatly-saddened-by-the-death-of-the-brilliant-progressive-heterodox-economist-john-weeks-on-26-july |title=We are greatly saddened by the death of the brilliant progressive heterodox economist John Weeks on 26 July |date=27 July 2020 |website=Brave New Europe|access-date=27 July 2020}}</ref>
