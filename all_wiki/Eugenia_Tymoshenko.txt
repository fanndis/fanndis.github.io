'''Eugenia '''(Yevhenia)''' Oleksandrivna Tymoshenko''' ({{lang-uk|Євге́нія Олекса́ндрівна Тимоше́нко}}; born 20 February 1980) is a Ukrainian entrepreneur, philanthropist and activist who is the President of the Association of Public Organizations "Social Protection". She is the daughter of former [[Prime Minister of Ukraine|Ukrainian Prime Minister]] [[Yulia Tymoshenko]].

==Biography==
Tymoshenko was born on 20 February 1980 to [[Oleksandr Tymoshenko]] and [[Yulia Tymoshenko]] in [[Dnipropetrovsk]], [[Ukraine]]. In 1994, she started studying in the United Kingdom. She is a graduate of the [[London School of Economics]] in the field of politics and philosophy; she obtained a bachelor's degree, BSc "Government", and a master's degree, MSc "Russian and post-Soviet political studies".

According to her, she owns a restaurant and "also business in [[Dnipropetrovsk]]".<ref name=YTrestaurant/> She has repeatedly stated she is not going to become a politician.<ref name=YTrestaurant>{{in lang|uk}} [http://www.pravda.com.ua/news/2011/12/22/6856931/ Донька Тимошенко знову клянеться, що не піде в політику], [[Ukrayinska Pravda]] (22 December 2011)</ref>

==Philanthropy==
<!--This tag remains while the merger discussion is ongoing. Make your case on the talk page. -->
Tymoshenko is the president of the charitable organization Association "Social Protection" ({{lang-uk|Асоціація "Соціальний захист"}}) and co-founder of the charity children's art festival Follow Your Dream ({{lang-uk|Назустріч Мрії}}),<ref>{{cite web|url =http://www.mriy.org/ua/pro-festival/zasnovniki|title=Founders|publisher=mriy.org|date =2014}}</ref> working with orphans and underprivileged children and promoting [[human rights and development]] of children's talents and creativity. She is also the founder of [[The Voice of Peace (marathon)|The Voice of Peace]] ({{lang-uk|Голос Миру}}), a charitable marathon in Ukraine that unites media, business, artists and public figures to volunteer to help former battalions and refugees.<ref>[http://dyvys.info/kultura/blahodijnyj-marafon-holosu-myru-prolunav-u-starobilsku-foto.html Charity marathon "Voice of Peace" was heard in Starobilsk] dyvys.info(17 December 2014)</ref><ref>[http://batkivshchyna.od.ua/news/492-blagotvoritelny-marafon-golos-mira-ustroil-kontsert-vo-lvovskom-voennom-gospitale Charity Marathon «The Voice of Peace» gave a concert at the Lviv military hospital] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150423045158/http://batkivshchyna.od.ua/news/492-blagotvoritelny-marafon-golos-mira-ustroil-kontsert-vo-lvovskom-voennom-gospitale |date=23 April 2015 }}  batkivshchyna.od.ua(12 November 2014)</ref>

==Release of Yulia Tymoshenko==
Eugenia Tymoshenko was her mother's defender during the trial against her in the summer-autumn 2011.
For two and a half years of the former prime minister's imprisonment Eugenia conducted an active campaign for her release: held dozens of meetings with leaders of the [[European Union]], the Western countries and international organizations, delivered speeches in the US Senate,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.foreign.senate.gov/hearings/ukraine-at-a-crossroads-whats-at-stake-for-the-us-and-europe|title =Ukraine at a Crossroads: What's at Stake for the U.S. and Europe? |publisher=foreign.senate|date= 1 February 2012}}</ref> at the meeting of the Committee on Foreign affairs of the Italian Parliament,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tymoshenko.ua/en/article/yulia_tymoshenko_21_3_2012_1|title=Eugenia Tymoshenko to address Italian Parliament|publisher=tymoshenko.ua|date=21 Mar 2012|access-date=3 September 2014|archive-url=https://archive.is/20140903112222/http://www.tymoshenko.ua/en/article/yulia_tymoshenko_21_3_2012_1|archive-date=3 September 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref> at the Bornholm conference in Denmark,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.novostimira.com.ua/news-eng-59236.html|title =Tymoshenko's daughter to take part in the Bornholm conference|publisher =novostimira|date= 14 June 2013}}</ref> at the 20th Congress of the European People's Party,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.epp.eu/interview-eugenia-tymoshenko-daughter-yulia-tymoshenko|title=interview Eugenia Tymoshenko|publisher=epp.eu|date=17 December 2012|access-date=3 September 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131221002530/http://www.epp.eu/interview-eugenia-tymoshenko-daughter-yulia-tymoshenko|archive-date=21 December 2013|url-status=dead}}</ref> at the congresses of the German Christian Social Union<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.tymoshenko.ua/en/article/yulia_tymoshenko_10_01_2014_01|title= Eugenia Tymoshenko to address CSU party convention in Germany|publisher= tymoshenko.ua|date= 10 Jan 2014|access-date= 3 September 2014|archive-url= https://archive.is/20140903112225/http://www.tymoshenko.ua/en/article/yulia_tymoshenko_10_01_2014_01|archive-date= 3 September 2014|url-status= dead}}</ref>
and the Italian party "The Union of Christian and Centre Democrats",<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.ukrainebusiness.com.ua/news/10315.html|title= Eugenia Tymoshenko meets with leaders of Italian Christian Democratic party|publisher= ukrainebusiness|date= 15 September 2013|access-date= 3 September 2014|archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20130917073607/http://www.ukrainebusiness.com.ua/news/10315.html|archive-date= 17 September 2013|url-status= dead}}</ref> in the media.<ref>{{cite web|url=  https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/europe/ukraine/10684187/Eugenia-Tymoshenko-My-mother-can-help-to-build-a-new-Ukraine.html|title=Eugenia Tymoshenko: 'My mother can help to build a new Ukraine'|publisher=telegraph.co.uk|date= 7 March 2014}}</ref>

[[Yulia Tymoshenko]] was awarded the Manuel Carrasco I Formiguera medal by representatives of the Spanish political party "Unió Democràtica de Catalunya"<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tymoshenko.ua/en/article/yulia_tymoshenko_11_07_2013_01 |title=Yulia Tymoshenko awarded for her contribution to defending democracy |publisher=tymoshenko.ua |date=11 Jul 2013 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140223050204/http://www.tymoshenko.ua/en/article/yulia_tymoshenko_11_07_2013_01 |archivedate=23 February 2014 |df=dmy }}</ref> for her contribution to the defense of democracy and freedom and the struggle for the restoration of the rule of law in Ukraine. The award was received by Eugenia Tymoshenko on behalf of her mother in Barcelona, Spain, on 11 July.

In February 2012, Eugenia Tymoshenko submitted a statement to the Prosecutor General of Ukraine to demand investigation in prison of the facts about the torture of her mother.<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.ukrinform.ua/eng/news/yevhenia_tymoshenko_files_statement_with_prosecutor_generals_office_about_torture_of_her_mother|title=Yevhenia Tymoshenko files statement with Prosecutor General's Office about torture of her mother|publisher=ukrinform|date=10 February 2012}}</ref>

On 24 November 2013, Eugenia Tymoshenko made a speech on the stage on [[Maidan Nezalezhnosti]] during [[Euromaidan]], in particular read her mother's letter addressed to the Ukrainians.<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.kyivpost.com/content/politics/backstage-at-euromaidan-as-100000-people-watch-332392.html|title =Backstage at EuroMaidan as 100,000 people watch|publisher=kyivpost|date= 24 November 2013}}</ref> 
On 21 February 2014 during the meeting with Vice-President of the European Parliament Gianni Pittella and Speaker of the Italian Parliament Laura Boldrini Eugenia Tymoshenko urged the European Union to immediately impose sanctions against representatives of Yanukovych's regime responsible for the bloodshed in Ukraine.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tymoshenko.ua/en/article/yulia_tymoshenko_21_02_2014_01|title=Eugenia Tymoshenko: EU sanctions against the regime – now!|publisher=tymoshenko.ua|date=21 February 2014|access-date=3 September 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140225082809/http://www.tymoshenko.ua/en/article/yulia_tymoshenko_21_02_2014_01|archive-date=25 February 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref>

On 4 October 2014, Eugenia Tymoshenko in [[Milan]], [[Italy]] presented the book "Ukraine, Gas and Handcuffs: The Trial of Yulia Tymoshenko", it. "Ucraina, gas e manette: il processo a Yulia Tymoshenko". Author of books on political persecution of [[Yulia Tymoshenko]] at the time of the Yanukovych regime is an Italian analyst Mateo Cazzulani. The title "Ukraine, Gas and Handcuffs: The Trial of Yulia Tymoshenko" demonstrates a clear understanding that energy is the key source of Ukraine's dependence. The author draws a clear parallel between Ukraine and the fate of Yulia Tymoshenko, who was also denied her freedom because of her fight against corruption, the oligarchy and the dependence of the Ukrainian energy sector on Russian energy.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www2.tymoshenko.ua/en/news-en/book-on-persecution-of-yulia-tymoshenko-presented-in-italy/|title=Book on persecution of Yulia Tymoshenko presented in Italy|publisher=tymoshenko.ua|date=4 October 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url= http://milano.corriere.it/notizie/cronaca/14_ottobre_05/figlia-yulia-tymoshenko-diritti-umani-negati-mia-madre-0f965f8c-4c87-11e4-8c5c-557ef01adf3d.shtml|title=La figlia di Yulia Tymoshenko  "Diritti umani negati a mia madre"|publisher=milano.corriere.it|date=5 October 2014}}</ref>

==Awards==
On 20 October 2012, Tymoshenko received the Crans Montana Forum Medal for her contribution for defending democracy and human rights. The Crans Montana Forum was founded in 1986, works closely with the United Nations, [[UNESCO]], the [[European Union]] and other institutions to ensure the stability, security and respect for human rights in the world.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://ukrafoto.com/reportages.php?id=15256&photo=262318&lng=english|title=Eugenia Tymoshenko receives medal for contribution to protection of democracy and human rights|publisher=ukrafoto|date=20 October 2012|access-date=3 September 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140903233334/http://ukrafoto.com/reportages.php?id=15256&photo=262318&lng=english|archive-date=3 September 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Tymoshenko was married to Englishman Sean Carr (1968 – 2018), a rock musician (leader of the British-Ukrainian band "Death Valley Screamers")<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5YiOBbEDcVM Death Valley Screamers – "Bonnie & Clyde" Moon Records. 2011.]</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.deathvalleyscreamers.co.uk/links.html |title=Site of "Death Valley Screamers", 2011. |access-date=14 January 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120611084713/http://www.deathvalleyscreamers.co.uk/links.html |archive-date=11 June 2012 |url-status=dead }}</ref><ref name=YTDVS21212/> and owner of several stores in the UK.
