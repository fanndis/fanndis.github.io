"'''Lemon'''" is a song by American band [[N.E.R.D.]] from their fifth studio album, ''[[No One Ever Really Dies]]'' (2017).<ref>{{cite web |last=Ortiz |first=Edwin |url=http://www.complex.com/music/2017/11/nerd-debut-new-album-complexcon-performance |title=N*E*R*D Debut New Album at ComplexCon With Electrifying Show |work=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]] |date=November 5, 2017 |accessdate=November 13, 2017}}</ref> The song features vocals from Barbadian singer [[Rihanna]], and was released as the album's lead single on November 1, 2017.<ref name="itunes">{{cite web |url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/lemon-single/1300805696 |title=Lemon – Single by N.E.R.D |website=[[iTunes Store]] (US) |accessdate=November 15, 2017}}</ref>

Former Pennsylvania [[U.S. Senator]] [[Arlen Specter]] is sampled in the song as the man saying "wait a minute."<ref>{{cite web |last1=Rolen |first1=Emily |title=Song on Ariana Grande's new album samples clip of man shouting at 2009 Pa. town hall meeting |url=https://www.phillyvoice.com/song-ariana-grandes-new-album-samples-clip-man-shouting-sen-arlen-specter-2009-pa-town-hall-meeting/ |website=Philadelphia Voice |accessdate=25 October 2019}}</ref>

Several alternate versions of the songs were produced, including a version without the Rihanna feature (which sees Pharrell rapping a similar verse), a radio edit, and an edited version submitted to streaming services that omits Pharrell's first verse. The remix featuring [[Drake (musician)|Drake]] was released on March 17, 2018.<ref name="Remix iTunes"/><ref name="Billboard Remix"/><ref name="NME">{{cite news|url=http://www.nme.com/news/music/drake-remix-nerd-rihanna-lemon-2267414|title=Drake remixes N.E.R.D and Rihanna's 'Lemon'|last=Morgan Britton|first=Luke|date=March 18, 2018|work=[[NME]]|accessdate=June 16, 2018}}</ref>

==Music video==
The music video for "Lemon" was uploaded to N.E.R.D.'s [[Vevo]] page on YouTube on November 1, 2017. The video was directed by Todd Tourso and Scott Cudmore, and produced by Stacey Thiel and stars dancer [[Mette Towley]].
