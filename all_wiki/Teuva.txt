'''Teuva''' ({{lang-sv|Östermark}}) is a [[municipalities of Finland|municipality]] of [[Finland]].

It is located in the [[South Ostrobothnia]] [[regions of Finland|region]]. The population of Teuva is {{formatnum: {{Data Finland municipality/population count|Teuva}}}} ({{#time: j F Y|{{Data Finland municipality/population count|sourcedate}} }})<ref name="population_count" />  and the municipality covers an area of {{convert|{{Data Finland municipality/land area|Teuva}}|km2|abbr=on}} of which {{convert|{{Data Finland municipality/inland waters area|Teuva}}|km2|abbr=on}} is inland water ({{#time: j F Y|{{Data Finland municipality/total area|sourcedate}} }}).<ref name="total_area" /> The population density is {{convert|{{Data Finland municipality/population density|Teuva|round=2}}|PD/km2|abbr=on}}.

The municipality is unilingually [[Finnish language|Finnish]].

== History ==
In August 2010, more than fifty pocket-sized [[sauna]]s were gathered on a lakeside within Teuva for the fifth annual [[Mobile Sauna Festival]]. Some six-thousand visitors attended the festival, doubling the population of Teuva for the weekend.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/gallery/2010/aug/10/mobile-sauna-festival-teuva-finland|title=Mobile Sauna Festival in Teuva, Finland|date=2010-08-10|website=the Guardian|access-date=2016-07-21}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pbs.org/pov/steamoflife/photo-gallery-mobile-sauna-festival/|title=Finlands Mobile Sauna Festival {{!}} Steam of Life {{!}} POV {{!}} PBS|last=POV|language=en-US|access-date=2016-07-21}}</ref>
