'''Savage X Fenty''' is a [[lingerie]] line by [[Rihanna]]. Founded in 2018,<ref name="COS">{{Cite web|date=2020-09-17|title=Rihanna reveals star-studded lineup for 2020 Savage x Fenty show with Travis Scott, Rosalía, more|url=https://consequenceofsound.net/2020/09/rihanna-2020-savage-x-fenty-travis-scott-rosalia/|access-date=2020-09-19|website=Consequence of Sound|language=en-US}}</ref> the brand has an optional membership program.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Tiffany|first=Kaitlyn|date=2019-08-28|title=We may never know how many people have accidentally signed up to pay Rihanna $50 a month|url=https://www.vox.com/the-goods/2019/8/28/20833663/rihanna-savage-fenty-vip-membership-fee-fabletics|access-date=2020-09-19|website=Vox|language=en}}</ref>

==Fashion shows==
The brand's first fashion show was held at [[Brooklyn Navy Yard]] in 2019, as part of [[New York Fashion Week]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Rihanna’s Ground-Breaking Savage X Fenty Show Is Back|url=https://www.vogue.co.uk/news/article/rihanna-savage-fenty-show-2020|access-date=2020-09-19|website=British Vogue|language=en-GB}}</ref> The second show, ''[[Savage X Fenty Show|Savage X Fenty Show Vol. 2]]'', aired on [[Amazon Prime Video]] on October 2, 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-09-17|title=Rihanna Is Bringing Her Annual Savage X Fenty Show Back to Amazon Prime Video|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/9451173/rihanna-savage-x-fenty-back-to-amazon-prime-video|access-date=2020-09-19|website=Billboard}}</ref> Slated performers include [[Travis Scott]], [[Rosalía (singer)|Rosalía]], Miguel, [[Bad Bunny]], Mustard, [[Roddy Ricch]], and [[Ella Mai]].<ref name=COS/> The show featured black breast cancer survivors as part of [[Breast Cancer Awareness Month|Breast Cancer Awareness month]] and to benefit [[Clara Lionel Foundation]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=CNN|first=Jacqui Palumbo|title=Rihanna's latest Savage X Fenty campaign stars Black breast cancer survivors|url=https://www.cnn.com/style/article/savage-x-fenty-breast-cancer-awareness-campaign/index.html|access-date=2020-10-22|website=CNN|language=en}}</ref>
