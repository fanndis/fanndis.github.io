'''Eknath Gaikwad''' (born 1 January 1940) is an Indian politician from the [[Indian National Congress]] (INC) political party. He was a member of the [[15th Lok Sabha]] and the [[14th Lok Sabha]] of [[India]].

== Career ==
He represented the [[Mumbai South Central (Lok Sabha Constituency)|Mumbai South Central]] constituency of [[Mumbai]]. He lost the seat to [[Rahul Shewale]] in 2014. He has been three times a [[Member of Legislative Assembly (India)|Member of Legislative Assembly]] from [[Dharavi (Vidhan Sabha constituency)|Dharavi]] and also twice state minister of Maharashtra state cabinet. Since 1985, he has represented [[Dharavi]] which is Asia's largest slum.

== Positions held ==
* 1985-1990: Member of [[Maharashtra Legislative Assembly]]
* 1990-1995: Member of Maharashtra Legislative Assembly
* 1999-2004: Member of Maharashtra Legislative Assembly
* 2004-2009: [[Member of Parliament, Lok Sabha]]
* 2009-2014: Member of Parliament, Lok Sabha
