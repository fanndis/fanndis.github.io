The '''Producers Guild of America Award for Best Limited Series Television''', also known as the '''[[David L. Wolper]] Award for Outstanding Producer of Limited Series Television''', is an annual award given by the [[Producers Guild of America]]. It was first awarded at the [[Producers Guild of America Awards 2018|30th Annual Producers Guild Awards]] after the guild announced to split the [[Producers Guild of America Award for Best Long-Form Television|award for Outstanding Producer of Long-Form Television]] into two: this accolade and the [[Producers Guild of America Award for Best Streamed or Televised Movie|award for Outstanding Producer of Streamed or Televised Movies]].<ref>{{cite web |last1=Hipes |first1=Patrick |title=PGA Awards Splits Longform TV Categories Into Two |url=https://deadline.com/2018/08/producers-guild-awards-longform-tv-categories-changes-split-1202442448/ |website=Deadline Hollywood |accessdate=January 6, 2020 |date=August 8, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=The PGA Creates Two New Accolades In Long-Form Television For 2019 Awards |url=https://www.producersguild.org/news/412506/The-PGA-Creates-Two-New-Accolades-In-Long-Form-Television-For-2019-Awards.htm |publisher=Producers Guild of America |accessdate=January 6, 2020 |date=August 8, 2018}}</ref>
