'''''Country Standard Time''''' is a website dedicated to [[country music]] and related genres including [[Americana (music)|Americana]], [[bluegrass music|bluegrass]] and [[rockabilly]]. It provides news and musical reviews pertaining to the genre.<ref>{{cite web|author = Jeffrey B. Remz|title=Country Standard Time - About|url= http://www.countrystandardtime.com/about.asp|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090324025520/http://www.countrystandardtime.com/about.asp|archivedate=March 24, 2009|accessdate=March 26, 2009}} [http://google.com/search?q=cache:3984nixJYrQJ:www.countrystandardtime.com/about.asp+Country+Standard+Time+about&cd=2&hl=en&ct=clnk&gl=us&client=firefox-a Alt URL]</ref> It was established in 1993 by Jeffrey B. Remz as a print magazine, which was first published only in [[New England]] but went nationwide in 1995.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://nl.newsbank.com/nl-search/we/Archives?p_product=BG&p_theme=bg&p_action=search&p_maxdocs=200&p_topdoc=1&p_text_direct-0=0F4110343306497E&p_field_direct-0=document_id&p_perpage=10&p_sort=YMD_date:D&s_trackval=GooglePM|title=Country music magazine thriving in northern city|last=Rowland|first=Christopher|date=June 6, 2002|work=[[Boston Globe]]|accessdate=March 27, 2009}}</ref> The magazine has had a website since 1997,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://nl.newsbank.com/nl-search/we/Archives?p_product=WT&p_theme=wt&p_action=search&p_maxdocs=200&p_topdoc=1&p_text_direct-0=0EB0F248F65E5C03&p_field_direct-0=document_id&p_perpage=10&p_sort=YMD_date:D&s_trackval=GooglePM|title=Country music spins own Web|date=January 4, 1997|work=The Washington Times|accessdate=March 27, 2009}}</ref> and ended its print publication in January 2009.<ref>{{cite web|title=History of Country Standard Time|url=http://www.countrystandardtime.com/about.asp|publisher=Country Standard Time|accessdate=November 21, 2015}}</ref>

The web site has features, news and CD, concert and book reviews and attracts about 50,000 page views per month.
