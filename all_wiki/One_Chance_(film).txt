'''''One Chance''''' is a 2013 British-American biographical film about opera singer and ''[[Britain's Got Talent]]'' winner [[Paul Potts]],<ref name="Metro">{{cite web |url=http://metro.co.uk/2013/07/12/james-corden-is-britains-got-talent-winner-paul-potts-in-first-image-from-one-chance-3880793/ |title=James Corden is ''Britain's Got Talent'' winner Paul Potts in first image from ''One Chance''|accessdate=9 August 2013 |work=Metro}}</ref> directed by [[David Frankel]] and written by [[Justin Zackham]]. It was screened in the Special Presentation section at the [[2013 Toronto International Film Festival]].<ref name="TIFF">{{cite web |url=http://tiff.net/filmsandschedules/festival/2013/onechance |title=''One Chance''|accessdate=9 August 2013 |work=TIFF |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130809200852/http://tiff.net/filmsandschedules/festival/2013/onechance |archivedate=9 August 2013  }}</ref>

==Production==
Director [[David Frankel]] reduced his directing fee of US$6 million to US$1.2 million in exchange for a larger share of the film's gross.<ref name="Salaries">{{cite web |url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/lists/hollywood-salaries-2016-who-got-933037/item/hollywood-salaries-revealed-who-got-933050 |title=Hollywood Salaries 2016: Who Got Raises (and Who Didn't), From Movie Stars to Showrunners |author=THR Staff |work=[[The Hollywood Reporter]] |date=29 September 2016 |accessdate=1 October 2016}}</ref> ''One Chance'' was shot in Italy and the United Kingdom.<ref name="ScreenDaily">{{cite web |url=http://www.screendaily.com/news/filming-wraps-on-david-frankels-one-chance/5049631.article |title=Filming wraps on David Frankel's ''One Chance'' |accessdate=2 September 2013 |work=Screen Daily}}</ref><ref name="DigitalSpy">{{cite web |url=http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/movies/s107/britains-got-talent/news/a442534/paul-potts-biopic-one-chance-filming-wraps.html |title=Paul Potts biopic ''One Chance'' filming wraps |accessdate=2 September 2013 |work=Digital Spy}}</ref>

==Release==
The film was shown at the [[2013 Toronto International Film Festival]] on 9 September 2013 and released in cinemas on 25 October 2013.

==Reception==
The film received mixed to positive reviews. {{As of|April 2019}} it holds an approval rating of 63% on Rotten Tomatoes, based on 43 reviews, with an average score of 5.4 out of 10; the consensus states: "Predictable and sentimental, yet thoroughly agreeable, ''One Chance'' is an unapologetic crowd-pleaser that achieves its admittedly modest goals."<ref name="Rotten Tomatoes">{{cite web |url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/one_chance/ |title=''One Chance''|accessdate=8 October 2014 |work=Rotten Tomatoes}}</ref> On Metacritic, the film has a 49/100 rating based on 12 critics, signifying "mixed or average reviews".<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.metacritic.com/movie/one-chance | title=''One Chance''| work=[[Metacritic]] | publisher=[[CBS Interactive]] | accessdate=8 October 2014}}</ref>
