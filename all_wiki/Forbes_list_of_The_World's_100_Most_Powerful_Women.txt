Since 2004, ''[[Forbes]]'' has compiled a list of the 100 most powerful women in the world. It is edited by notable ''Forbes'' journalists, including [[Moira Forbes]], and is based on visibility and economic impact. [[Chancellor of Germany|German Chancellor]] [[Angela Merkel]] has remained at the top spot since 2006, with the brief exception of 2010 where she was temporarily supplanted by then [[U.S. First Lady]] [[Michelle Obama]].
