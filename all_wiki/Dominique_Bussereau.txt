'''Dominique Bussereau''' (born 13 July 1952 in [[Tours]], [[Indre-et-Loire]]) is a [[France|French]] politician.  
He is president of the [[Departmental council (France)|departmental council]] of [[Charente-Maritime]] since
2008 and president of the {{ill|Assembly of the Departments of France|fr|Assemblée des départements de France}} since 2015.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Dominique Bussereau a été élu patron des départements de France|url=https://france3-regions.francetvinfo.fr/nouvelle-aquitaine/charente-maritime/dominique-bussereau-ete-elu-patron-des-departements-de-france-723985.html|access-date=2020-06-08|website=France 3 Nouvelle-Aquitaine|language=fr}}</ref> 

He was Secretary of State for Transport within the government of [[François Fillon]]. Appointed to the post on 18 May 2007, he was previously Minister of Agriculture (2004–2007), Minister-Delegate for Aviation and Maritime Affairs (2002–2004) and Minister-Delegate for Budgets (2004).

==Political career==

'''Governmental functions'''

Secretary of State for Transport : 2007–2010.

Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries, Food and Rural Affairs : 2004–2007.

Secretary of State for the Budget : March–November 2004.

Secretary of State for Transport and Sea : 2002–2004.

'''Electoral mandates'''

'''''National Assembly of France'''''

[[National Assembly of France|MP]] of the [[Charente-Maritime's 4th constituency]] : 1986–1988 / 1993–2002 (Became secretary of State in 2002) / Re-elected in 2007, but he stays minister. Elected in [[1986 French legislative election|1986]], re-elected in [[1993 French legislative election|1993]], [[1997 French legislative election|1997]], [[2002 French legislative election|2002]], [[2007 French legislative election|2007]], [[2012 French legislative election|2012]].

'''''Regional Council'''''

[[Poitou-Charentes Regional Council|Regional councillor]] of [[Poitou-Charentes]] : 1992–1993 (Resignation) / March–April 2004 (Resignation) / March–September 2010 (Resignation). Re-elected in 2004, 2010.

'''''General Council'''''

President of the [[Charente-Maritime]]'s [[General councils (France)|General Council]] : Since 2008.

General councillor of [[Charente-Maritime]] : Since 1985. Re-elected in 1992, 1998, 2004.

'''''Municipal Council'''''

Mayor of [[Saint-Georges-de-Didonne]] : 1989–2002 (Resignation). Re-elected in 1995, 2001.

Deputy-mayor of [[Saint-Georges-de-Didonne]] : 2002–2008.

Municipal councillor of [[Saint-Georges-de-Didonne]] : 1989–2008. Re-elected in 1995, 2001.

Deputy-mayor of [[Royan]] : 1983–1989.

Municipal councillor of [[Royan]] : 1983–1989.
