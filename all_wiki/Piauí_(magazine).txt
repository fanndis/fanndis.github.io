'''''piauí''''' is a [[Brazil]]ian [[culture]] [[magazine]], created by the [[documentarian]] [[João Moreira Salles]]. It is edited by [[Editora Alvinegra]] and printed and distributed by [[Editora Abril]].  The first edition of ''piauí'' was launched in October 2006. 

[[Piauí]] is also the name of a Brazilian state in the northeastern part of the country.

These are the main sections in '''piauí''' nowadays:

* Chegada (Arrival)
* Colaboradores (Collaborators)
* Esquina (Corner)
* Diário (Journal)
* Poesia (Poetry)
* Cartas (Letters)
* Perfil (Profile)
* Quadrinhos (Comics)
* Ficção (Fiction)
* Concurso (Literary contest)
* Tipos brasileiros (Brazilian characters)
* Despedida (Farewell)
