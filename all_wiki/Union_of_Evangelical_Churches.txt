The '''Union Evangelischer Kirchen''' (German: ''Union Evangelischer Kirchen'', '''UEK''') is an organisation of 13 [[United church|United]] and [[Reformed]] evangelical churches in [[Germany]], which are all member churches of the [[Evangelical Church in Germany]].

== History ==
The UEK was founded on July 1, 2003. The organisation succeeded the former organisation [[Evangelical Church of the Union]] ({{lang-de|Evangelische Kirche der Union}}, EKU). The [[seat (legal entity)|seat]] of the organisation used to be [[Berlin]]. For structural reasons, it was moved to the seat of the [[Evangelical Church in Germany]] (EKD) in [[Hanover]] though.
On November 9, 2019 Union of Evangelical Churches allowed [[Blessing of same-sex unions in Christian churches|blessings of same-sex marriages]].<ref>[https://www.evangelisch.de/inhalte/162309/09-11-2019/uek-ordnung-fuer-die-trauung-von-ehepaaren-gleichen-geschlechts  UEK: Ordnung f체r die Trauung von Ehepaaren gleichen Geschlechts (German)]</ref>
