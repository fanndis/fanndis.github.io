'''Sabrina Annlynn Carpenter'''<ref>{{cite web|url=https://ohmy.disney.com/music/2014/10/13/sabrina-carpenter-takes-the-playlist-pop-quiz/|title=Sabrina Carpenter Takes the Playlist Pop Quiz|work=Oh My Disney|author=Emily Brandon|date=October 13, 2014|access-date=November 10, 2017}}</ref> (born May 11, 1999<ref name=medianet />) is an American singer and actress,<ref>{{cite web|last1=Naoreen|first1=Nuzhat|title='Girl Meets World' Star Sabrina Carpenter Talks Working With Meghan Trainor and BeyoncÃ©'S Decision to Pass on 'All About That Bass'|url=http://blog.peopleschoice.com/2014/12/26/sabrina-carpenter-working-with-meghan-trainor/|website=People's Choice|access-date=January 17, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160801110718/http://blog.peopleschoice.com/2014/12/26/sabrina-carpenter-working-with-meghan-trainor/|archive-date=August 1, 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref> who is signed to [[Hollywood Records]].<ref name="ew-201301312">{{cite news|url=http://insidetv.ew.com/2013/01/31/boy-meets-world-spin-off-maya/|title='Boy Meets World' spin-off casts Riley's best friend|last=Hibberd|first=James|date=January 31, 2013|work=[[Entertainment Weekly]]|access-date=February 1, 2013|publisher=[[Meredith Corporation]]|location=New York city}}</ref> After making her acting debut with an appearance in the crime series ''[[Law & Order: Special Victims Unit]]'', she starred in a recurring role as the young version of Chloe Goodwin in the television series ''[[The Goodwin Games]]''. Carpenter was later cast as Maya Hart in the [[Disney Channel]] series ''[[Girl Meets World]]'', from 2014 to 2017.<ref name="ew-20130131">{{cite news|first=James|last=Hibberd|title='Boy Meets World' spin-off casts Riley's best friend |url=http://insidetv.ew.com/2013/01/31/boy-meets-world-spin-off-maya/|access-date=February 1, 2013 |work=[[Entertainment Weekly]]|publisher=[[Meredith Corporation]]|location=New York city|date=January 31, 2013}}</ref> Carpenter has starred in the feature films [[Horns (film)|''Horns'']] (2013), [[The Hate U Give (film)|''The Hate U Give'']] (2018), and starred as Jenny in the [[Disney Channel Original Movie]] ''[[Adventures in Babysitting (2016 film)|Adventures in Babysitting]]'' (2016). As a voice actress, she had a recurring role as Princess Vivian in the [[Disney Channel]] animated series ''[[Sofia the First]]'' from 2013 to 2018. In 2016, she began voicing Melissa Chase on the [[Disney XD]] animated series ''[[Milo Murphy's Law]]''.

Carpenter's debut EP, ''[[Can't Blame a Girl for Trying]],'' was released in 2014, and in the same year, she released her debut single [[Can't Blame a Girl for Trying (song)|of the same name]]. She released her debut album, ''[[Eyes Wide Open (Sabrina Carpenter album)|Eyes Wide Open]]'' in 2015, and has since released another three studio albums: ''[[Evolution (Sabrina Carpenter album)|Evolution]]'' (2016), ''[[Singular: Act I]]'' (2018), and her most recent studio album, ''[[Singular: Act II]]'', was released on July 19, 2019.
