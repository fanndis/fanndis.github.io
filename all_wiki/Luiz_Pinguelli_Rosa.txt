'''Luiz Pingueli Rosa''' (born 1942) is a [[Brazil]]ian [[nuclear physics|nuclear physicist]], [[researcher]] and professor at the [[Federal University of Rio de Janeiro]]. He is also a scientific leader. He lives in the city of [[Rio de Janeiro]].

One of Rosa's fields activity is [[renewable energy]].
