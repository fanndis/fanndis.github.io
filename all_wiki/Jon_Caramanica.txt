'''Jon Caramanica''' (born 1975) is an American journalist and pop music critic who writes for ''[[The New York Times]]''.<ref name=nyt>{{Cite web |url=https://www.nytimes.com/by/jon-caramanica |title=Jon Caramanica |website=The New York Times |access-date=2017-08-24}}</ref><ref name=adweek/> He is known for writing about [[hip hop music]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.npr.org/sections/therecord/2014/02/20/280207349/a-phony-article-epitomizes-hip-hops-struggling-underclass |title=A Phony Article Epitomizes Hip-Hop's Struggling Underclass |last=Ex |first=Kris |date=2014-02-20 |website=NPR.org |language=en |access-date=2017-08-24}}</ref>

==Biography==
Born in [[Brooklyn]], [[New York (state)|New York]], Caramanica received his bachelor's degree from [[Harvard University]] in 1997, after which he attended [[Goldsmiths, University of London]] where he did PhD work but failed to get a PhD.<ref name=nyt/><ref name=adweek>{{Cite web |url=http://www.adweek.com/digital/vibe-adds-four-new-staffers/ |title=Vibe Adds Four New Staffers |date=2006-08-09 |website=Adweek |language=en-US |access-date=2017-08-24}}</ref> He has published articles in ''[[Rolling Stone]]'' and ''[[Spin (magazine)|Spin]]'', before becoming a senior contributing writer for ''[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]''. In 2006, he left ''XXL'' to become the music editor for ''[[Vibe (magazine)|Vibe]]'',<ref name=adweek/> a position he held until leaving the magazine in 2008.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://observer.com/2008/03/dont-stop-the-music-itimesi-hunts-at-slate-ivibei-for-new-pop-critic/ |title=Donâ€™t Stop the Music! Times Hunts at Slate, Vibe for New Pop Critic |last=Koblin |first=John |date=2008-03-18 |website=Observer |language=en-US |access-date=2017-08-24}}</ref> He began working for ''[[The New York Times]]'' in 2010, after previously having freelanced for the paper.<ref name=nyt/> He also hosts the music podcast ''Popcast''.<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/06/27/arts/music/popcast-kanye-west.html |title=Kanye West: You Asked, We Answered |date=2018-06-27 |work=The New York Times |access-date=2018-09-01 |language=en}}</ref>
