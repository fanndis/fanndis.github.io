The '''Satellite Awards''' are annual awards given by the [[International Press Academy]] that are commonly noted in entertainment industry journals and blogs.<ref>{{cite news |title='Silver Linings Playbook' Wins Five Satellite Awards, Including Best Picture |date=December 16, 2012 |accessdate=January 19, 2018 |first=Gregg |last=Kilday |work=[[The Hollywood Reporter]] |url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/race/silver-linings-playbook-wins-satellite-403098 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170114052433/http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/race/silver-linings-playbook-wins-satellite-403098 |archive-date=January 14, 2017 |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title='Silver Linings,' 'Les Miz' lead Satellite awards noms |first=Anneta |last=Konstantinides |date=December 3, 2012 |accessdate=January 19, 2018 |work=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]] |url=https://variety.com/2012/film/news/silver-linings-les-miz-lead-satellite-awards-noms-1118063038/ |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170619231454/http://variety.com/2012/film/news/silver-linings-les-miz-lead-satellite-awards-noms-1118063038/ |archive-date=June 19, 2017 |url-status=live }}</ref> The awards were originally known as the '''Golden Satellite Awards'''. The award ceremonies take place each year at the [[InterContinental]] Hotel in [[Century City, Los Angeles]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.pressacademy.com/schedule/ |title=2012 Satellite Awards Schedule |publisher=[[International Press Academy]] |accessdate=December 29, 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303231156/http://www.pressacademy.com/schedule/ |archive-date=March 3, 2016 |url-status=live }}</ref>

In 2011, Satellite nominations in the motion picture categories were pared from 22 to 19 classifications; the change reflects the merger of Drama and Comedy/Musical under a general Best Picture heading, including the Best Actor, Best Actress, Supporting Actor and Supporting Actress headings.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.pressacademy.com/2011/11/2011-satellite-award-film-categories-streamlined-new-website-to-debut/ |title=2011 Satellite Award Film Categories Streamlined & New Website to Debut |publisher=[[International Press Academy]] |date=November 11, 2011 |accessdate=November 11, 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111203224845/http://www.pressacademy.com/2011/11/2011-satellite-award-film-categories-streamlined-new-website-to-debut/ |archive-date=December 3, 2011 |url-status=live }}</ref>
