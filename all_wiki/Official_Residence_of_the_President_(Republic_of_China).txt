The '''Official Residence of the President of the Republic of China''' is provided by the [[Government of the Republic of China|Government of the Republic of China (Taiwan)]] for the [[President of the Republic of China|President]], while in office, along with his or her family. The current official residence is at the intersection of [[Chongqing South Road]] Section 2 and [[Aiguo West Road]], [[Zhongzheng District|Zhongzheng]], [[Taipei]], and has been in use since [[Lee Teng-hui]]'s presidency. Security of the residence is maintained by the Sixth Special Corps of the [[National Police Agency (Republic of China)|National Police Agency]] along with a wall along the perimeter of the complex.

==Alias==
The Official Residence takes on a different alias depending on the code name assigned by the [[National Security Bureau (Republic of China)|National Security Bureau]]. The code name is usually selected depending on the current state of the nation, the transfer of power between political parties, or the incoming President's style of governance and ideals.
