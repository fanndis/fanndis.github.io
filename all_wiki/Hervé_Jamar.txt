'''Hervé Jamar''' is a Belgian politician.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rtbf.be/info/regions/liege/detail_herve-jamar-et-sa-femme-hebergent-des-migrants?id=9827044|title=Le gouverneur et ancien ministre MR Hervé Jamar et sa femme hébergent des migrants|date=January 31, 2018|accessdate=1 September 2018|work=[[RTBF]]}}</ref> He is a member of the [[Reformist Movement]] (MR). He is mayor of Hannut from 1995 to 2014. He became Belgium's [[Minister of Budget (Belgium)|Minister of Budget]] in 2014 but resigned in 2015 in order to become Governor of the [[Provinces of Belgium|Province]] of [[Liège Province|Liège]].
