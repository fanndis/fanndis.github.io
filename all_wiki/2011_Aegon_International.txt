The '''2011 [[Aegon International]]''' was a combined men's and women's [[tennis]] tournament played on [[Tennis court#Grass courts|outdoor grass courts]]. It was the 37th edition of the event for the women and the 3rd edition for the men. It was classified as a [[WTA Premier tournaments|WTA Premier tournament]] on the [[2011 WTA Tour]] and as an [[ATP World Tour 250 series]] on the [[2011 ATP World Tour]]. The event took place at the Devonshire Park Lawn Tennis Club in [[Eastbourne]], United Kingdom from June 11 through June 18, 2011. The men's doubles final was originally scheduled to take place on June 17, but due to rain delays it was eventually played on June 19 at Roehampton instead.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.atpworldtour.com/News/Tennis/2011/06/24/Doubles-Sunday-Eastbourne.aspx|date=19 June 2011|accessdate=19 June 2011|title=Erlich/Ram Win First Title Since 2008|publisher=atpworldtour.com}}</ref>

==Champions==

===Men's singles===
{{main|2011 Aegon International – Men's Singles}}
{{flagicon|ITA}} '''[[Andreas Seppi]]''' defeated {{flagicon|SRB}} [[Janko Tipsarević]], 7–6<sup>(7–5)</sup>, 3–6, 5–3 ret.
*It was Seppi's 1st career title.

===Women's singles===
{{main|2011 Aegon International – Women's Singles}}
{{flagicon|FRA}} '''[[Marion Bartoli]]''' defeated {{flagicon|CZE}} [[Petra Kvitová]], 6–1, 4–6, 7–5
*It was Bartoli's 1st title of the year and 6th of her career. It was her 2nd career Premier-level title. It was her first grass title.

===Men's doubles===
{{main|2011 Aegon International – Men's Doubles}}
*{{flagicon|ISR}} '''[[Jonathan Erlich]]''' / {{flagicon|ISR}} '''[[Andy Ram]]''' defeated {{flagicon|BUL}} [[Grigor Dimitrov]] / {{flagicon|ITA}} [[Andreas Seppi]], 6–3, 6–3

===Women's doubles===
{{main|2011 Aegon International – Women's Doubles}}
