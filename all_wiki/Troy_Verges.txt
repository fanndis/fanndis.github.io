'''Troy Verges''' is an American [[songwriter]] of [[country music|country]] and [[pop music]] from [[Louisiana]].

==Career==
His first cut as a songwriter was a track on [[Faith Hill]]'s ''[[Breathe (Faith Hill album)|Breathe]]''.<ref>{{cite news
| title       = Words & Music
| first      = Jim| last      = Bessman

| page        = 36

}}</ref>

His credits include the singles "[[Wanted (Hunter Hayes song)|Wanted]]", recorded by Hunter Hayes; "Beer Money", recorded by Kip Moore; "[[Who I Am (Jessica Andrews song)|Who I Am]]", recorded by [[Jessica Andrews]]; "[[Blessed (Martina McBride song)|Blessed]]", recorded by [[Martina McBride]]; "[[Wasted (Carrie Underwood song)|Wasted]]", recorded by Carrie Underwood; "Day Drinking", recorded by Little Big Town;<ref>[http://www.umgnashville.com/news/ittle-big-town-strikes-gold-with-no-1-day-drinking/].</ref> "[[Shotgun Rider]]", recorded by Tim McGraw;<ref>[http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/6363732/tim-mcgraw-25th-hot-country-songs-no-1-shotgun ''Billboard Magazine:'' Hot Country Songs].</ref> and "I Want Crazy",<ref>[http://www.allaccess.com/net-news/archive/story/121212/hunter-hayes-i-want-crazy-1].</ref> recorded by Hunter Hayes{{spaced ndash}} all of which went to number one on the country music charts.

"Wanted" was ACM nominated in 2013 for Song of the Year,<ref>[http://www.countryweekly.com/news/2013-acm-awards-nominees-winners].</ref> while "I Want Crazy" received a 2014 Grammy nomination<ref>[http://www.grammy.com/nominees?genre=8].</ref> and "Day Drinking" received a 2015 Grammy nomination.<ref>[https://www.usatoday.com/story/life/music/2014/12/05/2015-grammy-nomination-list/19954729/]. 
''[[USA Today]]''.</ref>

"Wanted", composed by Hunter Hayes and Troy Verges, took home Song Of The Year honors at the 2013 BMI Country Awards.<ref>[http://www.bmi.com/news/entry/2013_bmi_country_awards_honor_dillon_clawson_hayes_verges_and_others].</ref> and was also named one of Billboard's top ten songs of the decade <ref>https://www.billboard.com/charts/decade-end/hot-country-songs</ref>

Verges was named "Songwriter of the Year" in 2002 by [[Broadcast Music Incorporated]] and "Songwriter of the Year" by the [[Nashville Songwriters Association International]].

In 2011, Verges garnered both and Oscar nomination and a Golden Globe nomination in the Best Original Song category for his composition "Coming Home" from the film ''Country Strong''.

He produced and mixed the Anders Osborne album ''Coming Down''.<ref>[http://www.cduniverse.com/productinfo.asp?pid=7486189].</ref>
