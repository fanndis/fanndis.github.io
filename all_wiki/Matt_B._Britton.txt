'''Matt B. Britton''' (born January 24, 1975) is a new media entrepreneur and consumer trend expert based in [[New York City]].

Britton currently serves as Chief Executive Officer of Suzy, a real-time consumer intelligence software. Previously he served as Chief Executive Officer of MRY, a marketing and technology agency with over 500 employees that is part of the [[Publicis Groupe]].<ref>{{Cite web | url=http://everything-pr.com/mry-lbi-merger/240854/ |title = Publicis' MRY, LBi US Conclude Integration - Everything PR|date = 15 February 2013}}</ref> MRY, formed in 2013,  is the combination of Mr. Youth, (the agency Britton founded in 2002) and the former U.S. operations of [[LBi]]. The two agencies merged under Britton's leadership in January 2013 to become MRY.  MRY's clients include [[Visa Inc.|Visa]], [[Coca-Cola Company|Coca-Cola]], [[Bayer]], and [[Neutrogena]].<ref>[[Another Twist in Digitas-LBi Merger: LBi to Merge With MRY in U.S.]]</ref>  
== Career ==

=== The Magma Group ===
In January 1997, Britton (then a college senior at [[Boston University]]) founded The Magma Group, a college marketing firm focused on the hi-tech industry.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.thenyegotist.com/news/local/2011/december/12/acquired-lbi-mr-youth-looking-go-global |title=Archived copy |access-date=2014-02-09 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140222054318/http://www.thenyegotist.com/news/local/2011/december/12/acquired-lbi-mr-youth-looking-go-global |archive-date=2014-02-22 |url-status=dead }}</ref> The Magma Group's clients included [[Yahoo!]], [[Lycos]], [[eBay]], and [[Sony Music]]. At the time Britton founded the Magma Group, he was involved in a successful entertainment promotions venture with hospitality entrepreneur [[Jason Strauss]].  In December 2002, The Magma Group was acquired in part by [[YouthStream Media Networks]], where Britton was retained as the Director of Event Marketing Development.

Britton was named to the [[Entrepreneur Magazine]] "30 Under 30" list (the top 30 U.S. entrepreneurs under 30 years of age) in April 2000.<ref>Entrepreneur Magazine 2nd Annual Hot List http://216.39.100.211/magazine/businessstartupsmagazine/2000/april/23832-6.html/{{dead link|date=January 2018 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

=== CrowdTap ===

In July 2011, Britton executed the spinoff of CrowdTap, a social media marketing software platform.<ref>{{Cite web | url=https://venturebeat.com/2011/07/29/crowdtap-influence-funding/ |title = Crowdtap receives $7M so you can give big companies a piece of your mind|date = 29 July 2011}}</ref> Shortly thereafter CrowdTap completed $7 Million in Series A Funding from the Foundry Group.<ref>{{Cite web | url=https://techcrunch.com/2011/07/27/crowdtap-raises-7-million-to-help-brands-connect-with-their-influential-customers/ |title = Crowdtap Raises $7 Million to Help Brands Connect with Their Influential Customers}}</ref> The company raised another $5Mill in Series B funding in 2014 from Foundry Group and others.<ref>{{Cite web | url=https://techcrunch.com/2014/03/20/crowdtap-series-b/ |title = Brand Advocacy Startup Crowdtap Raises Another $5M}}</ref> Britton served CrowdTap as their Chief Advisor from July 2011 to February 2017.<ref>http://corp.crowdtap.com/about</ref> On February 2017, Britton became the Chief Executive Officer.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.adweek.com/agencyspy/mry-co-founder-matt-britton-becomes-chief-executive-officer-at-crowdtap/125465|title=MRY Co-Founder Matt Britton Becomes Chief Executive Officer at Crowdtap|website=www.adweek.com|language=en-US|access-date=2018-04-11}}</ref>

=== YouthNation ===

In April 2015 Britton published his first book entitled "YouthNation" with Wiley Publishing. The book explores the impact millennials have on the future of business and brand building.<ref>{{Cite web | url=http://www.wiley.com/WileyCDA/WileyTitle/productCd-1118981146.html/ |title = YouthNation: Building Remarkable Brands in a Youth-Driven Culture &#124; Wiley}}</ref> The book peaked at #8 on the Los Angeles Times bestsellers list.<ref>{{Cite web | url=http://projects.latimes.com/bestsellers/titles/youthnation/ |title = Bestsellers}}</ref>
