'''Anthony Mandler''' (born April 18, 1973) is an American film director, music video director, television commercial director and photographer. As a music video director, his most notable and frequent collaborator is [[Rihanna]]. The two have worked on sixteen music videos together throughout her career, beginning with "[[Unfaithful (song)|Unfaithful]]" in 2006 and most recently "[[Diamonds (Rihanna song)|Diamonds]]" in 2012.<ref name="Rihanna work">{{cite web|last=Montgomery|first=James|title=Does Rihanna's Director Know Something About 'Diamonds' That We Don't?|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/articles/1697489/rihanna-diamonds-music-video-director-secrets.jhtml|work=MTV|accessdate=17 May 2013}}</ref> He has also written and directed music videos for many other prominent artists including the [[Spice Girls]], [[Jay Z]], [[Beyoncé]], [[Eminem]], [[Usher (entertainer)|Usher]], [[Shakira]], [[Taylor Swift]], [[The Killers]], [[Selena Gomez]], [[Justin Bieber]], [[Christina Aguilera]], [[50 Cent]], [[Ne-Yo]], [[Nicki Minaj]], [[Drake (entertainer)|Drake]], [[Snoop Dogg]], [[Lenny Kravitz]], [[Cheryl Cole]], [[M.I.A. (rapper)|M.I.A.]], [[Mary J. Blige]], [[fun.]] and [[Lana Del Rey]].<ref name="Music video work">{{cite web|title=Anthony Mandler - Music Videos|url=http://blackhandcinema.com/black-hand-cinema-anthony-mandler-music-videos.php|work=BlackHandCinema|accessdate=17 May 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130521193628/http://www.blackhandcinema.com/black-hand-cinema-anthony-mandler-music-videos.php|archive-date=21 May 2013|url-status=dead}}</ref>

Beginning his career as a photographer, Mandler's work has been featured on the covers of major magazines such as ''[[Entertainment Weekly]]'', ''[[GQ]]'', ''[[Esquire (magazine)|Esquire]]'', ''[[Men's Health (magazine)|Men's Health]]'', and ''[[ESPN The Magazine]]''. His celebrity portraits include, among others, [[David Beckham]], [[Colin Farrell]], [[James Franco]], [[Heath Ledger]], [[Ryan Phillippe]], [[Eva Mendes]], [[LeBron James]], [[Taylor Lautner]], [[Andrew Garfield]], [[Kiefer Sutherland]] and [[Katie Holmes]].<ref name="Photography work">{{cite web|title=Anthony Mandler - Photography|url=http://artmixcreative.com/photo-gallery/anthony-mandler/celebrity|work=ArtMixCreative|accessdate=17 May 2013}}</ref> For commercials, he has worked with international clients such as [[Nike, Inc.]], [[Motorola]], [[Duracell]] and [[Cîroc]].<ref name="Commercials work">{{cite web|title=Anthony Mandler - Commercials|url=http://blackhandcinema.com/black-hand-cinema-anthony-mandler-commercials.php|work=BlackHandCinema|accessdate=17 May 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120802082808/http://blackhandcinema.com/black-hand-cinema-anthony-mandler-commercials.php|archive-date=2 August 2012|url-status=dead}}</ref>

In film, Mandler was set to make his feature film directorial debut with the film ''[[Tokyo Vice (film)|Tokyo Vice]]'' based on the [[Jake Adelstein]] book of the same name. [[J. T. Rogers]] would be providing the screenplay, with [[Daniel Radcliffe]] starring in the lead role.<ref name="Tokyo Vice">{{cite web|last=Fleming|first=Mike|title=Daniel Radcliffe To Star In Crime Saga ‘Tokyo Vice’|url=https://www.deadline.com/2013/05/daniel-radcliffe-to-star-in-crime-saga-tokyo-vice/|work=Deadline.com|accessdate=17 May 2013}}</ref> Mandler ultimately made his debut with the 2018 film ''[[Monster (2018 American film)|Monster]]''.

==Personal life==
Mandler was born in Los Angeles, California. He is married to actress [[Denise Vasi]], they wed in October 2013.<ref>{{cite web|title="Single Ladies" Star Denise Vasi Gets Engaged |url=http://globalgrind.com/entertainment/denise-vasi-engaged-anthony-mandler-fiance-photos-engagement-ring |publisher=GlobalGrind |date=July 10, 2012 |accessdate=May 17, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Single Ladies Star Denise Vasi Weds Anthony Mandler |url=http://globalgrind.com/entertainment/denise-vasi-engaged-anthony-mandler-fiance-photos-engagement-ring |publisher=VH1.com |date=October 14, 2013 |accessdate=December 19, 2013}}</ref> They have a daughter named Lennox Mae Mandler, who was born in February 2015.
