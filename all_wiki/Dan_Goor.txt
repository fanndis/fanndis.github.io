'''Daniel Joshua Goor''' (born April 28, 1975)<ref name=NYT>{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2003/06/22/style/weddings-celebrations-purvi-shah-daniel-goor.html|title=Wedding/Celebration; Purvi Shah, Daniel Goor|work=[[The New York Times]]|date=June 22, 2003}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Dan Goor on Twitter {{!}} Today is my birthday, and the only present I want is for everyone in America to watch #brooklyn99... |url=https://twitter.com/djgoor/status/990233218614886401 |website=Twitter |accessdate=23 September 2018 |language=en}}</ref> is an American comedy writer and television producer.  He has written for several comedy talk shows including ''[[The Daily Show]]'', ''[[Last Call with Carson Daly]]'' and ''[[Late Night with Conan O'Brien]]''.  He also worked as a writer, producer, and director for [[NBC]] primetime series ''[[Parks and Recreation]]'', and is executive-producer and co-creator of the [[Fox Broadcasting Company|Fox]]/[[NBC]] series ''[[Brooklyn Nine-Nine]]''.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[IndieWire]]|url=https://www.indiewire.com/2018/07/brooklyn-99-andy-samberg-season-6-fox-news-comic-con-1201985899/|title=‘Brooklyn Nine-Nine’: Andy Samberg Says It’s a ‘Good Moment’ for the Inclusive Comedy to Distance Itself From Fox|author=Travers, Ben|date=July 20, 2018}}</ref>

==Personal life==
