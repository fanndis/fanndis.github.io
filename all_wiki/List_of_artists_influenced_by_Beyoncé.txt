[[Beyoncé]] is an American singer, songwriter, dancer and actress. As a prominent figure within popular culture during the early 21st century, Beyoncé's contributions to music through recorded releases and live performances established her as a culturally influential icon.<ref>{{cite web|last=Bryant|first=Taylor|date=December 10, 2019|url=https://www.insider.com/beyonce-artist-of-the-decade-2010s-2019-12|title=Beyoncé is the artist of the decade|website=[[Insider.com]]|accessdate=September 14, 2020}}</ref> [[Clive Davis]] referred to her as the "First Lady of Music".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.essence.com/celebrity/clive-davis-beyonce-first-lady-music/|title=Clive Davis Declares Beyonce 'The First Lady Of Music'|first=Paula|last=Rogo|date=January 28, 2018|publisher=Essence}}</ref> She is cited by ''[[Time (magazine)|Time]]'' magazine as a [[pop icon]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://time100.time.com/2013/04/18/time-100/slide/beyonce/|title=The 2013 TIME 100|first=Baz|last=Luhrmann|publisher=|via=time100.time.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.com/culture/story/20150415-beyonc-voice-of-a-generation|title=Why Beyoncé speaks for a generation|first=Henry|last=Knight|publisher=}}</ref>
