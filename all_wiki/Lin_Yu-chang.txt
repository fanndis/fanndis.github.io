'''Lin Yu-chang''' ({{zh|t=林右昌|p=Lín Yòuchāng}}; born 10 March 1971) is a politician in the [[Republic of China]]. He is currently the [[Mayor of Keelung|Mayor]] of [[Keelung City]] since 25 December 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://focustaiwan.tw/news/aipl/201411290036.aspx|title=DPP's Lin Yu-chang declares win in Keelung mayoral election (update)|publisher=}}</ref>

==Early life==
Lin did his bachelor's degree in landscape architecture from [[Chinese Culture University]] and master's degree in building and planning from [[National Taiwan University]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.klcg.gov.tw/en/02/02_1.jsp|title=City Government-Mayor|first=Keelung City|last=Government|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141213105308/http://www.klcg.gov.tw/en/02/02_1.jsp|archivedate=2014-12-13}}</ref>

==Mayor of Keelung City==

===2014 Keelung mayoral election===
Lin was elected as the Mayor of Keelung City after winning the [[2014 Taiwan local elections|2014 Keelung City mayoral election]] held on 29 November 2014.
