'''Roberto Villanueva Puno''' (born March 27, 1962), also known as '''Robbie Puno''', is a [[Philippines|Filipino]] politician. A member of the [[Kabalikat ng Malayang Pilipino|KAMPI]] party, he was elected a Member of the [[House of Representatives of the Philippines]], representing the [[Legislative_districts_of_Antipolo_City#1st_District|1st District of Antipolo City]] from 2007 to 2016, and again in 2019.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.congress.gov.ph/members/search.php?congress=14&id=puno-rob |title=Member Information: Roberto V. Puno |accessdate=2008-03-05 |publisher=[[House of Representatives of the Philippines]] }}</ref>

Puno is a graduate of Ateneo de Manila University <ref>[http://ateneo.edu/]</ref> and is a staunch supporter of Philippine basketball.<ref>[https://southluzon.politics.com.ph/2017/12/16/basketball-life-antipolo-rep-robbie-puno-begins-preps-ph-hosting-fiba-world-cup-2023/]</ref>
