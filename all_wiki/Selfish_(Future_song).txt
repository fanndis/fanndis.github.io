"'''Selfish'''" is a song by American rapper [[Future (rapper)|Future]] for his sixth studio album, ''[[Hndrxx]]'' (2017), featuring Barbadian singer [[Rihanna]], the song was released as the lead single from ''Hndrxx'' on February 28, 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hiphop-n-more.com/2017/02/future-selfish-feat-rihanna-first-single-hndrxx/|title=Future Releases Rihanna Assisted 'Selfish' As First Single From 'HNDRXX'|work=HipHop-N-More|accessdate=March 3, 2017}}</ref> The track was produced by [[Detail (record producer)|Detail]], Major Seven and Mantra.

==Composition==
"Selfish" draws influences from the [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] and [[Pop music|pop]] music genres.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rap-up.com/2017/02/24/new-music-future-rihanna-selfish/|title=New Music: Future feat. Rihanna – 'Selfish'|website=Rap-up.com|accessdate=13 August 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/7709627/future-hndrxx-alter-egos-no-gimmick|title='FUTURE' vs. 'HNDRXX': Future Proves His Alter Egos Are No Gimmick|website=Billboard.com|accessdate=13 August 2018}}</ref> Future's voice is set with [[Auto-Tune]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/2986947/rihanna-future-selfish-duet/|title=Rihanna And Future Stir Up Romantic Vibes On Their New Song ‘Selfish’|website=Mtv.com|accessdate=13 August 2018}}</ref>
