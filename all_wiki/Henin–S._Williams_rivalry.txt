Tennis players [[Justine Henin]] from [[Belgium]], former world No. 1, and [[Serena Williams]] from the [[United States of America|USA]], also former World No. 1,<ref>[http://www.sonyericssonwtatour.com/page/Player/Stats/0,,12781~3541,00.html Sony Ericsson WTA Tour: Justine Henin stats]</ref>
<ref>[http://www.sonyericssonwtatour.com/page/Player/Stats/0,,12781~9044,00.html Sony Ericsson WTA Tour: Serena Williams stats]</ref>
who met 14 times during their careers. When Henin was an active player, their rivalry was one of the most heated and competitive between two female players on the [[WTA Tour]];<ref>[http://www.foxsports.com.au/story/0,8659,26565879-23216,00.html?from=public_rss Serena relishing Henin's return]</ref> their contests were not solely athletic in nature, but interpersonal as well.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.aolnews.com/2011/02/25/eight-years-later-serena-williams-cant-get-over-justine-henin/ |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2013-06-25 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140305193747/http://www.aolnews.com/2011/02/25/eight-years-later-serena-williams-cant-get-over-justine-henin/ |archivedate=2014-03-05 }}</ref>

As of June 30, 2013, Williams leads the overall head-to-head series, 8–6.<ref>[http://www.sonyericssonwtatour.com/headtohead/justine-henin_2255881_3541/0,,12781~3541~9044,00.html Sony Ericsson WTA Tour: Head-to-Head]</ref> Henin retired for the second time in January 2011.<ref>http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport2/hi/tennis/9377012.stm</ref>
