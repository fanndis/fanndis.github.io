'''''InStyle''''' is a monthly women's [[fashion magazine]] founded in 1994.<ref>{{cite news|first= Matthew|last= Rose|title='Real Simple' Magazine was Anything but for Time Inc.|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/SB960332052872538095|accessdate=December 12, 2015|work=The Wall Street Journal|date=June 7, 2000}}</ref> It is published in the US by [[Meredith Corporation]].<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.magazine-agent.com/instyle/magazine|title=InStyle Magazine|work=Magazine Agent|accessdate=March 21, 2012}}</ref>

==Description==
Along with advertising, the magazine offers articles about content such as beauty, fashion, home, entertaining, philanthropy and celebrity lifestyles. The current [[editor-in-chief]] is [[Laura Brown (fashion journalist)|Laura Brown]], and the publisher is Laura Frerer-Schmidt.

After originating in the United States, ''InStyle'' has expanded their brand and, as of 2012, distributes internationally to over 16 countries including [[Germany]], [[Brazil]], [[Greece]], [[South Korea]], [[Spain]], [[Turkey]], [[South Africa]], [[Romania]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.magxone.com/instyle/simona-patruleasa-instyle-romania-july-2012/|title=Simona Patruleasa, ''InStyle Romania'', July 2012|work= Magxone.com|date=July 23, 2012| url-status= dead| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20130311001146/http://www.magxone.com/instyle/simona-patruleasa-instyle-romania-july-2012/|archivedate= March 11, 2013|accessdate= July 25, 2017}}</ref> and [[Russia]]. In May 2017, ''InStyle China'' was launched as part of a partnership with an existing weekly magazine,{{Citation needed|date=August 2018}} which featured [[Victoria Beckham]] on its inaugural cover.<ref>{{cite web| url= http://wwd.com/business-news/media/instyle-expands-to-china-10879430/ |title= InStyle expands to China|work= Womne's Wear Daily|date=May 3, 2017|accessdate= February 13, 2018}}</ref> In 2018, the UK edition shifted from paper to digital version, and the Polish edition was cancelled after 10 years of publication.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.wirtualnemedia.pl/artykul/koniec-instyle-w-polsce-burda-skupia-sie-na-elle-glamour-i-gali|title=Burda zamyka "InStyle" i skupia się na "Elle", "Glamour" i "Gali"|access-date=November 18, 2018|language=pl}}</ref>

In 2018, ''InStyle'' became the first major fashion magazine to ban photography of and advertisements featuring fur.<ref>Taylor Harris, "[https://fashionweekdaily.com/instyle-bans-fur-laura-brown/ InStyle Bans Fur]," FashionWeekDaily.com, 3 April 2018.</ref>

In mid-July 2020, ''InStyle''{{'s}} Australian edition's publisher [[Bauer Media Group|Bauer Media Australia and New Zealand]], which had been acquired by the [[Sydney]]&ndash;based [[Mercury Capital]], axed the magazine, citing declining advertising revenue and travel restrictions caused by the [[COVID-19 pandemic]].<ref>{{cite news |last=Doyle |first=Michael |title=InStyle, Elle, Women's Health, Men's Health among Australian magazines axed by Bauer Media |url=https://www.abc.net.au/news/2020-07-21/elle-womens-health-ok-instyle-magazines-axed-by-bauer/12478658 |accessdate=4 September 2020 |work=[[ABC News (Australia)|ABC News]] |date=21 July 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200811093817/https://www.abc.net.au/news/2020-07-21/elle-womens-health-ok-instyle-magazines-axed-by-bauer/12478658 |archive-date=11 August 2020 |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Meade |first1=Amanda |title=Mercury Capital axes eight former Bauer magazines, including Harper's Bazaar, Elle and Men's Health |url=https://www.theguardian.com/media/2020/jul/21/bauer-media-australia-axes-eight-magazines-including-harpers-bazaar-elle-and-mens-health |accessdate=4 September 2020 |work=[[The Guardian]] |date=20 July 2020 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20200827050944/https://www.theguardian.com/media/2020/jul/21/bauer-media-australia-axes-eight-magazines-including-harpers-bazaar-elle-and-mens-health |archivedate=27 August 2020 |url-status=live }}</ref>

==Staff==
In 2016, Laura Brown, a former executive editor of ''[[Harper's Bazaar]]'', was announced as the new Editor-in-Chief, taking over for Ariel Foxman, who had been managing editor for eight years.<ref>{{cite news| url= http://wwd.com/business-news/media/harpers-bazaar-laura-brown-expected-to-be-named-instyle-editor-in-chief-10511644/ |title= Harper’s Bazaar’s Laura Brown Named InStyle Editor in Chief|quote= Brown most recently served as Harper's Bazaar's features and special projects and executive director.|first=Alexandra|last=Steigrad|website=WWD.com| publisher=Penske Media Corporation|date= August 22, 2016|access-date=July 25, 2017}}</ref>  Brown's first issue was December 2016.
