'''Bellary Lok Sabha constituency''' ({{lang-kn|ಬಳ್ಳಾರಿ ಲೋಕ ಸಭೆ ಚುನಾವಣಾ ಕ್ಷೇತ್ರ}}) is one of the 28 [[Lok Sabha]] (constituencies) in [[Karnataka]] state in southern [[India]]. This constituency is reserved for the candidates belonging to the [[Adivasi|Scheduled tribes]] after the delimitation exercise in 2008
