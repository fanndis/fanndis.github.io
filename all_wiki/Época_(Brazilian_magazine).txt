'''''Época''''' is a [[Brazil]]ian weekly [[newsmagazine|news and analysis magazine]]. The magazine was started in May 1998.<ref>{{cite news|author=Larry Rohter|title=A News Magazine War in Brazil|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1999/08/30/business/media-a-news-magazine-war-in-brazil.html|accessdate=8 August 2015|work=The New York Times|date=30 August 1999}}</ref><ref name=proo>{{cite news|title=A Flourishing Future for Magazines in Brazil|url=http://proof-reading.org/a-flourishing-future-for-magazines-in-brazil/|accessdate=4 August 2015|work=Proof|date=1 September 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|author1=Christina Holtz-Bacha|author2=Jesper Strömbäck|title=Opinion Polls and the Media: Reflecting and Shaping Public Opinion|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=WXiUiVxLO1kC&pg=PA148|accessdate=8 August 2015|date=5 April 2012|publisher=Palgrave Macmillan|isbn=978-0-230-37493-5|page=148}}</ref> It is published by [[Editora Globo]],<ref name=proo/><ref name=brab/> part of the [[Grupo Globo]] media conglomerate. ''Época''′s style is based on the German magazine ''[[Focus (German magazine)|Focus]]'',<ref name=proo/> emphasizing the use of images and graphics, and even their logos share some similarities.

==Circulation==
In 2010, ''Época'' had a circulation of 408,000 copies.<ref name=brab>{{cite news|title=Print Media Industry in Brazil|url=http://thebrazilbusiness.com/article/print-media-industry-in-brazil|accessdate=6 August 2015|work=The Brazil Business}}</ref> It dipped to 389,000 copies in 2012<ref name=brab/> and slightly rose to 393,000 in 2013.<ref>{{cite web |title=Circulação: Revistas Semanais - 2012 x 2013 |url=https://www.aner.org.br/dados-de-mercado/circulacao |website=ANER |accessdate=12 November 2018 |language=Portuguese}}</ref> In 2018, it circulates over 500,000 copies weekly and has 5.9 million unique digital users.<ref>{{cite web |title='ÉPOCA' has new editorial and graphic design |url=https://epoca.globo.com/sociedade/noticia/2018/03/epoca-tem-novo-projeto-editorial-e-grafico.html |website=ÉPOCA |accessdate=12 November 2018 |date=1 March 2018}}</ref>

==Delivery==
Beginning in 2018, subscribers receive ''Época,'' ''GLOBO,'' and ''Valor Econômico'' delivered together on Fridays.<ref>{{cite web |title='ÉPOCA' has new editorial and graphic design |url=https://epoca.globo.com/sociedade/noticia/2018/03/epoca-tem-novo-projeto-editorial-e-grafico.html |website=ÉPOCA |accessdate=12 November 2018 |date=1 March 2018}}</ref>
