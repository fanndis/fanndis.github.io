The [[Open Era]] is the current era of professional [[tennis]]. It began in 1968 when the [[Grand Slam (tennis)|Grand Slam]] tournaments allowed professional players to compete with amateurs, ending the division that had persisted in men's tennis since the dawn of the sport in the 19th century. The first "open" event was held in [[Bournemouth, England]],<ref>{{cite news
