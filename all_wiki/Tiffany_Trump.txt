'''Tiffany Ariana Trump''' (born October 13, 1993)<ref>{{Cite web|first=Ryan|last=Struyk|url=https://abcnews.go.com/Politics/donald-trumps-kids-eric-ivanka-miss-deadline-vote/story?id=38303943|title=Trump Kids Eric and Ivanka Miss Deadline to Vote in NY GOP Primary|website=ABC News|date=April 11, 2016|access-date=October 11, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171012105540/http://abcnews.go.com/Politics/donald-trumps-kids-eric-ivanka-miss-deadline-vote/story?id=38303943|archive-date=October 12, 2017|url-status=live}}</ref> is the fourth child of U.S. president [[Donald Trump]], and the only child with his second wife, [[Marla Maples]].

==Early life and education==
Tiffany Ariana Trump was born on October 13, 1993, at St. Mary's Medical Center in [[West Palm Beach, Florida]], two months before her parents married.<ref>{{cite magazine |url=http://www.vanityfair.com/news/2016/12/inside-ivanka-trump-and-tiffany-trump-complicated-sister-act |title=Inside Ivanka and Tiffany Trump Complicated Sister Act |last=Ellison |first=Sarah |date=February 2017 |magazine=[[Vanity Fair (magazine)|Vanity Fair]] |accessdate=December 23, 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161224142406/http://www.vanityfair.com/news/2016/12/inside-ivanka-trump-and-tiffany-trump-complicated-sister-act |archive-date=December 24, 2016 |url-status=live }}</ref> She is [[Donald Trump]]'s only child with his second wife, actress and television personality [[Marla Maples]], whom he married in December 1993.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://articles.sun-sentinel.com/1993-10-15/news/9310150237_1_marla-maples-donald-trump-baby |title=Tiffany Trump Greets Attention with a Snore |last=Singer |first=Glenn |date=October 15, 1993 |work=[[Sun-Sentinel]] |accessdate=December 23, 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161224095105/http://articles.sun-sentinel.com/1993-10-15/news/9310150237_1_marla-maples-donald-trump-baby |archive-date=December 24, 2016 |url-status=live }}</ref> She was named after [[Tiffany & Company]]; her father purchased the [[air rights]] above the [[Fifth Avenue]] jewelry store in the 1980s while building [[Trump Tower]] next door. Her parents divorced in 1999 after being separated for two years.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/stork-visits-donald-marla-article-1.2717344|title=The stork visits Donald & Marla|last=Stasi|first=Linda|date=October 14, 1993|work=[[New York Daily News]]|accessdate=October 14, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161019003745/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/stork-visits-donald-marla-article-1.2717344|archive-date=October 19, 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> She was raised by her mother in [[California]].<ref name=NYT-20161002>{{cite news |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2016/10/02/fashion/tiffany-the-other-trump.html |title=The Other Trump |newspaper=[[The New York Times]] |first=Alessandra |last=Stanley |date=October 1, 2016 |access-date=May 6, 2017 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170323011559/https://www.nytimes.com/2016/10/02/fashion/tiffany-the-other-trump.html |archive-date=March 23, 2017 |url-status=live }}</ref><ref name=Slate07202016>{{cite news |last=Graham |first=Ruth |date=July 20, 2016 |title=Tiffany Trump Sad, Vague Tribute to Her Distant Father |url=http://www.slate.com/blogs/xx_factor/2016/07/20/tiffany_trump_s_sad_vague_rnc_speech.html |newspaper=[[Slate (magazine)|Slate]] |access-date=July 24, 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160723074854/http://www.slate.com/blogs/xx_factor/2016/07/20/tiffany_trump_s_sad_vague_rnc_speech.html |archive-date=July 23, 2016 |url-status=live }}</ref>

==Career==
In 2011, Trump released a music single called "Like a Bird". She later told ''[[The Oprah Winfrey Show]]''<ref>{{cite web|title=Introducing Tiffany Trump|url=http://www.wherearetheynow.buzz/meet-tiffany-trump|accessdate=June 29, 2016|website=wherearetheynow.buzz|publisher=[[The Oprah Winfrey Show]] clip, [[Harpo Productions]], Inc.|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160722163450/http://www.wherearetheynow.buzz/meet-tiffany-trump|archive-date=July 22, 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> that she was evaluating whether to take her music career "to the next level as a professional".<ref>{{cite news|last1=Yousefi|first1=Ryan|date=April 15, 2016|title=Tiffany Trump Cannot Escape Her Pop Song|work=[[Broward New Times]]|url=http://www.browardpalmbeach.com/news/tiffany-trump-cannot-escape-her-pop-song-7717799|accessdate=June 9, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160610091810/http://www.browardpalmbeach.com/news/tiffany-trump-cannot-escape-her-pop-song-7717799|archive-date=June 10, 2016|url-status=live}}</ref>

"Like a Bird" received little media attention after its appearance on ''The Oprah Winfrey Show''. Following Trump's speech at the 2016 Republican National Convention, the song received renewed public interest and was criticized for its use of [[Auto-Tune]]-based pitch correction and received generally negative reviews.<ref>{{cite magazine|title=Tiffany Trump Recorded a Pop Song in 2011|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/7445761/tiffany-trump-like-a-bird|magazine=[[Billboard magazine|Billboard]]|accessdate=October 23, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191023080933/https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/7445761/tiffany-trump-like-a-bird|archive-date=October 23, 2019|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Tiffany Trump's "Like a Bird" Pop Song Reviewed|url=https://www.audiokite.com/blog/tiffany-trump-like-bird-pop-song-reviewed/|accessdate=October 23, 2019|publisher=[[Audiokite]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181010120722/https://www.audiokite.com/blog/tiffany-trump-like-bird-pop-song-reviewed/|archive-date=October 10, 2018|url-status=live}}</ref>

In 2015, Trump worked as an intern for ''[[Vogue (magazine)|Vogue]]'', and, in 2016, modeled for an [[Andrew Warren (fashion designer)|Andrew Warren]] fashion show during [[New York Fashion Week]].<ref>{{cite news|last1=Leone Shewfelt|first1=Raechel|date=February 16, 2016|title=Donald Trump's Daughter Tiffany Makes Her New York Fashion Week Debut|work=[[Yahoo News]]|url=https://www.yahoo.com/celebrity/donald-trumps-daughter-tiffany-makes-her-new-york-193922297.html|accessdate=June 9, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160808202156/https://www.yahoo.com/celebrity/donald-trumps-daughter-tiffany-makes-her-new-york-193922297.html|archive-date=August 8, 2016|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Personal life==
While studying at the [[University of Pennsylvania]], Trump met fellow student Ross Mechanic, with whom she was in a relationship from about October 2015 to March 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Schmidt|first=Ingrid|date=March 21, 2018|title=Tiffany Trump and Boyfriend Ross Mechanic Call It Quits|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/tiffany-trump-breaks-up-boyfriend-ross-mechanic-1096508|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180423081930/https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/tiffany-trump-breaks-up-boyfriend-ross-mechanic-1096508|archive-date=April 23, 2018|access-date=July 21, 2020|website=[[The Hollywood Reporter]]|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Polus|first=Sarah|date=February 20, 2018|title=The social media signs that Tiffany Trump and boyfriend Ross Mechanic may have broken up|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/reliable-source/wp/2018/02/20/the-social-media-signs-that-tiffany-trump-and-boyfriend-ross-mechanic-may-have-broken-up/|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191114215847/https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/reliable-source/wp/2018/02/20/the-social-media-signs-that-tiffany-trump-and-boyfriend-ross-mechanic-may-have-broken-up/|archive-date=November 14, 2019|access-date=July 21, 2020|website=[[The Washington Post]]|language=en}}</ref> A few months later that summer, while on vacation in [[Greece]] with actress [[Lindsay Lohan]], Trump met [[Michael Boulos]], a Nigerian-American billionaire heir and business executive whose family owns [[Boulos Enterprises]] and [[SCOA Nigeria]] in [[Nigeria]], the country in which Boulos grew up.
