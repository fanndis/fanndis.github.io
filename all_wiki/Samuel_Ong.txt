'''Samuel Ong''' (1945-2009) was a former deputy director of the [[National Bureau of Investigation (Philippines)|National Bureau of Investigation]] (NBI) of the [[Philippines]] and is a [[whistleblower]], declaring that the [[2004 Philippine general election|2004 national elections]] was rigged, starting an [[Philippine electoral crisis, 2005|electoral crisis]].<ref>

In 2005, Ong announced he was in possession of a set of four original audio tapes, made as part of a wiretapping operation conducted by Air Force T/Sgt. Vidal Doble of military intelligence, containing a recording of a conversation between [[Gloria Macapagal Arroyo|President Gloria Arroyo]] and [[Virgilio Garcillano]], an official of the [[Commission on Elections (Philippines)|Commission on Elections]]. Ong claimed that four of the tapes incriminate Arroyo and all of the other politicians who were officially declared winners of the 2004 elections.<ref name="pdi-09">
