'''Erling Lae''' (born 16 March 1947) is a [[Norway|Norwegian]] politician for the [[Conservative Party of Norway|Conservative Party]].

He was born in [[Oslo]], is a [[Candidatus philologiæ|cand.philol.]] by education and formerly worked as a journalist. From 1981 to 1985 he was a political advisor in the [[Norwegian Ministry of Children, Equality and Social Inclusion|Ministry of Consumer Affairs and Administration]]. He was elected to [[Oslo]] city council in 1991, and was a city commissioner between 1997 and 2000. From 2000 to 2009 he was the governing mayor of Oslo. As such he headed the executive branch of the city government in the capital city of Norway.<ref name=snl>{{cite encyclopedia|year=2007|title=Erling Lae|encyclopedia=[[Store norske leksikon]]|editor=Henriksen, Petter|publisher=Kunnskapsforlaget|location=Oslo|url=http://www.snl.no/Erling_Lae|language=Norwegian|accessdate=14 March 2010}}</ref>

He was a deputy leader of the Conservative Party from 2008 to 2010.<ref name=snl/> In 2010 he was announced as the new [[List of County Governors of Vestfold|county governor of Vestfold]], succeeding [[Mona Røkke]].<ref>{{cite news|url=http://ret-web01.int.retriever.no/services/webdocument.html?documentId=00231320100312128155316&serviceId=2|title=Erling Lae ny fylkesmann|last=Ulrichsen|first=Henrik|date=12 March 2010|work=Tønsbergs Blad|language=Norwegian|accessdate=14 March 2010}}</ref> He was, however, replaced in the summer of 2016 by [[Per Arne Olsen]].

Lae is [[Coming out|openly]] [[Homosexuality|gay]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dagsavisen.no/innenriks/article296501.ece|work=[[Dagsavisen]]|title=Lae hyllet i Berlin|first=Ida Svingen|last=Mo|date=18 June 2007|language=Norwegian|accessdate=14 March 2010|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090110092159/http://www.dagsavisen.no/innenriks/article296501.ece|archivedate=10 January 2009}}</ref> He is married to Jens Torstein Olsen, a [[Lutheranism|Lutheran]] [[Minister (Christianity)|minister]].<ref>Marianne Henriksen: [http://www.vestfoldblad.no/nor/Samfunn/Aktuelt/Baker-jula-inn/%288555%29 Baker jula inn] Vestfold Blad, 15 December 2010</ref>
