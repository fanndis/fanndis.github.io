'''Robert E. Lee High School''', also known as '''Midland Lee''', is a [[Public school (government funded)|public]], [[Coeducation|co-educational]] [[secondary school]] in [[Midland, Texas]]. Lee High School is a part of the [[Midland Independent School District]].  In October 2020, the Midland school board voted to rename the school "Legacy High School".<ref>{{cite news | url = https://www.mrt.com/news/article/Board-selects-Legacy-HS-as-school-s-new-name-15648892.php | title = Board selects Legacy HS as school’s new name | newspaper = [[Midland Reporter-Telegram]] | date = October 14, 2020 | first = Stewart | last = Doreen}}</ref>

==Demographics==
The demographic breakdown of the 2,288 students enrolled for 2018-19 was:
*Male - 53.2%
*Female - 46.8%
*Native American/Alaskan - 0.4%
*Asian - 2.8%
*Black - 8.3%
*Hispanic - 61.2%
*White - 26.0%
*Multiracial - 1.3%

30.3% of the students were eligible for free or reduced-cost lunch.<ref name="nces_sch" />
