'''''David Makes Man''''' is an American [[Coming-of-age story|coming-of-age]] [[Drama (film and television)|drama]] television series that premiered on August 14, 2019 on [[Oprah Winfrey Network|OWN]]. In December 2019, OWN renewed the series for a second season.<!--Per WP:CITELEAD, references are not needed in the lead if it is sourced in the body of the article!-->

==Premise==
''David Makes Man'' follows a 14 year old named David "from the projects who is haunted by the death of his friend and relied on by his hardworking mother to find a way out of poverty."<ref name="DMM">{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2018/06/tarell-alvin-mccraney-own-series-david-makes-man-cast-phylicia-rashad-akili-mcdowell-showrunner-dee-harris-lawrence-1202417511/|title=Tarell Alvin McCraney’s OWN Series Sets Cast Led By Phylicia Rashad & Akili McDowell, Gets Title & Showrunner|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|date=June 26, 2018|access-date=August 28, 2018}}</ref>

==Production==
===Development===
On August 16, 2017, it was reported that [[Oprah Winfrey Network]] had given the production a straight-to-series order created by [[Tarell Alvin McCraney]].<ref name="SeriesOrder">{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2017/tv/news/moonlight-writer-own-drama-series-michael-b-jordan-1202529738/|title=OWN Orders Drama Series From 'Moonlight' Co-Writer, Michael B. Jordan|last=Otterson|first=Joe|work=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]]|date=August 16, 2017|access-date=May 28, 2019}}</ref>
On June 26, 2018, it was announced that Dee Harris-Lawrence will be the showrunner and set to serve as an executive producer alongside with Tarell Alvin McCraney, Mike Kelley, Melissa Loy, [[Michael B. Jordan]], and [[Oprah Winfrey]]. Production companies involved with the series were slated to include [[Warner Bros. Television|Warner Horizon Scripted Television]], Page Fright, and Outlier Productions. The series is set to premiere in August 14, 2019.<ref name="DMM"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2019/tv/reviews/sxsw-david-makes-man-review-1203156638/|title=SXSW TV Review: 'David Makes Man'|first=Daniel|last=D'Addario|date=March 10, 2019|work=Variety|accessdate=May 28, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2019/05/summer-premiere-dates-2019-streaming-cable-broadcast-new-series-returning-1202509777/|title=Summer Premiere Dates 2019: New & Returning Series On Broadcast, Cable & Streaming|work=Deadline Hollywood|first=Erik|last=Pedersen|date=May 22, 2019|accessdate=June 23, 2019}}</ref> The pilot episode was premiered at [[South by Southwest]] on March 10, 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/review/david-makes-man-review-1193589|title='David Makes Man': TV Review - SXSW 2019|first=Daniel|last=Fienberg|date=March 10, 2019|work=[[The Hollywood Reporter]]|accessdate=May 28, 2019}}</ref> On December 19, 2019, OWN renewed the series for a second season.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2019/12/david-makes-man-renewed-second-season-own-tarell-alvin-mccraney-oprah-winfrey-1202813803/|title='David Makes Man' Renewed For Second Season At OWN|work=Deadline Hollywood|first=Dino-Ray|last=Ramos|date=December 19, 2019|accessdate=December 19, 2019}}</ref> On June 29, 2020, [[HBO Max]] acquired the streaming rights for the series which was debuted on July 16, 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2020/06/hbo-max-acquires-streaming-rights-tarell-alvin-mccraney-david-makes-man-1202972962/|title=HBO Max Acquires Streaming Rights To Tarell Alvin McCraney’s 'David Makes Man'|work=Deadline Hollywood|first=Dino-Ray|last=Ramos|date=June 29, 2020|accessdate=June 29, 2020}}</ref> The series' producer Dee Harris-Lawrence most recently signed a overall deal with Warner Bros. Television Studios.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/all-rise-david-makes-man-showrunner-inks-overall-deal-at-warner-bros-tv|title='All Rise,' 'David Makes Man' Showrunner Inks Overall Deal at Warner Bros. TV|work=The Hollywood Reporter|first=Rick|last=Porter|date=November 23, 2020|access-date=November 24, 2020}}</ref>

===Casting===
In June 2018, it was announced that Akili McDowell, Phylicia Rashad, Nathaniel Logan McIntyre, Isaiah Johnson, Ade Chike Torbert, Jordan Bolger, Cayden K. Williams, and Travis Coles are cast as series regulars.<ref name="DMM"/> In August 2018, Alana Arenas has been cast as a series regular while Gillian Williams, Ruben Santiago-Hudson, Elvis Nolasco, and Randy Gonzalez are cast in recurring roles.<ref name="cast1">{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2018/08/owns-david-makes-man-cast-directors-season-1-1202452820/|title=OWN's 'David Makes Man' Adds Five To Cast, Sets Directors For Season 1|last=Petski|first=Denise|work=Deadline Hollywood|date=August 27, 2018|access-date=August 28, 2018}}</ref> On October 25, 2018, Lela Rochon, Trace Lysette, Liza Colón-Zayas, Juanita Jennings, Lindsey Blackwell, Solomon Valdez, Teshi Thomas, and newcomers Nick Creegan, Kimaya Naomi and Logan Rozos have joined the series in recurring roles.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2018/10/owns-david-makes-man-cast-trace-lysette-lela-rochon-1202489601/|title=OWN's 'David Makes Man' Adds Ten To Cast|first=Denise|last=Petski|date=October 25, 2018|work=Deadline Hollywood|accessdate=May 28, 2019}}</ref> On August 6, 2020, Kwame Patterson and [[Arlen Escarpeta]] were cast in starring roles for the second season.<ref name="KwameP&ArlenE">{{cite web |url=https://deadline.com/2020/08/david-makes-man-kwame-patterson-arlen-escarpeta-join-season-2-of-own-series-1203006532/|title='David Makes Man': Kwame Patterson & Arlen Escarpeta Join Season 2 Of OWN Series|work=Deadline Hollywood|first=Denise|last=Petski|date=August 6, 2020|accessdate=August 6, 2020}}</ref>

==Reception==
<!--===Critical response===-->
=== Critical response ===
On [[review aggregator]] [[Rotten Tomatoes]], the series holds an approval rating of 100% based on 23 reviews, with an average rating of 8.98/10. The website's critical consensus reads, "Powerful, beautiful, and like nothing else on TV, ''David Makes Man'' blends dreamy aesthetics with an empathetically crafted story to create a truly unique viewing experience."<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.rottentomatoes.com/tv/david_makes_man/s01|title=David Makes Man: Season 1 (2019)|website=[[Rotten Tomatoes]]|accessdate=August 6, 2020}}</ref> On [[Metacritic]], it has a weighted average score of 81 out of 100, based on 11 critics, indicating "universal acclaim".<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.metacritic.com/tv/david-makes-man/season-1|title=David Makes Man: Season 1|website=[[Metacritic]]|accessdate=August 6, 2020}}</ref>
