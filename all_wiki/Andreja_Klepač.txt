'''Andreja Klepač''' (born 13 March 1986) is a professional Slovenian [[tennis player]]. On 14 July 2008, Klepač reached her best singles rankings of world number 99. On 16 July 2018, she peaked at world number 12 in the doubles rankings.

==Career==
She has made one [[WTA Tour]] singles final in [[Budapest]] in 2008 and won five WTA Tour doubles titles and appeared in six other finals.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.wtatennis.com/news/irina-camelia-begu-conquers-seoul|title=Irina-Camelia Begu Conquers Seoul|last=admin|date=27 September 2015|website=WTA Tennis}}</ref> <ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.wtatennis.com/news/zheng-saisais-big-breakthrough|title=Zheng Saisai's Big Breakthrough|last=admin|date=9 October 2014|website=WTA Tennis}}</ref> <ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.wtatennis.com/news/kvitova-storms-new-haven-title|title=Kvitova Storms To New Haven Title|last=admin|date=23 August 2014|website=WTA Tennis}}</ref> <ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.wtatennis.com/news/barthel-brings-it-home-bastad|title=Barthel Brings It Home In Bastad|last=admin|date=20 July 2014|website=WTA Tennis}}</ref>

In July 2013, she won her first WTA doubles title in [[Gastein Ladies|Bad Gastein]] with [[Sandra Klemenschits]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.wtatennis.com/news/speechless-meusburger-wins-bad-gastein|title=Speechless Meusburger Wins Bad Gastein|last=admin|date=21 July 2013|website=WTA Tennis}}</ref> 

In 2015, she reached the quarterfinals at Australian Open with [[Klaudia Jans-Ignacik]] and [[US Open (tennis)|US Open]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://sport.leggo.it/altrisport/us_open_doppio_tennis_errani_pennetta_semifinale-1233491.html|title=US Open, Errani-Pennetta in semifinale di doppio|website=sport.leggo.it}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sportskeeda.com/tennis/us-open-doubles-preview-day-9-hingis-mirza-play|title=US Open Doubles Preview: Mirza-Hingis, Murray-Peers on court tonight|date=8 September 2015}}</ref> with [[Lara Arruabarrena]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.wtatennis.com/news/hingis-pennetta-dodge-upset-bug|title=Hingis & Pennetta Dodge Upset Bug|last=admin|date=23 January 2015|website=WTA Tennis}}</ref>

In 2018 she has been playing together with [[María José Martínez Sánchez]]. They reached three finals, defeating in the way to them top pairs like [[Latisha Chan]]/[[Andrea Sestini Hlaváčková]] (Brisbane) and [[Timea Babos]]/[[Kristina Mladenovic]] (Doha). They lost tie-breakers in the semi-final of Madrid Masters against [[Ekaterina Makarova]]/[[Elena Vesnina]].

==World TeamTennis==

Klepac has played four seasons with [[World TeamTennis]] starting in 2016 when she debuted in the league with the [[Washington Kastles]]. In 2017, she played her first season for the [[Orange County Breakers]] and was named the Female MVP of the 2017 [[World TeamTennis]] Finals after leading the Breakers to the title. She played for Breakers for the 2018 and 2019 seasons as well. It was announced that she will be joining the [[Orange County Breakers]] during the 2020 WTT season set to begin July 12.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://wtt.com/2020/06/16/world-teamtennis-adds-stars-tiafoe-puig-raonic-bouchard-sock-as-rosters-set-for-2020/ |title=World TeamTennis Adds Stars Tiafoe, Puig, Roanic, Bouchard, & Sock As Rosters Set For 2020 |website=WTT.com |date=June 16, 2020 }}</ref>
