While many mixed doubles tennis players have won at least one title in each of the four Majors, a career slam, only one mixed doubles team has won the coveted "Grand Slam", all four titles in a single calendar year, [[Margaret Court]] and [[Ken Fletcher]] in 1963. Margaret Court and [[Owen Davidson]] also won an individual "Grand Slam" with different partners.
