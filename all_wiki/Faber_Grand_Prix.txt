The '''Faber Grand Prix''' is a defunct [[WTA Tour]] affiliated women's [[tennis]] tournament played from 1992 to 2000. It was held in [[Essen]] in Germany from 1992 to 1994 as well as in 1996, and subsequently in [[Hanover]] in Germany from 1997 to 2000. The tournament was played on indoor [[carpet court]]s.
