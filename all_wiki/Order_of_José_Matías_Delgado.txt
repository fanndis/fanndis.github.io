The '''National Order of José Matías Delgado''' is a distinction granted by the [[El Salvador|Republic of El Salvador]] to Heads of State, Salvadoran citizens, or foreigners who are distinguished by eminent services to the country through extraordinary civil virtues which are humanitarian, scientific, literary, artistic, political, or military.<ref name=assembly>{{cite web|url=http://www.asamblea.gob.sv/eparlamento/indice-legislativo/buscador-de-documentos-legislativos/crease-la-orden-nacional-jose-matias-delgado|title=Decreto de creación de la Orden Nacional José Matías Delgado|date=March 20, 2014|website=www.asamblea.gob.sv|publisher=Legislative Assembly of El Salvador|language=Spanish|access-date=August 3, 2020|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160305033708/http://www.asamblea.gob.sv/eparlamento/indice-legislativo/buscador-de-documentos-legislativos/crease-la-orden-nacional-jose-matias-delgado|archive-date=March 5, 2016|via=Wayback Machine}}</ref> The [[President of El Salvador]] is the Grand Master of the Order.

== Design ==

The order is named after [[José Matías Delgado]], a Salvadoran priest and doctor known as ''The Father of the Salvadoran Fatherland'' who lived from 1767 to 1832. He was a leader in the independence movement of El Salvador from [[Spain]] and then later [[First Mexican Empire|Mexico]].

== Grades ==

The order is awarded in 6 classes:

*Grand Cross with Gold Star and Special Distinction
*Grand Cross with Gold Star
*Grand Cross with Silver Star
*Grand Officer
*Commander
*Knight
