[[Serena Williams]] and [[Venus Williams]] won in the final 6–3, 6–3, against [[Daniela Hantuchová]] and [[Ai Sugiyama]].

Defending champions [[Alyona Bondarenko]] and [[Kateryna Bondarenko]] lost in the first round to [[Gisela Dulko]] and [[Roberta Vinci]].
