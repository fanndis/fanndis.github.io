'''''One Big Happy''''' is an American [[sitcom]] television series produced by [[Ellen DeGeneres]] starring [[Elisha Cuthbert]], [[Nick Zano]] and [[Kelly Brook]], about a [[lesbian|gay woman]], Lizzy, who is pregnant with her best friend Luke's baby, while he is in love with another woman, Prudence. The show was created by [[Liz Feldman]], and it premiered on March 17, 2015.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.zap2it.com/tv/one-big-happy/episodes/SH019210660000|title=One Big Happy - Episode Guide|accessdate=March 3, 2015|publisher=Zap2It}}</ref> Six episodes were ordered by [[NBC]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/showatch/one-big-happy/|title=Shows A-Z - one big happy on nbc - TheFutonCritic.com|publisher=|accessdate=29 March 2015}}</ref> The series was canceled after one season. It was produced by Visualized, Inc., in association with [[A Very Good Production]] and [[Warner Bros. Television]].

==Reception==
''One Big Happy'' received generally negative reviews from critics. At [[Metacritic]] (which assigns a weighted mean—out of 100—based on reviews from mainstream critics) the show received a weighted mean score of 37 from 21&nbsp;reviews.<ref name="Metacritic">{{cite web |url=http://www.metacritic.com/tv/one-big-happy |title=One Big Happy&nbsp;– Season 1 Reviews |work=[[Metacritic]] |publisher=[[CBS Interactive]] |accessdate=April 16, 2015}}</ref>
