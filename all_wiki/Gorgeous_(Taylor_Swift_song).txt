"'''Gorgeous'''" is a song recorded by American singer-songwriter [[Taylor Swift]] for her sixth studio album, ''[[Reputation (Taylor Swift album)|Reputation]]'' (2017). It was released on October 20, 2017, as the second [[promotional single]] from the album. It was later released as a single in the UK on January 12, 2018.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/8006719/taylor-swift-gorgeous-single-release|title=Taylor Swift's 'Gorgeous' New Single Has Arrived: Listen|last=Brandle|first=Lars|date=October 20, 2017|website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=October 20, 2017|archive-date=October 20, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171020094302/http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/8006719/taylor-swift-gorgeous-single-release|url-status=live}}</ref> "Gorgeous" is a fizzy [[electropop]] song with lyrics describing a [[Crush (psychology)|crush]]. 

Upon release, "Gorgeous" received generally positive reviews from music critics, who complimented the straightforward songwriting and the "delightful" production. The song reached the top 10 on charts in Australia, Canada, Hungary and Malaysia. It also peaked at number 13 on the US [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] chart and at number 15 on the [[UK Singles Chart]].

==Commercial performance==
In the United States, the song debuted at number thirteen on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]], number 16 on the [[Streaming Songs]] chart with 16.9&nbsp;million U.S. streams, and at number one on the [[Digital Songs]] chart with first-week downloads of 68,000 copies. It also became her 14th song to top the Digital Songs chart and her 13th to open at the summit, tying her with [[Rihanna]] for the most number one entries on the chart with 14 each.<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8015123/taylor-swift-gorgeous-rihanna-digital-song-sales-number-one|title=Taylor Swift Ties Rihanna for Most Digital Song Sales No. 1s With Debut of 'Gorgeous'|first=Gary|last=Trust|date=October 30, 2017|accessdate=November 9, 2017|journal=Billboard|archive-date=November 8, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171108020554/http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8015123/taylor-swift-gorgeous-rihanna-digital-song-sales-number-one|url-status=live}}</ref> 

In Australia, the song entered the [[ARIA Charts|ARIA Singles Chart]] at number 9.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ariacharts.com.au/news/2017/week-five-at-1-for-post-malone-s-rockstar|title=Week five at No. 1 for Post Malone's Rockstar|publisher=Australian Recording Industry Association|date=October 28, 2017|accessdate=November 9, 2017|archive-date=October 29, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171029070156/https://www.ariacharts.com.au/news/2017/week-five-at-1-for-post-malone-s-rockstar|url-status=live}}</ref>

After impacting UK radio as a single, the song also peaked at number 15 in the country.
