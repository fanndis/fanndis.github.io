The '''12th Critics' Choice Awards''' were presented on January 20, 2007, honoring the finest achievements of [[2006 in film|2006 filmmaking]]. The event was held at the [[Santa Monica Civic Auditorium]] in [[Santa Monica, California]] and was broadcast on [[E!]].

==Top 10 films==
(in alphabetical order)
*''[[Babel (film)|Babel]]''
*''[[Blood Diamond]]''
*''[[The Departed]]''
*''[[Dreamgirls (film)|Dreamgirls]]''
*''[[Letters from Iwo Jima]]''
*''[[Little Children (film)|Little Children]]''
*''[[Little Miss Sunshine]]''
*''[[Notes on a Scandal (film)|Notes on a Scandal]]''
*''[[The Queen (2006 film)|The Queen]]''
*''[[United 93 (film)|United 93]]''
