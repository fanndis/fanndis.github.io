'''Asbjørn Solberg''' (27 December 1893  &ndash;  23 June 1977) was a Norwegian politician for the [[Christian Democratic Party of Norway|Christian Democratic Party]].

He was born in [[Oslo]].

He was elected to the [[Stortinget|Norwegian Parliament]] from [[Østfold]] in 1945, and was re-elected on three occasions. He later served in the position of deputy representative during the term 1961&ndash;1965.

Solberg was a member of [[Degernes]] municipality council between 1945 and 1947, serving as mayor in 1945. In 1945 he was also a member of Østfold county council.
