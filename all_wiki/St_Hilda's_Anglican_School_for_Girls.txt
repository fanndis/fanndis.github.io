'''St Hilda's Anglican School for Girls''' is an Australian [[Independent school|independent]] [[Selective school|non-selective]] [[Anglican Church of Australia|Anglican]] [[Single-sex school|single-sex]] [[Primary school|primary]] and [[Secondary school|secondary]] [[Day school|day]] and [[Boarding school|boarding]] school for girls, located in [[Mosman Park, Western Australia|Mosman Park]], a western suburb of [[Perth, Western Australia]]. In addition, the school provides [[Mixed-sex education|co-educational]] [[Pre-school education|early learning]] education to both girls and boys.

Established in [[Claremont, Western Australia|Claremont]] in 1896, the school currently caters for approximately 1,200 students from Early Learning, through [[Junior Kindergarten]] to [[Year 12]], including 150 boarders in Years 7 to 12.<ref name=ABSA/>
