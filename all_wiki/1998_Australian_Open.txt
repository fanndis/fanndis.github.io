The '''1998 Australian Open''' was a [[tennis]] tournament played on [[Hardcourt|outdoor hard courts]] at [[Melbourne Park]] in [[Melbourne]] in [[Victoria (Australia)|Victoria]] in Australia. It was the 86th edition of the Australian Open and was held from 19 January through 1 February 1998.

==Seniors==

===Men's Singles===
{{main|1998 Australian Open – Men's Singles}}
{{flagicon|CZE}} '''[[Petr Korda]]''' defeated {{flagicon|CHI}} [[Marcelo Ríos]] 6–2, 6–2, 6–2
* It was Korda's only Grand Slam title.

===Women's Singles===
{{main|1998 Australian Open – Women's Singles}}
{{flagicon|SUI}} '''[[Martina Hingis]]''' defeated {{flagicon|ESP}} [[Conchita Martínez]] 6–3, 6–3
* It was Hingis' 4th career Grand Slam title and her 2nd Australian Open title.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.wtatour.com/page/Home/0,,12781,00.html |title=Archived copy |access-date=4 January 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110302094556/http://www.wtatour.com/page/Home/0,,12781,00.html |archive-date=2 March 2011 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

===Men's Doubles===
{{main|1998 Australian Open – Men's Doubles}}
{{flagicon|SWE}} '''[[Jonas Björkman]]''' / {{flagicon|NED}} '''[[Jacco Eltingh]]''' defeated {{flagicon|AUS}} [[Todd Woodbridge]] / {{flagicon|AUS}} [[Mark Woodforde]] 6–2, 5–7, 2–6, 6–4, 6–3
* It was Björkman's 1st career Grand Slam title and his 1st Australian Open title. It was Eltingh's 4th career Grand Slam title and his 2nd and last Australian Open title.

===Women's Doubles===
{{main|1998 Australian Open – Women's Doubles}}
{{flagicon|SUI}} '''[[Martina Hingis]]''' / {{flagicon|CRO}} '''[[Mirjana Lučić]]'''{{efn|Lučić became the first Croatian tennis player to win the Australian Open.}} defeated {{flagicon|USA}} [[Lindsay Davenport]] / {{flagicon|BLR}} [[Natasha Zvereva]] 6–4, 2–6, 6–3
* It was Hingis' 7th career Grand Slam title and her 4th Australian Open title. It was Lučić's only career Grand Slam title.

===Mixed Doubles===
{{main|1998 Australian Open – Mixed Doubles}}
{{flagicon|USA}} '''[[Venus Williams]]''' / {{flagicon|USA}} '''[[Justin Gimelstob]]''' defeated {{flagicon|CZE}} [[Helena Suková]] / {{flagicon|CZE}} [[Cyril Suk]] 6–2, 6–1
* It was Williams' 1st career Grand Slam title and her 1st Australian Open title. It was Gimelstob's 1st career Grand Slam title and his only Australian Open title.
