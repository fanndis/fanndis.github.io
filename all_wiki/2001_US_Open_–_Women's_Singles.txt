Defending champion [[Venus Williams]] defeated her sister [[Serena Williams]] in the final, 6–2, 6–4, to win the Women's Singles tennis title at the [[2001 US Open (tennis)|2001 US Open]]. Venus Williams won the title without dropping a set.
