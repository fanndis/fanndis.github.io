'''''Taste This''''' is a live album recorded during a [[stand-up comedy]] performance by [[Ellen DeGeneres]], in which she talks about common daily problems and enjoying situations that we all can relate to.  This is one of her well-known stand-up shows that was a huge success in the United States. As of late 2008, the CD is out of print.

The album peaked at number 30 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Top Heatseekers]] charts.{{citation needed|date=April 2020}}
