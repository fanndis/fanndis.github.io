{{about|the Rich List published in 1989|general information about the list|Sunday Times Rich List}}'''''The'' ''Sunday Times'' Rich List 1989''' is the 1st annual survey of the wealthiest people resident in the United Kingdom, published by ''The Sunday Times'' on 2nd April 1989.<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|date=2013-04-18|title=Rich List: Changing face of wealth|language=en-GB|work=BBC News|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/business-22188762|access-date=2020-07-23}}</ref>

In 1989 a wealth of £30 million was needed to make the top 200,<ref name=":0" /> (approximately £{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|UK|30000000|1989|r=-6}}}} in today's value).{{Inflation-fn|UK|df=y}}

The list at the time reported the Queen as the wealthiest person in the United Kingdom, with a wealth of £5.2 billion, which included state assets that were not hers personally,<ref>{{Cite news|date=2013-04-18|title=Rich List: Changing face of wealth|work=BBC News|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/business-22188762|access-date=2020-07-23}}</ref> (approximately £{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|UK|5200000000|1989|r=-6}}}} in today's value).{{Inflation-fn|UK|df=y}}
