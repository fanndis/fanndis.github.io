'''Karen Angelo Hækkerup''' (née Schmidt; born 12 June 1974)<ref>[http://www.dr.dk/nyheder/politik/find-politiker/Politiker/Karen%20H%C3%A6kkerup/1081/]</ref> is a [[Denmark|Danish]] [[politician]] representing the [[Social Democrats]]. She was [[Justice Minister of Denmark]] from 12 December 2013 to 10 October 2014.<ref>[http://www.dr.dk/Nyheder/Ligetil/Dagens_fokus/Indland/2013/12/Nye_ministre_besoeger_dronningen_i_dag.htm?rss=true] Nye ministre har hilst på dronningen</ref><ref>[http://www.b.dk/politiko/karen-haekkerup-bliver-ny-justitsminister] Karen Hækkerup bliver ny justitsminister</ref> Hækkerup resigned her post as Justice Minister in favour of a job as CEO of the Danish Agriculture and Food Council, the largest lobby organisation for the Danish agricultural industry.

She has been a member of the [[Folketing]] (the parliament of Denmark) since 2005. She is married to the Social Democrat, Ole Hækkerup, thereby being the daughter-in-law of [[Hans Hækkerup]] and the sister-in-law of the Danish Minister for European Affairs, Nick Hækkerup. She has three children and lives on [[Amager]].
