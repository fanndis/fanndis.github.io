'''Serhiy Anatol—ñyovich Terokhin''' is Ukrainian economist and politician. MP of Ukraine. [[Ministry of Economic Development and Trade (Ukraine)|Minister of Economy of Ukraine]] from 4 February to 27 September 2005. [[Batkivshchyna]] Party member.
