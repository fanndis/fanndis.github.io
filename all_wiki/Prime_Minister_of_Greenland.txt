{{Politics of Greenland}}

The '''prime minister of Greenland''' ({{lang-kl|Naalakkersuisut siulittaasuat}}, lit ''Leader of the government''; {{lang-da|Landsstyreformand}}), officially referred to as the '''premier''',<ref>[http://uk.nanoq.gl/Emner/Government/the_cabinet.aspx Memberslof the Cabinet] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130126232123/http://uk.nanoq.gl/Emner/Government/the_cabinet.aspx |date=2013-01-26 }} Government of Greenland</ref> is the head of the [[Naalakkersuisut|Government]] of [[Greenland]], the [[autonomous area|autonomous]] territory within the [[Denmark|Kingdom of Denmark]]. The incumbent prime minister is [[Kim Kielsen]] of the [[Siumut]] party. The prime minister is usually leader of the majority party in the [[Parliament of Greenland]]. [[Jonathan Motzfeldt]] became prime minister after [[home rule]] was granted to Greenland in 1979.
