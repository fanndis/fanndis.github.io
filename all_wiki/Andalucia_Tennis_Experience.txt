The '''Andalucia Tennis Experience''' was a professional women's [[tennis]] tournament played on outdoor [[clay court]]s. The inaugural edition was in 2009. The event is affiliated with the [[Women's Tennis Association]] (WTA) and is an [[WTA International Tournaments|International tournament]] on the [[WTA Tour]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sonyericssonwtatour.com/2/global/pdfs/events/2009/TourCalendar.pdf |title=2009 Sony Ericsson WTA Tour Calendar |publisher=WTA |accessdate=2009-01-29 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090117090639/http://sonyericssonwtatour.com/2/global/pdfs/events/2009/TourCalendar.pdf |archivedate=17 January 2009 |url-status=dead }}</ref> The scheduled 2012 edition was cancelled.
