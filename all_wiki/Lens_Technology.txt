'''Lens Technology''' is a Chinese [[technology company]] headquartered in [[Hunan]] that engages in the research, development, manufacture, and sale of lens products. It produces and sells touch panel cover glass, touch sensor modules, and touch panel covers.

Lens Technology was a member of the [[Forbes 2000]] list with market cap of $11.4B in 2015, but it dropped of in 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/companies/lens-technology/#5305750d6075 |title=#1747 Lens Technology |work=Forbes |date=6 May 2015 |access-date=2019-03-09}}</ref>

Its founder, [[Zhou Qunfei]], is the world's richest woman whose fortune is self made.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/chloesorvino/2017/03/08/the-worlds-56-self-made-women-billionaires-the-definitive-ranking/#4bdfbf5068a2 |title=The World's 56 Self-Made Women Billionaires: The Definitive Ranking |work=Forbes |date=8 March 2017 |access-date=2019-03-09}}</ref>
