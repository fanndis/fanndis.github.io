==Trivia==
===General===
* The award was not given at the [[1st Screen Actors Guild Awards]].
* 2004 is the only year not to have five nominees.

===Films===
* ''[[The Full Monty]]'', ''[[The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King]]'', and ''[[Parasite (2019 film)|Parasite]]'' are the only winners not to be nominated in any other category.
* ''[[Gosford Park]]'' has the largest winning cast (20 credited actors).
* ''[[Bobby (2006 film)|Bobby]]'' has the largest nominated cast (24 credited actors).
* ''[[Sideways]]'' has the smallest winning cast (4 credited actors).
* ''[[Million Dollar Baby]]'' and ''[[Beasts of No Nation (film)|Beasts of No Nation]]'' have the smallest nominated cast (3 credited actors).
* ''[[Black Panther (film)|Black Panther]]'' is the first superhero film to win the award.
* ''[[Parasite (2019 film)|Parasite]]'' is the first non-English language film to win the award.

Two non-English language films have been nominated:
* ''[[Life Is Beautiful]]''
* ''[[Parasite (2019 film)|Parasite]]''

Fourteen nominated films not to be nominated in any other category:
* ''[[3:10 to Yuma (2007 film)|3:10 to Yuma]]''
* ''[[Bobby (2006 film)|Bobby]]''
* ''[[Crazy Rich Asians (film)|Crazy Rich Asians]]''
* ''[[The Full Monty]]''
* ''[[Get Shorty (film)|Get Shorty]]''
* ''[[Hairspray (2007 film)|Hairspray]]''
* ''[[How to Make an American Quilt]]''
* ''[[Hustle & Flow]]''
* ''[[In America (film)|In America]]''
* ''[[The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King]]''
* ''[[The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers]]''
* ''[[Midnight in Paris]]''
* ''[[My Big Fat Greek Wedding]]''
* ''[[Parasite (2019 film)|Parasite]]''
* ''[[Straight Outta Compton (film)|Straight Outta Compton]]''

===Actors===
* [[Cate Blanchett]], [[Russell Crowe]] and [[Brad Pitt]] have the most nominations with six each.
* [[Cate Blanchett]] has the most consecutive nominations (four, from 2001–2004).
* [[Russell Crowe]] has the most losses of any nominee (six).

Seventeen actors have been double-nominated in one year:
* [[Ed Harris]] (1995, won for ''Apollo 13'')
* [[David Paymer]] (1995)
* [[John C. Reilly]] (2002, won for ''Chicago'')
* [[Meryl Streep]] (2002)
* [[Ludacris|Christopher "Ludacris" Bridges]] (2005, won for ''Crash'')
* [[Terrence Howard]] (2005, won for ''Crash'')
* [[Martin Sheen]] (2006)
* [[Josh Brolin]] (2007, won for ''No Country for Old Men'')
* [[Russell Crowe]] (2007)
* [[Benedict Cumberbatch]] (2013)
* [[Edward Norton]] (2014, won for ''Birdman'')
* [[Mahershala Ali]] (2016, won for ''Hidden Figures'')
* [[Janelle Monáe]] (2016, won for ''Hidden Figures'')
* [[Lucas Hedges]] (2017, won for ''Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri'')
* [[Caleb Landry Jones]] (2017, won for ''Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri'')
* [[Al Pacino]] (2019)
* [[Margot Robbie]] (2019)
