'''''I Am Greta''''' is a 2020 internationally co-produced [[documentary film]] directed by Nathan Grossman, following climate change activist [[Greta Thunberg]].

The film had its world premiere at the [[77th Venice International Film Festival]] on 3 September 2020. It is set to be released on [[Hulu]] on 13 November 2020.

==Production==

In December 2019, it was announced Nathan Grossman would direct the film documenting [[Greta Thunberg]], with [[Hulu]] distributing.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2019/12/greta-thunberg-documentary-hulu-greta-climate-crisis-b-reel-films-nathan-grossman-1202810111/|title=Greta Thunberg Documentary Set At Hulu, Chronicling Climate Activist's Global Rise|website=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|first=Nellie|last=Andreeva|date=16 December 2019|accessdate=1 August 2020}}</ref>

==Release==
''I Am Greta'' had its world premiere at the [[77th Venice International Film Festival]] on 3 September 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2020/07/venice-film-festival-2020-lineup-full-list-1202996720/|title=Venice Film Festival 2020: Competition Light On Studios, Strong On Global Arthouse & Women Directors – Full List|website=Deadline Hollywood|first=Nancy|last=Tartaglione|date=July 28, 2020|accessdate=August 1, 2020}}</ref> It also screened at the [[Toronto International Film Festival]] on 11 September 2020<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tiff.net/events/i-am-greta|title=I Am Greta|website=[[Toronto International Film Festival]]|accessdate=5 September 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2020/08/toronto-talks-halle-berry-claire-denis-barry-jenkins-david-oyelowo-matthew-heineman-films-1203022631/|title=Toronto Sets Talks With Halle Berry, Claire Denis & Barry Jenkins; Adds David Oyelowo, Matthew Heineman Films|website=Deadline Hollywood|first=Tom|last=Grater|date=25 August 2020|accessdate=5 September 2020}}</ref> and at the [[Filmfest Hamburg]] on 3 October 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.filmfesthamburg.de/de/programm/Film/35261/I_Am_Greta|title=I am Greta on Filmfest Hamburg|website=Filmfest Hamburg|first=|last=|date=|accessdate=2 October 2020}}</ref> The film is scheduled to be released in the United Kingdom and Germany on 16 October 2020 by [[Dogwoof]] and Filmwelt.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Ravindran|first=Manori|date=2020-09-04|title=Greta Thunberg Hulu Doc Gets European, North American Theatrical Release (EXCLUSIVE)|url=https://variety.com/2020/film/news/greta-thunberg-hulu-doc-european-north-american-theatrical-release-dogwoof-1234759221/|access-date=2020-09-12|website=Variety|language=en}}</ref> It is scheduled to be released in the United States on 13 November 2020.<ref>{{cite web|last=Hersko|first=Tyler|url=https://www.indiewire.com/2020/09/i-am-greta-trailer-hulu-1234585673/|title='I Am Greta' Trailer: Hulu Doc Follows Young Climate Change Activist|website=[[IndieWire]]|date=10 September 2020|accessdate=11 September 2020}}</ref>

==Reception==

===Critical reception===
''I Am Greta'' holds a 78% approval rating on the review aggregator website [[Rotten Tomatoes]], based on 74 reviews, with a [[Weighted arithmetic mean|weighted average]] of 7.00/10. The website's consensus reads, "Audiences might not learn anything new from ''I Am Greta'', but its stirring chronicle of the young activist's efforts is inspiring."<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/greta_2020|title=I Am Greta (2020)|website=[[Rotten Tomatoes]]|accessdate=5 December 2020}}</ref> On [[Metacritic]], the film holds a rating of 69 out of 100, based on 20 critics.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.metacritic.com/movie/i-am-greta|title=I Am Greta|website=[[Metacritic]]|accessdate=5 December 2020}}</ref>
