'''''Bild am Sonntag''''' ('''''BamS''''') is the largest-selling German national Sunday newspaper published in Berlin, Germany.

==History and profile==
''Bild am Sonntag'' was first published on 29 April 1956.<ref name=Boger>Helmut Böger, ''[[Bild]]'', 8 May 2011, [http://www.bild.de/news/inland/bild-am-sonntag/dienstaelteste-redakteur-erzaehlt-die-story-der-zeitung-17780678.bild.html Die Story von BamS ist auch seine Story]</ref> The paper is published weekly by [[Axel Springer AG]] and is a sister paper to the Monday-to-Saturday ''[[Bild]]''. Its editor from 2008 to 2013 was Walter Mayer.<ref name=Mayer>[http://www.axelspringer.de/presse/Personalien-Chefredakteurswechsel-bei-BILD-am-SONNTAG-B.Z.-B.Z.-am-SONNTAG-und-HAMBURGER-ABENDBLATT_157879.html Personalien: Chefredakteurswechsel bei BILD am SONNTAG, B.Z./B.Z. am Sonntag und Hamburger Abendblatt] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130617051013/http://www.axelspringer.de/presse/Personalien-Chefredakteurswechsel-bei-BILD-am-SONNTAG-B.Z.-B.Z.-am-SONNTAG-und-HAMBURGER-ABENDBLATT_157879.html |date=17 June 2013 }} ''Axel Springer''. 10 July 2008</ref> The new editorship is taken by Marion Horn since 2013. The paper publishes the ''[[Car of the Year|Goldenes Lenkrad]]'' (Golden Steering Wheel) award each year.

==Circulation==
The ''Bild am Sonntag'' as well as the ''[[Bild (Zeitung)|Bild-Zeitung]]'' are amongst the German Newspapers with the largest losses in circulation in recent years.

During the second quarter of 1992 the circulation of ''Bild am Sonntag'' was 2.6 million copies.<ref>{{cite web|author=Georg Hellack|title=Press, Radio and Television in the Federal Republic of Germany|url=http://files.eric.ed.gov/fulltext/ED353617.pdf|work=Inter Nationes|accessdate=3 April 2015|format=Report|date=1992}}</ref> Its circulation was 2.5 million copies in 1997.<ref>{{cite journal|author=Frank Esser|title='Tabloidization' of News|journal=European Journal of Communication|date=September 1999|volume=14|issue=3|url=http://ejc.sagepub.com/content/14/3/291.full.pdf+html|accessdate=8 April 2015}}</ref>

''Bild am Sonntag'' had an average circulation of 2,023,000 copies in 2003.<ref name=turku>{{cite web|title=European Publishing Monitor|url=http://edz.bib.uni-mannheim.de/daten/edz-du/gda/07/med-ind-germany_en.pdf|work=Turku School of Economics (Media Group)|accessdate=27 March 2015|format=Report|date=March 2007|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171010103816/http://edz.bib.uni-mannheim.de/daten/edz-du/gda/07/med-ind-germany_en.pdf|archive-date=10 October 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref> The paper had a circulation of 2,161,502 copies during the third quarter of 2004 and 2,042,128 copies during the third quarter of 2005.<ref name=adn8/> Its circulation was 2,014,352 copies during the third quarter of 2006,<ref name=adn8/> and was 2,164,000 copies for 2006 as a whole.<ref>{{cite book|author1=Helmut K Anheier|author2=Yudhishthir Raj Isar|title=Cultures and Globalization: The Cultural Economy|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=W0WV1yvMboEC&pg=PA460|accessdate=24 April 2015|date=17 September 2008|publisher=SAGE Publications|isbn=978-1-4462-0261-6|page=460}}</ref>

Its circulation fell to 1,873,354 copies during the third quarter of 2007 and to 1,780,934 copies during the third quarter of 2008.<ref name=adn8>{{cite web|title=New circulation figures for the third quarter 2008|url=http://www.adnative.net/en/global/media-news-events/news-detail/?newsid=24957&title=germany-new-circulation-figures-for-the-third-quarter-2008|work=Adnative|accessdate=10 April 2015|date=14 October 2008}}</ref> The circulation of the paper was 1,577,764 copies in 2010<ref>{{cite web|title=World Magazine Trends 2010/2011 |url=http://www.revistas-ari.com/attachments/209_WMT_2010_2011_Europe.pdf|work=FIPP|accessdate=2 April 2015 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402165145/http://www.revistas-ari.com/attachments/209_WMT_2010_2011_Europe.pdf|archivedate=2 April 2015 }}</ref> and 1,388,009 copies in 2012.<ref>{{cite web|title=Europe Economics – consultation on VAT 2013 |url=http://www.magazinemedia.eu/wp-content/uploads/20130403-EMMA-Questionnaire-to-periodical-magazines-trade-associations-Draft02.pdf |work=EMMA |accessdate=1 April 2015 |date=3 April 2013 }}{{dead link|date=November 2016 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>  During the second quarter of 2016 the paper had a circulation of 1,066,330 copies.<ref>{{cite web|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160131001628/http://www.mediaimpact.de/en/artikel/Advertising-rates-Magazines-Advertising-rates-Magazines-2016_24609292.html|title=Advertising rates Magazines 2016|url=https://www.mediaimpact.de/en/artikel/Advertising-rates-Magazines-Advertising-rates-Magazines-2016_24609292.html|publisher=Media Impact|accessdate=18 September 2016|archivedate=31 January 2016}}</ref>
