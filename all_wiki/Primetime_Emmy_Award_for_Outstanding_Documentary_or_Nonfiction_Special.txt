The '''[[Primetime Emmy Award]] for Outstanding Documentary or Nonfiction Special''' is handed out annually at the [[Creative Arts Emmy Award]] ceremony. The category was called Outstanding Nonfiction Special prior to 2013 and [[Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Informational Series or Special|Outstanding Informational Special]] before 1998.
