The '''United States District Court for the District of Arizona''' (in [[case citation]]s, '''D. Ariz.''') is a federal court in the [[United States Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit|Ninth Circuit]].

The District was established on June 20, 1910, pending Arizona statehood on February 14, 1912.<ref>[https://www.fjc.gov/history/courts/us-district-court-district-arizona-legislative-history U.S. District Courts of Arizona], Legislative history, ''[[Federal Judicial Center]]''</ref>

The United States Attorney's Office for the District of Arizona represents the United States in civil and criminal litigation in the court. The current '''United States Attorney''' is [[Michael G. Bailey]].

== Organization of the court ==
[[File:U.S. D. Ariz. Divisions.svg|thumb|right|250px|Map of the United States District Court for the District of Arizona with its subdivisions: <br> {{colorbox|#006d2c}} Prescott Division <br> {{colorbox|#0868ac}} Phoenix Division <br> {{colorbox|#b30000}} Tucson Division]]

The United States District Court for the District of Arizona is the sole federal judicial district in Arizona.<ref>{{USCode|28|82}}</ref> Court for the District is held at [[Flagstaff, Arizona|Flagstaff]], [[Phoenix, Arizona|Phoenix]], [[Prescott, Arizona|Prescott]], [[Tucson, Arizona|Tucson]], and [[Yuma, Arizona|Yuma]]. Magistrate courts, established to hear violations on federal lands, are additionally located in [[Grand Canyon National Park]], [[Kingman, Arizona|Kingman]], and [[Page, Arizona|Page]]. 

The District is further divided into three divisions, with each of these having a central office.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.azd.uscourts.gov/clerk/clerks-office|title=Clerk's Office|publisher=United States District Court District of Arizona|website=United States District Court District of Arizona|access-date=May 22, 2018}}</ref> The divisions are as follow:

*[[Phoenix, Arizona|'''Phoenix Division''']] 
**This district comprises the following counties: [[Gila County, Arizona|Gila]], [[La Paz County, Arizona|La Paz]], [[Maricopa County, Arizona|Maricopa]], [[Pinal County, Arizona|Pinal]], and [[Yuma County, Arizona|Yuma]]. Its offices are located in Phoenix, Arizona.

*[[Prescott, Arizona|'''Prescott Division''']]
**This district comprises the following counties: [[Apache County, Arizona|Apache]], [[Coconino County, Arizona|Coconino]], [[Mohave County, Arizona|Mohave]], [[Navajo County, Arizona|Navajo]], and [[Yavapai County, Arizona|Yavapai]]. Its offices are located in Phoenix, Arizona.

*[[Tucson, Arizona|'''Tucson Division''']] 
**This district the following counties: [[Cochise County, Arizona|Cochise]], [[Graham County, Arizona|Graham]], [[Greenlee County, Arizona|Greenlee]], [[Pima County, Arizona|Pima]], and [[Santa Cruz County, Arizona|Santa Cruz]]. Its offices are located in Tucson, Arizona.
