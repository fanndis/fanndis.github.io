First-seeded [[Martina Hingis]] was the defending champion, and she successfully defended her title by defeating [[Conchita Martínez]], 6–3, 6–3 in the final to win the Women's Singles tennis title at the [[1998 Australian Open]].

This was the first Grand Slam tournament in which [[Serena Williams]] competed in the main draw.<ref>{{cite web |author1=Steve Wilstein |title=Serena Williams Beats Irina Spirlea |url=https://apnews.com/e3e46d500793b2ac7c551feabd0a4ff8 |website=[[Associated Press|AP]] |date=19 January 1998}}</ref> She drew her older sister [[Venus Williams|Venus]] in the second round, and was defeated.<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=dztekQ1YB34C&pg=PA22&lpg=PA22&dq=serena+vs+venus+1998+australian+open+second+round&source=bl&ots=K87Kn-jaKq&sig=hkNnFLErg33a1A20hUMYt61m_f4&hl=en&sa=X&ei=-iTuUOSwOazPmAX01oGIAw&ved=0CDIQ6AEwADgK#v=onepage&q=serena%20vs%20venus%201998%20australian%20open%20second%20round&f=false Serena and Venus Williams - A. R. Schafer - Google Books]</ref>
