'''Bessie Bryant "Bess" Stinson''' (December 24, 1902 &ndash; July 19, 1996) was an American politician and businesswoman.

Born in [[Hammon, Oklahoma|Hammon]], [[Roger Mills County, Oklahoma|Rogers Mills County]], [[Oklahoma Territory]], Stinson went to the [[University of Oklahoma]] and Draughon's Business College in [[Clinton, Oklahoma]]. In 1938, Stinson moved to [[Phoenix, Arizona]]. She worked in financial and business offices of various businesses in Arizona and Oklahoma. During [[World War II]], Stinson served in the [[Women's Army Corps]]. She was involved in the [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican Party]]. From 1976 to 1971, Stinson served in the [[Arizona House of Representatives]]. Then, she served in the [[Arizona State Senate]] from 1971 to 1975.
