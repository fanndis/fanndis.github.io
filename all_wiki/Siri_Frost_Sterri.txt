'''Siri Frost Sterri''' (born 20 September 1944 in [[Trondheim]]) is a Norwegian politician for the [[Conservative Party of Norway|Conservative Party]].

She was elected to the [[Stortinget|Norwegian Parliament]] from [[Sør-Trøndelag]] in 1985, and was re-elected on three occasions. She had previously served in the position of deputy representative during the term 1981&ndash;1985.

Sterri was a deputy member of the executive committee of [[Malvik]] municipality council from 1975 to 1979, as well as a member of Sør-Trøndelag county council during the term 1979&ndash;1983.
