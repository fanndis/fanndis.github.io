'''''Challenges''''' is a French-language weekly business magazine headquartered in [[Paris]], [[France]].<ref name="Group2003">{{cite book|title=The Europa World Year Book 2003|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=XLvU9lroRuUC&pg=PA1658|date=10 July 2003|publisher=Taylor & Francis|isbn=978-1-85743-227-5|page=1658}}</ref> Its motto is ''Que dit l'économie cette semaine?'' ("What does the economy say this week?" in English).<ref name=cit/>

==History and profile==
''Challenges'' was established in 1982.<ref name=publ/> The magazine offers articles on economy and business-related events and on politics and world affairs.<ref name=publ>{{cite web|title=Publicitas International to represent 'Le Nouvel Observateur' of France|url=http://www.publicitas.com/switzerland/media-news-events/news-detail/?newsid=38706&title=switzerland-publicitas-international-to-represent-le-nouvel-observateur-of-france#.VQQz6WSsXRA|work=Publicitas|accessdate=14 March 2015|date=31 March 2010|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402101848/http://www.publicitas.com/switzerland/media-news-events/news-detail/?newsid=38706&title=switzerland-publicitas-international-to-represent-le-nouvel-observateur-of-france#.VQQz6WSsXRA#.VQQz6WSsXRA|archive-date=2 April 2015|url-status=dead|df=dmy-all}}</ref><ref name=sell>{{cite web|title=Challenges|url=http://www.sellmedia.com.au/mediachannels.php?objectID=193|archive-url=https://archive.today/20150315094114/http://www.sellmedia.com.au/mediachannels.php?objectID=193|url-status=dead|archive-date=15 March 2015|work=sellmedia|accessdate=14 March 2015}}</ref> It is published weekly on Thursdays.<ref name=cit>{{cite web|title=Economic News|url=http://www.citedeleconomie.fr/Economic-News,208|work=Cité de l'Économie et de la Monnaie|accessdate=7 August 2015}}</ref><ref name=kmed>{{cite web|title=Challenges|url=http://www.kmedianet.es/en/media/france/print-media/magazine/challenges-2|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130713082909/http://www.kmedianet.es/en/media/france/print-media/magazine/challenges-2|url-status=dead|archive-date=13 July 2013|work=K Media|accessdate=14 March 2015}}</ref><ref name=hkonp>{{cite web|title=Factsheet 2013. Challenges (France)|url=http://www.publicitas.com/fileadmin/uploads/hongkong/Factsheets/2013/Print/Challenges_2013.pdf|work=Publicitas|accessdate=7 August 2015|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150807092545/http://www.publicitas.com/fileadmin/uploads/hongkong/Factsheets/2013/Print/Challenges_2013.pdf|archivedate=7 August 2015|location=Hong Kong|date=21 December 2012}}</ref> Previously it was published monthly<ref name=mor/> and then biweekly.<ref name=Group2003/>

Le Nouvel Observateur Group is the owner and publisher of ''Challenges''.<ref name=publ/><ref>{{cite web|title=World Magazine Trends 2010/2011|url=http://www.revistas-ari.com/attachments/209_WMT_2010_2011_Europe.pdf|work=FIPP |accessdate=31 March 2015|url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402165145/http://www.revistas-ari.com/attachments/209_WMT_2010_2011_Europe.pdf|archivedate=2 April 2015|df=dmy}}</ref> The company also owns ''[[Le Nouvel Observateur]]''.<ref name=publ/> Former publisher of ''Challenges'' was  Croque Futur.<ref name=mor/> The magazine is published by Regie OBS.<ref name=hkonp/>

''Challenges'' was named as the Business Magazine of the Year in France in 2010.<ref name=kmed/>

==Circulation==
In 2001 ''Challenges'' had a circulation of 264,000 copies.<ref name=mor>{{cite web|title=Top 50 Finance/Business/News magazines worldwide (by circulation)|url=http://www.magazine.org.tw/events/school/report/wmt/top50finance.pdf|work=Magazine Organization|accessdate=3 May 2015|format=Report|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141213185512/http://www.magazine.org.tw/events/school/report/wmt/top50finance.pdf|archivedate=13 December 2014|df=dmy-all}}</ref> The circulation of the magazine was 260,020 copies in 2008.<ref name=sell/> It fell to 250,065 copies in 2009<ref name=publ/> and 232,000 copies in 2010.<ref>{{cite web|title=Western Europe Media Facts. 2011 Edition|url=http://conan.lib.muohio.edu/ebooks/Western_Europe_Market_MediaFact/2011/Western%20Europe%20Market%20&%20MediaFact%202011.pdf|work=ZenithOptimedia|accessdate=6 March 2016}}</ref> During the period of 2011-2012 its circulation was 232,430 copies.<ref name=hkonp/>
