'''Rui Falcão''' ({{IPA-pt|ˈʁuj fawˈkɐ̃w̃|br}}; born in [[Pitangui]], on 26 November 1943) is a [[Brazil]]ian [[politician]], former president of the [[Workers' Party (Brazil)|Workers' Party]] (Partido dos Trabalhadores) and current [[Chamber of Deputies (Brazil)|Federal Deputy]] representing the state of [[São Paulo (state)|São Paulo]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://terramagazine.terra.com.br/interna/0,,OI1929423-EI6783,00-Reacoes+em+cadeia.html|title=Reações em cadeira|publisher=Terra|first=Francisco|last=Viana|language=pt|date=22 September 2007|accessdate=3 June 2017}}</ref> He also served as the President of the Workers' Party in 1994 and was elected again president of the party to the terms 2011–2013, 2013-2015 and 2015–2017. Preceded senator [[Gleisi Hoffmann]], who took office on 3 June 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://noticias.uol.com.br/politica/ultimas-noticias/2017/06/03/re-na-lava-jato-senadora-gleisi-hoffmann-e-eleita-presidente-do-pt.htm|title=Ré na Lava Jato, senadora Gleisi Hoffmann é eleita presidente do PT|publisher=Uol|first=Bernardo|last=Barbosa|language=pt|date=3 June 2017|accessdate=3 June 2017}}</ref> He was the Secretary of Government<ref>{{cite web|url=http://brasil.estadao.com.br/noticias/geral,despejo-ameaca-2-5-mil-familias-da-zona-leste-de-sp,20020715p18594|title=Despejo ameaça 2,5 mil família na zona leste de SP|publisher=Estadão|language=pt|date=15 July 2002|accessdate=3 June 2017}}</ref> of [[São Paulo]] during [[Marta Suplicy]]'s administration.
