'''''The Daily Front Row''''' is a fashion-industry publication, commonly known as ''The Daily''. Brandusa Niro is the editor in chief. In 2011, Niro bought a controlling interest in the magazine, which was formerly owned by [[IMG (company)|IMG]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/jeffbercovici/2011/01/06/img-sells-the-daily-to-founder-name-dispute-still-unresolved/ |title=IMG Sells The Daily to Founder; Name Dispute Still Unresolved |work=[[Forbes]]|date= January 6, 2011 |first= Jeff |last= Bercovici |accessdate=October 28, 2013}}</ref>

Niro founded ''The Daily'' in November 2002, with the first issue launching for [[New York Fashion Week]] in February of the following year. ''The Daily'' is distributed every day during [[New York Fashion Week|Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week]] in New York, chronicling the goings-on from the front rows to behind the scenes. The [http://www.fashionweekdaily.com/daily-in-print magazine] is distributed for free at the site of Fashion Week. For the remainder of the year, the online magazine is updated every weekday with fashion news, party reports, features, and ''The Daily''{{'}}s blog, [https://web.archive.org/web/20140209031226/http://www.fashionweekdaily.com/chic-report Chic Report]. In an article in ''[[Vanity Fair (magazine)|Vanity Fair]]'', her friend [[Graydon Carter]], the magazine's editor, called ''The Daily'' "the guiltiest pleasure of Fashion Week in New York."<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.nytimes.com/2007/01/07/nyregion/nyregionspecial2/07wearts.html |title= Building a Fashion Empire |first= Tammy |last= La Grace |date= January 7, 2007 |work=[[The New York Times]]}}</ref>

''The Daily'' also produces print issues during [[Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week Miami|Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week Swim in Miami]], and has produced issues for Fashion Weeks in Sydney, Australia; Toronto, Canada; Moscow, Russia; and Mexico City, Mexico. In addition, ''The Daily'' produces issues covering various fashion trade shows, including [http://www.enkshows.com/coterie Coterie], [http://www.wsashow.com WSA], and [http://www.nouveaucollectivetradeshows.com Collective].

In December 2009, ''The Daily'' branched out from fashion for the first time to produce a special issue for [[Art Basel|Art Basel Miami]]. In May 2010, three issues were produced for the [[Tribeca Film Festival]].
