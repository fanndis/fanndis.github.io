The '''LennonOno Grant for Peace''' is an award presented by artist and [[peace]] [[Activism|activist]] [[Yoko Ono]]. The grant, a sum of $50,000, has been awarded biennially to people and organisations chosen by Ono herself since 2002, in honour of Ono's late husband [[John Lennon]].<ref name=year2010>{{cite web|url=http://imaginepeace.com/archives/13031|title=The LennonOno Grant For Peace 2010 – IMAGINE PEACE|publisher=|accessdate=7 December 2016}}</ref>
