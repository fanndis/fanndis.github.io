'''Antônio Delfim Netto''' (born May 1, 1928) is a [[Brazilian people|Brazilian]] [[economist]], former [[Ministry of Finance (Brazil)|Minister of Finance]], [[Ministry of Agriculture (Brazil)|Agriculture]], and [[Ministry of Planning, Budget, and Management|Planning]] of Brazil, [[professor]]<ref name="OESP790315" /> and [[National Congress of Brazil|congressman]]. During his incumbency as Minister of Finance of Brazil, the country experienced the so-called [[Brazilian Miracle|''Milagre Econômico'' (the Economic Miracle)]], a time of unprecedented economic growth.<ref name="OESP790315" />
