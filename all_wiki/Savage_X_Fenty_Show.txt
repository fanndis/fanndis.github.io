'''''Savage X Fenty Show''''' is an American television special about the annual fashion show for [[Rihanna]]'s lingerie brand [[Savage X Fenty]]. Volume 1 premiered on [[Prime Video]] on 20 September 2019 and Volume 2 premiereed on Prime Video on 2 October 2020.

==Background==
In 2018, Rihanna launched a new lingerie line called Savage X Fenty.<ref name="vogue1">{{cite news |last1=Newbold |first1=Alice |title=Savage X Fenty: Everything We Know About Rihanna's Underwear Line |url=https://www.vogue.co.uk/article/fenty-savage |accessdate=1 October 2020 |publisher=Vogue |date=29 June 2018}}</ref><ref name="rs1">{{cite news |last1=Chan |first1=Tim |title=Travis Scott, Bad Bunny, Rosalia Performing at Rihanna’s Savage X Fenty Show on Amazon Prime |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/product-recommendations/lifestyle/watch-savage-x-fenty-show-live-stream-amazon-prime-1064126/ |accessdate=1 October 2020 |publisher=Rolling Stone |date=21 September 2020}}</ref> On September 12, 2018, the first Savage X Fenty Show took place at [[New York Fashion Week]].<ref name="vogue2">{{cite news |last1=Yotka |first1=Steff |title=At Savage x Fenty’s Runway Show, All Women Are Goddesses |url=https://www.vogue.com/article/savage-x-fenty-runway-show-rihanna |accessdate=1 October 2020 |publisher=Vogue |date=13 September 2018}}</ref>

==Release==
