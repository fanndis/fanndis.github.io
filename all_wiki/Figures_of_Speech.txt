'''Figures of Speech''' is a [[hip hop]] group consisting of [[Master of ceremonies|emcees]] "Eve" ([[Ava DuVernay]]) and Jyant. <ref>{{cite web |last1=Setaro |first1=Shawn |title=Before She Was Directing Blockbusters, Ava DuVernay Was an 'Excellent' MC |url=https://www.complex.com/pop-culture/2018/03/another-reason-to-love-ava-duvernay-used-to-be-a-rapper |website=Complex Networks |accessdate=8 August 2020 |date=8 March 2018}}</ref> They performed at the [[Good Life Cafe]] in the early 1990s <ref>{{cite news |last1=Hardy |first1=Ernest |title=Middle Of Nowhere Director (and Former Rapper) Ava DuVernay on the Future of Black Film |url=https://www.laweekly.com/middle-of-nowhere-director-and-former-rapper-ava-duvernay-on-the-future-of-black-film/ |accessdate=8 August 2020 |work=LA Weekly |date=11 October 2012}}</ref>and were featured on the ''[[Project Blowed]]'' compilation.<ref>{{cite web |last1=McKinney |first1=Jessica |title=Before She Was A Famous Director, Ava DuVernay Used To Rap |url=https://www.vibe.com/2018/03/ava-duvernay-rap-group-figures-of-speech-before-directing |website=Vibe |accessdate=8 August 2020 |date=8 March 2018}}</ref>

Eve went on to direct the feature-length documentary about the Good Life open-mic nights titled ''[[This Is the Life (2008 film)|This Is the Life]]'' (2008).
