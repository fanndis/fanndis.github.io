This is a list of '''[[UNICEF]] [[Goodwill ambassador|Goodwill Ambassadors]] and Advocates''', who work on behalf of the [[UNICEF|United Nations Children's Fund]] for [[children's rights]].

{{TOC limit|3}}

==History and purpose of the role==
[[Danny Kaye]] was the first to hold the position, with the title of Ambassador-at-Large granted in 1954. Other celebrities have followed, acting as international, regional or national ambassadors, depending on their profile, interests, and desired level of responsibility. The goal of the program is to allow celebrities with a demonstrated interest in UNICEF issues to use their fame to draw attention to important issues. This may take the form of public appearances and talks, visits to troubled regions, which draw attention from the media, and use of their political access to advocate UNICEF causes.<ref>{{cite web |title=Goodwill Ambassadors & Advocates |url=http://www.unicef.org/people/people_ambassadors.html |publisher=[[UNICEF]] |accessdate=20 February 2012 |date=18 May 2010}}</ref>
