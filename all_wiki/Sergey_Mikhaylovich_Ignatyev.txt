'''Sergey Mikhaylovich Ignatyev''' ({{lang-ru|Серге́й Михайлович Игнатьев}}; born&nbsp;January&nbsp;10, 1948 in [[Saint Petersburg|Leningrad]], [[Soviet Union]]) is a [[Russians|Russian]] economist, banker, and official.

From September&nbsp;13, 1996, to April&nbsp;5, 1997, he was an aide to President [[Boris Yeltsin]] for economy. From April 1997&nbsp;– March 2002, he worked as a First Deputy Finance Minister of Russia.

From March&nbsp;20, 2002, to June&nbsp;24, 2013 he was the Chairman of the [[Central Bank of the Russian Federation]].
