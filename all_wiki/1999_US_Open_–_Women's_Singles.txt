[[Serena Williams]] defeated world No. 1 [[Martina Hingis]] in the final, 6–3, 7–6<sup>(7–4)</sup>, to win the Women's Singles tennis title at the [[1999 US Open (tennis)|1999 US Open]]. This was Williams' first Grand Slam singles title, and she became the first [[African American]] woman to win a Grand Slam tournament in the [[Tennis Open Era|Open Era]].

[[Lindsay Davenport]] was the defending champion, but lost to Serena Williams in the semifinals.
