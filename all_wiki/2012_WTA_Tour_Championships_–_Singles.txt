{{main|2012 WTA Tour Championships}}
[[Petra Kvitová]] was the defending champion, but withdrew from the tournament after her first match because of [[nasopharyngitis]].

[[Serena Williams]] defeated [[Maria Sharapova]] in straight sets in a rematch of their [[2004 WTA Tour Championships – Singles|2004 final]]. Williams did not lose a set in the entire tournament, and as of 2019 remains the most recent champion to do so.     

[[Angelique Kerber]] and [[Sara Errani]] made their debut in the event.
