'''Marie Ulven Ringheim''' (born 16 February 1999), also known as '''Marie Ulven''' or professionally as '''Girl in Red''' (stylized in [[all lowercase]]), is a Norwegian [[indie pop]] singer-songwriter. She is known for appealing to people with her "[[bedroom pop]] anthems about romance and mental health."<ref name="thelineofbestfit">{{cite web|title=A teenage queer icon for queer teenagers, Marie Ulven is breaking out of the bedroom as girl in red.|url=https://www.thelineofbestfit.com/features/interviews/on-the-rise-girl-in-red|last1=Hannah|first1=Andrew|date=28 November 2018|website=[[The Line of Best Fit]]|url-status=live|accessdate=16 March 2019}}</ref> Her single "I Wanna Be Your Girlfriend" has gained over 150 million [[Streaming media|streams]] online, and was listed at No. 9 on [[The New York Times]] list of "The 68 Best Songs of 2018". Since 2018, Ulven has released two extended plays from her bedroom studio and has amassed over 7 million [[List of most-streamed artists on Spotify|monthly streamers]] on Spotify.

Ulven has been named a "[[queer]] icon" by ''[[Paper (magazine)|Paper]],''<ref name=":0" /> and a "phenomenon" that is "one of the most astute and exciting singer-songwriters working in the world of guitar music" by ''[[The New York Times]]''.<ref name=":2" /> Her music, which is made from the comfort of her bedroom, has amassed over 150 million streams as of October 2019.<ref name=":5">{{Cite web|url=https://www.awal.com/blog/spaces-girl-in-red|title=SPACES: girl in red Hit 150M Streams With Inclusive Self-Love And A $150 Mic|date=October 21, 2019|website=[[AWAL]]|access-date=October 24, 2019}}</ref> Since 2019, Ulven has been on two North American tours and two European tours,<ref name=":9">{{Cite web|url=https://p3.no/musikk/p3-gull-de-nominerte-til-arets-nykommer-er-klare/|title=P3 performed at festivals such as [[Lowlands (festival)|Lowlands]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.readdork.com/news/james-blake-the-vaccines-and-more-have-joined-the-bill-for-lowlands|title=James Blake, == Early life ==
Marie Ulven Ringheim was born in the town of [[Horten]], [[Norway]] on 16 February 1999.<ref name=":14">{{Cite web|url=https://www.nme.com/big-reads/girl-in-red-interview-nme-100-2020-star-2592144|title=The Big Read – Girl In Red: "World domination. That's what I want"|last=Trendell|first=Andrew|date=3 January 2020|website=[[NME]]|language=en-GB|url-status=live|access-date=10 January 2020}}</ref> She grew up in the town with her sisters and her divorced parents,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.them.us/story/girl-in-red-chapter-2-interview|title=Girl in Red's Music is Touching, Gorgeous, and Unabashedly Queer|last=Bennett|first=Willa|date=2 September 2019|website=them.|url-status=live|access-date=2 October 2019}}</ref> and described it as "quiet and kinda boring" in an interview with [[Triple J]] in October 2019.<ref name=":6">{{Cite web|url=https://www.abc.net.au/triplej/programs/2019-with-richard-kingsmill/girl-in-red/11615666|title=GIRL IN RED: from a Norwegian bedroom to beyond|authorlink=Richard Kingsmill|date=October 18, 2019|website=[[triple j]]|access-date=2019-10-24}}</ref> Her mother worked with technology and her father worked as a policeman.<ref name=":2">{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2019/03/27/arts/music/marie-ulven-girl-in-red.html|title=She's Becoming the Gay Musical Role Model She Never Had|last=Caramanica|first=Jon|date=27 March 2019|work=The New York Times|access-date=2 October 2019|issn=0362-4331}}</ref> Her grandfather could play the guitar and piano, however she grew up without instruments in the home.<ref name="thelineofbestfit" /> Ulven received her first guitar as a Christmas gift from her grandfather in 2012, but didn't start playing it until 2013 after losing interest in [[Fingerboard (skateboard)|fingerboarding]].<ref name=":6" /> She credits her grandfather for sparking her interest in music.<ref name=":6" /> Whilst in high school, Ulven was curious about becoming a teacher before she was introduced to the guitar and songwriting at the age of 14.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.papermag.com/girl-in-red-2639222000.html|title=Meet Girl in Red: The Queer Artist Writing Teen Love Songs|last=Kaplan|first=Ilana|date=17 July 2019|website=[[Paper (magazine)|Paper]]|url-status=live|access-date=2 October 2019}}</ref> She had taught herself piano, guitar and [[Record producer|music production]] from the comfort of her bedroom.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://diymag.com/2019/03/25/neu-recommended-girl-in-red-scalping-nov3l-more|title=Neu Recommended (Girl In Red, SCALPING, NOV3L & more)|date=March 25, 2019|website=[[DIY (magazine)|DIY]]|access-date=2019-10-11}}</ref> Ulven began by writing and releasing [[Music of Norway|Norwegian music]] and planned to study music, but never concluded that she was going to become a musician.<ref name=":0" />

== Career ==
=== 2015–2017: SoundCloud and "I Wanna Be Your Girlfriend" ===
After getting a [[Blue Microphones|Blue Yeti]] microphone from her father in 2015,<ref name=":6" /> Ulven began writing and releasing [[Music of Norway|Norwegian music]] to [[SoundCloud]] under the moniker of "Lydia X".<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bewaremag.com/girl-in-red-king-princess-question-du-genre-sexualite/|title=De Girl in Red à King Princess, qui sont ces jeunes artistes qui redéfinissent les questions du genre et de la sexualité ?|date=27 September 2019|website=Beware Magazine|language=fr-FR|url-status=live|access-date=2 October 2019}}</ref> She stopped attending guitar lessons after six months once her teacher refused to acknowledge her interest in songwriting and producing.<ref name=":6" /> Ulven coined the stagename "Girl in Red" after attempting to identify herself in a crowd to a friend via [[Text messaging|text]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.complex.com/pigeons-and-planes/2019/02/girl-in-red-interview|title=No Mixed Signals: An Interview With Girl in Red|last=Aquino|first=Tara|date=14 February 2019|website=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]|url-status=live|access-date=2 October 2019}}</ref> Using the new moniker, she published her debut single "I Wanna Be Your Girlfriend" on SoundCloud in November 2016,<ref>{{Citation|last=girl in red|title=i wanna be your girlfriend|date=November 11, 2016|url=https://soundcloud.com/lyfsuxx/i-wanna-be-your-girlfriend|work=[[SoundCloud]]}}</ref> where it got about 5 thousand streams within five months.<ref name=":5" /> Following the single's feature on the Norwegian music website ''NRK Urørt'', "I Wanna Be Your Girlfriend" amassed thousands of streams and gained Ulven a large online following.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/girl-in-red-mn0003783307/biography|title=girl in red {{!}} Biography & History|last=Donelson|first=Marcy|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Ulven now resides in the [[Grünerløkka]] district of [[Oslo]] and is openly [[gay]].<ref>{{Cite tweet|number=1321790104420163584|user=_girlinred_|title=For those who need to know:|author=Girl in Red|date=2020-10-29}}</ref> She studied music production and songwriting at [[Westerdals Oslo School of Arts, Communication and Technology]].<ref name=":2" />
