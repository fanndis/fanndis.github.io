'''Nicole Zefanya''' (born 24 January 1999), known professionally as '''NIKI''', is an Indonesian singer, songwriter and record producer; she is currently based in the United States. She is signed with the record label [[88rising]].

==Early life==
Nicole Zefanya, born 24 January 1999, was raised in [[Jakarta]], Indonesia. Her music is partly influenced by her mother, who listened to 1990s R&B like [[Destiny's Child]] and [[Aaliyah]] when she was young.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.vice.com/en_id/article/3kvjq9/we-talked-to-indonesias-randb-princess-niki-about-going-international-and-90s-nostalgia|title=We Talked to Indonesia's 'R&B Princess' NIKI About 'Going International,' and '90s Nostalgia|date=14 November 2017|work=Vice|first=Ethan|last=Harfenist|accessdate=11 June 2018}}</ref> She went to [[Pelita Harapan School]] from kindergarten to twelfth grade in [[Jakarta, Indonesia]], Indonesia.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-10-23|title=Biography: Nicole ‘NIKI’ Zefanya of 88rising|url=http://asiapacifictimes.net/biography-nicole-niki-zefanya-of-88rising/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2020-09-20|website=Asia Pacific Times.net}}</ref>

== Career ==
At 15, she was the opening act for [[Taylor Swift]]'s [[The Red Tour]] in Jakarta after winning a competition arranged by Swift and the ice-cream brand Walls, called Ride to Fame.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Bartleet |first1=Larry |title=NIKI: "Name one Asian artist that’s popping right now. I want to be the girl that sets that up" |url=https://www.nme.com/blogs/nme-radar/niki-interview-2018-2316838 |work=NME |accessdate=28 May 2018}}</ref> The gig became a catalyst for her YouTube channel, where she uploaded original songs and covers, gaining over 40,000 subscribers. She published two songs, Polaroid Boy and Anaheim, in 2016 and 2017 respectively, which can be found on Spotify under Nicole Zefanya.

In 2017, Zefanya moved to [[Nashville, Tennessee]] to study Music at [[Lipscomb University]]. She released two singles, “See U Never” and “I Like U”.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://milk.xyz/articles/in-the-studio-with-niki/|title=In The Studio with NIKI|publisher=milk.xyz|date=1 January 2017|accessdate=20 November 2017}}</ref> under the American mass media company [[88rising]] as an artist under their label.<ref name="vice.com"/> She released the song “Vintage” in May 2018 as the first single for her EP titled ''Zephyr''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8442670/niki-vintage-video-interview|title=NIKI Reminisces About a Former Love in 'Vintage' Video: Premiere|work=Billboard|date=2 May 2018|accessdate=7 May 2018}}</ref>

Zefanya has stated that she tries to empower Asians and Asian Americans through her music.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://popspoken.com/culture/2020/04/88risings-niki-opens-up-about-the-value-of-creative-freedom-and-pushing-past-her-anxieties|title= NIKI Opens Up About the Value of Creative Freedom and Pushing Past Her Anxieties|website=[[Popspoken]]|accessdate=23 April 2020}}</ref> In the [[Head in the Clouds (album)|Head in The Clouds]] music festival, NIKI took a moment to address the 10,000-plus audience gathered in Los Angeles’ Historic State Park. “I just want to say, as an Asian female, I do not take this day and this stage for granted.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.refinery29.com/en-us/2019/08/241493/niki-zenfaya-head-in-the-clouds-88-rising-interview|title=NIKI Wants Asians To Own Their Heritage|last=Morin|first=Natalie|website=refinery29.com|language=en|access-date=2019-10-31}}</ref> My hope is that above everything else today, that you feel heard, you feel understood, but most of all that you feel represented." NIKI's mission is to empower young Asian artists under-represented in the American music industry.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.scmp.com/culture/music/article/2153847/indonesian-singer-nikis-mission-empower-young-asian-artists-under|title=Indonesian singer’s mission to break America’s bamboo ceiling|date=5 July 2018|website=South China Morning Post|language=en|access-date=31 October 2019}}</ref>

After 88rising announced their collaboration with popular food festival [[626 Night Market]] to curate a food section at Head in the Clouds, Zefanya made a special guest appearance with a meet-and-greet at the 626 Night Market event at [[Santa Anita Park]] in [[Arcadia, California]] on 11 August 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/8509783/head-in-the-clouds-festival-adds-jackson-wang|title=Jackson Wang, iKON, DPR Live, Dumbfoundead Join Head in the Clouds 2019 Festival: Exclusive|website=Billboard|access-date=31 January 2020}}</ref>

In addition,, Nicole has a small participation in the final choirs of the remix of Midsummer Madness, entitled '''"Midsummer Madness 20"''' by the Venezuelan singer, graphic designer and producer, of Latin music, reggaeton, dancehall and moombahton, Daniel Alejandro Morales Reyes, better known as [[Danny Ocean (singer)|Danny Ocean]]. Also in this remix, [[Joji (musician)|Joji]], AUGUST 08, [[Rich Brian]] & [[Higher Brothers]] participate.
