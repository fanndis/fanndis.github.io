The [[Silver Jubilee of Queen Elizabeth II]] marked the [[Silver Jubilee|25th anniversary]] of Queen [[Elizabeth II]]'s accession to the thrones of the United Kingdom and other [[Commonwealth realm]]s. It was celebrated with large-scale parties and parades throughout the United Kingdom and the [[Commonwealth of Nations|Commonwealth]] throughout 1977, culminating in June with the official "Jubilee Days", held to coincide with the [[Queen's Official Birthday]]. The anniversary date itself was commemorated in church services across the land on 6 February 1977, and continued throughout the month. In March, preparations started for large parties in every major city of the United Kingdom, as well as for smaller ones for countless individual streets throughout the country.
