'''Julia Schramm''' (born 30 September 1985, in Frankfurt am Main) is a [[Politics of Germany|German politician]] and [[publicist]]. From April to October 2012, she was a member of the federal executive of the [[Pirate Party Germany|Pirate Party]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.spiegel.de/politik/deutschland/doppel-ruecktritt-erschuettert-piratenpartei-a-863683.html |title=Doppel-Rücktritt erschüttert Piratenpartei - SPIEGEL ONLINE |publisher=Spiegel.de |date= |accessdate=2013-05-29}}</ref> She left the party in 2014 and joined [[the Left (Germany)|the Left]] in 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.zeit.de/politik/deutschland/2016-01/piratenpartei-ex-mitglieder-unterstuetzen-linke |title=Ex-Piraten docken bei der Linken an |publisher=Die ZEIT |date=2016-01-21|accessdate=2016-06-23}}</ref>

Her book ''Click Me'', along with her (or her publisher's) pursuit of [[Copyright infringement|illegally distributed]] copies of the book, has caused controversy in Germany.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.spiegel.de/international/germany/pirate-party-member-julia-schramm-slammed-for-defending-copyright-a-856977.html |title=Pirate Party Member Julia Schramm Slammed for Defending Copyright - SPIEGEL ONLINE |publisher=Spiegel.de |date= |accessdate=2013-05-29}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://piratetimes.net/julia-schramm-and-dmca-takedowns-a-pirate-and-her-copyright/ |title=Julia Schramm and DMCA Takedowns – a Pirate and Her Copyright |publisher=Piratetimes.net |date=2012-09-19 |accessdate=2013-05-29}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://torrentfreak.com/fail-prominent-pirate-party-politician-polices-book-pirates-120918/ |title=FAIL – Prominent Pirate Party Politician Goes After Book Pirates |publisher=TorrentFreak |date=2012-09-18 |accessdate=2013-05-29}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.techdirt.com/articles/20120918/02161220415/top-pirate-party-member-has-dmca-takedown-notices-issued-her-name.shtml |title=Top Pirate Party Member Has DMCA Takedown Notices Issued In Her Name |publisher=Techdirt |date=2012-09-18 |accessdate=2013-05-29}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Morris |first=Kevin |url=http://www.dailydot.com/news/german-pirate-party-julia-schramm/ |title=German Pirate Party member stops people from pirating her book |publisher=The Daily Dot |date=2012-09-19 |accessdate=2013-05-29}}</ref> In 2016 she published a book on [[Angela Merkel]] called ''Fifty Shades of Merkel''.
