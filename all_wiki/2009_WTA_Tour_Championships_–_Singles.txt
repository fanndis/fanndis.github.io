{{main article|2009 WTA Tour Championships}}

[[Venus Williams]] was the defending champion, but lost to her sister [[Serena Williams]] in the final, 6–2, 7–6<sup>(7–4)</sup>. With the victory, Serena claimed the year-end No. 1 ranking for the second time in her career.
