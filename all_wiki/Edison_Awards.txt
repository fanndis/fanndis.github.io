'''Edison Awards''' is an American company that runs an annual competition honoring excellence in innovation in a broad range of categories.

==Company==

The Edison Awards were established by the [[American Marketing Association]] in 1987 and has been an independent enterprise since 2008.<ref name=Sprouse/>
According to [[crunchbase]], Edison Awards is a private, for-profit company with fewer than ten employees.<ref>{{citation |url=https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/edison-awards#section-overview |accessdate=April 10, 2020 |title=Edison Awards |work=crunchbase}}</ref>

==Awards==

The Edison Awards are named after the inventor [[Thomas Edison]]. 
They honor innovations in product and service development, marketing and human-centered design.<ref>{{citation |url=https://gopro.com/pt/st/news/max-wins-edison-award-2020 |accessdate=April 11, 2020 |title=MAX TAKES HOME SILVER IN 2020 EDISON AWARDS |publisher=GoPro |date=April 1, 2020}}</ref>
Categories range from green technology to medical breakthroughs.<ref name=HuffP2011/>
Entrants must meet strict innovation criteria and competencies as defined in the book ''Innovate Like Edison: The Success System of America's Greatest Inventor'' (Gelb & Caldicott 2007).<ref name=Sprouse>{{citation |url=https://science.howstuffworks.com/edison-awards4.htm |accessdate=April 11, 2020 |title=How the Edison Awards Work |author=Elizabeth Sprouse |work=How Stuff Works}}</ref>
The Edison Awards Steering Committee reviews nominations; the finalists are then voted on by a large panel drawn from business professionals, scientists and academia.<ref name=PCMagMusk/>
Entries are judged based on societal impact, creativity and marketplace success.<ref name=HuffP2011>{{citation |url=https://www.huffpost.com/entry/edison-awards-innovation_b_846402 |accessdate=April 11, 2020 |author=Sebastian Howard |author2=Clare Richardson |title=Your Substantive Awards Show: 2011 Edison Awards Honor Innovation in Technology|date=April 7, 2011|work=Huffington Post}}</ref>
In 2013, there were 14 categories and 38 sub-categories, with 131 award recipients.<ref>{{citation |url=https://techli.com/edison-awards-highlight-innovation-across-american-industry-titans/45250/ |accessdate=April 11, 2020 |title=Edison awards highlight innovation across American industry titans |date=April 26, 2013 |author=Sonali Basak |work=Techli}}</ref>
