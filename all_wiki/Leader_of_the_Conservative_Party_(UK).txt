The '''Leader of the Conservative Party''' in the [[United Kingdom]] is the elected head and most senior politician of the governing body of the [[Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party]]. To date, two of the 28 leaders have been women: [[Margaret Thatcher]] and [[Theresa May]]. The post is currently held by [[Boris Johnson]], [[2019 Conservative Party leadership election|elected in 2019]] as May's successor.
