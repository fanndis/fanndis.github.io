'''Nude by Rihanna''' is the third [[fragrance]] for women by [[Barbadians|Barbadian]] singer [[Rihanna]]. The fragrance was released on November 23, 2012 ([[Black Friday (shopping)|Black Friday]]), under the perfume line, Parlux Fragrances.<ref name=Huffington>{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2012/11/23/black-friday-2012-rihanna-mac_n_2155112.html?ir=Style|title=Black Friday Deals 2012: Rihanna's New Fragrance Plus Big Deals On Smashbox, MAC Cosmetics And More (PHOTOS)|publisher=[[Huffington Post]]|date= 2012-11-23}}</ref>

==Information==
The fragrance is described as a sweet, floral [and] fruity fragrance with a vanilla background. It also consists of woody and skin musky notes. Fruity aromas of guava, mandarin and pear are located at the opening of the composition. The heart is blended out of white flowers: gardenia petals, velvety Sambac jasmine and creamy orange blossom. The base consists of sandalwood, vanilla orchid and "second skin" musk.<ref name=fragrantica>{{cite web|url=http://www.fragrantica.com/perfume/Rihanna/Nude-16630.html|title=Nude Rihanna for women|publisher=Fragrantica.com |date= 2012-11-26}}</ref>
