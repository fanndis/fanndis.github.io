The '''Young Hollywood Awards''' was an award presented annually which honors the year's biggest achievements in [[pop music]], [[movies]], [[sports]], [[television]], [[fashion]] and more, as voted on by [[teenagers]] aged 13–19 and young adults. The award ceremonies also honor rising and promising young aged performers in Hollywood. The award ceremony usually features a high number of celebrities and musical performers such as [[Taylor Swift]], [[Selena Gomez]], and [[Nick Jonas]].<ref name="people">{{cite web|url=http://www.glamourvanity.com/celebrities/nick-jonas-wins-artist-of-the-year-at-young-hollywood-awards/|title=Nick Jonas wins Artist Of The Year at Young Hollywood Awards|last=Tan|first=Michelle|date=July 2, 2010|publisher=[[Time Warner]]|work=Glambourvanity.com|accessdate=July 15, 2010}}</ref> New artists such as [[Black Cards]] and [[Brazzabelle]] have also performed.

The Young Hollywood Awards was started in 1999.

==Categories==
In the 2010 awards, the following categories were present:

*Young Hollywood Newcomer of the Year<ref name="people" />
*Young Hollywood Artist of the Year<ref name="people" />
*Young Hollywood Superstar in the Making<ref name="people" />
*Young Hollywood Breakthrough of the Year<ref name="people" />
*Young Hollywood Comedian of the Year<ref name="people" />
*Young Hollywood Cast to Watch<ref name="people" />
*Young Hollywood Making their Mark<ref name="people" />
*Young Hollywood Style Icon<ref name="people" />

The categories which have been removed from the awards are Superstar of Tomorrow and Best Role Model.<ref name="people" />
