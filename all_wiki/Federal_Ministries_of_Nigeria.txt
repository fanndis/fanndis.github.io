The '''Federal Ministries of Nigeria''' are civil service departments that are responsible for delivering various types of government service. Each ministry is headed by a Permanent Secretary who reports to a Minister in the [[Cabinet of Nigeria|Federal Cabinet]].<ref>{{cite web
 |url=http://www.commonwealth-of-nations.org/Nigeria/Government/Government_Ministries
 |title=Government Ministries in Nigeria
 |publisher=Commonwealth of Nations
 |accessdate=2009-12-21}}</ref> 
Some government functions are provided by "commissions" or [[parastatal]]s (government-owned corporations) that may be independent or associated with a ministry.<ref>{{cite web
