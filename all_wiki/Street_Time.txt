'''''Street Time''''' is an American [[Drama (film and television)|drama]] television series created by Richard Stratton. The series stars [[Rob Morrow]], [[Michelle Nolden]], [[Alexander Conti]], [[Scott Cohen (actor)|Scott Cohen]], [[Erika Alexander]] and Christopher Bolton. The series aired on [[Showtime (TV network)|Showtime]] for two seasons from June 23, 2002, to October 29, 2003.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.popmatters.com/review/street-time/ |title=Street Time |publisher=PopMatters |date=2003-08-11 |accessdate=2017-04-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Terry Kelleher |url=http://people.com/archive/picks-and-pans-review-street-time-vol-57-no-24/feed/ |title=Picks and Pans Review: Street Time |publisher=People.com |date=2002-06-24 |accessdate=2017-04-01}}</ref>
