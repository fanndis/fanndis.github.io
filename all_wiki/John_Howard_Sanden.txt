'''John Howard Sanden''' (born 1935 in [[Austin, Texas]]) is an American [[portrait painting|portrait artist]].

==Biography==

===Early life===
Sanden graduated from the [[Minneapolis School of Art]] in 1956 with a bachelor of fine arts degree in illustration.<ref name=alumnus>[http://mcad.edu/125/alumni/john-howard-sanden Alumni Careers - John Howard Sanden, â€™56], Minneapolis College of Art and Design. Accessed 2013-12-04.</ref>

===Career===
Sanden was the [[Art director|Art Director]] for the [[Billy Graham Evangelistic Association]] from 1955 to 1970. He founded the [[Portrait Institute]].  He was also an instructor at The [[Art Students League of New York]]. He won the 2005 [[Portraits, Inc.]] Lifetime Achievement Award, for which a [[Portraits, Inc.#Scholarship foundation|scholarship]] is named in his honor.<ref>{{cite news |first=Michael |last=Huebner |title=Presidential portrait artist John Howard Sanden to speak Tuesday at Birmingham Museum of Art |url=http://www.al.com/entertainment/index.ssf/2013/09/presidential_portrait_artist_j.html |newspaper=AL |publisher=Alabama Media Group |date=September 23, 2013}}</ref>

For thirty-three years, Sanden had studios on the eleventh and then tenth floors of the South Studio Tower of [[Carnegie Hall]], [[New York City]], and in [[Ridgefield, Connecticut]].

On May 31, 2012, Sanden's [[John Howard Sanden's Portraits of President George W. Bush and Mrs. Laura Welch Bush|official White House portraits]] of former President [[George W. Bush]] and First Lady [[Laura Bush]] were unveiled.<ref>{{cite news| url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-us-canada-18290003 | work=[[BBC News]] | title=Wise-cracking George W Bush likes presidential portrait | date=May 31, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nysun.com/editorials/hanging-george-bush/ |work=[[The New York Sun]] | title=Hanging George Bush | date=May 31, 2012 |accessdate=2013-12-04 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.newstimes.com/local/article/Ridgefield-artist-paints-official-Bush-portraits-3608509.php |work=The New Times |publisher=Hearst Media Services Connecticut | title=Ridgefield artist paints official Bush portraits | date=June 4, 2012  |accessdate=2013-12-04 }}</ref>
