'''''The Red Sari''''' (El sari rojo), which is a dramatized biography of the life of [[Sonia Gandhi]], originally written in [[Spanish language|Spanish]] by [[Javier Moro]] and published in October 2008 in Spain.<ref name=sari1>{{cite news|title=Latest target for Congress censors: book on Sonia's life|url=http://www.indianexpress.com/news/latest-target-for-congress-censors-book-on-sonias-life/628265/0|accessdate=11 August 2013|newspaper=[[Indian Express]]|date=2 June 2010|author=Charmy Harikrishnan}}</ref> It ran into some controversy in India.<ref name=10qs>{{cite news|last=Reddy|first=Sheela|title=10 Questions: Javier Moro|url=http://www.outlookindia.com/article.aspx?266086|accessdate=20 February 2014|newspaper=[[Outlook India]]|date=12 July 2010}}</ref><ref name=IE_latest>{{cite news|title=Latest target for Congress censors: book on Soniaâ€™s life|url=http://indianexpress.com/article/news-archive/web/latest-target-for-congress-censors-book-on-sonias-life/99/|accessdate=20 February 2014|newspaper=[[The Indian Express]]|date=2 June 2010|author=Charmy Harikrishnan}}</ref> [[Javier Moro]]'s Spanish and Italian publishers received emails from [[Indian National Congress]] lawyers and spokesperson [[Abhishek Singhvi]] with demands to withdraw the book from stores. Singhvi stated that book distorted facts and misinterpreted details.<ref name=IE_latest/>  The author always denied that the book is malicious.<ref name=10qs/>

The book tells the story of the Gandhi family through the eyes of Sonia Gandhi. Abhishek Singhvi claimed that the book violated a person's privacy for monetary gain.<ref name=sari3>{{cite news|title=Congress hits back at Spanish author|url=http://www.deccanherald.com/content/73382/congress-hits-back-spanish-author.html|accessdate=11 August 2013|newspaper=[[Deccan Herald]]|date=4 June 2010}}</ref> The book was finally released in India in January 2015 and was greeted with a big success.<ref name=RedSariNowOut>{{cite news|title=Controversial Sonia Gandhi book 'Red Sari' now out in India|url=http://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/politics-and-nation/controversial-sonia-gandhi-book-red-sari-now-out-in-india/articleshow/45901280.cms|accessdate=17 January 2015|work=[[The Economic Times]]|date=15 January 2015}}</ref>
