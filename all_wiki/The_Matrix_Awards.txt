'''The Matrix Awards''' is an annual awards ceremony held by the [[Association for Women in Communications]].<ref>{{cite web|title=National Review|url=http://www.nationalreview.com/blyth/blyth200504140804.asp}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Media Bistro|url=http://www.mediabistro.com/fishbowlny/parties/the_matrix_awards_media_revolutionaries_34762.asp}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=HuffPost|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/ariane-de-bonvoisin/the-matrix-awards_b_95602.html}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Portfolio.com|url=http://www.portfolio.com/views/blogs/mixed-media/2008/04/07/on-the-scene-at-the-matrix-awards/}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Nywici|url=http://www.nywici.org/matrix}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Awcdc.net|url=http://www.awcdc.net/matrixawards_09.shtml}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bizbash.com/newyork/content/editorial/7626_aol_hosts_packed_matrix_awards.php|title=Bizbash.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Zimbio.com|url=http://www.zimbio.com/pictures/lsAys0Xpuks/2009+Matrix+Awards/3Qmd39vor2g/Meredith+Vieira}}</ref>

It started in 1970 to honor exceptional women in the fields of arts, advertising, entertainment, film, television, theater, books, broadcasting, magazines, newspapers, public relations and [[new media]].
