'''Peter Jäger''' is a [[German people|German]] [[arachnologist]], and current Head of Arachnology at the [[Naturmuseum Senckenberg|Senckenberg Research Institute and Natural History Museum]] in [[Frankfurt, Germany]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Arachnology Staff|url=http://www.senckenberg.de/root/index.php?page_id=13589|website=Senckenberg Research Institute and Natural History Museum|accessdate=10 May 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171024103753/http://www.senckenberg.de/root/index.php?page_id=13589|archive-date=24 October 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref>

He named the spider ''[[Heteropoda davidbowie]]'' in honour of the singer [[David Bowie]].<ref name=Jage08>{{Citation |last1=Jäger |first1=P. |date=2008 |title=Revision of the huntsman spider genus ''Heteropoda'' Latreille 1804: species with exceptional male palpal conformations from southeast Asia and Australia (Arachnida, Araneae: Sparassidae: Heteropodinae) |journal=Senckenbergiana Biologica |volume=88 |pages=239–310 |mode=cs1 |ref=harv }}</ref>

In June 2020, Jäger named a new new genus of [[huntsman spider]]s from [[Madagascar]] after [[Greta Thunberg]]. The new spider is named Thunberga greta.<ref>{{cite news |last=Staff |first=Writer |title=New spider species named after Greta |work=[[France 24]] |date=2020-06-12 }}</ref>
