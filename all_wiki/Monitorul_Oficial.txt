'''''Monitorul Oficial al României''''' is the official [[government gazette|gazette]] of [[Romania]], in which all the [[promulgation|promulgated]] bills, [[President of Romania|presidential]] decrees, [[Government of Romania|governmental]] ordinances and other major legal acts are published.
