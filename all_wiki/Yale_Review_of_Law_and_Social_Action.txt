The '''''Yale Review of Law and Social Action''''' was a student-edited quarterly that was published by [[Yale University]] from 1970 to 1973. [[Hillary Rodham]] served on its Board of Editors and was an associate editor while attending [[Yale Law School]].<ref>http://www.encyclopediaofarkansas.net/encyclopedia/entry-detail.aspx?entryID=2744</ref><ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=toXIpmQ_TD0C&lpg=PA65&ots=UMoSHYKVmh&dq=yale%20review%20of%20law%20and%20social%20action&pg=PA66#v=onepage&q=yale%20review%20of%20law%20and%20social%20action&f=false Google Books - A Woman in Charge: The Life of Hillary Rodham Clinton]</ref>
