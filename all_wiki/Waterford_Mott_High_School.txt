'''Waterford Mott High School''' is a public high school in the [[Waterford School District]] located in [[Waterford, Michigan|Waterford]], Michigan. The official name of the high school is [[Charles S. Mott]] High School, named for the automotive industry pioneer.

==About the school==

Waterford Mott High School opened in 1969, with the first graduating class in June 1970.

In 1983, the school district consolidated high schools and closed Waterford Township High School. This left two high schools in the district; Mott and Waterford Kettering High School.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://waterfordmi.gov/484/Schools|title=Schools – Waterford, MI|author=|date=|website=waterfordmi.gov|accessdate=May 25, 2017}}</ref><ref name="waterford.k12.mi.us">{{cite web|url=http://www.waterford.k12.mi.us/pages/61/mott|title=Waterford School District-Mott High School|author=|date=|website=www.waterford.k12.mi.us|accessdate=May 25, 2017}}</ref>

In the fall of 1990, ninth graders joined the Mott student body.

As of the 2018–2019 school year, its enrollment was 1,559 students in grades nine through twelve.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mhsaa.com/schools/waterfordmott|title=MHSAA > Schools|author=|date=|website=www.mhsaa.com|accessdate=October 17, 2018}}</ref>

Students are offered a variety of classes in all four core areas as well as art, business, [[computer science]], engineering, [[foreign language]], [[health occupations]], music, [[physical education]], technology, theater, and [[video production]].<ref name="waterford.k12.mi.us"/>

==Extracurricular activities==

Waterford Mott hosts a variety of clubs and programs including: the [[Anti-Defamation League]], Bible Club, Board Game Club, [[Chess Club]], DECA Marketing Club, [[Destination Imagination]], Drama Club, [[International Thespian Society]] (ITS), [[Gay/Straight Alliance]], Hazards of Tobacco (HOT), [[Link Crew]], [[National Art Honor Society]], National Technical Honor Society [[National Honor Society]] (NHS), Positive Peer Influence (PPI), [[Quiz Bowl]], [[Students Against Destructive Decisions]] (SADD), [[Student Council]], Student Leadership, [[Robotics]], and [[Freestyle Walking]].

Waterford Mott also offers thirty-two different [[sports]] to both boys and girls students.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mottathletics.com/|title=Mott Corsairs Athletics|author=|date=|website=mottathletics.com|accessdate=May 25, 2017}}</ref> The Corsairs compete in the [[Kensington Lakes Activities Association]] and are members of the [[Michigan High School Athletic Association]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mhsaa.com/about-the-mhsaa/leagues-conferences/league-membership/leagueid/6931|title=MHSAA > About the MHSAA > Leagues & Conferences > League Membership|author=|date=|website=www.mhsaa.com|accessdate=May 25, 2017}}</ref>
