'''Mieke Offeciers-Van De Wiele''' (born 12 August 1952 in [[Kruibeke]]) is a [[Belgium|Belgian]] businesswoman. From 7 March 1992 to 5 September 1993, she was Belgium's Minister of Budget.

== Biography ==
Mieke Offeciers has a law degree.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.lesoir.be/art/offeciers-la-grande-mechante-louve-skiait-_t-19920311-Z0535J.html|title=OFFECIERS, LA GRANDE MECHANTE LOUVE, SKIAIT...|website=Le Soir|language=fr|access-date=2019-10-28}}</ref> She started her career at the engineering department of the [[VOKA|VEV]] in 1975.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.gva.be/cnt/oid88150/archief-mieke-offeciers-weg-bij-vev|title=Mieke Offeciers weg bij VEV|website=Gazet van Antwerpen|language=nl-BE|access-date=2019-10-28}}</ref> Since 1986, she was head of the study department of the VEV, often lobbying against the shady financial flows between [[Flanders]] and [[Wallonia]].<ref name=":1" />

From 7 March 1992 to 5 September 1993, she was Belgium's Minister of Budget for the [[Christelijke Volkspartij|CVP]] party.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.commissionroyalehistoire.be/belelite/nl/pers/personsid/0/1883|title=Index van ministers en staatssecretarissen|last=|first=|date=|website=Belelite|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-10-29}}</ref> After a tenure of 18 months, she returned to work for the VEV as CEO, succeeding to [[René De Feyter]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nieuwsblad.be/cnt/g99j6lmr|title=Zij gingen hem voor|website=Het Nieuwsblad|language=nl-BE|access-date=2019-10-28}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.lecho.be/actualite/archive/mieke-offeciers-a-la-presidence-du-serv/8445706.html|title=Mieke Offeciers à la présidence du SERV|date=2013-08-29|website=L'Echo|language=fr|access-date=2019-10-28}}</ref>

She left the VEV in June 2000 <ref name=":0" /> to work for [[KPMG]]. Since 2004, she has been working for [[Interel]], a public affairs and strategic communications consulting firm. In 2004, she also set up a small business to renovate and furnish houses.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://trends.knack.be/economie/magazine/een-bouwbedrijf-als-hobby/article-normal-1257783.html|title=Een bouwbedrijf als hobby|date=2004-07-01|website=Site-Trends-NL|access-date=2019-10-28}}</ref>

In 2008, she received a remuneration as board member of Infrabel through a proxy company, bvba MVD Consulting.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hln.be/geld/economie/offeciers-ontvangt-vergoeding-infrabel-via-bvba~a18fb522/|title=Offeciers ontvangt vergoeding Infrabel via bvba|last=|first=|date=5 February 2008|website=HLN|language=nl|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-10-28}}</ref>

== Other roles ==

* Former member of the [[Coudenberg group]], a Belgian federalist [[think tank]].
*Director of the board of Infrabel<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://infrabel.be/en/about-us|title=About us|website=Infrabel|language=en|access-date=2019-10-28}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
She is married to Erwin Offeciers, a doctor.<ref name=":1" />
