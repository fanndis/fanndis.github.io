'''''Little Fires Everywhere''''' is an American [[Drama (film and television)|drama]] [[streaming television]] miniseries, based on the [[Little Fires Everywhere (novel)|2017 novel of the same name]] by [[Celeste Ng]]. It premiered on [[Hulu]] on March 18, 2020 and consists of eight episodes.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Huff|first1=Lauren|title=Hulu releases first three episodes of Little Fires Everywhere early|url=https://ew.com/tv/little-fires-everywhere-hulu-releases-first-three-episodes-early/|website=[[Entertainment Weekly]]|accessdate=March 17, 2020|date=March 18, 2020}}</ref> The series stars [[Reese Witherspoon]] and [[Kerry Washington]], both of whom were also executive producers, alongside [[Liz Tigelaar]], Lauren Neustadter, and Pilar Savone. Set in the [[Cleveland]] suburb of [[Shaker Heights, Ohio]] during the late 1990s, it features Witherspoon and Washington as mothers from different socioeconomic backgrounds.

==Premise==
''Little Fires Everywhere'' follows "the intertwined fates of the picture-perfect Richardson family and an enigmatic mother and daughter who upend their lives. The story explores the weight of secrets, the nature of art and identity, the ferocious pull of motherhood – and the danger in believing that following the rules can avert disaster."<ref name="SeriesOrder">{{cite web|last1=Andreeva|first1=Nellie|title=Hulu Nabs 'Little Fires Everywhere' Limited Series Starring Reese Witherspoon & Kerry Washington|url=https://deadline.com/2018/03/hulu-series-order-little-fires-everywhere-reese-witherspoon-kerry-washington-1202336463/|website=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|accessdate=March 12, 2018|date=March 12, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180313021937/http://deadline.com/2018/03/hulu-series-order-little-fires-everywhere-reese-witherspoon-kerry-washington-1202336463/|archive-date=March 13, 2018|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Production==
===Development===
The book was discovered by [[Reese Witherspoon]] and Lauren Neustadter before it was published. Witherspoon went on to choose the book as her September 2017 book club pick and soon after it was published it became a bestseller. Witherspoon then brought the book to [[Kerry Washington]], and together the pair approached [[Liz Tigelaar]] to adapt and showrun the novel as a limited series. The project began its development at ABC Signature, the cable/streaming division of ABC Studios where Witherspoon's [[Hello Sunshine (company)|Hello Sunshine]] has a network-only deal and Washington's Simpson Street has an overall deal.<ref name="SeriesOrder"/>

On March 2, 2018, the production was officially announced but a network had yet to be determined. The series was set to be written and showrun by Tigelaar who will also executive produce alongside Witherspoon, Washington, Lauren Neustadter, and Pilar Savone. The author of the novel, Ng, would act as producer. ===Filming===
[[Principal photography]] took place in [[Los Angeles, California]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tvguide.com/news/kerry-washington-reese-witherspoon-little-fires-everywhere-hulu-photo/|title=Kerry Washington and Reese Witherspoon Are Cooking Up Trouble in this Little Fires Everywhere Pic|website=[[TV Guide]]|first=Amanda|last=Bell|date=June 11, 2019|accessdate=April 20, 2020}}</ref> and ran from May 31 to October 23, 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.elle.com/culture/movies-tv/a27542281/little-fires-everywhere-spoilers-rumors-news-date-cast/|title=Little Fires Everywhere: Everything We Know About The Hulu Miniseries|website=[[Elle (magazine)|Elle]]|first1=Starr|last1=Bowenbank|first2=Savannah|last2=Walsh|date=March 2, 2020|accessdate=April 20, 2020}}</ref> The exterior of the Warren home is located in [[Pasadena, California|Pasadena]], with the exterior of the Richardson home in [[Hancock Park, Los Angeles|Hancock Park]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.refinery29.com/en-us/2020/03/9572686/little-fires-everywhere-filming-location-setting|title=Where You Can Find The World Of Little Fires Everywhere IRL|website=[[Refinery29]]|first=Rachel|last=Paige|date=March 18, 2020|accessdate=April 20, 2020}}</ref>

===Music===
The score was composed by [[Mark Isham]] and [[Florence and the Machine|Florence & the Machine]] keyboardist [[Isabella Summers]].<ref>{{Cite web| url=https://filmmusicreporter.com/2020/02/11/mark-isham-isabella-summers-scoring-hulus-little-fires-everywhere/ |title = Mark Isham & Isabella Summers Scoring Hulu's 'Little Fires Everywhere'| website=filmmusicreporter.com| date=February 11, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web| url=https://www.bustle.com/p/the-songs-in-little-fires-everywhere-will-make-you-want-to-break-out-your-discman-22632985| title=The Songs In 'Little Fires Everywhere' Will Make You Want To Break Out Your Discman| first=Rebecca| last=Patton| date=March 19, 2020| website=bustle.com}}</ref> The series' official soundtrack was released on April 17, 2020 by [[Hollywood Records]] and featured cover songs recorded for the drama and performed by Judith Hill, BELLSAINT, Ruby Amanfu, Lauren Ruth Ward <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://filmmusicreporter.com/2020/04/16/little-fires-everywhere-soundtrack-album-details/|title=‘Little Fires Everywhere’ Soundtrack Album Details {{!}} Film Music Reporter|language=en-US|access-date=April 28, 2020}}</ref> and an original song written by [[Ingrid Michaelson]]. <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2020/artisans/news/little-fires-everywhere-ingrid-michaelson-song-build-it-up-1234587095/|title=How Ingrid Michaelson Penned a Perfect Song for ‘Little Fires Everywhere’|last=Tangcay|first=Jazz|date=April 22, 2020|website=Variety|language=en|access-date=April 28, 2020}}</ref>

== Release ==
''Little Fires Everywhere'' premiered on March 18, 2020 on Hulu. Hulu's corporate sibling [[Disney+ Hotstar]] premiered the series in India concurrent with US telecast<ref name="Singh 2020">{{cite web | last=Singh | first=Anvita | title=Little Fires Everywhere: A beguiling tale of motherhood | website=The Indian Express | date=2020-06-21 | url=https://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/web-series/little-fires-everywhere-reese-witherspoon-kerry-washington-show-review-6460166/ | access-date=2020-08-23}}</ref>. Internationally, the series premiered on [[Amazon Prime Video]] in Europe, Latin America, Canada and Australia on May 22, 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Amazon Prime Video UK on Instagram: “We challenge you to watch this 15-second trailer for Little Fires Everywhere without saying “oh my god” about at least one cast member (PS…”|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/CAdZgw0IBYG/|website=Instagram|language=en|access-date=2020-05-26}}</ref>.

==Reception==
<!--AUDIENCE RECEPTION AND RATINGS: Do not include them! Wikipedia does not support user-generated content in its articles, and thus audience ratings cannot be included. On [[review aggregator]] website [[Rotten Tomatoes]], the series holds an approval rating of 79% based on 76 reviews, with an average rating of 7.09/10. The website's critics consensus reads: "Though ''Little Fires Everywhere'' at times plays it too safe, sparks fly when it lets well-matched leads Kerry Washington and Reese Witherspoon dig into the difficult questions it does dare to ask."<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rottentomatoes.com/tv/little_fires_everywhere/s01|title=Little Fires Everywhere: Season 1 (2020)|website=[[Rotten Tomatoes]]|access-date=April 29, 2020}}</ref> On [[Metacritic]], it has a weighted average score of 70 out of 100, based on 30 critics, indicating "generally favorable reviews".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.metacritic.com/tv/little-fires-everywhere/season-1|title=Little Fires Everywhere: Season 1|website=[[Metacritic]]|access-date=March 18, 2020}}</ref>

According to [[Hulu]], in its first 60 days online ''Little Fires Everywhere'' was the most-watched drama ever on the streaming service.<ref>https://twitter.com/jasonlynch/status/1275141844960030721</ref>
