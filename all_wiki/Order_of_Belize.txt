The '''Order of Belize''' is the second highest [[Order (honour)|honour]] awarded to foreigners in Belize by the government of [[Belize]].  It was instituted on 16 August 1991.<ref name="honours">{{cite web|title=NATIONAL HONOURS AND AWARDS ACT CHAPTER 162|url=http://www.belizelaw.org/lawadmin/PDF%20files/cap162.pdf|work=THE SUBSTANTIVE LAWS OF BELIZE REVISED EDITION 2000|publisher=Government Printer|accessdate=3 December 2011|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120206010238/http://www.belizelaw.org/lawadmin/PDF%20files/cap162.pdf|archivedate=6 February 2012}}</ref>
