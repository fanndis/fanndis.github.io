The '''2003 WTA Tour''' was the elite professional tennis circuit organized by the [[Women's Tennis Association]] (WTA) for the 2003 tennis season. The 2003 WTA Tour included the four [[Grand Slam (tennis)|Grand Slam]] tournaments, the [[WTA Tour Championships]] and the [[WTA Tier I]], Tier II, Tier III, Tier IV and Tier V events. ITF tournaments were not part of the 2003 WTA Tour, although they award points for the WTA World Ranking.
