'''Phebe Novakovic''' ({{lang-sr-Cyrl|Фиби Новаковић}}) is an [[United States|American]] businesswoman and former intelligence officer. She serves as the Chairwoman and Chief Executive Officer of [[General Dynamics]]. As of 2018, she is listed as the world's 25th most powerful woman in business by ''Forbes''.<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/vickyvalet/2018/12/04/the-worlds-most-powerful-women-in-business-2018/|title=The World's Most Powerful Women In Business 2018: Driving Change From The Corner Office|first=Vicky|last=Valet|website=Forbes|accessdate=Oct 28, 2020}}</ref>

==Biography==

===Early life===
Novakovic is of [[Serbs|Serbian descent]].<ref>http://www.infobalkans.com/2014/03/20/phebe-novakovic-provided-plane-take-saric-podgorica</ref>
Phebe Novakovic graduated from [[Smith College]] in [[Northampton, Massachusetts]] in 1979 and received an M.B.A. from the [[Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania]] in [[Philadelphia, Pennsylvania]] in 1988.<ref name="forbes">{{Cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/profile/phebe-novakovic/|title=Phebe Novakovic|website=Forbes|accessdate=Oct 28, 2020}}</ref><ref name="businessweek">{{Cite web|url=http://www.businessweek.com/person/phebe-n-novakovic.html|title=Bloomberg BusinessWeek|accessdate=Oct 28, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.smith.edu/about-smith/news/2016-smith-medalists|title=Outstanding Alumnae Named 2016 Smith Medalists|website=Smith College|accessdate=Oct 28, 2020}}</ref>

===Career===
She worked for the [[Central Intelligence Agency]].<ref name="forbes"/> From 1997 to 2001, she worked for the [[United States Department of Defense]].<ref name="forbes"/><ref name="businessweek"/>

She joined [[General Dynamics]] in 2001.<ref name="businessweek"/> She became president and Chief Operation Officer in 2012.<ref name="businessweek"/> She has served as the Chairman of the Board and Chief Executive Officer of [[General Dynamics]] since January 2013.<ref name="forbes"/><ref name="businessweek"/><ref name="abbott">[http://www.abbott.com/global/url/content/en_US/70.45.10:10/general_content/General_Content_00083.htm Abbott Laboratories Board of Directors] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130825004945/http://www.abbott.com/global/url/content/en_US/70.45.10%3A10/general_content/General_Content_00083.htm |date=2013-08-25 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.gd.com/|title=General Dynamics &#124; Home|website=www.gd.com|accessdate=Oct 28, 2020}}</ref>

She has sat on the Board of Directors of [[Abbott Laboratories]] since 2010.<ref name="abbott"/> As of 2018, she is listed as the 25th most powerful woman in the world by ''Forbes'',<ref name="auto"/> rising from 56th in 2015 and 65th in 2014.

===Personal life===
She is married.<ref name="forbes"/> In 2012, she earned US$6,887,772.<ref name="businessweek"/> As CEO in 2014, she earned $18.99 million, a majority of which is in company equity.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://seekingalpha.com/news/2388436-general-dynamics-ceo-earned-19m-in-2014|title=Stock Market Insights &#124; Seeking Alpha|website=SeekingAlpha|accessdate=Oct 28, 2020}}</ref>
