Project motivation

The goal of the project is to delve deep into the most powerful women, figure our their most immediate connections using data found online and come up with a recipe to become more powerful. Do the most powerful women in the world have something in common?

The data

The world is filled with data and for this project the data could be endless. To limit the scope of the project however the data that will be used is from three sources. First the list of 100 most powerful women of 2019 as well as lists of the top 10 most powerful women from 2004-2018 is gathered from the Forbes website. Then the Little sis website as well as wikipedia articles are used to gather information about the relationships the women have with organizations. Communities

The Louvain method for community detection is used to detect communities within the network. 43 communities were found and will the word clouds of the top 6 communities with the biggest number of nodes be listed here below.

  • The first community, and the biggest, centers around technology and finance. In the community there are around 40 of the most powerful women. To name a few: Abigail Johnson, Aileen Lee, Amy Hood, Julie Sweet, Lynn Good and Ruth Porat are all in this community. The women have one noticable thing in common, they are successful business women in the financial sector.

  • The second community revolves around the game Tennis. Serena Williams is the only powerful woman in this community which is understandable since there are no other female athletes on the most powerful lists.

  • The third community centers around the music industry. In the community there are 3 powerful women: Beyonc√©, Lady Gaga and Rihanna. These women have an obvious connection, they are all famous musicians

  • The fourth community revolves around the entertainment and arts industries. In the word cloud there are words such as Dreamgirls, Presley and Hurston which relate to the industry mentioned. In the community there are three powerful women: Ava Duvernay, Oprah Winfrey and Bonnie Hammer, who all are in the entertainment industry.

  • The fifth community revolves around Great Britain. In the word cloud there are words such as Salmond, Farage and Corbyn who are all politicans in Britain. In the community there are five powerful women: Ana Botin, Judith Mckenna, Kaherine Viener, Nichola Sturgeon, Theresa May who all have in common that they are either of british descent or live and work in Britain.
