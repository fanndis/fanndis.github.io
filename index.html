Forbes most powerful women

Each year Forbes creates a list of the top 100 most powerful women.
These women come from all over the world, they are politcians, musicians, CEOs,
presidents and activists. Are all of these women somehow connected?
Is there a certain formula that guarantees a woman to become more successful?


Project motivation

The goal of the project is to delve deep into the most powerful women, figure our their most immediate connections using data found online and come up with a recipe to become more powerful. Do the most powerful women in the world have something in common?

The data

The world is filled with data and for this project the data could be endless. To limit the scope of the project however the data that will be used is from three sources. First the list of 100 most powerful women of 2019 as well as lists of the top 10 most powerful women from 2004-2018 is gathered from the Forbes website. Then the Little sis website as well as wikipedia articles are used to gather information about the relationships the women have with organizations. The two networks

The two networks, the network containing only the little sis data and the network containing only the wikipedia data are plotted using the force atlas algorithm.
