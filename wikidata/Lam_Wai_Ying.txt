'''Lam Wai Ying''' ({{zh|c=林惠英|p=Lín Huìyīng}}) is a Chinese billionaire businesswoman, and the chairman and 49% owner of Biel Crystal Manufactory, manufacturer of touch screens for smartphones. She co-founded Biel with her husband [[Yeung Kin-man]] (楊建文).

== Career ==
Lam's Biel Crystal is based in Kwun Tong, Hong Kong,<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.hktdc.com/manufacturers-suppliers/Biel-Crystal-HK-Manufactory-Limited/en/1X095RL6 |title=Biel Crystal (HK) Manufactory Limited |publisher=hktdc.com |accessdate=December 20, 2018}}</ref> and its manufacturing facilities are based in China, which employs about 100,000 people.

As of September 2020, Lam's net worth was estimated at US$3.9 billion.<ref name="forbes.com"/>

In November 2017, Lam was assessed by ''[[Forbes]]'' as being the 67th most powerful woman in the world.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/power-women/list/2/#tab:overall|title=World's Most Powerful Women|author=|date=|website=forbes.com|accessdate=2 November 2017}}</ref>

Lam is also the co-owner of High Grand Development, a real estate development company.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.scmp.com/property/article/2065778/founder-iphone-screen-vendor-pays-hk28-billion-peak-mansion |title=Founder of iPhone screen vendor pays HK$2.8 billion for The Peak mansion |publisher=scmp.com |date=January 26, 2017 |accessdate=December 20, 2018}}</ref>
In 2018, Lam is ranked #5 as the Top 10 Richest Self-Made Women in the World.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.thedailyrecords.com/2018-2019-2020-2021/world-famous-top-10-list/celebrities/richest-self-made-women-world-america-net-worth/16261/#5_Lam_Wai_Ying-_41_billion |title=Top 10 Richest Self-Made Women in the World |publisher=the daily records.com |accessdate=December 20, 2018}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Lam's husband is Yeung Kin-man, co-founder and CEO of Biel Crystal. They live in [[Hong Kong]].<ref name="forbes.com"/><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.newsweek.com/who-are-richest-women-world-1012162 |title=Who Are The Riches Women in the World? |publisher=Newsweek.com |first=Eve |last=Watling |date=July 9, 2018 |accessdate=December 20, 2018}}</ref>
